13 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Vikas Dubey, the mastermind of the Kanpur shootout scandal, was killed in an encounter by the police. Yes, Vikas Dubey, the main accused in the killing of eight policemen, has been killed in a police encounter. STF team brought Vikas Dubey from Ujjain in Madhya Pradesh to Kanpur this morning. On arrival in Kanpur, the police car overturned on the way. Meanwhile, Vikas Dubey tried to escape by snatching arms from a policeman. During this encounter between Vikas Dubey and the police … Vikas Dubey was seriously injured in this encounter and was rushed to the hospital, where doctors declared him dead.
