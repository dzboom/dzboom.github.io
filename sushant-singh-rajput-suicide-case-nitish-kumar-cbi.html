Sushant Singh Rajput Suicide Case: Nitish Kumar ने की CBI जांच की सिफारिश वनइंडिया हिंदी

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar recommended a CBI investigation into the death of Sushant Singh Rajput today, shortly after the actor’s father pressed for it in a phone call.
