Rajasthan Political Crisis: Sachin Pilot के करीबी Vishwendra ने दिया बड़ा संकेत वनइंडिया हिंदी

16 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Vishvendra Singh, who is close to Sachin Pilot and removed from the post of Rajasthan cabinet minister, has said that today was 20-20, the test match is on since tomorrow. Besides removing Sachin Pilot from the post of Deputy CM, Vishvendra Singh and Ramesh Meena have also been removed from the post of ministers.
