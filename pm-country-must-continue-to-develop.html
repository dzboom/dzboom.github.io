PM: Country must continue to develop

31 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Malaysia must overcome challenges to become a developed nation whose successes can be enjoyed by all its rakyat, says Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin.
The Prime Minister said that in his National Day address on Sunday and pledged to make ‘Prihatin Rakyat’ (Caring for the People) as the main guiding principle of the government that has been translated in the hitherto-announced economic stimulus packages.
