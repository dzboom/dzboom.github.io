Vidya Balan in 'Halla Bol': In awe of Pankaj Kapur, I didn't know how to meet him

23 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Popular Bollywood actress Vidya Balan on the film ‘Halla Bol’ that she starred alongside Ajay Devgn, Pankaj Kapur directed by Rajkumar Santoshi. Vidya Balan: ‘Halla Bol’ is actually a call to war. Also in street theatre its been used again as almost a call to awaken people. So I think it’s being used in the same mood for the same purpose in ‘Halla Bol’, the film too. When You watch the film you will know exactly why the title is Halla Bol. The credit goes to them for not making me feel like a newcomer. They never made me feel any different in a sense that I was junior or anything of the sort. Rajji is a very lively person on the sets and on the sets there will always be banter.
