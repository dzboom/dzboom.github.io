Kerala Rains, Landslide : మళ్ళీ కేరళ అతలాకుతలం కుండపోత వర్షాలు, ఉప్పొంగి ప్రవహిస్తున్న నదులు...!!

08 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Flood-like situation continues in Kerala’s Wayanad district And other districts due to continuous rainfall since last three days. Heavy rains lead to waterlogging in Panamaram Village of Wayanad district. Officials say the landslide happened in the Rajamalai area of the district, which is around 25 km from the tourist town of Munnar
