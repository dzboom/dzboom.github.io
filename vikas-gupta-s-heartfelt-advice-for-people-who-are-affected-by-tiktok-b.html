Vikas Gupta’s Heartfelt Advice For People Who Are Affected By Tiktok Ban

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Indian Government recently banned 59 Chinese apps including popular video sharing app TikTok in the wake of India China face off in Ladakh’s Galwan Valley. However, before governments crucial decisions of banning the app, ban on TikTok came as shocker for many while others celebrated as it was spreading hate and cringy content. However, there were people who were making motivational and inspiring videos are the most affected by the ban. Many celebrities too came in support if ban on TikTok, on the other hand Bigg Boss fame Vikas Gupta shares a piece of advice for those who are upset and are unable to find any other work, sharing the video, Vikas wrote, "Let’s welcome new opportunities. Only when someone leaves you is when you have someone new coming in ur life. There are new opportunities waiting for you al . I have met some really amazing and hardworking souls through #Tiktok the first two were @thefizahkhan and #danish and from there onwards saw a bunch of talented artists. From #teentigada to #team07 from #Riyaz who when he met me first cried talking about #danishzehen to the #Houseguests in season 2 #akshaykakkar to #khushichoudhary some of the kids are the sole earning members of their family to some sending their sibling to study abroad. #Sunnychopra made on his own with sheer hardwork. Please remember this. Everyone of you. None of your hard work will got to waste. Just be ready for double the dedication and energy. All the best"
