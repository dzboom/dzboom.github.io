Chinese girl knocked over by ticket machine gates at train station

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A girl was knocked over by the ticket machine gates at a train station in eastern China.
In the CCTV video, shot in the city of Nanjing in Jiangsu Province on June 21, a mother scanned her card to open the gates and attempting to let both of her twin daughters to go through.
However, the gates closed when the second girl was about to walk through it, knocking the girl over onto the floor.
Fortunately, the girl was not injured seriously.
The video was provided by local media with permission.
