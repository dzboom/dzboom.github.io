Girl Uses Shoelace to Loop Around Car's Lock After Locking Herself Out

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This girl locked herself out of her car with the keys inside. However, she had a clever trick up her sleeve to unlock the car. She took the shoelace out of her shoe and passed it through the door crack, looping it around the lock to unlock the door. She successfully managed to open the door and saved herself from unnecessary trouble.
