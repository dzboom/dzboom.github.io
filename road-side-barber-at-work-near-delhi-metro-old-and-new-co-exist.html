Road-side barber at work near Delhi Metro: old and new co-exist

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The old and the new co-exist in India, often not in too happy a harmony, but they still get by together… A road-side barber at work near Delhi Metro: old and new co-exist. He has an Old Spice bottle on his table, and gives a hair-cut to a DMRC (Delhi Metro Rail Corporation) security agency guard before our camera.
