Sushant Singh Rajput's Last Chat With His Sister On June 10

05 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sushant Singh Rajput’s sister Shweta has shared the screenshot of last chat with the late actor. In the chats, Sushant’s sister who lives in the US can be seen asking him to visit her to the US. To this, Sushant had replied, ‘Bahut mann karta hai di.’ His sister then insisted him to come for a month. She wrote to him, “To aa jao na baby…come here for a month. Just chill here…Acha lagega tumhe…I won’t tell my friends that you are here…so koi botheration and milna jhulna bhi nhi hoga….we can just have a good time. Go for nice walks and hikes and long drives.” This conversation of the brother-sister duo happened on June 10 and 4 days later, on June 14, Sushant was found dead in his apartment. Sushant’s sister wrote in the caption, “Both of us got too busy with our lives. Bhai got into Bollywood and kept making us all proud with his accomplishments but I remained protective about him. I would often ask him to come and visit me in the US so that we can relive our childhood once again away from all the noise. I only wish I could have protected him from everything….I still wish I will wake up to see my Bhai… just right next to me and will realize this whole episode was just a nightmare and nothing more.” Seeing Sushant’s last message to his sister is heartbreaking and makes us think about the what ifs! What if he had considered going to US to stay with his sister! Meanwhile, Mumbai Police is currently investigating his case.

Sushant Singh Rajput,Dil Bechara,Chhichhore
