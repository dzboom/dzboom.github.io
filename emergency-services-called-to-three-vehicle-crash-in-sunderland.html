Emergency services called to three-vehicle crash in Sunderland

11 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Tyne and Wear Fire and Rescue Service have confirmed that firefighters attended a three-vehicle collision on Atkinson Road, at 8.08pm, on Tuesday, September 8.
A fire service spokeswoman said that the North East Ambulance Service also attended the incident but no one was taken to hospital.
Emergency services have left the scene.
