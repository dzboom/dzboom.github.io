Can This Airy GEMINI Determine Love vs. Friendship & Reject A Bad Date To Their Face? Elite Daily

We told this Gemini he had to make an actual decision and it was… not easy.
Astrology by Valeria Mesa and
Geminis classically get along with just about anybody. And while that’s all fine and dandy for making friends, it can really hurt them when it comes to love if they can’t make a decision about what a relationship is. Did we forget to mention Geminis classically can’t make decisions either? We put people-pleasing Gemini, AJ, on dates with a Libra, Scorpio, Capricorn, and Pisces and told him he HAD to decide whether it was a love connection or not. If it was NOT a love connection, he had to tell them to their faces. Based on astrology, these signs may not be the best match for him, so watch and see if he can overcome his Gemini nature and get vulnerable about who he truly wants to date again.
