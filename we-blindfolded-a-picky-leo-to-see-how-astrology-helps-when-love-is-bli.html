We BLINDFOLDED A Picky Leo To See How Astrology Helps When Love Is Blind Elite Daily

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

It’s like a low-budget ‘Love is Blind’, but instead of pods and weddings, we used blindfolds and astrology.
Astrology by Valeria Mesa and
Leos are the most egotistical sign in the zodiac and therefore, the most picky. We tried to give Morgan (Leo Sun, Taurus Moon, Scorpio Rising) a leg up on her personality and take looks out of the equation to see if she could form a personal connection first. We set her up with two Libras, a Capricorn, a Pisces, and an Aquarius to see if astrology could lead her to love or if it would be the blind leading the blind. She went on a blindfolded date with each man and then decided to see half of them face-to-face before deciding who to take on a second date. Check out this episode to see if astrology can find a match when love is blind.
Do you trust your horoscope for dating advice? Star-Crossed puts astrological theory to the test by using zodiac signs to match people for their best (or worst) love connections.
