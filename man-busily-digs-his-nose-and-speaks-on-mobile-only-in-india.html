Man busily digs his nose and speaks on mobile - only in India

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Ber and Kamrak for sale: Diwali buzz with man digging his nose Retail on Delhi’s streets - mobile shops on bicycles in Delhi, even as a man retails ber or Indian jujube (Zizyphus) and kamrak or star-fruit. another digs his nose while speaking on the mobile. Yet another has a sweaty armpit with a patch of sweat under his arm. Diwali is a major festival in India, more commonly known as the festival of light. This festival starts with Dhanteras and this day is considered as the beginning of financial year. This festival signifies the victory of good over evil. The actual meaning of deepavali is the awareness of the inner light. Its celebrated by burning crackers, lighting diyas and decorating houses & eating sweets.
