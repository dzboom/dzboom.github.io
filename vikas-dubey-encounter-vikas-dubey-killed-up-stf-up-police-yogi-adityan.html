Vikas Dubey Encounter Vikas Dubey Killed UP STF UP Police Yogi Adityanath वनइंडिया हिंदी

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Gangster Vikas Dubey, accused of Kanpur shootout scandal, was killed in an encounter with Uttar Pradesh Special Task Force. This encounter took place between 6 to 7 am on Friday morning. ADG Prashant Kumar of Kanpur Police in Uttar Pradesh held a press conference. He told the whole story of Vikas Dubey’s encounter. He said that earlier Vikas Dubey was asked to surrender but he fired with the intention of killing the policemen. After which the police fired on him in defense. And big news of the day.
