Guy Plays Catch With His Furry Pet Pine Marten as They Jump at him in Loop

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This guy played catch with his adorable pet pine marten. They stood at a specific spot on the couch and threw themself at their owner to be caught by him. Once he caught them and put them back, they jumped at him all over again and tirelessly went on. The duo looked hilarious as they played their unique game.
