ENG vs WI Stat Highlights 3rd Test 2020 Day 3: Stuart Broad Pushes Windies To Brink Of Defeat

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

West Indies find themselves on the brink of a series defeat after Stuart Broad took two late wickets to leave the visitors struggling on 10/2 at stumps on Day 3 of the England vs West Indies 3rd Test match. Broad moved just one wicket short of becoming just the second England cricketer and seventh overall to take 500 wickets in Tests. West Indies are still 389 runs behind with just eight wickets in hand and two full days of cricket still remaining.
