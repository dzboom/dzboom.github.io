Bikers encounter 6 trophy bull elks in Colorado's Rocky Mountain National Park

07 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A father and son went on a bike ride in Estes Park, Colorado, when they encounter six trophy bull elks strolling towards their path on Saturday (June 27).
"My son and I both riding our bikes…in Old Fall River Road at Rocky Mountain National Park and came across a group of six trophy bull elks strolling towards us," the filmer said.
"We stopped, waited, and watch these magnificent creatures pass us.
"It was a once in a lifetime experience."
