Cocktails with Cruising World Featuring David Thoreson

11 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Photographer, adventurer and sailor David Thoreson has made multiple voyages to the high latitudes, including the Northwest Passage. In a conversation with Cruising World editors Herb McCormick and Mark Pillsbury, he talks about the Arctic today and he and McCormick, who were shipmates on one Northwest Passage adventure, swap stories.
