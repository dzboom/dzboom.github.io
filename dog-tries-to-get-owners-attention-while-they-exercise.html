Dog Tries to get Owners' Attention While They Exercise

19 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Nova, the dog, tried to get their owners’ attention while they exercised indoors since it was raining outside. She initially bothered the couple by climbing all over them and going around the place when they did push-ups, to gain their attention. When they got up post-push-ups, they showed her affection by petting her and got ready to do squats, while she tried to include herself by jumping around them. She also got her ball and tried to play fetch with them as an attempt to gain their attention.
