Bizarre cow born with SIX legs and TWO tails in Vietnam

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A cow with SIX legs and TWO tails lives at a small family-run farm in Thanh Hóa Province, northern Vietnam.
Bizarre footage, captured on July 26, shows the six-legged cow that has two legs growing from its shoulder.
The two-year-old cow was born this way, according to its owner, and many curious people travel to witness the unusual animal.
