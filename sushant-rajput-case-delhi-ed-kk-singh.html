Report

The mystery of Sushant Singh Rajput’s death has not been solved yet … In this case, the ED is investigating the account of the actor and the transaction of money. In this case, many people have been questioned and recorded their statement. Has gone The ED had recorded the statement of his sister Mitu Singh from Sushant’s family recently. Now ED has also recorded the statement of Sushant’s father. According to sources, ED has recorded the statement of Sushant’s father in Delhi. The ED asked him about the missing money from Sushant’s bank account.
