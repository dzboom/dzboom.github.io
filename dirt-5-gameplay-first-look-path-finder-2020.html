29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Dirt 5 gameplay footage looks at the Path Finder events.
Codemasters has released some brand new gameplay footage of Dirt 5, with the footage focusing on the brand new Path Finder events.
Dirt 5 will be released on October 9th for PS4, Xbox One, and PC. It will also release on PS5 and Xbox Series X, with free upgrades available for those consoles, as well as Google Stadia.
