Munmun Dutta Refutes Rumour Of Her Participating In Bigg Boss 14

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Bigg Boss 13 which ended with lots of controversy, fun, and drama is all set to return to our television screens, the makers are leaving no stones unturned to find the suitable contestants for the show. Recently it was reported that the 14th season might be delayed due to the pandemic situation but nothing was confirmed. Now in the latest development, a source close to the show has revealed that Taarak Mehta fame Munmun Dutta will be part of the show. Later when the news of her involvement started making rounds on the internet, Munmun came in forward and declined the news, she wrote, "Hello everyone. The news about me appearing in the next season of Bigg Boss is NOT True… I enjoy watching the show but I am not going inside the house dont believe in fake pages. Thank You." Seeing the current pandemic situation, the production is taking all the precautionary measures to safeguard their crew along with contestants, and due to which they’re only contacting celebs who haven’t been to another country. Reportedly, Bigg Boss 14 will be heavily influenced by the social distancing rule and tagline of this season might be, ‘Bigg Boss 14 Hoga Rocking’.
