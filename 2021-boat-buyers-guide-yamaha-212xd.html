2021 Boat Buyers Guide: Yamaha 212XD

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Yamaha knows how to give you a big boat experience in a compact vessel that’s easy to tow and easy to stow. The Yamaha 212XD comes equipped with its own trailer, plus it has a huge touchscreen display, water sports capabilities, ergonomic and versatile seating, and tons of storage. Everything on the 212XD is standard––there are no options to select or overlook––making your purchase really easy.
