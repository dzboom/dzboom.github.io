Sophie Turner, Joe Jonas Welcome Baby

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas are delighted to announce the birth of their baby!
A representative for Turner confirmed the birth in a statement provided to CNN.
The couple married last year in two ceremonies.
One was a casual affair in Las Vegas and the other was a more lavish event in France.
Jonas, 30, is a member of The Jonas Brothers.
Turner, 24, is best known for her role as Sansa Stark in HBO’s "Game of Thrones."
