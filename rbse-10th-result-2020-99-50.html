Report

During the examination of Sikar, ten hand operations of the father and an atmosphere of worry throughout the day. By defeating all these concerns and challenges, the promising Hansraj Phogawat of Sikar made history in the result of tenth. As soon as the result came, the blurred picture of gum in the house suddenly sparked happiness with the success of the son. In the Class X result of Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education, the promising Hansraj Phogawat of Sikar has written a new success story by scoring 99.50 percent marks. Hansraj, a resident of Tasar Badi, has secured 100 out of 100 in five subjects.
