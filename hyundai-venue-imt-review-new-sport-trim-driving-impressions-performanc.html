Hyundai Venue iMT Review New Sport Trim Driving Impressions, Performance, Handling & Details

01 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

#HyundaiVenueiMT #VenueiMTReview #DriveSpark
Hyundai recently introduced a brand-new iMT (intelligent Manual Transmission) gearbox option on their popular Venue Compact-SUV. The new Hyundai Venue iMT comes on a new ‘Sport Trim’ offering, which is powered by the brand’s 1.0-litre T-GDi petrol engine option.
The Hyundai Venue iMT comes with a six-speed manual gearbox, with a clutchless two-pedal configuration. The iMT gearbox combines the ease of an automatic transmission with the fun of shifting gears manually. Here are our first impressions driving the new Hyundai Venue iMT.
