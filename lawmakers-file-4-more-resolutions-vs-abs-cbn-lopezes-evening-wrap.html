Lawmakers file 4 more resolutions vs ABS-CBN, Lopezes Evening wRap

31 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Today on Rappler – the latest news in the Philippines and the world:
- House probes loom over ABS-CBN QC property, P1.6-B condoned Lopez loans. The House of Representatives is not yet done with ABS-CBN and its owners.
- House panel approves 12% ‘Netflix, Lazada’ tax. The House committee on ways and means approves a 12% tax on digital transactions on Wednesday, July 29.
- Solicitor-General Jose Calida is now 2nd highest paid government official. The Commission on Audit reports Calida earned a total of P16.9 million in 2019.
- Senator Risa Hontiveros calls for special audit of gov’t spending on COVID-19 response. In the resolution, Hontiveros and senators Ralph Recto, Frank Drilon, Sonny Angara, Ping Lacson, Kiko Pangilinan, and Leila de Lima urge the COA to present its findings to Congress before it begins deliberating the 2021 national budget.
