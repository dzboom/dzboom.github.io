Real life Yogi Bear! Black bear casually joins a family for a picnic of peanut butter sandwiches at woodland cabin in Maryland

18 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A group staying in a cabin in remote woodland in Maryland were joined by a Black Bear who took a seat at their picnic table as they enjoyed lunch last Thursday. Remarkable pictures and video shared to social media show one of the men sitting at the table telling the bear to wait as he makes a peanut butter sandwich that the animal then eats straight from his hand. Onlookers cry in disbelief as the bear continues to wait for more to be made. It then sticks around for a few selfies before disappearing into the woods once more
