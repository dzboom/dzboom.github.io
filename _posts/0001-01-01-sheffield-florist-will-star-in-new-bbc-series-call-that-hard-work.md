Lucy Ashton, who launched The Dandelion Clock in 2010, is about to expose the realities of her day job to the nation as part of a new BBC show.  <br>‘Call That Hard Work?’ is a ten-part series which will see people immerse themselves in one another’s jobs – from actor to zookeeper, florist to factory worker – competing to see whose is the hardest.  <br>Lucy’s episode will air on BBC One at 3.45pm this Wednesday August 19.
