Donald Trump signed a presidential memorandum on Tuesday, reports CNN.  <br>It excludes undocumented immigrants from being counted in congressional districts.  <br>That is... when district lines are redrawn next year.  <br>Like the attempt to add the question onto the census, the memorandum marks another effort.  <br>This effort would likely impact the balance of power in states and the House of Representatives.  <br>The power is actually based on total population, meaning several factors weigh every move.
