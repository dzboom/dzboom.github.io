Rounding off a day of high drama in Madhya Pradesh, Governor Lalji Tandon Saturday ordered a floor test in the state Assembly on Monday, setting the stage for the Kamal Nath led Congress government to attempt prove its majority. The Governor said the test would take place immediately after his address in the House.
