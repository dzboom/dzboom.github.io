Sri Lankan cricketer Kusal Mendis was released on bail on Monday, a day after he was arrested for running over a pedestrian. The accident resulted in the death of the 64-year-old man.The Panadura Assistant Magistrate granted bail to Mendis on two sureties of 1 million Sri Lankan Rupees.
