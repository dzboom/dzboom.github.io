Muar MP Syed Saddiq Syed Abdul Rahman has brushed off criticisms from his former mentor Dr Mahathir Mohamad, over his plans for a new youth-centric political party.  <br>Syed Saddiq said his job was not to just unite the Malays, but to unite all Malaysians.  <br>Met in Kota Kinabalu yesterday, he said that he had great respect for Mahathir but firmly believes that diversity in Malaysia is our strength.
