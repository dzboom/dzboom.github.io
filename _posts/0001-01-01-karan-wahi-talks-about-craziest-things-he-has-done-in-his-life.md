Dil Mil Aaye actor Karan Wahi does not consider himself an adventurous person, though he does not mind trying out adventure activity. In an with a news portal, the actor recollected his experience of skydiving in Dubai. “I went to Dubai a few years ago and jumped off the plane. Nothing can be scarier. It was one of the craziest things I have done in life. It’s stupid. We were given a demo and a half-hour training schedule of what to do if a panic situation happens.” The Remix star has a dream to get clicked in front of the Seven Wonders of the World. On the work front, Karan is currently seen exploring his adventurous side on the Rohit Shetty’s Khatron Ke Khiladi: Made In India.
