The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has summoned actress Rhea Chakraborty again for questioning in connection with the money laundering case lodged against her after death of actor Sushant Singh Rajput. Family of Sushant accused Rhea of siphoning off Rs 15 crore from the bank account of late actor. Rhea, who was grilled for over 8 hours on Friday, has been asked to appear before ED on Monday.
