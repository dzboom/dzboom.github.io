And now for your daily dose of &quot;huh?&quot; Paraglider [Hasan Kaval]( has basically created a living room in the sky by fastening a couch, a TV and even a lamp to his parachute. Based on the guy's calm demeanor, you'd never guess he was soaring hundreds of feet up in the air — but then again, the level of chill required to kick back on a flying couch without a harness in the *first place* is probably pretty high. We only feel comfortable saying this knowing that Hasan landed safely — but that was *awesome!*
