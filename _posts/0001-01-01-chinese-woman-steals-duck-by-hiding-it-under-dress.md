A woman was captured stealing a bag of duck by hiding it under her dress in eastern China.  <br>In the video, shot in the city of Wuhu in Anhui Province on August 17, a woman walked into the kitchen of a food shop and put a bag of duck under her dress.  <br>The woman then pretended that she was talking on the phone and left.  <br>According to reports, the woman said she needed to buy over £100 cooked food for a baby's 1-month-old party and walked into the kitchen in the food shop.  <br>Police have arrested the woman and the case is in further investigation.
