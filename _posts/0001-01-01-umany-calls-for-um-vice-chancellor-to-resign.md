The Universiti Malaya Association of New Youth (Umany) has called for the resignation of UM vice-chancellor Abdul Rahim Hashim over his alleged negligence in handling sexual harassment claims made against a lecturer.  <br>In a statement, Umany alleged that Abdul Rahim's reported response, citing unspecified actions taken against the accused, was an attempt to &quot;cover-up&quot; the reported cases of sexual harassment against students.
