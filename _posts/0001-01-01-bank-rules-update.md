In the Corona Crisis, where people are facing economic crisis, some banks have given a shock to their account holders. Bank of Maharashtra, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank and RBL Bank have announced a change in the rules to impose a charge on the minimum balance from 1 August.
