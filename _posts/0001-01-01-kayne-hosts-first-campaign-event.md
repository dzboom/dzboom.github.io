Kanye West is running for President.  <br>West hosted his first campaign event since declaring himself a presidential candidate.  <br>Business Insider reports that at the rally West touched on a number of topics ranging from abortion and religion to international trade and licensing deals.  <br>West also spoke against historical figure Harriet Tubman, the conductor of the Underground Railroad.  <br>He says Tubman &quot;never actually freed the slaves, she just had them work for other white people.&quot;  <br>West wore a bullet proof vest at the event.  <br>At the rally, West revealed that his now-wife Kim Kardashian almost aborted their child North.  <br>Fans and supporters were disturbed by the rally and claim that Kanye needs help.  <br>Still, he's plowing ahead with his campaign.  <br>West has missed the deadline to qualify for the presidential ballot in four states.
