Prime Minister Narendra Modi has made a big announcement for taxpayers on Thursday. The PM said that the jugaad and recommendation for transfer posting from the new system will end. Due to the system being faceless, the impact of the income tax department will also end. Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Thursday launched Transparent Taxation-Honoring the Honest Platform for Taxpayers.
