One-third of COVID tests came back positive for the novel virus in Miami-Dade County, Florida.  <br>The county had a 14-day average of 24.87 percent, according to CBS News.  <br>Even 20 percent would be considered very high by most experts and medical professionals.  <br>The U.S. has set record-high rates for newly confirmed cases of the virus six out of the past 10 days.  <br>There were just shy of 60,000 reported nationwide on Thursday.  <br>Of those nearly 60,000, over 11,400 were in Florida, which has seen over 244,000 cases in total.
