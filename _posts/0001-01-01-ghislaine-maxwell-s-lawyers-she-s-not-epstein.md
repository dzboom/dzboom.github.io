Ghislaine Maxwell's lawyers told a judge on Friday that Maxwell and Jeffrey Epstein weren't terribly close.  <br>According to Newser, they made the statement in asking a judge to free her on a $5 million bond. Maxwell was arrested last week in New Hampshire.  <br>Prosecutors say Maxwell, 58, helped Epstein recruit, groom, and sexually abuse girls for years.  <br>Her lawyers told the court their client denies the charges, saying, 'Ghislaine Maxwell is not Jeffrey Epstein.'  <br>Maxwell is due in court Tuesday on her request to be released until her trial.
