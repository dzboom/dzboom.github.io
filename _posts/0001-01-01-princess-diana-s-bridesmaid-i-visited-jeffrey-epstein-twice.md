The Jeffrey Epstein scandal has reached its tentacles all the way back to the marriage of Britain's Prince Charles and Lady Diana Spencer.  <br>According to Elle Magazine, British writer Clementine 'Clemmie' Hambro was a young bridesmaid at the royal couple's wedding.  <br>Hambro has issued a statement clarifying her connection to Epstein, and the purpose of two trips she made to visit him. One of those trips was to his private island.  <br>She says one trip was with female colleagues, to look at Epstein’s new Santa Fe home to discuss what art he was going to buy.  <br>Hambro said the visit to Epstein's island was by personal invitation. She says was not abused, and saw no one else being abused on either trip.  <br>My heart breaks for all the survivors, now I know what happened on that island. Clementine 'Clemmie' Hambro Statement
