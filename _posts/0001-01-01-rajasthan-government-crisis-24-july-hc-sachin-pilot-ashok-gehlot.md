On Tuesday, the petition of Sachin Pilot was heard in the Rajasthan High Court. After hearing the arguments of both sides, the court has decided to pronounce the verdict on July 24. The High Court has stayed the Speaker's proceedings till 24 July. Mukul Rohatgi, who is pleading on behalf of the Sachin Pilot faction, said in the court that we are in the midst of the Corona crisis and are getting three days to respond. According to the rules, it should be extended for seven days. Mukul Rohatgi also said that the speaker showed haste and did not give any reason while issuing the notice.
