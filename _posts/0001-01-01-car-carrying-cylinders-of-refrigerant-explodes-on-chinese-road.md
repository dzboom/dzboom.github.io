A car carrying three Freon cylinders exploded when driving along a road in eastern China.  <br>The dramatic video, shot in the city of Jiaxing in Zhejiang Province on August 17, shows a front windscreen flying out and doors being blown open after a ball of fire suddenly burst out of an SUV moving on a road.  <br>According to reports, the motorist named Li carried three cylinders in his car while on his way to fix air-conditioners for customers when the explosion happened.  <br>Fortunately, Li was not injured and nearby people extinguished the fire.  <br>Li said the valve on the cylinder may not have be tightened up, causing the gas leaked out. The case is in further investigation.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
