The Republican governor of Missouri is pushing for children to return to school.  <br>Gov. Mike Parson’s controversial comments came during a Friday interview on KFTK.  <br>“These kids have got to get back to school... if they do get COVID-19, which they will — and they will when they go to school — they’re not going to the hospitals. They’re not going to have to sit in doctor’s offices. They’re going to go home and they’re going to get over it.”  <br>While the symptoms of COVID-19 are generally more mild in children than adults...  <br>...There have been many cases of serious illness and even deaths among juveniles.  <br>An 8-year-old girl in North Carolina died in early June four days after testing positive for COVID-19.  <br>A school-aged child in Oklahoma died this month of complications related to the disease.  <br>As of Monday, a child no older than 5 became the youngest person to die from COVID-19 in MN.
