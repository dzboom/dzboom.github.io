The 10th season of Big Bash League (BBL 2020-21) will start along with the first Test between Australia and India on December 3, and the final is scheduled on February 6. The Cricket Australia (CA) has also the WBBL fixtures. The WBBL will run from October 17 until November 29. The BBL will begin on December 3 with a clash at the Adelaide Oval.
