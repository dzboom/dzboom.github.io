Quinton de Kock has said AB de Villiers could have come out of international retirement for the 2020 T20 World Cup, which has been postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. De Villiers retired in 2018 but speculations over his return refuse to die down. &quot;He was definitely in line. If fit, I would have loved to have AB de Villiers,&quot; de Kock said on Star Sports. &quot;I think any team would have loved to have AB de Villiers in their team.
