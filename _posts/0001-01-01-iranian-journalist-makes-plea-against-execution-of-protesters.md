Iranian journalist and women’s rights activist, Masih Alinejad makes a powerful plea for three young men sentenced to execution in Iran.  <br>Amirhossein Moradi, Mohammad Rajabi, and Saeed Tamjidi are all reported to be in their 20's and were arrested in November 2019 for taking part in mass protests against the regime.    <br>The trio's execution has been suspended after a hashtag against their execution was used millions of times online.  <br>Another five demonstrators, Hadi Salehi, Mohammad Bastami, Abbas, Mohammadi, Majid Nazari, and Hadi Kiani, who were arrested during the 2017 protests, have now been sentenced to death.  <br>Masih Alinejad recorded her appeal from New York on July 18.
