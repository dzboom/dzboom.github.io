Wealth manager UBS did a survey of investors thoughts on the 2020 Presidential race.  <br>According to Business Insider, investors favor US President Donald Trump to support the economy.  <br>They also favor presidential nominee Joe Biden to manage COVID-19 effectively.  <br>A majority of wealthy investors said Biden would get their vote, while business owners said they would prefer a Trump reelection.  <br>Business owners have become more confident as their hiring intentions have risen since the pandemic-induced layoffs that began in March.   <br>Investor sentiment towards the short-term market outlook has improved.  <br>USB said 50% of investors expressed optimism on stocks compared to just about 44% in July.
