As Japan marks 75 years since the devastating attacks on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, the last generation of nuclear bomb survivors are working to ensure their message lives on after them. The &quot;hibakusha&quot; -- literally &quot;person affected by the bomb&quot; -- have for decades been a powerful voice calling for the abolition of nuclear weapons.
