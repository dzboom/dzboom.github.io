Over the past few years, German DJ and producer Alexander Ridha (best known as Boys Noize) has become a go-to collaborator for the likes of Frank Ocean, Lady Gaga and Rico Nasty. Still, his wizardry is at its sharpest when it’s just him and his modular synths, as exhibited in his FADER Fort set. Check it out above, and then head over here to learn more about his chosen charity, The Innocence Project, which aims to “exonerate the wrongly convicted through DNA testing and reform the criminal justice system to prevent future injustice.”
