Deputy Communications and Multimedia Minister Zahidi Zainul Abidin, who had accused a YouTuber of lying after she made a video of herself climbing up a tree for an online examination due to poor internet connectivity, said he got his information from Kudat MP Abdul Rahim Bakri.  <br>Zahidi said Abdul Rahim had, in turn, got his information from a lecturer at Universiti Malaysia Sabah (UMS). Therefore, Zahidi said, he had no reason to believe they were lying when he conveyed the information in the Dewan Negara.
