India has received five Rafale aircraft in the first batch. All the aircraft are flying to India from France. After a continuous seven-hour journey on Sunday, the aircraft landed at the Al Dafra airbase of the United Arab Emirates. Now Rafale planes have taken off from Al-Dafra and now they have no halt in between. He will reach India directly on Wednesday. After flying from Al-Dafra, Rafael was refueled at an altitude of 30 thousand feet in the air on the way.
