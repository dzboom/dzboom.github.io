Indian Railways gave gift to Bangladesh, flagged off 10 diesel locomotives. India Railways has given a big gift to Bangladesh Railways today. Foreign Minister S Jaishankar and Railway Minister Piyush Goyal have flagged off 10 diesel locomotives to Bangladesh today.
