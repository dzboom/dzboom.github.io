CVS will soon start stocking Stryx, a makeup brand geared towards men.  <br>Business Insider reports that Stryx will be sold at 2,000 of its retail locations.  <br>The Stryx brand was founded in New York by Devir Kahan.  <br>It offers products like cleansers and concealers.  <br>At CVS, Stryx products will be placed alongside items like shaving cream to help normalize makeup created for men.
