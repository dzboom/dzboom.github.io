Singaporeans started casting votes in a general election on July 10, 2020, with polls scheduled to be open from 8am to 8pm for the island state’s 2.65 million registered voters. The ruling People’s Action Party is widely expected to be able to comfortably extend its 61-year rule after the ballots are counted.  Video source: AP/AFP
