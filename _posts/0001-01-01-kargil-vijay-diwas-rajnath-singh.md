On the occasion of Kargil Vijay Diwas, Defense Minister Rajnath Singh has once again warned Pakistan and China. Rajnath Singh said that India does not attack first, but does not back down from giving a befitting reply if the attack occurs. Rajnath Singh said this in a message issued to the brave soldiers of the country.
