After the release of CBSE 12th result, now the students of 10th are also eagerly waiting for their result .... Meanwhile there is news of relief for the students of 10th also ... their wait will also end on Wednesday ... yes, the date of 10th result has also been announced. Union Human Resource Development Minister Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank has told that the result of CBSE 10th board will be announced tomorrow i.e. on Wednesday.
