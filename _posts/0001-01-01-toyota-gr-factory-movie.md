The production of the new GR Yaris will be carried out at the GR FACTORY, a dedicated production line that has been established at Toyota's Motomachi Plant which has a long and solid track record of manufacturing sports cars. This line produces high quality rigid bodies, assembles engines and strives for racing car quality through careful construction by the hands of highly skilled technicians, also called ‘Takumi’ team members. As a result, the GR Yaris is built with meticulous care and attention.
