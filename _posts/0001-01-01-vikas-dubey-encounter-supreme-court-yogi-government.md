The petition on the questioning of the infamous historyheater Vikas Dubey encounter of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh was heard. After hearing everyone's plea, the Supreme Court has ordered the formation of an inquiry committee in the Vikas Dubey encounter case. The court said that the government should issue the notification as soon as possible and the committee should start the investigation in a week. The inquiry committee should submit its report to the court within two months.
