Former Bigg Boss contestants Mahira Sharma and Paras Chhabra have reunited for a music video. Mahira says it has all the elements that their fans loved while watching them on Bigg Boss 13. The song is called Ring, and its video sees Mahira and Paras getting into cute fights. Talking about the song, Mahira said, “We wanted to give a Bigg Boss feel, show how we are in reality, without acting. People liked the fun and love, we have put all that in the music video.” As for music videos, she simply loves them. “I have always liked doing music videos. We can’t do much because of coronavirus. There are TV show offers also, but I don’t want to take risks. It’s all in Mumbai and shooting everyday…I am just waiting for this pandemic to get over.”
