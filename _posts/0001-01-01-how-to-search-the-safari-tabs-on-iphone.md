How to Search the Safari Tabs on iPhone?  <br>Step 1: Open 'Safari Browser' App.  <br>Step 2: Tap on 'Tabs' button at the bottom right, this will bring all tabs that you have opened.  <br>Step 3: Go at the top, and swipe down. This will bring the 'Search tabs.'   <br>Step 4: Now, Type the word or phrase relevant to the tab, and then tap 'Search' button on the keyboard. This will bring the  Tabs that you were looking for.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
