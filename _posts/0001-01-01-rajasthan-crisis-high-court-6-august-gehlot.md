The political crisis that continues in Rajasthan for several days is still quiet. And it looks like this is going to remain till the assembly session that starts on 14 August. The political battle of Rajasthan is being fought in the court. The appeal was filed in the High Court on Wednesday on the appeal filed by the BSP MLAs in the Congress merger case. The court will hear the case again on Thursday at 10.30 am.
