In mid July 2020, August Alsina extended the conversation about his recently revealed past relationship with Jada Pinkett Smith by dropping a new song titled “Entanglements.” It features a verse from Rick Ross and is co-produced by Alsina with Xeryus L. Gittens, The Exclusives, Cardiak, Tee Romano, and OG Parker.  The track finds Alsina giving his own definition of an entanglement, which is how Pinkett Smith described their relationship on her talk show, 'Red Table Talk.'
