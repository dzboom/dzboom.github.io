Every year in India, 5 September is celebrated as Teachers' Day. On this day the entire nation remembers the former Vice President of India, eminent educationist and great philosopher Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan and celebrates his birth anniversary as Teacher's Day.This day gives the students a chance to express their feelings towards the guru, who makes the path of life easier and shows how to overcome every difficulty. Special programs are organized on this day in schools and colleges.
