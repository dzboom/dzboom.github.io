The drug supply racket issue is rocking Bollywood following the death of actor Sushanth Singh Rajput   now the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB), has also busted Sandalwood (Kannada) actors for the same. Given the huge ruckus around consuming drugs, here’s taking a look at how Bollywood films have shown the usage of drugs. From Go Goa Gone to Masaba Masaba, Bollywood films have depicted drug usage, drug dealing as well as drug trafficking, but in the process, has Bollywood inadvertently normalised drug use?
