This girl took advantage of a beautiful winter day in Australia by practicing tricks with a multicolored hula hoop slinky. She placed the hula hoops around her waist, covering her torso and spun them around. The multiple joined hoops opened up to cover her entire body and then subside to its original position around her waist.
