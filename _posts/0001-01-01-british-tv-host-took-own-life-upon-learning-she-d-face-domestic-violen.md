A British TV presenter took her own life after learning she was to be prosecuted for allegedly assaulting her boyfriend.  <br>According to CNN, friends of Caroline Flack say she had believed the case would be dropped.  <br>Coroner Mary Hassell said on Thursday that the way the case &quot;played out in the national press&quot; was 'incredibly difficult for her.'  <br>Flack's assault on her boyfriend, 27-year-old Lewis Burton, took place when Burton was sleeping.  <br>As host of 'Love Island' since its inception in 2015, Flack was one of Britain's most well-known celebrities.
