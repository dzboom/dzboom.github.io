It was recently Ernő Rubik's birthday, and if that name sounds familiar, it's because he's widely known for inventing the Rubik's Cube puzzle. So, what better way to celebrate his birthday than by making a portrait of him out of Rubik's Cubes? Most of us wouldn't have the time, patience, or ability to do something like that, but [BigWendy]( was up for the challenge. In fact, she took it up a notch by doing it blindfolded! Wow, and here we thought it was impressive when we got one side of the cube all the same color.
