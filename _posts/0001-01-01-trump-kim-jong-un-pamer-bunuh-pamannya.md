KOMPAS.TV - Presiden Amerika Serikat Donald Trump mengatakan Pimpinan Korea Utara Kim Jong Un pernah memamerkan bahwa ia membunuh pamannya Jang Song Thaek pada Trump.   <br>Jang Shong merupakan tokoh sangat kuat di rezim kepemimpinan Kim. Jang Shong dieksekusi karena berkhianat dan korupsi pada 2013.   <br>Hal ini terungkap dalam sebuah wawancara Donald Trump dengan Jurnalis Senior Investigasi Washington Post Bob Woodward dalam bukunya &quot;RAGE&quot;.   <br>&quot;Woodward wrote that Trump said he was impressed with Kim when he first met the North Korean leader in Singapore in 2018 and that Kim was &quot;far beyond smart.&quot; Trump also said that Kim &quot;tells me everything&quot; and even gave the president a graphic account of how Kim had his own uncle killed,&quot; tulis kantor berita AP (10/9/2020).   <br>CTVNews juga menuliskan bahwa Trump menyebutkan Kim Jong Un membunuh pamannya dan meletakkan mayatnya tepat di tangga dan kepalanya diletakkan di dada.   <br>&quot;He killed his uncle and he put the body right in the steps, And the head was cut, sitting on the chest,&quot; ujar Trump dilansir dari AFP (11/9/2020).     <br>
