On the heels of the Michael Jordan documentary &quot;The Last Dance&quot;, there appears to be a boon in his memorabilia.  <br>According to CNN, one pair of the former Chicago Bulls star's shoes is expected to bring in over $500,000 at auction.  <br>The online &quot;Original Air&quot; auction is being organized by Christie's and sneaker consignment store Stadium Goods.  <br>The auction items are described as &quot;the most comprehensive sneaker record of Michael Jordan's era-defining Chicago Bulls career.&quot;  <br>Browsing opens on July 23, with bidding taking place between July 30 and August 13.
