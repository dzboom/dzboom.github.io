'Clueless' is an iconic teen film from the 1990s.  <br>Business Insider dug up some facts even 'Clueless' superfans may not know.  <br>Alicia Silverstone's performance in an Aerosmith music video helped her land the role of Cher.  <br>Silverstone ad-libbed the mispronunciation of &quot;Haitians.&quot;  <br>Director Amy Heckerling also used her own fear of driving as inspiration for Dionne's freeway scare.  <br>Silverstone got to keep all of her outfits from the film.  <br>According to Heckerling, the slang from the film came from a variety of people.
