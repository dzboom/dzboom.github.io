Actor Shilpa Shetty has welcomed Lord Ganesh at her home amid the coronavirus pandemic. She was spotted outside her residence as she stepped out to collect Lord's idol. She wore a face mask and hand gloves as precautionary measures. Shilpa Shetty and her husband Raj Kundra celebrate the festival with utmost spirit and joy every year. However, due to coronavirus pandemic, the celebrations are a bit low-key.
