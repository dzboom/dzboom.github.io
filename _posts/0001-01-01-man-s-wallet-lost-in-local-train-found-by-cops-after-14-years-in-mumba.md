A man named Hemant Padalkar, lost his wallet in 2006 in a train at Vashi Railway Police Station in Mumbai. According to a police official, Vashi Police Station police nabbed the thief and recovered the wallet at that time but cannot give it back to the complainant as it didn’t find him on the given address. During a campaign, police gave the wallet back to Hemant Padalkar.   <br>
