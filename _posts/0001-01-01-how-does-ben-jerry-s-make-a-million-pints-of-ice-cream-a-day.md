Ben &amp; Jerry's is the best-selling single ice cream brand in the world.  <br>It's gained a cult following thanks to classic flavors like Half Baked and Cherry Garcia.  <br>The company is on a mission to use ice cream to fight for equality.  <br>Business Insider visits the plant in St. Albans, Vermont, to see how Ben &amp; Jerry's pumps out nearly 1 million pints a day.  <br>It takes hundreds of workers, special machinery, and a 24/7 operation to package up these pints.
