The Health Ministry held a press conference on Thursday about the current condition of Corona in the country. The ministry said that we are the second largest country in the world on the basis of population. Despite having such a large population, we have done satisfactory work on the corona. In terms of population, we are at the bottom of the corona compared to other countries of the world. The ministry said that there are 15 deaths per 10 lakh population in the country.
