Nishant Singh Malkani who plays a Sikh character in Guddan Tumse Na Ho Payega, says he is enjoying every bit of it. Nishant essays a Sikh in the comedy drama in the show. Talking about his role, he says, “It’s the first time I am playing a Sikh character on-screen and I am totally loving this new look and the impact it brings to my character. In fact, my look is very similar to that of Ranbir Kapoor’s Rocket Singh, right from the tucked in shirts and the colourful turbans as well as his demeanour and body language I depict via my character.”
