The family of Sunderland teenager Josie King, who died aged 15, have spoken about how much it means to them that their girl is remembered across her home city as they helped mark two years since she died of cancer.  <br>While her relatives held a balloon release, her friends held their own, with a candlelit vigil held in the evening.
