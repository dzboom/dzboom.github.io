For the first time ever, Oprah Winfrey put someone other than herself on the cover of her O magazine.  <br>This month, the late Breonna Taylor graced the cover.  <br>The 26-year-old EMT was shot to death by police executing a no-knock warrant. They were not looking for her.  <br>Despite calls for the officers involved in Taylor's death to be arrested and charged, only one officer has been fired, and no charges have been filed.  <br>Winfrey has put up 26 billboards of Taylor around Taylor's hometown of Louisville, Kentucky.  <br>The billboards also direct people to the website of the social justice group UntilFreedom.com, which is focusing on Taylor's case.
