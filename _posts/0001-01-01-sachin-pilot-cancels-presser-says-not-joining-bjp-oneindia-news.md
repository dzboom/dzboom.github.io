After being removed from all key posts in Rajasthan Congress Sachin Pilot said today that he is not joining the BJP and still remains a Congress man.  He said I would like to make it clear that I have no plans to join the BJP. The BJP link is an attempt to malign me in the eyes of the Gandhis. He insisted on still being a member of the Congress party. Vishwendra Singh, a rebel Congress MLA in Sachin Pilot's camp, echoed the statement: &quot;As far as we are concerned, we are with the congress. Speculation intensified after Sachin Pilot first put off, then cancelled his press briefing today. Pilot has said that he felt humiliated after being asked to answer questions in an investigation started by the Chief Minister into alleged attempts to dislodge the Congress government.
