Protesters took to Belarus' Gomel on August 15 to show opposition to the controversial election win of Alexander Lukashenko.  <br>Footage shows demonstrators flying an opposition banner up a flag pole at full mast as cheers ring out and a woman is heard saying &quot;This is the best day of my life,&quot; in Belarusian.  <br>The red and white flag is the country's former flag and was in use from 1991 to 1995.  <br>The filmer explained that the crowd is shouting &quot;Zhive Belarus,&quot; which translates as &quot;Belarus lives.&quot;
