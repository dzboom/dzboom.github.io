Nintendo surged as much as 6% on Thursday, reports Business Insider.  <br>The video game company said first-quarter earnings soared 428% while sales more than doubled.  <br>Video game companies have seen a surge in business since the COVID-19 pandemic outbreak.  <br>Consumers are looking to entertain and distract themselves while sheltering in place.  <br>Driving the strength in Nintendo's business included growing demand for its Nintendo Switch.  <br>The popular software hit &quot;Animal Crossing: New Horizon&quot; is a major draw for buyers.
