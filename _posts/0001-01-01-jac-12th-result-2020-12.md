Jharkhand Academic Council is going to declare the result of 12th today ... Let us tell you that the results of the three streams of science, commerce and arts of 12th will be declared today. The results will be announced at 1 pm. Education Minister Jagarnath Mahato will release the result at one o'clock in the afternoon.
