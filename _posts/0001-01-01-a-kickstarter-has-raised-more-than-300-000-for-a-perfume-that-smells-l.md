If you've ever wondered what space smells like, you're not alone.  <br>According to Gizmodo, plenty of people are curious about what kind of scent may be out there.  <br>As od Sunday, a Kickstarter has received more than $321,000 for a NASA-Designed Perfume that smells like space.  <br>The campaign’s organizers say NASA designed the perfume decades ago for training purposes.  <br>&quot;Eau de Space&quot;’ has been a closely guarded NASA secret, but the Kickstarter organizers have acquired the recipe.  <br>The group is raising funds to manufacture the scent and make it widely available.  <br>Currently, you can buy a bottle of Eau de Space on Kickstarter beginning at $29.  <br>For every bottle four-ounce bottle purchased, the group will donate a bottle to a local K-12 STEM program.  <br>Shipping is scheduled to begin in September.
