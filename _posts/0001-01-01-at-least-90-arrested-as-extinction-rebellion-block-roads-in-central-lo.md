At least 90 people have been arrested by police after environmental campaign group Extinction Rebellion blocked roads in their latest climate change ‘uprising’. Hundreds of activists gathered in Westminster, London to block access routes into Parliament. The group is calling on MPs to support their proposed climate and ecological emergency bill.
