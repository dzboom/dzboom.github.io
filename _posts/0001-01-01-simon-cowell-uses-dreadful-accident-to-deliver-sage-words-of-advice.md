British talent judge Simon Cowell has been hospitalized for an accident in which he broke his back.  <br>According to CNN, Cowell's spokeswoman said the former 'American Idol' judge broke his back in several places while testing a new electric bike.  <br>Cowell tweeted his thanks to hospital staff and well-wishers and reminded first-time electric bike users to read the manual first.  <br>The accident took place in the courtyard of Cowell's home in Malibu, California.  <br>The 60-year-old was with his family at the time. He has one son with his partner, Lauren Silverman.
