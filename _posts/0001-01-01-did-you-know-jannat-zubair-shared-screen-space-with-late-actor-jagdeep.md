Hindi film industry lost its yet another legendary star Jagdeep. The actor who entertained everyone with his impeccable acting and spot-on comic timing took his last breath in Mumbai. While his real name was Syed Ishtiaq Ahmed Jafri, but he was famous by his stage name, Jagdeep. The Sholay actor started his acting career as a child artist and went on to do more than 400 films, including Ramsey brother's horror film Purana Mandir. While everyone knows how great his screen presence was but many don't know he also acted in popular TV show Phulwa, yes you heard that right. The show that featured popular TikTok star Jannat Zubair in the lead role as a child artist, Jagdeep played the character of Bade Baba in the show and had a few scenes with Jannat. Meanwhile, the legendary star who died due to age-related issues was buried in a Mumbai cemetery.
