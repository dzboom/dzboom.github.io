Dominic Bess hails James Anderson as the greatest fast bowlers of all time|Oneindia Sports Dom Bess, the off-spinner has heaped praises and described James Anderson as the greatest player to represent England ever after the 38-year-old senior pacer is approaching to a 600 Test wickets landmark. Besides, he is on course to be the first one to do so.
