Opel has tailored the Vivaro-e to cater for a variety of customer needs, including crew cab, platform cab and people carrier, with payloads of up to 1,275kg and gross vehicle weights ranging from 2,800 to 3,100kg. The particularly compact 4.60-metre version closes the gap between the van and the transporter segments. With a turning circle of only 11.3m, it is also very agile in tight spaces. As most variants are about 1.90m tall, the Vivaro-e can access parking in basements or shopping centres with low roofs.  <br>Thanks to the practical FlexCargo load-through on the front passenger side, the 4.6m3-cargo capacity of the S increases to 5.1m3. This enables transportation of objects up to 3.32m in length.
