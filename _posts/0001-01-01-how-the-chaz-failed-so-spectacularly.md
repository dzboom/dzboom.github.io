Seattle's Capitol Hill Autonomous Zone (CHAZ) is gone.  <br>It was created in the wake of nation wide protests against police brutality.  <br>Business Insider says CHAZ was set up along several blocks.  <br>It created a space for tents, free food, art-making, and rallies around the city’s abandoned East Precinct.  <br>&quot;CHAZ has symbolic meaning that once the police withdraw, it’s not that communities descend into chaos, but that we can create safe, democratic, autonomous spaces.”  <br>Business Insider said that without police the zone collapsed.  <br>Intentions were good but the group did not do enough when violence and lawlessness erupted.  <br>Some of the crimes include noise complaints, rampant drug use, rapes, lootings, assaults, property damage ....   <br>.... theft, muggings, harassment, public sex, public urination and defecation, shootings, and several brutal murders....  <br>The area has now been dismantled.
