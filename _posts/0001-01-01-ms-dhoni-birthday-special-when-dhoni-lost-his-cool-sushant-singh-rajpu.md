Former India skipper MS Dhoni’s financial advisor Arun Pandey, who was also the producer of the cricketer’s biopic – was someone who knew Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput well. Recalling an incident, Pandey said that Dhoni was at a point of time irritated with Sushant’s inquisitive nature as he asked too many questions. “Once I remember, Dhoni rolled his eyes and said: “Arre bhai, kitne questions poochta hai tu!” I remember Sushant’s reply.
