Another FIR has been lodged with the Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB) in connection with the audio tapes. Rebel MLA Bhanwar Lal Sharma has been named in the FIR. The complaint was filed based on the complaint of Congress chief Mahesh Joshi. Earlier, Sanjay Jain was arrested by the Special Operations Group (SOG) on Friday based on another complaint by Congress over the leaked audio tapes.
