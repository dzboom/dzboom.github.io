Logan Jamal was driving his Tesla on July 10 when his bumper suddenly and alarmingly fell off.   <br>It was a rainy day in Boca Raton, Florida, when Logan pulled over thinking he was rear-ended by another car. He later realized the bumper of his Tesla just fell off.   <br>&quot;I was decelerating getting ready to go into the turning lane to turn into the plaza when we heard a huge BOOM,&quot; Logan said. &quot;I thought we got nailed in the back by another car! We pulled into the closest parking lot and were in shock when we saw my whole rear bumper torn off.&quot;  <br>&quot;I was in shock. I took pictures and watched the video right away. Then I called the Tesla Roadside Assistance and walked over to where it happened. I watched dozens of cars drive over the same spot with no issues.&quot;   <br>&quot;My dad had to email the manager with our lawyer, and the manager just called my dad and said it's a known issue, and they are covering it all for me and bringing a tow to my house. They're ordering new parts for it and fixing it.&quot;
