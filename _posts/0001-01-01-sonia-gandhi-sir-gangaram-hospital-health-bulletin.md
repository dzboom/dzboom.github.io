The health of Congress President Sonia Gandhi has suddenly deteriorated. After which Sonia Gandhi has been admitted to Sir Gangaram Hospital in Delhi. Dr. DS Rana, Chairman of Sir Gangaram Hospital, released the Health Bulletin of Sonia Gandhi. And told that Congress President Sonia Gandhi has been admitted to the hospital at 7 pm on Thursday. She has been admitted here in connection with routine checkups and health checks.
