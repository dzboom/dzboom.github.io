Jammu and Kashmir Principal Secretary for Power and Information, Rohit Kansal, on September 01 informed that over 12.5 lakh Domicile Certificates have been issued in J&amp;K so far. He said, “So far, over 12.5 lakh domicile certificates have been issued. These have been issued mostly to those who were erstwhile permanent residents or holders of Permanent Resident Certificates. The process will be further accelerated.”
