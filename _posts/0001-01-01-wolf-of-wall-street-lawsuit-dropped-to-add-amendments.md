Salor assemblyman Datuk Husam Musa and lawyer Matthias Chang opted to withdraw their class action lawsuit against Red Granite Pictures in order to add amendments to their initial complaint. The US production company is being accused of using funds from 1Malaysia Development Berhad to produce blockbuster hit &amp;apos;The Wolf of Wall Street&amp;apos;.
