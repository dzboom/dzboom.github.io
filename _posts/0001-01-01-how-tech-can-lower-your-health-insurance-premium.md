Coronavirus pushed us all online – from work to socializing and even health care. CSS Insurance CEO Philomena Colatrella says the increased use of telemedicine shows there has been a change in mindset among customers, and it’s something she plans to build on. CSS says it will roll out more telemedicine in the future, which will help to reduce premiums.
