Have you heard of black wheat? Otherwise, know today. This is a special variety of wheat, which is cultivated in a special way. Black wheat is said to have about 60 percent higher iron concentration than normal wheat. However, the amount of protein, nutrients and starch remains the same. Black wheat is not generally cultivated in India, but a farmer from Dhar district in Madhya Pradesh has grown a black wheat crop.
