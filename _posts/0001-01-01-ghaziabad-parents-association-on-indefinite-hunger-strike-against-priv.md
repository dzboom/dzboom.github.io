Members of Ghaziabad Parents’ Association are on indefinite hunger strike for the last six days.   <br>They are demanding that private schools waive off their fees for the lockdown period since all the institutions are shut owing to the coronavirus pandemic.   <br>The protesting parents lit candles, flashed mobile lights and clanged utensils to mark their indefinite hunger strike.
