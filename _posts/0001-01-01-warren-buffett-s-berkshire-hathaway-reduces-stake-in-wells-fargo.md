Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway has cut its Wells Fargo.  <br>Its stake in Wells Fargo is now at a 17 year low.  <br>On Friday, Berkshire Hathaway said it owns 3.3% of the company, its slimmest ownership position since 2003.  <br>In 1994, Berkshire owned 13.3% of the bank.  <br>It held more than 500 million shares worth more than $27 billion in 2016.  <br>Buffett's company now holds fewer than 140 million shares, worth about $3.4 billion.
