Karan Wahi who is currently busy shooting for Rohit Shetty’s stunt based reality show Khatron Ke Khiladi: Made In India, says he was initially sceptical to go outdoors and resume work, owing to the Covid-19 pandemic. Speaking about working during these trying time, Karan said, “I was sceptical. I mean it was not easy stepping out knowing that your health is at risk. I signed my contract just one day before the shoot. I first made sure that proper safety precautions are being followed on the sets. Personally, I was very scared. So, I had a detailed discussion with the team.” Karan added, “The production team was very cooperative, and I do trust the channel. So, I said yes and eventually I have to work. I haven’t been doing anything for so many months, so I feel it’s okay to shoot for the show to make me mentally, emotionally and physically sane.”
