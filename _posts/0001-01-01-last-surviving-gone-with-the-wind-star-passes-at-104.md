Legendary actress Olivia de Havilland has died at her home in Paris. She was 104.  <br>Elle reports the actress was the last surviving member of the 'Gone with the Wind' cast.  <br>De Havilland was also known for playing Maid Marian in The Adventures of Robin Hood (1938), opposite Errol Flynn.  <br>She also did an enormous favor for her fellow Hollywood actors, winning a high-profile court case against Warner Bros. in the 1940s.  <br>The win resulted in The De Havilland Decision, in which it was ruled that a contract's duration relates to calendar years, not actual days spent on the job.
