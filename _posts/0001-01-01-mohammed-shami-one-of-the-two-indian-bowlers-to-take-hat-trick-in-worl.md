Mohammed Shami is a right-arm pacer and among the fastest bowlers produced by India. He represents Bengal in domestic cricket and has the ability to swing the ball both ways at a menacing speed. Shami made his international debut for India in the third One-Day International of the series against Pakistan in January 2013 in Delhi.
