Rakshabandhan is an auspicious festival to celebrate the bond between brothers and sisters. Sisters tie rakhi, a holy thread to their brothers and ask for protection. In lieu to that, the brother promises her to protect her throughout and gifts her with presents. Police officers work hard, give up their personal commitments and work towards the city's protection. To value that, girls tied rakhi to police officers at Chandani mahal police station delhi.    <br>#Rakshabandhan2020 #Chandanimahal #Coronavirus
