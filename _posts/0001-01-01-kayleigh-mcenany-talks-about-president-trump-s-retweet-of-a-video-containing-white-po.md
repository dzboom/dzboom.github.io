During a briefing at the White House on June 29, press secretary Kayleigh McEnany answered a reporter's question about President Donald Trump retweeting a video containing a person shouting &quot;white power&quot; from a golf cart. McEnany said &quot;He did not hear that particular phrase when he tweeted out the video.&quot; McEnany also commented on Mississippi removing the confederate emblem from their state flag, saying, &quot;I would say that's a decision for Mississippi to make, it's commendable that they took this action in a lawful manner and took the appropriate steps rather than trying to tear down statues and monuments.&quot;
