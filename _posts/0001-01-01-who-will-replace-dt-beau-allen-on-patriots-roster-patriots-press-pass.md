Who Will Replace DT Beau Allen on Patriots Roster? | Patriots Press Pass  <br>The New England Patriots are placing defensive tackle Beau Allen on injured reserve. Allen missed the majority of training camp with an undisclosed injury, but is eligible to return from IR as soon as three weeks into the season. The move opens up another roster spot for the Patriots to fill.  <br>Sierra Goodwill and Evan Lazar discuss whether the Patriots will fill the roster spot with kicker Nick Folk, a veteran DT or another position.
