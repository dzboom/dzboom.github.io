Political crisis continues in Rajasthan. All the MLAs of the Congress of CM Ashok Gehlot camp have been shifted to Jaisalmer. Muslim MLAs of Congress shifted to Jaisalmer celebrated the festival of Eid ul Azha at Suryagarh Resort. In the hotel itself, 9 Muslim MLAs of the Congress offered prayers to each other by offering Eid ul Azha prayers and prayed for peace, peace and prosperity in the state and country.
