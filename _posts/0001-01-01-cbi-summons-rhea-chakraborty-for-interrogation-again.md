After grilling her for 10 hours yesterday (August 28), the CBI has again summoned Rhea Chakraborty today (August 29). The actress is being questioned by the CBI in connection with the Sushant Singh Rajput death case.   <br>After Sushant's father KK Singh filed a complaint against Rhea in Patna police station on July 25, Rhea remains to be a prime suspect in Sushant's death case. Apart from Rhea, her brother Showik Chakraborty was also grilled by the CBI. On Thursday (August 27), Showik was questioned for 14 hours by the investigation agency.   <br>Watch as India Today TV follows Rhea Chakraborty as she is enroute to the CBI office.
