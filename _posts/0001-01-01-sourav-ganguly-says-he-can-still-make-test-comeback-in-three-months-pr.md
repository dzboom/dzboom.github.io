Former India captain and current BCCI president Sourav Ganguly said he can score runs in Test cricket again if he trains for three months and plays a few Ranji Trophy matches. Ganguly’s comments came while recollecting the last phase of his career in an interview with Bengali newspaper Sangbad Pratidin. “If I was given two more series in ODIs, I would’ve scored more runs. If I hadn’t retired in Nagpur, I would’ve scored runs in the next two Test series too.
