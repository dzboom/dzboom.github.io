Former President of India Pranab Mukherjee passed away on Monday. He was 84 years old. On the death of Pranab Mukherjee, daughter Sharmistha sent an emotional tweet and said goodbye to her father. He wrote in a tweet that I salute everyone. Baba, through the lines of your favorite poet, I am telling everyone your last good bye. You spent your life in service to the people in the national service.
