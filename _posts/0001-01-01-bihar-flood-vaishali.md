Bhagwanpur of Vaishali where Baya river has started to wreak havoc, due to which water has entered into many villages, as a result, thousands of villagers have been caught in a disaster. The life of the people has become difficult due to the Baya river water entering into the houses, due to the spread of water on the rural roads, the area also lost contact with the Bhagwanpur block headquarters.
