Foreign Minister Hishammuddin Hussein apologised after being pinpointed by a netizen vaping during Parliament session this week.  <br>“Sorry, I didn't realise - it's a new habit.  <br>“I apologise to the Dewan (Rakyat) and promise to not do it again,” he said.  <br>Umno's Sembrong MP issued the apology on Twitter in response to the video of the House session, in which Hishammuddin could be seen taking a puff as he was seated behind Transport Minister Wee Ka Siong, who was speaking during proceedings.
