Today marks 21 years of Kargil Vijay Diwas. On this occasion, Defense Minister Rajnath Singh reached the War Memorial to remember the brave soldiers. Along with Rajnath Singh, the three army chiefs were also present there. Rajnath Singh paid tribute to the martyrs. Rajnath Singh also described the soldiers as an inspiration. People following social distancing were also seen on the war memorial due to Corona virus.
