The nominees for the 54th annual County Music Association Awards were announced on Tuesday.  <br>Miranda Lambert leads the pack with seven nominations, followed by Luke Combs with six.  <br>The two entertainers are nominated for entertainer of the year.  <br>Other nominees for the night's top prize include Eric Church, Carrie Underwood, and Keith Urban.  <br>According to CNN, the evening's winners will be announced on Wednesday, November 11, on ABC.
