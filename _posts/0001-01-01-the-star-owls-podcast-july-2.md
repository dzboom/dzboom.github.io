Sheffield Wednesday's bright restart may have dimmed with the defeat to West Brom but there's no reason to feel too pessimistic. And it is with positive attitudes that we convene again for the latest episode of The Star Owls podcast. Joining Liam Hoden to reflect on the defeat to the Baggies are The Star's Wednesday writers Alex Miller and Joe Crann. They talk how the game was decided, discuss Moses Odubajo's unfortunate penalty record and ponder whether changes will be required for Sunday's game with Swansea City. And then there are the off-field matters - late payment of wages, THAT Sam Hutchinson interview and contracts for a raft of youth team players.
