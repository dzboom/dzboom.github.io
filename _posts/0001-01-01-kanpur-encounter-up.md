Police have started clamping down on the accomplices of Vikas Dubey, the brutally murdered eight policemen in Bikaru village of Kanpur. The UP police has registered a case against 15 of its operatives who supported Vikas on the night of the crime. The UP Police has released photographs on a poster of these 15 accused. Some of these Padrahan accused are from Bikaru village and some from other villages.
