In these trying times, it can be hard to promote a new piece of content, what with all the clutter of all the other content out there, but somehow Jackass star Steve-O has figured out a way – by duct-taping himself to a billboard in Los Angeles.  <br>&quot;I’m attached to a billboard right now (swipe to see the whole thing)&quot; Steve-O wrote on Instagram &quot;and want to emphasize that a team of real professionals rigged everything safely. There is zero chance of me falling, and it’s important to me that we not waste any valuable city resources on this. I’m happy to just hang out, and really want the world to know about this project I worked so hard on.&quot;  <br>The project is a new special, which you can order on steveo.com.
