(CNN) Carl Reiner, the writer, actor, director and producer whose many decades' worth of credits -- including &quot;The Dick Van Dyke Show&quot; and &quot;The 2000 Year Old Man&quot; -- showcased a ready wit and a generous spirit, has died, his son, director Rob Reiner, announced in a tweet. &quot;Last night my dad passed away,&quot; Rob Reiner wrote. &quot;As I write this my heart is hurting. He was my guiding light.&quot; Carl Reiner died Monday of natural causes at his home in Beverly Hills, his assistant Judy Nagy told CNN in a statement.
