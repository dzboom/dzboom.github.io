COVID-19 causes heart problems that range from irregular heartbeats to angina.  <br>It also worsens pre-existing cardiovascular conditions, according to an analysis and UPI.  <br>Just over 14% of people hospitalized due to infection with the new coronavirus...  <br>...Have developed heart complications due to the disease, the researchers said.  <br>That's in addition to the nearly 13% of those hospitalized who had pre-existing heart conditions.  <br>Roughly 10% of those with heart conditions who were infected have died.
