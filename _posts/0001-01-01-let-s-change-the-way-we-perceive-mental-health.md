“Hua Kya Tha” is a reflection of how mental health is perceived by a larger section of society. Many consider it as a taboo which we should avoid discussing in public. It’s only time that we started giving our answers, collectively.   <br>We believe this performance piece would help people address the issue, accept its existence and eventually lead to prevention of many suicide cases. Let’s connect with each other on a more empathetic level. Let’s care more.
