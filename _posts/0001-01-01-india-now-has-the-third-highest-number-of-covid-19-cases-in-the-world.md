India just moved up a step on a list no country wants to be on.  <br>According to Business Insider, India on Sunday surpassed Russia in its number of new novel coronavirus COVID-19 cases.  <br>India's total number of cases is now 697,836, compared to Russia's 681,251.  <br>India is now behind the United States, in the top spot, and Brazil.  <br>The hotspot is India's capital, Delhi, which leads the country in new COVID-19 cases.  <br>Though the subcontinent only just recorded more cases than Russia, it has counted almost twice as many deaths.
