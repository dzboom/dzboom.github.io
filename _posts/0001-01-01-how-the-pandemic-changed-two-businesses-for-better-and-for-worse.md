Consumer confidence, social distancing measures and government guidelines have changed the way we spend our money. For some businesses that has been a good thing, but for others it has not been so kind. Here we speak to Max Geddes, co-founder of bike-repair shop The Cyclry in Lewisham which has seen a huge boom in business since lockdown measures eased. We also speak to Jason Jenkins, founder of Little Dinosaurs soft play centre and garden who is still awaiting permission to reopen.
