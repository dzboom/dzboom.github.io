The 10-day strict lockdown began in Pune to curb the spread of coronavirus. The lockdown will be imposed in two phases till July 23. Police were seen keeping a tab on vehicular movement. Pune has nearly 40,000 confirmed cases of coronavirus, and the city is seeing a rise of over 1000 infections daily.   <br>
