Florida couple James and Kimberly Snead are living personifications of the adage 'no good deed goes unpunished.'  <br>The Sneads were the last foster parents of Nikolas Cruz. Cruz carried out a mass shooting at Marjory Stoneman Douglas high school in 2018.  <br>As part of a legal agreement to settle numerous civil lawsuits, the Sneads were required to write a letter of apology to the victims and their families.  <br>According to Newser, they said they should have believed what they were told.  <br>Namely, that Cruz was homicidal, untrustworthy, and infatuated with firearms.  <br>We thought we could handle this troubled young man, unfortunately, we were wrong. James and Kimberly Snead
