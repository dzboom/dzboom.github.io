Nick Cannon has been dropped by ViacomCBS.  <br>The move comes after Cannon made anti-Semitic comments on his podcast “Cannon’s Class” in a June 30 episode.  <br>“You can’t be anti-Semitic when we are the Semitic people. When we are the same people who they want to be. That’s our birthright. We are the true Hebrews.”  <br>Viacom/CBS released a statement to Variety.  <br>“We are deeply troubled that Nick has failed to acknowledge or apologize for perpetuating anti-Semitism, and we are terminating our relationship with him.”  <br>Cannon tweeted about the comments on Monday: “Anyone who knows me knows that I have no hate in my heart nor malice intentions. I do not condone hate speech nor the spread of hateful rhetoric.”
