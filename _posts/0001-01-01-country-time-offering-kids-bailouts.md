Country Time Lemonade company is issuing $100 &quot;bailout&quot; checks to children around the country who have been forced to close lemonade stands due to the coronavirus.  <br>&quot;We want to help kids foster their entrepreneurial spirit by offering a small relief to those who can't operate their lemonade stands this summer,&quot; Andrew Deckert, a brand manager at Country Time, said in a press statement.   <br>The effort comes after Country Time's 2018 &quot;Legal-Ade&quot; campaign.  <br>The &quot;Legal-Ade&quot; campaign paid fees for lemonade stands in select regions with outdated permit policies.  <br>That prompted states like Colorado and Texas to update legislation.
