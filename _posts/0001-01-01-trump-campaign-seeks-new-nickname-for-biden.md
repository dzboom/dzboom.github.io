Business Insider reports that the Trump campaign is scrambling for a more damaging nickname for Joe Biden.  <br>The campaign has used &quot;Sleepy Joe&quot; to tag Biden.  <br>Trump's advisers are reportedly concerned that &quot;Sleepy Joe&quot; won't generate the hostility to destroy Biden's candidacy.  <br>Advisers are reportedly weighing other attack lines for Biden.  <br>This includes trying to paint Biden as corrupt, a puppet for radical leftists or as being in cognitive decline.  <br>Trump is currently behind by Biden in several key polls.
