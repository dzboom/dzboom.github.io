The festival of greenery Teej is celebrated on the Shukla Paksha of Saavan month. This time the festival is on Thursday 23 July. The festival of Hariyali Teej is especially important for the Suhagan women. This festival is related to Lord Shiva and Mother Parvati. It is believed that Lord Shiva was pleased with the penance of Mata Parvati and on the same day, the story of her past birth was also told to Mata Parvati.
