The Promise Season 1 Episode 11 Part 1 ( with english subtitles ) - Yemin  <br>When Mr Hikmet is given a terminal diagnosis he wonders what will become of the fortune he has worked so hard to amass - he can't leave everything to his frivolous son Emir.
