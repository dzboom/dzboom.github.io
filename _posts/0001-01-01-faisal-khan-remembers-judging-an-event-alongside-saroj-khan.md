Legendary choreographer Saroj Khan who passed away a few days back after suffering from cardiac arrest has left so many great memories with her industry people, as many celebs came out and shared how Saroj Khan inspired them. Dancer Faisal Khan who was last seen in Nach Baliye shares a beautiful memory with ace choreographer. In an interview, Faisal said, &quot;We had once gone to an event outside of Mumbai where I was a judge alongside her and Shakti Mohan. Initially, I was too overwhelmed to be judging beside her, but she made me very comfortable and ensured I sit next to her while judging. It was a very proud moment for me back then. We had a lovely time while traveling to the event and back, discussing dance related topics and our love for dance, which was amazing!&quot; He further remembered, &quot;I had even performed a guest dance on stage for that event, and in her traditional style she had given me a Rs. 500 note which I till today have kept with me! So for me and the entire dancing fraternity, it is a huge loss of a legendary dancer in our industry!&quot; Saroj Khan was admitted to Guru Nanak Hospital at Bandra in Mumbai where she took her last breadth. The master choreographer was likely to be discharged from the hospital in the next two to three days. She last choreographed Madhuri Dixit in the song Tabaah Ho Gaye from Karan Johar’s production Kalank in 2019. The last rites of Saroj Khan were performed at Malvani in Malad, Mumbai.
