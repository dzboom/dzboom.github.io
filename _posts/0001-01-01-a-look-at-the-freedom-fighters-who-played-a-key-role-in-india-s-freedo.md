Millions of people took part in India's freedom struggle, but some of them were revolutionaries who played the most important role in India's freedom struggle and will always be remembered. Lets take a look at such freedom fighters who have gone down in the history for their contribution in India's independence. India’s freedom struggle was a mass movement. However, there was one person - Gandhi - who led the nation in its non-violent resistance, which shook the very foundation of British rule. Subhas Chandra Bose was one of the most eminent freedom fighters of India. He coined the term ‘Jai Hind’. Pundit Jawaharlal Nehru was the first Prime Minister of India and a leading figure in the Indian independence struggle and the process of political development in the country post independence. Bhagat Singh was a brave freedom fighter and revolutionary of the India Independence movement. Chandrashekhar Azad was a great Indian revolutionary.
