Nine people have been rescued by firefighters following a blaze at a Hartlepool flat in the early hours of the morning.  <br>Cleveland Fire Brigade crews were called to a fire at a flat on York Road at 7.14am on Monday, September 14.  <br>Five engines attended the scene from Billingham, Hartlepool and Stockton.  <br>Nine people rescued from the flat by firefighters, the fire service has confirmed.
