Power announced its second-annual ranking of the car companies with the best brand loyalty.  <br>Honda came in third with 58.7% of Honda owners went with the brand again when buying a new car.  <br>Toyota is number two with 60.3%.  <br>The study found that 60.5% of Subaru owners went with the brand again when buying a new car.  <br>The worst performer?  <br>Fiat, which earned a loyalty rate of just over 10%.
