Quick turnaround of test results and contact tracing improve the odds of successfully limiting COVID-19 spread.  <br>By providing test results within one day, communities might be able to reduce onward transmission.  <br>It also helps save 80% of the people with whom patients have come into contact with.  <br>It also helps reduce the spread of the virus by 80% per COVID-19 patient, reports UPI.  <br>Contact tracing is a public health approach used to contain the spread of infectious diseases.  <br>This is by attempting to track people who could have been exposed to an infected person.
