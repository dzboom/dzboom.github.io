Italian motorcycle manufacturer, Benelli has launched its Imperiale 400 BS6 model in India starting at Rs 1.99 lakh, ex-showroom. The motorcycle can be booked via the brands dealer network, and via the company’s website for an amount of Rs 6,000.   <br>The motorcycle is now powered by a 374cc single-cylinder engine that produces 20 brake horsepower and 29Nm of torque, and comes mated to a five-speed gearbox.   <br>Watch the video to know more about the Benelli Imperiale 400 BS6 model.  <br>
