This kid's mom made some chalk paint at home with flour and food dye to keep her children busy with painting before nap time. The mom soon discovered that one of her sons had woken up and wandered off to continue playing. She discovered to her dismay, that he had not only painted the driveway but also the cars parked in the garage in white along with his cycle and other toys.
