Karnataka Congress chief DK Shivakumar is the latest addition to the list of politicians testing positive for Coronvavirus.  DK Shivakumar tweeted out confirming that he has tested positive for Covid-19. He said that he was doing well but has been hospitalised as a precautionary measure. He also urged all those who had come in contact with him to take precautions. He said that he will be back soon and that he was postponing his tour of the flood-affected parts of the state.  Senior Karnataka Congress leader Eshwar Khandre and JD(S) leader HD Kumaraswamy both wished Shivakumar a speedy recovery from the COVID-19 infection.
