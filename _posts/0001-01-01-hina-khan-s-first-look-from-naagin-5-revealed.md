Since Ekta Kapoor shared that Naagin 4 is going off air, the fans were waiting for the makers to announce the next season. Although the makers have already started working on the next season, a month back, the Creative head of the show, Mukta Dhond shared the first look poster of season 5 on her Instagram account, however nothing special was revealed. Now fans are in treat as the first look of Hina Khan as Naagin is making rounds on the internet, in a post shared by fan account of the show, they wrote, “Raaz gehra samnay layega kis ka chehra !!” Reportedly, Hina will be doing a cameo in the supernatural show, while Surbhi Chandna will play the lead Naagin. There were speculations that Hina Khan or Krystle D’souza might be doing the primary role, but the makers have kept everything under wraps and have left fans to wonder about the next Naagin.
