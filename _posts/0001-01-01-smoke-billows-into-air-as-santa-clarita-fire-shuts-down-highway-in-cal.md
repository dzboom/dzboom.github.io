The northbound 14 Freeway in Santa Clarita, California was shut down on Monday (August 3) and evacuations were ordered after a 130-acre brush fire began burning.  <br>Footage shows clouds of smoke looming over the 14 Freeway.   <br>The fire has since been brought under control and evacuation orders were lifted.
