Fire crews tackled a blaze at a building in Eastbourne late last night, July 15, 2020. Crews from Eastbourne, Pevensey, Bexhill and Hailsham attended the fire in Fort Lane at 11.20pm, East Sussex Fire and Rescue said. Firefighters used breathing apparatus, a main jet and hosereel to tackle the blaze. The fire is under investigation, the service added. Video by Colin Whitethread.
