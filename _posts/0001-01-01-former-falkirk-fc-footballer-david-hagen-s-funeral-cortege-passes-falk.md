Falkirk Stadium. The funeral of David Hagen. Former Falkirk FC footballer David Hagen’s funeral cortege passes Falkirk Stadium as part of his funeral route from his hometown Bonnybridge to Falkirk Crematorium. During his football career, David played for Rangers, Hearts, Falkirk, Livingston, Clyde, Peterhead and Bo'ness Utd football clubs. Then he became a taxi driver in the Falkirk district before his MND diagnosis.
