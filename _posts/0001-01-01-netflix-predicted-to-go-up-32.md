Netflix is on the verge of reporting a blockbuster second quarter earnings report, according to Goldman Sachs.  <br>Business Insider reports that Goldman Sachs is basing their judgement on strong app download trends   <br>On Friday, Goldman raised its Netflix price target to $670, representing upside potential of 32% from Thursday's close.  <br>The firm said COVID-19 continues to accelerate the trend towards video streaming services.  <br>Goldman Sachs said Netflix is a prime beneficiary of that trend.  <br>Goldman believes Netflix could add at least 12.5 million total net paid subscribers in the second quarter.  <br>That figure would be well ahead of Netflix's guidance of 7.5 million.
