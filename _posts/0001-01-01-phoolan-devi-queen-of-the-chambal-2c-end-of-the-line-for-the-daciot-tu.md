Mulayam Singh, leader Samajwadi Party says family of Phoolan Devi is sad now. Phoolan Devi was a brave lady. She was very popular among the people. On 25 July 2001, Phoolan Devi was shot dead by three masked gunmen outside of her Delhi bungalow. She was hit five times: three shots to her head and two to her body. The gunmen fled the scene in a Maruti car. She was taken to a nearby hospital but was declared dead. The prime suspect, Sher Singh Rana (alias Pankaj Singh), later surrendered to the police. Rana allegedly claimed to have murdered Phoolan Devi in revenge for the men she gunned down in the Behmai massacre. In the latest ruling, on August 14, 2014, the court sentenced Rana to life in prison and a fine.
