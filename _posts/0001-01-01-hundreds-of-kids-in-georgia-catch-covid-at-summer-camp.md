Georgia is facing a massive COVID-19 outbreak at an overnight summer camp this past June.  <br>Hundreds of children of all ages and teenage staff members have tested positive.  <br>The overnight camp opened on June 17, following the state’s easing of restrictions on May 31.  <br>200 staff members and more than 300 children in total attended the camp, says Gizmodo.  <br>Two days into the camp, a staff member was sent home after feeling chills a day earlier.  <br>260 people have tested positive so far, with more cases expected to develop.
