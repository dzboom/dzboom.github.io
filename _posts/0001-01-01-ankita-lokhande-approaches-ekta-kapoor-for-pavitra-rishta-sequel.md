While the majority of us are heartbroken by the death of Sushant Singh Rajput, but more than us it's Ankita Lokhande who has been affected by the actor's death. The duo was in relationship after meeting on the sets of their show Pavitra Rishta, Sushant had even proposed to Ankita on Jhalak Dikhlaja. Fans wanted to see them getting married but unfortunately, the couple parted ways due to their personal reasons. Now that Pavitra Rishta is so close to Sushant and Ankita's heart as it gave them a platform to prove their skills, Ankita recently approached Ekta Kapoor to bring back the classic show. Reportedly, a source close to the actor revealed that &quot;The show was close to Sushant’s heart as it gave him the platform to scale new heights and both Ekta and Ankita feel that a new season will be the best tribute to the deceased actor. Ekta has spearheaded several sequels on TV, including Hum Paanch, Kasautii Zindagii Kay and Naagin.” It was solely Ankita's idea to bring back the show as a tribute to Sushant. Reportedly, Ekta is so elated by the idea that she immediately agreed to do the show. She will soon sit with her team to explore how to take the daily soap forward. The report further states that the shoot will start once everyone is satisfied with the script and characters.
