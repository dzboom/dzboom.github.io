Powerful thunderstorms brought heavy rain and wind up to 100 miles per hour in central Iowa Monday, August 10, leaving significant damages.  <br>Gusty winds and heavy rain downed trees, damaged roofs, cut power, and destroyed structures throughout the state. According to report, about 250,000 customers in the Iowa City area are without power.
