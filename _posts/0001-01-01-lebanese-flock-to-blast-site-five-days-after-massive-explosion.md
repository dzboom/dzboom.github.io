Scores of Lebanese joined media on Sunday (August 9th) at the scene of Tuesday's massive blast in Beirut which killed at least 158 people.  <br>On Saturday, protesters stormed government ministries during a fresh demonstration over the blast.  <br>Many are furious at the failure to prevent the explosion at a warehouse storing more than 2,000 tonnes of ammonium nitrate.
