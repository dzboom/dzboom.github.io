Dr. Kafeel Khan of Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, who has been jailed for the last 8 months after being rasuka, is very concerned about his safety. Because of this, Dr. Kafeel Khan arrived in Jaipur with his family to seek refuge. He says the family has talked to Priyanka Gandhi. There is a Congress government in Rajasthan and security has been assured. Kafeel Khan said that my inhumane treatment has been done in jail. UP government can file any new case anytime.
