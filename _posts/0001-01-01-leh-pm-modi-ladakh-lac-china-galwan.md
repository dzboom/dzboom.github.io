Prime Minister Narendra Modi, while addressing the Indian soldiers in Ladakh on Friday, encouraged their enthusiasm and also gave a loud voice to China. Modi clearly told China that we have the right to the Galvan Valley. Modi said that the signs of valor of Indian soldiers are spread all over Ladakh. Referring to the soldiers in his address, PM Modi said that from Leh, Ladakh to Kargil and Siachen, from the snowy peaks here to the cold water stream of Galvan valley.
