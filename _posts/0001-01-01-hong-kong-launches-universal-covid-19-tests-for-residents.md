Hong Kong residents began the en masse testing process for the coronavirus as the city’s government launched its universal screening programme on September 1, 2020, despite calls from activists and some health workers for a boycott. There are 141 sample collecting centres across all 18 districts and the programme will last at least one week.  (Photo: SCMP / Felix Wong)
