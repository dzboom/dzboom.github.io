At least 3 men have died after violence broke out in eastern Bengaluru over a 'derogatory' Facebook post shared by the nephew of a Congress MLA. Around 60 police personnel have also been injured in the clash which broke out last evening. Hundreds of people belonging to a minority community gathered outside Congress MLA Srinivas Murthy’s house in Kaval Byrsandra area and started pelting stones.  <br>#Bengaluru #Facebookpost #Riots
