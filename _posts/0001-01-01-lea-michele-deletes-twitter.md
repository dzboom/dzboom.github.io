Lea Michele has deleted her Twitter account over the disappearance of her Glee co-star Naya Rivera.  <br>Michele’s account on the platform disappeared on Saturday.  <br>Michele has not yet addressed the deletion.  <br>The Independent says her account had in recent days been tagged in a number of tweets demanding she comment on Rivera’s disappearance.   <br>Others had tagged her directly in abusive messages.  <br>Michele and Rivera were at the centre of a number of feud rumors when 'Glee' was on the air.  <br>Michele's career has been ruined by accusations of diva behavior from many of her co-stars.
