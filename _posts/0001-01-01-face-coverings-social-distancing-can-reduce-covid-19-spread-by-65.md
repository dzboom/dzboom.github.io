Wearing a mask, social distancing, and regular handwashing could reduce the spread of COVID-19.  <br>In fact, it could reduce the spread by as much as 65%, according to reports at UPI.  <br>Researchers from the Netherlands also applaud government-imposed social distancing measures.  <br>This includes closure of non-essential businesses and &quot;stay-at-home&quot; orders.  <br>These moves could delay the peak of an epidemic by up to seven months.  <br>This delay would allow the healthcare system time to prepare for efforts and treatment.
