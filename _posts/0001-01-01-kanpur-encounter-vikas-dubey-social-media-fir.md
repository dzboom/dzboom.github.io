Gangster Vikas Dubey, an accused in the Kanpur encounter after the murderof eight policemen, is absconding. The police of Uttar Pradesh are raiding various places in search of Vikas Dubey. However, Vikas Dubey has also been supported by many people on social media. Now the police have registered a case on those who justify the work done by Vikas Dubey. FIR was registered in Fazalganj police station and Kakadev police station in Kanpur.
