Millions of people lost their jobs when the pandemic hit.  <br>So, having a steady job amid the pandemic can be considered a privilege.  <br>Even people who still have their jobs during the coronavirus can experience stress and anxiety.  <br>Business Insider suggested talking to your boss or company if you're experiencing issues during the pandemic.  <br>You can write down specifically what you need, like a few days off or a lightened workload.  <br>You should also ask for it sooner rather than later.  <br>Business Insider reminds you not to wait until you've reached your breaking point to seek help.
