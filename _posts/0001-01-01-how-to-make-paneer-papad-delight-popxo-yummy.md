Paneer Papad Delight are are basically paneer fritters made with bread crumbs and papad. The snack is very easy to make and is a delicious crispy treat that every member of your family can enjoy.   <br>Watch today's episode of POPxo Yummy to make paneer papad delight from the comfort of your home.   <br>Step 1) Take 4 tbsp tomato sauce in a bowl  <br>Step 2) Add 1 tbsp mustard sauce and 1 tbsp chilli sauce  <br>Step 3) Add 1 tbsp red chilli powder and salt to taste and mix well  <br>Step 4) Add 2 tbsp corn flour and mix again.  <br>Step 5) Add 250 grams of paneer cubes and marinate the paneer in the mixture  <br>Step 6) Crush 10-12 papads and roll the paneer cubes in them  <br>Step 7) Fry the paneer cubes and serve with sauce of your choice.   <br>
