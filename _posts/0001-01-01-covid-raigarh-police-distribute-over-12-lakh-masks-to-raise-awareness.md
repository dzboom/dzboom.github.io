Police in Raigarh distributed over 12 lakh face masks on Raksha Bandhan under ‘Raksha Sutra Mask’ campaign amid COVID-19. SP Raigarh said, “Other organisations are also contributing in our campaign. In between 12 to 15 lakh masks have been distributed till now.” The campaign began at 9 am on August 03 with the help of 362 different organisations and 7,500 volunteers.   <br>
