The Current Covid-19 Pandemic Is Pushing Consumers To Digitally Interact With Their Banks, With Banking Institutions Focused On Customer Experience Gaining Significant Market Traction. According To The World Retail Banking Report 2020, 57% Of Consumers Now Prefer Internet Banking, Up From 49% During The Pre-Covid-19 Period.
