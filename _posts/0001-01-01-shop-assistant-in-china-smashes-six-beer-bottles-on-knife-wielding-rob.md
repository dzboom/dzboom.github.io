A shop assistant smashed six beer bottles on a knife-wielding robber's head to subdue him in northern China.  <br>The CCTV video, shot in the city of Dalian in Liaoning Province on June 17, shows a man attempting to rob a shop by using a knife to threaten a shop assistant.  <br>When the robber tried to put the knife onto the assistant's neck, he grabbed the nearby beer bottles and smashed them on the robber's head.  <br>After the robber fell onto the floor, the assistant ran out of the shop to call the police.  <br>Police has arrested the robber named Liu and he confessed the crime, while the shop assistant‘s action was justified as self-defence. The case is in further investigation.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
