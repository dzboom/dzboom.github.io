For the most part, Hilary Duff tends to keep her eye makeup pretty simple — we're talking mascara, sometimes eyeliner, and that's it. This week though, the 32-year-old actress decided to get out of her comfort zone and try a fun holographic liner look to wear with her face mask, and well, it turned out mesmerizing. The Younger star debuted the shimmery look on her Instagram Story in a selfie with her eyes closed, which really helped to showcase the full effect. As for what she used, that would be the blue shade from Lorac's Diamond Eye Gloss Trio.
