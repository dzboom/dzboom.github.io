The Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) has arrested Showik Chakraborty, and Sushant Singh Rajput house manager, Samuel Miranda, in the drug probe being carried out in connection with the Sushant death case. The NCB raided homes of both Showik Chakraborty and Samuel Miranda on Friday (September 4) morning. After the raids, the two were taken for further questioning by the NCB officials. NCB Deputy Director KPS Malhotra has had exclusive conversation with Aaj Tak on the issue. Watch this report.
