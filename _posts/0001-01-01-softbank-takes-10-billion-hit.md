On Tuesday, SoftBank stock took a hit.  <br>SoftBank lost 7.7% over two days following reports that the conglomerate had been making huge and risky bets on tech stocks.  <br>The losses have wiped roughly $10 billion off the market value of SoftBank.  <br>SoftBank shored up its investment in Amazon, Microsoft, and Netflix.  <br>The Wall Street Journal said that the options generated an exposure of about $50 billion.  <br>The size of the bets means that SoftBank (SFTBF) CEO Masayoshi Son is now being called a &quot;whale.&quot;  <br>A &quot;whale&quot; is a heavy hitter with the power to move markets on his own.  <br>His company's share holders are nervous because major investments in tech could lead to huge losses is the bubble bursts.
