Rhea Chakraborty, who was accused of allegedly abetting actor Sushant Singh Rajput for suicide, was questioned by the Enforcement Directorate for 8 hours on Friday. Riya Chakraborty reached the Mumbai office for questioning at the call of the Directorate of Promotion (ED) at 12 noon on Friday. . She exited the ED office at around 8.35 pm. Riya was questioned for nearly eight hours in the ED office. During this period, statements were also made with Rhea Chakraborty, along with his brother Shouvik Chakraborty, his business manager Shruti Modi and Rhea's CA Ritesh Shah.
