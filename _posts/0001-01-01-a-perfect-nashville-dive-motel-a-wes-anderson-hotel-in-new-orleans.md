The Condé Nast Traveler Hot List highlights the best hotel openings from around the world, and today we’re spotlighting a pair of properties that achieve a timeless cool in very different ways. The Dive Bar Motel and Swim Club in Nashville from Urban Cowboy curates Americana kitsch into a set of 23 unique rooms, each a party waiting to happen. At New Orleans' Maison de la Luz, Ace Hotel has taken an old office building and turned the Beaux-Arts and Art Deco vibes up to 10, making a visit to the concierge feel a little like stepping into a scene from a Wes Anderson film.
