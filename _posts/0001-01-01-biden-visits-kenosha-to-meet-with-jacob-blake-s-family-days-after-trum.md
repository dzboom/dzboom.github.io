CNN reports Democratic nominee Joe Biden is traveling to Wisconsin on Thursday.  <br>He plans to hold a community event in Kenosha and then meet with the family of Jacob Blake.  <br>Jacob Blake is the 29-year-old Black man who was recently shot in the back seven times by police.  <br>Biden's trip comes two days after President Donald Trump visited Wisconsin.  <br>Trump did not speak or meet with the family of Jacob Blake on his trip.  <br>Biden and Sen. Kamala Harris, have both spoken with Blake's family prior to the trip.
