A new poll from the Trafalgar Group shows Pres. Donald Trump is up by 3 points.  <br>The poll was conducted between September 1st and 3rd 2020.  <br>1022 likely 2020 voters responded.  <br>The majority of respondents were white and 45 and older.  <br>Democratic candidate Joe Biden has seen his double digit lead shrink over the the summer.  <br>He is now leading Trump by an average of 7 points nationally.  <br>The Presidential election will be held November 3rd, 2020.
