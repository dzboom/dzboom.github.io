Kalen Allen, food critic extraordinaire, is known for his viral reactions to some of the internet's wildest (and most questionable) recipe videos. After becoming a permanent fixture on Ellen DeGeneres' show and a mainstay on EllenTube, Kalen's career has taken off—he's now starring in Seth Rogen's new HBO Max film &quot;An American Pickle&quot;. In this episode of Food Diaries, Kalen breaks down a typical day of eats (on days when he remembers to eat), his go-to breakfast, and his secret to flavored pasta.
