The Indian Air Force (IAF) on Thursday formally inducted the much talked about fourth-generation flying machine, Rafale in its arsenal. The first batch of five aircrafts was formally inducted into the IAF's inventory at an impressive ceremony held at the strategic Ambala airbase. The event was attended by Union defence minister, Rajnath Singh and Florence Parly, minister of the armed forces of France.
