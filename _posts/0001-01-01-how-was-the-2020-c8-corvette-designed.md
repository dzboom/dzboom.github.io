Corvette is the longest-running sports car in America.  <br>The 2020 Corvette marks a big departure from Corvette design with its mid-engine layout.  <br>Kirk Bennion, the design manager for the new Corvette, talked to Business Insider about how the 2020 Corvette was designed.  <br>Bennion says the mid-engine layout is &quot;really good from a performance standpoint.&quot;  <br>He said the change allowed them to move the driver and occupant forward16 1/2 inches.  <br>The mid-engine Corvette gives the car performance attributes, like excellent speed, precision, handling, and braking.
