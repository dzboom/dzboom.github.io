World Health Organisation (WHO) spokeswoman Margaret Harris says that the spread of the coronavirus does not appear to be influenced by seasons of the year. Her remarks on July 28, 2020, countered false claims that the virus that causes Covid-19 is “seasonal” and that summer is a safer time.   Video source: UNTV
