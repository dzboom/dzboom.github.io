National shuttler Datuk Lee Chong Wei brought the house down in Yong Peng, Johor when he came to play badminton with the locals.  <br>Meanwhile, Ayer Hitam incumbent MP Datuk Seri Dr Wee Ka Siong said Lee’s appearance was to fulfill a longstanding invitation for him to play in Yong Peng.  <br>Dr Wee said this when attending the ‘A Moment with Datuk Lee Chong Wei’ event on Saturday.
