Four years after their last group project, Filthy America…It’s Beautiful, The LOX return with their fourth studio album, 'Living Off Xperience.' The Yonkers rap trio sat down with Genius' VP of Content Strategy Rob Markman on the latest episode of 'For The Record' to discuss how the project came together. During the interview, Jadakiss, Styles P and Sheek Louch reflected on their decades-long career, explained how Biggie’s death impacted their decision to leave Bad Boy, and confirmed the existence of a “Victory” reference track.
