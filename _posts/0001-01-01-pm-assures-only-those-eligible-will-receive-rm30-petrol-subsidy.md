Addressing the new Targeted Subsidy Programme (PSP) for petrol after the tabling of Budget 2020 in Parliament on Friday, Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad assured that the fixed amount of RM30 will be deposited into eligible car owners to ensure that only the correct group will benefit from it.
