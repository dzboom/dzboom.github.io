In a shocking incident caught on camera, A group of men ordered the popular south Indian dish sambar-dosa at a well-known restaurant in central Delhi's Connaught Place, but instead they got the shock of their lives,  a dead lizard in their bowl. In a video, which is being widely circulated on social media, two men are seen holding out the carcass of the lizard on a spoon; half-eaten food is visible in the background. The men are seen yelling at who appear to be the restaurant staff.
