Daily lives of people have been adversely affected in Karnataka’s Belagavi due to release of water from Naviluteertha Dam. Houses and farmland have been partly submerged in water. Sunnal village of Belagavi is witnessing severe waterlogging. Waterlogging has led to blockage of road connected Sunnal to Ramdurg village. People in the village are forced to leave their houses to move to another village.   <br>
