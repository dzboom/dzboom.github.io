Carrying excess weight increases the risk of hospitalisation, admission to intensive care, and death from Covid-19, according to Public Health England (PHE). PHE's Chief Nutritionist Dr Alison Tedstone says &quot;it's too early to understand why obesity is interacting with Covid-19” but added that excess weight &quot;puts more pressure on the body which makes it harder to fight infections&quot;.
