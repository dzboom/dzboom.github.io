Kites are great. We use them for fun, but has a kite ever been used as a tow truck? Friends Stuart and Chase were in the mood for some waves, but the ocean swell was too small for Chase to practice his wake foiling. Then, the genius idea struck. Using the kite as a &quot;tow,&quot; Stuart was able to take Chase through the ocean, successfully navigating it so Chase could wake foil his way through the ocean blue!
