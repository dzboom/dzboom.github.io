The worldwide number of confirmed coronavirus cases surpassed 25 million on August 30, 2020. Infections continued to rise in the worst-hit regions of the US and Brazil, as well as India, which is dealing with a surge in the number of cases in what is considered the world’s fastest-growing outbreak. Even nations that had the situation largely under control, such as South Korea and New Zealand, are seeing increasing numbers of infections.
