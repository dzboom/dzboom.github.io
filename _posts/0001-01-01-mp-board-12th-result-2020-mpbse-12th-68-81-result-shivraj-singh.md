The Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education released the results of class 12 on Monday. This year, 68.81% of the total students have passed the Class 12th examination. Compared to last year, this year the pass percentage has decreased. Last year, 72.37% students achieved success. This year, Khushi Singh has secured first position in the examination with 486 marks. After that Madhulata Atmaja got 479 points and Nikita Patidar got 474 points in second place.This year, girls have achieved top 5 rank in arts stream.
