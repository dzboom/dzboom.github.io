In a recent conversation with former India cricketer Aakash Chopra on his Youtube chat show ‘Aakash Vani’, Chawla recalled that once a selector criticised him for always picking wickets with googly delivery. “I remember once I spoke to a selector. That selector told me that I take most wickets from googly,” Chawla said. The 31-year-old was part of India team that won the 2007 T20I World Cup and 2011 ODI World Cup as well.
