Sugaring is a traditional, &quot;painless&quot; form of hair removal that uses a paste made of only three simple ingredients — water, lemon, and sugar — to remove hair from the root, making it a good wax alternative for sensitive skin. Sugaring is usually done at a salon, but with many salons closed, people have been attempting to sugar off body hair at home.
