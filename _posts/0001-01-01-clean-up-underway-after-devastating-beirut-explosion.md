Beirut got to work today (August 8th) cleaning up the debris of Tuesday's devastating explosion.  <br>Footage showed teams of street-sweepers and local residents attempting to restore order while surrounded on all sides by destruction.  <br>The explosion left at least 154 dead.  <br>The blast was caused by a huge stock of ammonium nitrate that had been seized from a ship but never moved.
