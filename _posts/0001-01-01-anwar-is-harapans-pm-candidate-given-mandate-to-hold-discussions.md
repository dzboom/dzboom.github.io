The Pakatan Harapan presidential council today decided to commit to its earlier decision that PKR president Anwar Ibrahim will be the coalition’s prime ministerial candidate.  <br>The council also said that Anwar now has a full mandate from the coalition to continue discussions with all parties to return the people's mandate to Harapan.
