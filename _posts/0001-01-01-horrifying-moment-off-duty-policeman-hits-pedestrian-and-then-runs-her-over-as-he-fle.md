A security camera in the Indian capital has captured the horrifying moment an off-duty police officer hit a female pedestrian in his car and then ran her over as he was attempting to flee.  <br>The disturbing scene took place on July 3rd.   <br>The footage shows the hatchback hit the woman, knocking her to the ground.  <br>As passers-by surround her to help her to her feet, the driver now runs her over and flees the scene.   <br>The driver has been identified as a 56-year-old Delhi Police sub-inspector and has been arrested.  <br>The woman, described as &quot;elderly&quot;, was badly injured in the incident and is still undergoing treatment.
