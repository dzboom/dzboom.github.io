Dr Mahathir Mohamad announced the formation of an &quot;independent&quot; Malay party which would uphold Bersatu's original anti-corruption struggle.  <br>The reason for this move was because he claimed Bersatu president Muhyiddin Yassin had “hijacked” the party.  <br>Mahathir also claimed all other Malay-based parties had become more focused on protecting “elites” rather than the Malays.
