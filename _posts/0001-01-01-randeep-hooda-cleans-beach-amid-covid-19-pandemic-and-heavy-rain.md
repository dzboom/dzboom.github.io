Amid the coronavirus pandemic and heavy rainfall in Mumbai, actor Randeep Hooda stepped out to be a part of clean-up drive of Versova beach. He shared a few pictures from the cleanup drive and wrote, “This is the backyard of humanity and it’s not pretty” as he picked up bottles, cans, plastic packets and other waste that has been dumped by people. He also mentioned that all social distancing norms and precautions were taken as they cleaned the beach. On the work front, Randeep was last seen in the Hollywood film ‘Extraction’ alongside Chris Hemsworth. And will be next seen in Salman Khan’s upcoming film titled ‘Radhe’.
