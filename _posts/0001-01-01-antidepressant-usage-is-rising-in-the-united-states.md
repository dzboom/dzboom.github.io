Nearly 18% of all adult women in the US were prescribed antidepressant medication between 2015 and 2018.  <br>That is in stark comparison to just over 8% of men.  <br>During the decade between 2009-2010 and 2017-2018, antidepressant use increased to 14% from 11%,.  <br>In 2018, slightly more than 7% of people over 18 in the US said they suffered from a &quot;major depressive episode&quot;.  <br>Depression is a disorder in which sufferers experience a persistent depressed mood or loss of interest in activities.  <br>Antidepressant medications are used to reduce the symptoms of depression.
