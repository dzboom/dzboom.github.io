Patriots News: Julian Edelman Snubbed From NFL Top 100 List  <br>Patriots star wideout Julian Edelman turned out an impressive season in 2019-2020. The veteran wideout caught 100 balls, had over 1,110 yards and scored six touchdowns as one of the lone weapons on the New England offense. Still, Edelman was not named to the NFL Top 100 list for his efforts. A list that is voted on by Edelman's fellow peers in the NFL.  <br>It's not the first time Jules has been snubbed and it should only serve as more motivation for this season.
