Amid the ongoing debate over privatization of airports, the central government has indicated that it will increase further in the coming times. Union Civil Aviation Minister Hardeep Singh Puri said that Air India has been an important asset for us. But at the same time, he said that it is not the responsibility of the government to run airports and airlines.
