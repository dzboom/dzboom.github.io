This guy showed some amazing pool tricks. He pounded the cue ball with a cue stick, which struck another ball right into a table pocket. He did this trickshot while laying other balls in different formations on the table like a puzzle. However, not once did the cue ball touch any other object on the table.The guy also kept glasses on the table along with the balls and displayed a similar trickshot. In the end, he struck the cue ball with his foot and hand, scoring points.
