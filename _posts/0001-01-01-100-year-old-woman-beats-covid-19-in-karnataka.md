A 100-year-old woman from Karnataka’s Huvina Hadagali won the battle against Covid-19. Hallamma is one of the oldest patient diagnosed with Covid-19 and recovered from it in the state. She was treated at her home along with her son, daughter-in-law and grandson. Hallamma tested positive for the virus on July 16.
