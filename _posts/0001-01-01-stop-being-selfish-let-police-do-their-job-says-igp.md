The Inspector-General of Police Tan Sri Abdul Hamid Bador has told off the many &quot;experts&quot; who have spoken out on the M. Indira Gandhi case to stop being selfish and let the police do their job.  <br>He said there are many religious and NGO champions who are trying to score political points.   <br>It was reported that the lawyers of Indira were asking if Hamid knew the location of her daughter Prasana Diksa and ex-husband K. Pathmanathan (who is now known as Muhammad Riduan Abdullah) – who have been missing for 11 years.
