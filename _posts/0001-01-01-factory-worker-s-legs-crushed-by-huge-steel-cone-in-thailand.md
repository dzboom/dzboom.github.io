A three-tonne steel coil fell onto a factory worker while he was replacing the rolls in a warehouse.  <br>The worker was crushed by the huge steel rolls while on duty in the industrial parts factory in Samut Prakan, central Thailand on August 3.  <br>CCTV footage shows the man controlling a pulley to move the steel coils when one one toppled over onto his legs.  <br>Struggling to breath, he tried to push the heavy 3,000 kilogram metal off his body while shouting for help from his colleagues.  <br>One of his co-workers who walked by the scene saw him suffering under the steel roll and rushed to help him while another worker helped removed the metal.   <br>The emergency services took the worker to the hospital where he was found to be seriously injured with two broken legs.  <br>Police said the incident was being dealt with internally by the factory and the injured worker.
