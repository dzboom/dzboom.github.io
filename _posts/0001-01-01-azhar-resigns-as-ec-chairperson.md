Azhar Azizan Harun has resigned as chairperson of the Election Commission (EC) effective June 29.  <br>The EC said that its deputy chairperson Azmi Sharom will assume the duty of EC chairperson until a new one is appointed in accordance with Article 114 (7) of the Federal Constitution.
