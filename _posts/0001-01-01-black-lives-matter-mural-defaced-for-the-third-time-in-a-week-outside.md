The Black Lives Matter street mural in front of Trump Tower in New York has been defaced for the third time in a week.  <br>The latest incident filmed Saturday afternoon (July 18) shows two women pouring blue paint over the mural before smearing it into the letters.  <br>&quot;Black Lives Matter? But you want to defund the police for black people?!&quot; the woman in the video can be heard shouting.  <br>&quot;The women were wearing shirts with Jesus on them. Both women were arrested and rushed away from the scene. The scene lasted for a while, one officer seemingly struggling to contain one of the perps,&quot; the filmer told Newsflare.  <br>One of the officers at the scene slipped on the paint and injured his head and arm, according to local reports, he is recovering in a local hospital.
