FierceHealthCare reports that CVS Health's pharmacy benefit manager (PBM), added five new solutions to its health care platform.  <br>The new solutions allow its customers to sift through digital health solutions to include in health plans.  <br>PBMs have traditionally been responsible for administering prescriptions and negotiating the costs of drugs for large employers and health insurers.  <br>However, according to Business Insider, CVS Caremark began using this same model for digital health products in June 2019.  <br>CVS wants to enable payers to formalize the reimbursement and distribution for digital tools.  <br>Among the newly added solutions are mental health tools designed to manage anxiety.
