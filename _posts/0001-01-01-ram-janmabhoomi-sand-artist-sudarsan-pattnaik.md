In Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, Prime Minister Narendra Modi will perform Bhoomi Pujan for the construction of Ram temple. This wait, being done for almost 492 years, will end today. On this special occasion, Sand Artist Sudarshan Patnaik has created a wonderful artwork of Ram temple. Happiness is being celebrated today in the entire country regarding the construction of Ram temple.
