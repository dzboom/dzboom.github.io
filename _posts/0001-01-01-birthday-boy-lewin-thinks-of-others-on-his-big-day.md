Lewin Tubuna is walking 100 miles during August to support Alice House Hospice and even the pandemic won’t stop him.  <br>Lewin originally planned to do the Yorkshire Three Peaks Challenge – climbing the peaks of Pen-y-ghent (694m), Whernside (736m) and Ingleborough (723m) – in under 12 hours.  <br>But the coronavirus restrictions meant he had to put that idea on hold.  <br>Lewin, though, came up with an alternative challenge closer to home. He is walking 100 miles this month for the hospice and has already got £1,337 towards his £3,000 total.
