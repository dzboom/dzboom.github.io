A new study from Iceland provides reassurance that our antibodies are long-lasting.  <br>Covid-19 antibodies can last at least four months according to reports at Gizmodo.  <br>That is, assuming that we produce them in the first place.  <br>The study looked at blood samples collected from over 30,000 people in Iceland.  <br>That is a bit under 10% percent of the small Nordic country’s total population.  <br>That's more than 4,000 people who had either tested positive or were exposed to someone with the virus.
