Thousands of inmates in California state prisons could be released as early as August.  <br>This is in an attempt to curb the spread of covid-19, a frequently deadly disease.  <br>The disease itself is caused by the novel coronavirus strain SARS-CoV-2 reports Gizmodo.  <br>An estimated 8,000 incarcerated individuals could be released by the end of next month.  <br>This measure is meant to create space to allow prisons to better implement safety protocols.  <br>Currently, standards like social distancing and quarantining cannot be met inside the prison.
