Pelosi Issues Obligatory Mask Rule  for the House Chamber The House Speaker's new order comes  after Texas GOP Rep. Louie Gohmert  tested positive for COVID-19. According to HuffPost, Gohmert and other  Republican lawmakers have defied suggestions  to wear masks in areas of the House. Nancy Pelosi,  via HuffPost Fox News reports that the House side of  the Capitol and its office buildings require masks. The rule considers not  covering one's face to be a  &quot;serious breach of decorum.&quot; Refusing to wear a mask could result  in one's removal from the building. Nancy Pelosi, via HuffPost
