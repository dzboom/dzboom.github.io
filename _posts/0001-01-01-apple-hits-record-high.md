Shares of Apple have surged as much as 5% to an intraday high of $446.55 per share on Monday.  <br>That continues a post-earnings rally for the tech company.  <br>The surge pushed Apple's market capitalization to $1.92 trillion.  <br>If Apple gains another 5%, it will become the first company to hit a $2 trillion market value.  <br>Business Insider reports that Apple has had quite the year, gaining more than 48% even amid the coronavirus crisis.
