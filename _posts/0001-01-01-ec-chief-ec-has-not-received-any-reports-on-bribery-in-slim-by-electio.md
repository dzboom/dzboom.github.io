Election Commission (EC) chairperson Abdul Ghani Salleh said they have not received any complaints related to bribery in the recent Slim by-election.  <br>He said there was “none at all” of such complaints lodged with the EC but said that if there was any, it would be handed over to the MACC to be investigated.
