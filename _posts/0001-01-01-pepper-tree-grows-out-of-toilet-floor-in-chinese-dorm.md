A pepper tree was spotted growing out of the toilet floor in a dorm at South China University Of Technology.  <br>The video, filmed in the city of Guangzhou in Guangdong Province on August 29, shows a pepper tree growing out the side of a toilet.  <br>Since it was not convenient for the students to use the toilet, they broke the tree to remove it.  <br>According to reports, the students were not in the dorm for eight months due to the coronavirus outbreak.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
