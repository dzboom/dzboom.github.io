Ilhan Omar represents Minneapolis, Minnesota in congress.  <br>She is facing a stiff primary challenge from lawyer Antone Melton-Meaux.  <br>Melton-Meaux raised an absurd $3.2 million in the second quarter of this year to Omar’s $471,000.  <br>Omar represents a sizable Jewish community in her district.  <br>She has been the face of multiple national controversies for her perceived use of coded anti-Semitic language.  <br>Slate reports there’s a new round of controversy brewing about the language with which Omar’s mailers criticize Melton-Meaux’s donors.  <br>Omar is also languishing in the polls behind Melton-Meaux.
