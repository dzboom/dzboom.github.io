Amid the Corona virus crisis, Prime Minister Narendra Modi hoisted the tricolor on the occasion of 74th Independence Day at the Red Fort today. After hoisting the tricolor, PM Narendra Modi addressed the country. In his address, PM Modi also talked about the corona vaccine. The people of the country told that at present, three different vaccine trials of the Corona virus are going on.
