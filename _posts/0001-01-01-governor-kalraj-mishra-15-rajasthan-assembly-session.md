The political struggle of Rajasthan's Ashok Gehlot Government is going on. In the midst of the demand for an assembly session, Governor Kalraj Mishra has expressed deep concern over the terrible condition of Corona in the state. He said that the growing active case of Corona in the state is a matter of concern for the state.
