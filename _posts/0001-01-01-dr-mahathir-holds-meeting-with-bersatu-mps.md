Interim Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad is holding a meeting with Parti Pribumi Bersatu Bersatu MPs Friday morning.  <br>He arrived at at Yayasan Albukhary in Kuala Lumpur around 9.10am in a dark blue Mercedes Benz.  <br>On Thursday, Dr Mahathir Mohamad said a special sitting of the Dewan Rakyat would be held on March 2 to determine the candidate who commands majority support to be the next prime minister.
