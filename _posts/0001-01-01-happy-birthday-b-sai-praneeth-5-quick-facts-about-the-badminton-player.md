Born on August 10th, 1992, B Sai Praneeth is one of the most successful badminton players produced by India. He came into limelight after defeating Muhammad Hafiz Hashim of Malaysia at the 2013 Thailand Open Grand Prix Gold tournament in the first round. Praneeth currently plays in Hyderabad and trains at the Gopichand Badminton Academy.
