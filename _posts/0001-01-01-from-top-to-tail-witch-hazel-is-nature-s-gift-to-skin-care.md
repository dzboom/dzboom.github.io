Ten years ago, toners were notorious for being too harsh on the skin. So much so, in fact, that dermatologists often discouraged their use.  <br>But according to Allure, toners, essences, and face mists now contain gentler ingredients that aren't so rough on the complexion.  <br>Witch hazel is a botanical extract derived from a flowering plant found in North America and Japan.  <br>It has a number of skin benefits including being soothing, acting as an anti-inflammatory, and having antioxidant properties.  <br>Another benefit is treating acne, by removing excess oil from the skin. It can be drying if overused, though, so be careful not to over-apply.  <br>Besides its blemish-busting abilities, witch hazel is actually used in many hemorrhoid treatments.  <br>So if you need to de-puff your eyes one morning, just grab some refrigerated medicated hemorrhoid pads or cream and apply!
