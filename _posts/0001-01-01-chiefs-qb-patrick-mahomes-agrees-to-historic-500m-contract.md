Chiefs QB Patrick Mahomes Agrees  to Historic $500M Contract On Monday, the Kansas City Chiefs  announced that Mahomes has extended  his contract with the agency. According to the Chiefs, Mahomes' $503 million  agreement makes him &quot;the first half billion dollar  player in sports history.&quot; The contract includes  $477 million in guarantee  mechanisms and a no-trade clause. The NFL Network's Tom Pelissero  tweeted a year-by-year breakdown.  Chiefs CEO Clark Hunt called  Mahomes &quot;one of the most prolific  athletes in all of sports.&quot; Clark Hunt, via statement Chiefs coach Andy Reid called the  24-year-old Super Bowl MVP one of the most  impressive athletes he's coached in the NFL.
