We still remember back in 2011 when we put Maureen Wroblewitz on the September issue of Preview Magazine, that the Filipino-German model would have loved to be a beauty vlogger. Obviously her career path went a little differently since she won Asia's Next Top Model that same year. Since then, Maureen has had quite a commercial modeling career, started a YouTube channel, and even tried her hand at hosting for a noon time show! Recently she's found her way to another social media phenomenon, and Maureen has been quite the hit on TikTok!   <br>For this episode of Barefaced Beauty, we asked the young model to take off her makeup on camera and cracks a few jokes while she's at it, too. Somehow proving that the content creator inside her will always be present. And did we mention she only used ONE cotton pad to clean her entire face?
