The E-Tour du Mont-Blanc (E-TMB) is the flagship event of the VERBIER E-BIKE FESTIVAL and the second stage is in the books. By invitation only, elite e-bike riders will take part in a 3 day, 300km race, (including 260km of single tracks) around Mont- Blanc, through Switzerland, Italy, and France. The E-TMB is the only event in the festival reserved for experienced e-bikers, and is an exceptional e-race.   <br>The Verbier E-bike Festival will take place for the 2nd time this summer in Val de Bagnes. 4 days dedicated to e-biking. With 5 different activities on offer: E-bike test, Rando Gourmande, Discovery Trails, E-Tour du Val de Bagnes or Bosch E-MTB Challenge.
