유엔 &quot;코로나19가 에이즈 대응 위협…치료제 보급 차질&quot;  <br>The World Health Organization has warned that the world could run out of HIV/AIDS treatment as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.  <br>During a virtual international AIDS conference organized by the UN,... the WHO said 24 countries were reporting either a critically low stock or disruptions in the supply of such life-saving drugs.  <br>The head of the WHO stressed that countries need to do all they can to ensure access to the treatment,... adding the world cannot let COVID-19 undo the hard-won gains in the global response to HIV/AIDS.  <br>
