Amid tensions, Prime Minister Narendra Modi suddenly reached Leh on Friday on the Line of Actual Control. Modi's visit is also a message to China that the country is standing with the army. Enthusiasts meeting PM Modi shouted slogans of Bharat Mata ki Jai and Vande Mataram. PM Modi also encouraged Indian soldiers by addressing them.
