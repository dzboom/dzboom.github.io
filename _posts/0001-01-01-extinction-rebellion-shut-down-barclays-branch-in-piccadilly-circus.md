Activists from Extinction Rebellion ended up temporarily shutting down a branch of Barclays in London's Piccadilly Circus on Wednesday (September 2).  <br>Protesters gathered outside the branch over Barclays' investments into fossil fuels. One put tape on the pavement outside reading &quot;climate crime scene&quot;  <br>The staff inside the branch refused to let customers in after the protesters arrived and one managed to enter &amp; demonstrate inside the bank.
