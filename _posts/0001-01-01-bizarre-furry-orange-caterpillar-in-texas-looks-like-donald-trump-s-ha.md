A bizarre golden caterpillar looked like Donald Trump's hair as it crawled along a wall.  <br>The venomous creature was seen shimmying up the house in Austin, Texas.  <br>Onlooker Boba Carrera said he was painting the walls when the little caterpillar passed by and caught his eye, with the sunlight reflecting from its hairs.  <br>Boba nicknamed the caterpillar 'Donald' after noticing its resemblance to the President's distinctive blonde barnet.  <br>He said: &quot;It’s caterpillar season so we have lots here, all super cute. Sometimes I find them crawling inside my house as well. I pick them up and place them in the garden.  <br>''I will call him Donald because of its similarities to the President.''  <br>The creature was later identified as a puss caterpillar. Their hairs are venomous and cause severe pain. They are found in trees, shrubbery around buildings.
