Nearly 100 bikers joined a funeral procession this afternoon (29th June) as fellow motorcyclist Andrew Kitson 44 was laid to rest.  <br>Bikers gathered in a car park near to where Mr Kitson lived at Lofthouse, West Yorkshire, then joined in a convoy to pay their respects, as the procession began around 3pm  <br>Mr Kitson was tragically killed on 9th June, when he was struck by a car being chased by police, instantly killing him.  <br>A 22 year old man was arrested at the scene, not knowing he had hit a pedestrian, until a neighbour discovered Mr Kitsons body in their garden, who then alerted police.  <br>Andrew was a keen motorcyclist &amp; so posts were shared on social media, asking for fellow bikers to join the procession, which started in Lofthouse and finished at Kettlethorpe Crematorium.
