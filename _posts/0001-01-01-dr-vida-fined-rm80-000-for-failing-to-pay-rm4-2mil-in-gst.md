Beauty products entrepreneur Datuk Seri Hasmiza Othman, better known as Dr Vida, has been fined RM80,000 by the Ipoh Sessions Court after she pleaded guilty to charges of failing to pay the Goods and Services Tax (GST) totalling RM4.2mil.
