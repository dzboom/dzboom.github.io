On Monday, Lamborghini announced buyers can now customize their new cars through virtual consultations.  <br>Normally, customers would have to fly out to the company's headquarters in Italy to create their vehicles.   <br>To promote the new service, Lamborghini launched a limited run of 10 Aventador SVJ Xago models.   <br>The Xago-edition Aventadors come with special hexagonal patterns inside and out.  <br>Customers are required to personalize them via the new virtual option.
