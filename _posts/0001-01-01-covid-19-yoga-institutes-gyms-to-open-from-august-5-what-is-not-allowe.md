Under the third phase of unlocking the Covid-19 lockdown, government has allowed gymnasiums and yoga institutes to open for public from August 5. As per the guidelines issued by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Yoga institutes and gymnasiums located in Covid-19 containment zones shall not be permitted to open. Govt has issue guidelines to ensure proper precautionary measures against the spread of Coronavirus.     <br>Elderly people above the age of 65 years, people with co-morbidities, pregnant women and children below the age of 10 years have been directed not to use gymnasiums or yoga institutes in closed spaces.   <br>#Covid-19 #Yogainstitutes
