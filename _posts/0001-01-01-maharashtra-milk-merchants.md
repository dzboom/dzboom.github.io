Amidst the Corona crisis, milk traders in Maharashtra continue to perform. The Swabhimani Shetkari organization is continuously performing. The workers of the organization started milking on the streets of Sangli. The organization demands that the minimum rate of cow's milk should be fixed at Rs 25 per liter. Significantly, in Sangli, Maharashtra, the protest of the workers of the Swabhimani Shetkari Organization is going on with regard to the price of milk.
