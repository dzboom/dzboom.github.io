China's remote sensing satellite Jilin-1 Gaofen-02C satellite missed orbit on Saturday. China satellite launched with half-baked preparations failed to reach its target And crashed in the middle of the sky. The satellite was launched from Kuikhu-1A rocket from the Jikuan Satellite Launch Center at 1.20 pm local time on Saturday.
