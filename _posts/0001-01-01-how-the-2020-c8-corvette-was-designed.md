Corvette is the longest-running sports car in American history. Every generation is tied together with distinctive features like the double-digit tail lights, watermark, and other styling cues. The latest installment, the C8, is a bit of a departure from Corvettes of the past. It sports a completely new layout, but still somehow aligns with Corvette architecture. Chevrolet design manager Kirk Bennion shares insights on the design of the new 2020 C8 Corvette.
