This is the moment a seven-year-old boy cried as firefighters cut away a bicycle ball bearing ring that was stuck on his finger.  <br>The youngster was sent to the hospital after he curiously put the metal casing on his finger but could not remove it.  <br>Doctors unsuccessfully used lubricants and a splint before calling the local rescue team for help in Krabi, southern Thailand.  <br>Footage shows one of the men carefully and slowly cutting the bearing ring away from the boy's finger while he was sobbing throughout the process.  <br>The boy's parents said that their son was careless and should learn from this mistake.  <br>The mother said: &quot;I think it is normal for a child to be curious but they have to learn so they do not repeat the mistake again.&quot;
