Rebel Congress leader Sachin Pilot has said last one year has been a struggle to protect his self-respect with Ashok Gehlot and his loyalists ganging up on him after Rahul Gandhi quit as the Congress chief. In an exclusive chat with India Today Magazine, Sachin Pilot said, Rahul Gandhi is no more the Congress president. After he quit last year, Gehlot ji and his friends in AICC ganged up against me. Since then it became a struggle for protecting my self-respect. Sachin Pilot was on Tuesday sacked from the posts of Rajasthan Deputy CM and state Congress chief. Watch video.
