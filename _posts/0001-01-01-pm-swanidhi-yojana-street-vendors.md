There is good news for street vendors. The Modi government at the Center has made the terms of the PM Swanidhi scheme easier. Those who do not have identity card and sales certificate will also be able to take advantage of this scheme. Regarding this, the Union Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has started a letter of recommendation i.e. LOR system. Under this, eligible street vendors will be given certificates or identity cards.
