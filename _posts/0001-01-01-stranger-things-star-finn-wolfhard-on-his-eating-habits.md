Actor Finn Wolfhard known for portraying the role of Mike Wheeler in the Netflix hit series Stranger Things opened up about his eating habits. He revealed that his eating habits are that of a four-year old child and at times he is left feeling crappy because of it. Finn said, &quot;I still eat like a four-year-old child, which is not a good thing when you're constantly travelling and feeling crappy because of your diet.” Well, Finn didn’t say if it means like he is fussy eater like a toddler or just consumes the amount of food like a 4-year old. Speaking of Finn Wolfhard, he joined Stranger Things back in 2016 and recalling the memories he had earlier said that he thought of quitting acting shortly before his audition for now world-renowned series. But alas, now looking back he cannot believe that how popular the show has become over the past few years. In a recent interaction Finn said, &quot;We just thought we were filming this secret thing that no one knew about. Which we were. No one knew what we were doing. Netflix were kind of hands-off. We thought maybe it would become a cult classic, and we'll come back to it in 30 years and be really proud of doing it.&quot; However, the good thing is despite the success of the show, Finn has kept himself away from the trappings of fame. Finn was only 13 years old when he became a part of the Stranger Things squad but he said that he’s been lucky to grow up in a ‘very positive’ environment.
