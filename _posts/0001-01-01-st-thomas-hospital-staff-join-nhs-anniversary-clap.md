Staff from St Thomas' Hospital in central London joined the nationwide applause to celebrate the 72nd birthday of the NHS on Sunday (July 5).  <br>Members of the public also participated, including a cyclist with great balance and a man with a Union Jack flag which read &quot;Thank you NHS and all key workers&quot;.  <br>St Thomas' Hospital is where Prime Minister Boris Johnson was treated when he caught COVID-19.
