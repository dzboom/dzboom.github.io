Corona is spreading rapidly in the country, now the total number of infected people in the country has crossed 23 lakh. According to the latest data of the Health Ministry, 23 lakh 29 thousand 638 people have been infected with Corona so far in the country. Of these, 46,091 people have died, while 16 lakh 39 thousand people have also recovered. In the last 24 hours, 60,963 new cases of corona virus were reported and 834 deaths occurred.
