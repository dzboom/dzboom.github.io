DJ Erick Morillo has died according to Miami Beach Police.  <br>According to CNN, the popular DJ was found dead at his Florida home on Tuesday morning.  <br>The Colombia-born artist had a huge influence on house music in his career spanning 30 years  <br>Morillo had a hit single early in his career with the El General single, &quot;Muevelo.&quot;  <br>But he is best known for his Reel 2 Real 1994 song &quot;Go On Move,&quot; also known as &quot;I Like To Move It&quot;.  <br>The cause of death has not been determined yet. He was 49 years old.
