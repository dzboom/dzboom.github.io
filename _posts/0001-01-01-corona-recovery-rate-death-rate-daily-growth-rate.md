The corona virus continues to wreak havoc. So far, nine lakh people in the country have fallen victim to corona. At the same time, the Health Ministry has said that the daily growth rate of corona virus is continuously falling, so the death rate has come down to 2.6%. The Ministry's OSD Rajesh Bhushan has also said that 86 percent of the cases in the country are in 10 states.
