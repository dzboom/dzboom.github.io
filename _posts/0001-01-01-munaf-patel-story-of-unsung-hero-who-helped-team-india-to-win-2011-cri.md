Munaf Patel, born July 12, 1983, is an Indian pacer who was once touted to be the fastest bowler in India. Munaf was born in Ikhar, a farming village of barely 8,000 people near Bharuch in Gujarat. While Gujarat and Baroda were both vying for Munaf’s services, he was approached by Tendulkar to play for Mumbai. Munaf had to wait till 2006 before making his Test debut. But when he finally debuted. Munaf was the unsung hero of India’s ICC Cricket World Cup 2011 title victory.
