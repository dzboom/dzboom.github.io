This is the cute moment an enthusiastic six-year-old girl invites people to visit her “paradise” Italian seaside region for the summer season as she wants to make coronavirus sadness &quot;fly away&quot; for everyone.  <br>Little Paoletta lives in the Italian region of Marche, traditionally popular with tourists attracted by its rich history and cultural heritage, as well as its traditional seaside resorts.  <br>And now people are reportedly booking up in region once again, possibly in part thanks to the little girl's promotional video, which was posted online by Valeria Mancinelli, the Mayor of Ancona, a city in the region.  <br>
