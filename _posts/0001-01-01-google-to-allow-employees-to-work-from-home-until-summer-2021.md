Google employees will be allowed to work from home until at least July 2021.  <br>According to CNN, the tech company made the announcement concerning their employee's on Monday.  <br>The company had initially said employees would be working remotely through the end of 2020.  <br>The decision to extend the work from home policy into next year indicates they are bracing for a long pandemic.  <br>The tech industry has led the charge on remote work during the pandemic, with many making the option permanent.
