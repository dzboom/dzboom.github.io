The number of COVID-19 cases in India is increasing steadily, 19,148 new cases of coronavirus have been reported in the last 24 hours in India. 434 deaths have been recorded in one day. With this, by Thursday morning i.e. July 2, 2020, the total number of pandemic Kovid-19 in the country has increased to 6,04641. If we talk about the recovered patients, a total of 3,59,860 people have been cured of the disease so far, while the total death toll in the country has reached 17,834.
