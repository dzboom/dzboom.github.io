Madonna launched her first tour in 1985 - The Virgin Tour. Shortly after, in 1987 she toured the world for the first time in support of &quot;Who's That Girl.&quot; Over the next three decades Madonna would put on the most controversial and dynamic pop shows in the world, selling out nearly every tour in seconds. To date, Madonna has performed over 700 shows across 11 concert tours.
