Mumbai Police investigation is going on in actor Sushant Singh Rajput suicide case. Big celebrities of Bollywood have been questioned. In this episode, today, the statement of producer-director Mahesh Bhatt has been recorded at Santacruz police station on Monday. Mahesh Bhatt reached the police station at around 12 noon. He was questioned for about 2 hours.
