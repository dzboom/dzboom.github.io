The holy month of Shravan (also spelt as Sawan or Savan) commenced on July 6 this year as per the Purnimant calendar. Every Monday or Somwar is considered auspicious for performing a vrat to pay ode to Lord Shiva. But the third Sawan Somwar of this year 20 july 2020, is extra special because it concurs with Amavasya or the New Moon day. And it is unique because an Amavasya coinciding with Shravan Somwar happens rarely. Somvati Amavasya is a New Moon Day that falls on a Monday.
