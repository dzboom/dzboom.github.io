New York Attorney General Letitia James has announced she will attempt to dissolve the NRA.  <br>The NRA, or National Rifle Association, has been accused of breaking the law, says CNN.  <br>It's senior leadership is accused of violating laws governing non-profit groups.  <br>They are also accused of using millions from the organization's reserves for personal use and tax fraud.  <br>James has alleged that current and former NRA leadership &quot;instituted a culture of self-dealing mismanagement&quot;.  <br>This culture benefited themselves, family, friends, and favored vendors.  <br>This led the organization to &quot;lose&quot; more than $63 million in three years.
