In Lucknow, the police have registered a case against 4 people in front of the CM office on Monday for attempting to self-immolate. According to the police, in Amethi, MIM district president Kadir Khan, Amethi Congress leader Anoop Patel, apart from two other people named Asma and Sultan, hatched the conspiracy. These four provoked the mother-daughter. The police have arrested Asam and Qadir Khan.
