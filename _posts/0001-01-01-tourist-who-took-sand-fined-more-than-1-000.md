A French tourist has been ordered to pay a €1,000 ($1,200) fine.  <br>This is after attempting to fly out of Sardinia with more than four pounds of local sand in his luggage.  <br>The Italian island's idyllic white sand is protected, according to reports at CNN.  <br>Tourists face fines and even jail time for removing it from local beaches.  <br>At the end of the year, the airport usually has many bottles of sand accumulated from travelers.  <br>The regulations were introduced because the incidents were causing damage to the beaches.
