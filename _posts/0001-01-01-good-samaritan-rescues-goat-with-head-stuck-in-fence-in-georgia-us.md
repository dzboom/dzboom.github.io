A good samaritan in Kingston, Georgia rescued a goat who got its head stuck in a fence.  <br>Footage shows amateur animal rescuer, Joe, release the animal's head from the fence where it promptly runs off.  <br>Joe told Newsflare: &quot;Me and my fiancé were driving down a backroad and we were late to our horse back riding session, we saw this little goat had his head stuck in the fence line.   <br>&quot;We reversed and I jumped out to see if I could get his head pulled out of the fence.   <br>&quot;He was hesitant at first but then he understood and let me pop his head out.   <br>&quot;He gave a happy little hop as he was freed!&quot;  <br>This footage was filmed on July 18.
