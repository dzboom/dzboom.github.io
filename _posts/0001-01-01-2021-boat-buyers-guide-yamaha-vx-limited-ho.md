The all-new 2021 Yamaha VX Limited HO, as its name suggests, is a part of the VX series, which is the most popular PWC in the history of the boating industry. The VX Limited HO is the flagship model, and what makes this the top of the line is a 1.8L high-output engine, a factory-installed audio system, plenty of storage, LED lighting and more. The deck was redesigned for enhanced ergonomics, complete with a narrower seat and wider, deeper footbeds with drains.
