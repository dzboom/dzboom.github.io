5 Tips for Making the Most out of  Virtual Personal Training Sessions These tips, provided by PopSugar,  can help you to safely and successfully  achieve your fitness goals at home. 1. Schedule time for troubleshooting According to certified personal trainer Julie Wandzliak,  it takes time to find &quot;the proper placement and distance  we both needed to have an overall view of their workouts.&quot; 2. Ask your instructor for  advice about equipment They can help you to utilize  what you already own or  advise you about what to invest in. 3. Discuss any injuries  or pains beforehand This will enable your trainer to  properly plan your fitness regimen  while reducing the risk of further injury. 4. Create a routine If you create a routine with your  trainer, you're more likely to stick  with a plan and achieve your goals. 5. Have patience Having to do so many things virtually because  of COVID-19 can get frustrating, but it can also  be beneficial. Try to look for the silver lining.
