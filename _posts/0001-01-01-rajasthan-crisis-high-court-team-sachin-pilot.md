Sachin Pilot faction has got relief from the High Court of Rajasthan. The notice given by the assembly speaker has just been stayed, that is, the assembly speaker will not be able to disqualify the MLAs. However, there will still be a hearing in the High Court on other matters. That is, it is not the last decision. The law of this case will be discussed in further hearings.
