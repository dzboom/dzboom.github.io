Kannada actress Ragini Dwivedi's bail hearing, which was supposed to happen today, has been postponed to September 14. The Central Crime Branch (CCB) of Bengaluru recently arrested Ragini Dwivedi in connection with Kannada film industry's ongoing drug racket case. Ragini has been under CCB custody for almost 8 days now and was hoping to be out on bail today.
