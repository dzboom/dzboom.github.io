The Beatles first concert tour launched in 1963, and by the next year, they kicked off their first world tour. 1965 marked the peak of Beatlesmania, but by 1966 they embarked on their last commericial tour, taking place in the US. Their very last show was a spontaneous pop-up concert on the rooftop of the Apple Corps Headquarters in 1969, only a year before they disbanded for good. The Beatles legacy knows no bounds, From 1961-1966, they played over 1000 concerts across 7 tours.
