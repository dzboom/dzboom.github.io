A man from Pune in India is so obsessed with gold that he's wearing a coronavirus facemask made from the precious metal.  <br>Shankar Kurade's gleaming personal protective equipment is worth 300,000 rupees or 4,000 US dollars and even has tiny holes in it so that this Indian Goldfinger can breathe easily.   <br>Kurade says he got the idea for the blinging mask after seeing a silver one on TV.
