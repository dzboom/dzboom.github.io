Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad visited an art exhibition Tun M: A Forever Legacy hosted by the Perdana University which features more than 100 artwork inspired by the Prime Minister by artists from 40 countries.  <br>Dr Mahathir who is also the founding chancellor of the university,was  accompanied by his wife Tun Dr Siti Hasmah Mohd Ali.
