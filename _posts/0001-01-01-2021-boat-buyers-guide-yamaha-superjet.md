The 2021 Yamaha SuperJet has a 30-year iconic history, but it's been completely revamped from the ground up to be much more approachable for a whole new generation of rider. Stand-up PWC in the past have had a bit of a learning curve, but thanks for the SuperJet's L-Mode, it brings the power level of the machine down so you can sharpen your skills or learn as a complete beginner in no time at all.
