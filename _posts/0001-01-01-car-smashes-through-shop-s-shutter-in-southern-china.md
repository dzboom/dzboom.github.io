A car suddenly smashed into a shop through its shutter in southern China.  <br>The terrifying video, shot in the city of Yulin in Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region on August 27, shows an SUV smashing into a shop through the roller shutter door.  <br>According to reports, the female driver left the site after the incident. The case is being investigated by the police.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
