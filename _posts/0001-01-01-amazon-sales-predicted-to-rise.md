Amazon's global ecommerce sales will reach $416.48 billion in 2020, according to Business Insiders latest latest estimates.  <br>Consumers are relying more heavily on e-commerce due to COVID-19.  <br>The new figure is about $12 billion more than our pre-pandemic estimates.  <br>Most of Amazon's sales come from the US.  <br>However, the pandemic has also fueled growth in Germany, the UK and Japan.  <br>Amazon's US business will be up nearly $9 billion compared with our pre-pandemic figure, reaching $269.41 billion in 2020.
