Pakistan’s Babar Azam has been one of the hottest properties in world cricket over the past couple of years. A promising talent, Azam has developed into his team’s premier batsman across all formats. He is in fact the only batsman in the world currently who ranks in the top five of the ICC men’s rankings across all formats, leading the rankings in T20Is.
