Drivers honked their horns in the Belarusian capital on Friday (August 14th) in support of protests against leader Alexander Lukashenko.  <br>Belarus opposition leader Svetlana Tikhanovskaya has called for peaceful rallies across the country after she was forced to leave for Lithuania in the wake of a disputed election.
