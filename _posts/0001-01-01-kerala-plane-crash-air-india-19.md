Air India plane crashed on the runway at Kozhikode Airport in Kerala on Friday evening. Due to which a big accident happened ... After the plane slipped on the runway, the plane crashed and broke into two. Let us tell you that this aircraft of Air India was coming to India from Dubai under Vande Bharat Mission, in which 190 passengers were boarded. Many people have been injured in this accident.
