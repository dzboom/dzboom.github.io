A man installed his late wife’s hyper-realistic wax figure into his newly constructed bungalow in southern India's Karnataka.  <br>The footage was filmed in Koppal district, where the man introduced the life-sized statue resembling his spouse on August 8.  <br>According to reports, the man identified as 57-year-old businessman Srinivas Gupta, approached over 25 architects to make the newly-built house special for his wife.  <br>On August 8, guests who were invited to the housewarming ceremony at Srinivas Gupta's residence were left speechless after they walked through the door to see a life-like statue of his late wife Madhavi in the living room.  <br>The video showcases his wife, Madhavi, sitting on a sofa donning a beautiful pink saree and golden ornaments.  <br>Reportedly, Madhavi was on a trip to Tirupati in Andhra Pradesh, with her daughters, three years ago, when their driver rear-ended a speeding truck.  <br>While the daughters sustained minor injuries, Madhavi, who was seated on the passenger seat, passed away in the accident.
