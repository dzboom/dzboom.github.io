Shiv Sena leader and 5 other people arrested on the charge of beating a former naval officer in Mumbai have got bail from the police station. All the accused have been released on a bond of 5 thousand. Now the politics in Mumbai is hot. BJP has started protesting against the bail of the accused. BJP workers demonstrated in front of the Office of the Additional Commissioner of Police.
