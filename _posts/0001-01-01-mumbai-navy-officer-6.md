Forwarding a cartoon against the Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Uddhav Thackeray, was overshadowed by a former Navy officer. Around four o'clock on Friday evening, some five-six Shiv Sainiks beat up a retired Navy officer Madan Sharma badly.
