As per a report in the New Indian Express, the BCCI has left the decision of family members being involved in the league up to the franchises. If a relative, in fact, joins a player in UAE, he or she would need to remain in the bubble for the entire duration of the stay. The report further claims that barring a couple of franchises, none are willing to entertain the subject of relatives' involvement.
