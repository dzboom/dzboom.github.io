This is the bizarre moment a giant gold Buddha appeared to be levitating and moving on its own.  <br>The religious statue was seen on a raised highway in Bangkok, Thailand, on August 28.   <br>Locals living in an apartment block opposite noticed the shiny gold idol - often connected with supernatural powers.  <br>So they were amazed when the Buddha - highly revered in Thailand - began appearing mysteriously hovering along the road.  <br>The actual explanation was that the statue was being transported on the back of a pickup truck which was not visible due to the raised barrier on the side of the highway.
