A car collided with a bus after suddenly drifting and losing control on a highway in southern China.  <br>The video filmed in Hubei shows a bus was preparing to turn at the dotted line on the extension section of He Yi Road. A yellow car in the opposite lane drifted to the left suddenly and struck the bus.  <br>The traffic police determined the driver of the yellow car to be fully responsible for the accident.  <br>The yellow car driver said that the accident happened because the passenger sitting next to her in the co-pilot &quot;helped&quot; push the handbrake in a panic.  <br>It turned out that at the time of the incident, the passenger in the yellow car saw that the bus was about to turn, and the female driver did not intend to slow down.   <br>He was afraid that the two cars would collide, so he helped pull the handbrake in a hurry. It was unhelpful, and the two vehicles still collided with each other.  <br>The video was filmed on July 22 and provided by local media.
