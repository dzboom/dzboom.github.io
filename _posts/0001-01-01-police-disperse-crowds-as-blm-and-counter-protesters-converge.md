Police officers dispersed crowds of left and right-wing protesters on Friday (August 21st) as they threatened to clash on the streets of  Los Angeles.   <br>According to an eyewitness, the scene turned &quot;rowdy&quot; when around 100 BLM supporters arrived on the same street as scores of counter-protesting Trump supporters.  <br>Police placed themselves between the two groups to prevent clashes and used smoke bombs and possibly tear gas to disperse the crowds.   <br>According to an eyewitness, several rocks were thrown at police.
