Today is the first death anniversary of Bharatiya Janata Party veteran and former Union Minister Arun Jaitley. He died a day ago today .. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has tweeted on Arun Jaitley's first death anniversary. The Prime Minister has said that he misses his friend very much.
