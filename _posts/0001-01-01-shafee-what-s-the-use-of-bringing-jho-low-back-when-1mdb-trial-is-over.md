Tan Sri Muhammad Shafee Abdullah, who represents former prime minister Datuk Seri Najib Razak in his 1MDB corruption trial, on Wednesday questioned the authorities’ intention of bringing back fugitive businessman, Low Taek Jho or Jho Low at the end of the year. Shafee said by then, all the trials relating to the 1MDB scandal would be over.
