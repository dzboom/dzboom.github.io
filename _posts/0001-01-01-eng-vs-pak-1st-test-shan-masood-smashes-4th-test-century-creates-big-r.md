Shan Masood has become the sixth Pakistan batsman to score tons in three consecutive innings. He also became the first Pakistan opener in 24 years to score a Test hundred in England. By taking a double off James Anderson, Shan Masood brought up his fourth Test century and his first against and in England. The southpaw made his maiden against Sri Lanka five years ago.
