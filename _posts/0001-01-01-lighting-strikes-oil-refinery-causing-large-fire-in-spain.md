Lighting struck a large oil storage tanker at a refinery in Puertollano, Spain on Monday (August 10), causing a large fire.  <br>Footage shows the Repsol petrochemical complex in Puertollano billowing thick black smoke after a bolt of lightning from an earlier storm struck the refinery.  <br>Local reports confirmed that the emergency services concluded there were no injuries and the incident is now under control.
