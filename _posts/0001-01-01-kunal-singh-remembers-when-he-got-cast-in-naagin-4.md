Actor Kunal Singh has wrapped up shooting for the television show Naagin 4. “It’s hurtful to say goodbye to the most loved show. I still remember the feeling of joy when I got cast in it. I was on cloud nine. But who knows about destiny,” he said about the Ekta Kapoor show. “I’m thankful to the makers for trusting and loving me. I want to work with the makers again,” the Naagin 4 actor added. Kunal feels that every ending has a new and beautiful beginning. The Naagin 4 star said, “Like every sunset has a new sunrise, similarly every ending has a new and beautiful beginning. I’m looking forward to playing some challenging roles. I’m up for signing a new project.”
