The dispute has been going on for the last two months on the India-China border. Recently, 20 Indian soldiers were killed in a skirmish in the Galvan Valley. Since then, there is an atmosphere of anger throughout the country. On the one hand, while people are boycotting Chinese goods, some people are also leaving the job .... The latest case is related to Kolkata where more than 100 Zomato delivery buoys have announced to quit.
