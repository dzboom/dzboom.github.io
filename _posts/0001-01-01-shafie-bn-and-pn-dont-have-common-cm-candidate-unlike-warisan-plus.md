Warisan President Shafie Apdal said Warisan Plus has agreed on one Sabah chief ministerial candidate, unlike their rivals who are still divided on the state's top post.  <br>&quot;Be it DAP, PKR, or Amanah (which contest in the election), there's only one candidate for the chief minister post, and that is Warisan president,&quot; he told the media after the nomination process in Semporna.  <br>He said when asked for his comment on the situation concerning the state opposition coalition comprising PN, BN, and PBS which have yet to finalise their candidate for the post.
