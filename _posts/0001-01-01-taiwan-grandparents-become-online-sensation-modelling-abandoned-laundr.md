Grandparents who run a laundry service in Taiwan have become one of the island’s trendiest couples after they went online to model items of clothing abandoned at their shop. Their  social media outreach, initially a way to remind customers about forgotten belongings, soon struck a chord with people drawn to the couple’s online enthusiasm.   (Photo: AFP/Reef Chang)
