This family wanted to celebrate their grandma's 100th birthday. However, they had to keep in mind the social distance regulations during the Covid-19 pandemic. They built a decorative cake sculpture for her and drove off with it to her nursing home. Together, they sang for her as she sat on the other side of the glass window, watching her family wishing her while maintaining social distance.
