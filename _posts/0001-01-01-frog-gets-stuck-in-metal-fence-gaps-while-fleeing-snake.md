A frog was stuck in the hole of an iron fence while trying to escape a predator snake.  <br>Mee Meemuk, 62, contacted the rescue team to catch a python that had slithered into her house in Buriram, northeastern Thailand.  <br>The woman watched the rescuers catch the 6ft long serpent in her kitchen and as the rescuers brought the snake out she spotted a toad stuck in the fence.  <br>The amphibian was found stuck in a hole in her fence 2ft above the ground and was helpless before the house owner removed it and let it go.  <br>The house owner said she believed the toad might have tried to escape the snake before it was stuck in the hole.  <br>She said: &quot;I think the snake entered the house looking for food, found the toad in the kitchen, and chased it before I spotted it.  <br>&quot;It appeared that the toad survived the hunt but unfortunately was stuck in the hole as it jumped out of the house.&quot;
