Amazon's Q2 2020 was its biggest online sales quarter ever due to the coronavirus pandemic.  <br>Amazon racked up $45.8 billion in global online store net sales in the second quarter of 2020.  <br>Amazon exceeded the $45.7 billion it brought in during the forth-quarter of 2019.  <br>Business Insider reports that Its Prime subscription thrived in the second quarter, building Amazon's base of loyal customers.
