Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has been tested positive for Covid-19. He is hospitalised in Mumbai’s Nanavati Hospital. Big-B tweeted the information on his twitter handle. “I have tested positive for Covid-19 and have been shifted to hospital”, tweeted the actor. His family has also been tested for Covid-19 and the report is yet to come.
