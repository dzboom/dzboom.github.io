International youth day comes with a big relief to the students of Punjab.The Punjab government starts distributing smartphones to students for free from August 12. These smartphones will be a great help in online studies during the  coronavirus  crisis.To avoid large gatherings, the launch of the distribution scheme has been organised at 26 different locations in Chandigarh and Punjab.
