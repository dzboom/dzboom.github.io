Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway disclosed a $6 billion bet on five of Japan's biggest trading companies.  <br>The news was released Sunday night in a press release.  <br>The billionaire investor's company revealed that it owns just over 5% of Mitsubishi, Mitsui, Itochu, Marubeni, and Sumitomo.  <br>Business Insider reports that all five firms rose between 4% and 10% on Monday.  <br>Buffett said &quot;I am delighted to have Berkshire Hathaway participate in the future of Japan and the five companies we have chosen for investment.&quot;
