St Aidan's Catholic Academy mini buses used to deliver daily food parcels to families each day since school closure have been damaged in an attempted theft.   <br>It was reported that between 10pm on Saturday and 3pm on Sunday offenders had cut the lock to the school’s gates and caused damage to two vehicles that were parked inside.  <br>
