Things look like a war on Line Actual Control (LAC) between India and China. The forces of both countries have deployed tanks, machine guns and modern weapons near LAAC and the strength of the air force is also being increased, India-China tension on LAC in Ladakh has been there for the last time. The brigade-commander-level talks were held in Chushul on Saturday to settle the border dispute which was inconclusive.
