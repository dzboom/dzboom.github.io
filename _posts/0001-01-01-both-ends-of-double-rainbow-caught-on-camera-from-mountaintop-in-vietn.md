Two beautiful parallel rainbows were filmed from a mountaintop in Yen Bai, Vietnam.  <br>According to the filmer, he accidentally discovered the two rainbows while watching the scenery from the top of the mountain on June 30.  <br>The rainbows appeared after a rain shower and crossed two valleys at the foot of the mountain.  <br>This was the first time the man had seen two rainbows parallel to each other.
