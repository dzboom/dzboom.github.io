'K방역' 자동차 이동형 선별진료소, 국제표준화 첫 관문통과  <br>South Korea has won praise around the world for its innovative drive-thru model for testing people for the coronavirus.  <br>Now the drive-thru model is being considered by the International Organization for Standardization to be made the global standard.  <br>According to Seoul's trade ministry, the ISO decided to take up the proposal in a vote whose results were released today.  <br>South Korea started drive-thru testing back in February, which helped it get the local outbreak under control quickly and efficiently.  <br>The ministry said it will take at least three years for the ISO to adopt it as the standard system globally.  <br>
