Preparations have been completed at five locations across the country for the third and final human trial of Corona Vaccine, developed towards Oxford-AstraZeneca for treatment of Kovid-19. Renu Swaroop, Secretary, Department of Biotechnology, gave this information on Monday. He told that this is an important step, because it is necessary to have data available inside the country before giving vaccine to Indians.
