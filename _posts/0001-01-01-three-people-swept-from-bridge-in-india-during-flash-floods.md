Three people were swept from a bridge today (July 5th) when severe flash floods hit the state of Gujarat.   <br>Footage shows passengers of a Bolero car attempting to climb into a truck before they and their vehicle are swept away by the fast-flowing water coursing over the bridge.   <br>Suddenly, the force of the water pushes the car into the river below and the three are carried away with it.  <br>According to police, two of the passengers have been found alive but one man is still missing.
