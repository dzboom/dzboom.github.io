JCPenney has added two more stores to the list of locations it plans to close.   <br>Both stores are located in New York City.  <br>One location is in the Manhattan Mall.  <br>The other location is in Brooklyn Kings Plaza.  <br>The department store chain filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in May.  <br>JCPenney later said it planned to close about 30% of its stores.
