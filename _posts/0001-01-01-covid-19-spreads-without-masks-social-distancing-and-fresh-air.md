Passengers on buses are at significant risk for catching COVID-19, reports UPI.  <br>If a fellow traveler is infected and face coverings or social distancing isn't being used, the risk is high.  <br>Those who traveled on a bus with one infected passenger are more likely to be sickened.  <br>COVID-19 spreads easily when people are not covering their mouths and noses.  <br>The CDC recommends maintaining 6 feet distance from anyone you do not live with, at all times.  <br>COVID-19 also lives on surfaces so it is recommended to disinfect anything you bring into your house.
