Dozens of hot air balloons could be seen hovering over Bristol as organisers of the cancelled Bristol International Balloon Fiesta organised a secret alternative.  <br>Footage filmed by Andrew Cleaver on August 3 shows numerous balloons taking off from a park in the city.  <br>Cleaver told Newsflare: &quot;There is no fiesta this year due to COVID-19 but I was lucky enough to stumble upon this special ‘fly past’ launch where 45 balloons took to the skies over Bristol!&quot;  <br>Organisers came up with the flypast alternative so people could watch the balloons from the safety of their homes.
