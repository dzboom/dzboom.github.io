Defense Minister Rajnath Singh has directly warned China in the midst of heightened tension following the violent clash between India and the Chinese army in the Galvan Valley of eastern Ladakh. The Defense Minister, who arrived in Delhi to take stock of a Covid Center, said that India is ready on every front.
