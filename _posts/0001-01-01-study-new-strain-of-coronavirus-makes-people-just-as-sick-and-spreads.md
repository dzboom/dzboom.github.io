A new strain of the novel coronavirus is rapidly spreading from Europe to the US.  <br>According to Business Insider, a new study says the virus is more infectious than its predecessor but is not making people any sicker than before.  <br>According to Business Insider, researchers call the new strain G614 and the previous strain D614.  <br>One researcher says the new mutation is &quot;now the dominant form infecting people.&quot;  <br>The US has reported a total of 2,795,437 cases of the disease. 129,438 people have died of it since it gained a foothold within the country in January.
