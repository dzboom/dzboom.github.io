TREE-tops will glisten and the gardens will sparkle with a stunning new festive lights trail – Christmas At Belton is going ahead.  <br>And tickets are expected to sell fast with any other major seasonal events in the region postponed or looking in doubt due to Covid-19 restrictions.   <br>That could lead to bumper bookings and organisers are advising people to book early, to avoid missing out on the magical illuminated trail of the National Trust's Belton House, near Grantham, in Lincolnshire, selective dates from November 27 to January 3.  <br>Popular early evening and weekend time slots are expected to be in most demand.  <br>BUY TICKETS: Tickets, on sale now, in advance are from £19 adult, £13.50 child,  £60 family (two adults and two children), free for carers and two-year-olds and under.
