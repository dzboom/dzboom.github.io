Piaggio has announced that they will be launching the Vespa Racing Sixties scooter in the Indian market on the 1st of September 2020. The new Vespa Racing Sixties will be a limited edition model based on the brand’s SXL150 scooter offering, featuring a special livery/paint scheme.  <br>The Vespa Racing Sixties although remains mechanically identical to the standard model.
