Sukma, Chhattisgarh, four Naxalites were trapped in an encounter between security forces and Naxalites. An encounter with security forces has taken place in the den of Naxalites, in which the police has given information about the death of 4 Naxalites. Of these four Naxalites, 2 were in uniform and two in rural control.
