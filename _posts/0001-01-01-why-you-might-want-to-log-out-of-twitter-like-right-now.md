Business Insider reports hackers linked to a cryptocurrency scam have broken into dozens of high-profile Twitter accounts.  <br>Among the known victims so far are Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos and presidential hopeful Joe Biden.  <br>The accounts of Tesla CEO Elon Musk and Kanye West, among others, tweeted requests to people to send thousands of dollars in Bitcoin.  <br>The tweets said they would double the payment in return.  <br>As it's unclear whose accounts are in the hackers' crosshairs, it might be a good idea to log out of Twitter until the platform is secured.
