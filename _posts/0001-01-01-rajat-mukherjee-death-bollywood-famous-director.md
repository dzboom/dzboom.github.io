Rajat Mukherjee Dies. Manoj Bajpayee, Anubhav Sinha and others Tweet Tributes. Filmmaker Rajat Mukherjee who was known for directing films like Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya and Road, died due to health complications in Jaipur on Sunday.
