블랙핑크 신곡, 유튜브 사상 최단 시간 1억뷰 뮤비  <br>The newest music video by K-pop group Blackpink has become the fastest video ever to top 100 million views on Youtube.  <br>Accorindg to the group's management agency, YG entertainment, the video for the song &quot;How You Like That&quot; reached the milestone on Sunday Korea time at around 2:23 AM.  <br>The previous record was set by their fellow K-pop stars BTS... in April 2019 with the song &quot;Boy With Luv.&quot;  <br>With their new single, Blackpink has also topped the iTunes charts in 60 countries.  <br>They first performed the song live this past Friday on the 'The Tonight Show' with Jimmy Fallon.  <br>
