Yamaha's brand new 255XD is the most highly-engineered, surf-capable boat we've seen from Yamaha yet. With triple ballast tanks, the Wake Booster, and customizable surf settings accessible on the display, this boat is ideal for all levels of wakesurfers. The 255XD also comes with all sorts of creature comforts like spacious lounges, stadium seating, tons of cupholders and storage, and a great sound system.
