Dr. Anthony Fauci is the top infectious disease expert in the United States.  <br>On Tuesday he said he considers himself &quot;more a realist than an alarmist&quot; reports CNN.  <br>This comes after President Donald Trump on Sunday labeled him &quot;a little bit of an alarmist&quot;.  <br>Trump's comments about Fauci were made even as the coronavirus pandemic worsens.  <br>The White House continues to make a concerted effort to discredit Fauci.  <br>Fauci has become increasingly vocal about concerns over reopening amid a national surge in COVID cases.
