Sonu Sood, who led hundreds of people to his home in lockdown, has come forward this time to help the sailor families of Kashi. The sailors of Varanasi have been troubled since the sudden lockdown in March. Unlock started but lack of tourists did not lead to earning opportunity. People coming from Varanasi or nearby districts became the support of the sailors. Meanwhile, due to floods in the Ganges, the district administration once again stopped the operation till September 15.
