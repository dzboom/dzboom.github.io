Security has been beefed up at Ambala Air Force Station ahead of the arrival of Rafale jets and the station is fully prepared to welcome Rafale jets, which will arrive in India after covering a distance of almost 7,364 kilometres. It is learnt that around 3 kilometer area near Ambala Air Force Station has been declared as a no-drone zone.
