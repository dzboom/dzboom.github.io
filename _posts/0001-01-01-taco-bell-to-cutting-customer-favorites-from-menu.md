Taco Bell confirmed rumors that it is cutting popular 12 items from its menu in August.  <br>Business Insider reports the items to be removed include fan favorites such as Cheesy Fiesta Potatoes, Nachos Supreme, and Loaded Grillers.  <br>To make it up to disappointed customers Taco Bell is also adding two new options to the menu: a $5 Grande Nachos Box and a $1 beef burrito.  <br>Customers are angry the items are going to go.  <br>Taco Bell released a statement addressing the menu changes.  <br>&quot;While change is hard, a simplified menu and innovation process will leave room for new fan favorites, continued progress in categories such as plant-based diets, and even opportunities for the return of some classics on a limited time basis,&quot; Taco Bell said in a press release.    <br>Customers said Taco Bell's statement is&quot; fake&quot;, &quot;condescending&quot;, and &quot;patronizing.&quot;
