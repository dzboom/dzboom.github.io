Late Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput's death probe has turned into a war of words between Bihar and Maharshtra as both the state forces investigate. The investigation has led to allegations and counter-allegations from both sides. A four-member team of Patna Police, which reached Mumbai on Tuesday, recorded the statements of at least six people, including Sushant Singh Rajput’s sister on Wednesday in connection with the case. They were acting on the FIR filed by the 34-year-old actor’s father against actor Rhea Chakraborty in Bihar on Tuesday.
