PKR will be sending letters of demand to 20 former party elected representatives claiming a sum of RM10 million each, including current Perikatan Nasional (PN) ministers Azmin Ali, Zuraida Kamaruddin, and Saifuddin Abdullah.  <br>In a statement, the party said this was the sum “agreed” upon by each of the affected MPs and assemblypersons for defecting from Pakatan Harapan.
