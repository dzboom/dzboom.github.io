Former &quot;Glee&quot; actress Naya Rivera has gone missing from a lake in Southern California, authorities said.  <br>CNN reports that Rivera vanished from Lake Piru in Ventura County on Wednesday evening.  <br>The Ventura County Sheriff's Office confirmed her identity, and said it's looking for a possible drowning victim at the lake.  <br>Rivera went to the lake Wednesday afternoon and rented a boat with her young child.   <br>Both were seen going out on the lake together.  <br>About three hours after they left the dock, another boater discovered a boat drifting with the child asleep onboard.
