The nation celebrated festival of Onam on August 31. The devotees visited Thrikkakara Vamana Moorthy Temple in Kochi and were seen following social distancing due to COVID-19 pandemic.  <br>Meanwhile, devotees also visited Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram on the occasion of Onam.  <br>The temples reopened for public from Aug 26, with COVID-19 precautionary measures in Kerala for the people.
