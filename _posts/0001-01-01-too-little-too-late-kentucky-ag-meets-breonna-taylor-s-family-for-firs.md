The investigation remains ongoing, and our office continues to review all the facts in the case to determine the truth.&quot;  <br>Breonna Taylor was asleep in her house when 3 cops knocked her door down and killed her.  <br>The no-knock warrant was actually for a different address and the man it was for had already been arrested.
