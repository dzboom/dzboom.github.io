In the Turkish city of Izmit, a stray dog ​​interrupted an outdoor show to try to help an actor 'in distress'.  <br>During his performance, actor Numan Ertugrul Uzunsoy fell to the ground, creating the impression of being in enormous pain. The artist did not realize then that a dog approached him to try to help him. &quot;I felt very happy when I felt the kisses of the dog. It moved me a lot. It was like an angel who wanted to help me. It was a very emotional moment. I didn't expect it,&quot; Uzunsoy confessed.
