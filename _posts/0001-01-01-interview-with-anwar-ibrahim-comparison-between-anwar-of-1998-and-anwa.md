The Star and Sinar Harian sat down for an interview with PKR de facto leader Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim who led Malaysia’s reformasi (reform) movement which is an integral part of Pakatan Harapan that ended Barisan Nasional’s 61-year rule in the May 9 polls. He spoke about a wide range of topics including his development in terms of political ideologies and his foe-turned-ally Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad.
