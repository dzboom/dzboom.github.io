The rare comet C/2020 F3 NEOWISE was discovered by NASA's Near-Earth Object Wide-field Infrared Survey Explorer telescope, will pass by the Earth on July 14. In India, it will be visible for around 20 minutes every day for 20 days starting July 14. The comet can be seen with the naked eyes. You can use a pair of binoculars for a clearer view of the celestial event. On July 22, the comet will be the closes to Earth.
