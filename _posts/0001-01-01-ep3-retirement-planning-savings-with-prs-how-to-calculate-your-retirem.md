For Episode 3 of this six-part weekly video series brought to you by Private Pension Administrator Malaysia (PPA), in support of the Financial Education Network, find out how PPA’s retirement calculator can help you to plan for your retirement.  <br>All it takes is filling in simple basic information about yourself and the retirement calculator will generate an accumulation plan - based on a few assumptions, to show you how much you need for your retirement savings.  <br>Knowing how much you need in advance will go a long way in ensuring you get to enjoy a financially secured retirement.  <br>Private Retirement Schemes (PRS) is a voluntary long-term savings and investment scheme designed to help you save more for retirement.
