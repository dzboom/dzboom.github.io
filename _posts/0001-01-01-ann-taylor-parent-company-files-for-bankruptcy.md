Ascena Retail Group has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy.  <br>The company is planning to close some stores.  <br>Its portfolio brands include Ann Taylor, LOFT, and Lane Bryant.   <br>Coronavirus-related store closures hurt the business.  <br>Ascena sales had suffered when mall traffic began to decline even before the pandemic.
