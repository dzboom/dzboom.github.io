Stuart Broad takes the final wicket, and his tenth of the match, as England beat the West Indies to regain the Wisden Trophy at Old Trafford. Chasing 399 for victory, West Indies got bowled out for 129 runs in 37.1 overs to suffer their second successive defeat after the second Test at this same venue. Woakes got 5 wickets while Broad got 4 to finish the match with figures of 10 wickets for 67 runs, his 3rd-best performance in Test cricket.
