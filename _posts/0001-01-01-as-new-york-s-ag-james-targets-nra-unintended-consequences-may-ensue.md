New York Attorney General Letitia James has readied, aimed, and fired her first shot at the National Rifle Association.  <br>CNN reports James has launched a stunningly bold suit to dissolve America's top gun lobby group.  <br>The NRA is one of the most feared forces in American politics, spending billions to weaken gun control initiatives after mass shootings.  <br>It has also transformed the American constitutional right to bear arms into a cultural cause that dominates conservative politics.  <br>James accuses LaPierre and three other top NRA officers of years of breathtaking corruption.  <br>While destroying the organization over it would make her a hero among liberals, it may also have unintended consequences.  <br>Democrats who need to win rural, conservative, swing-state voters may wish she had waited until after the election.
