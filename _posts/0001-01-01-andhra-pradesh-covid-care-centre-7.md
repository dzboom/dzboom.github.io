A fierce fire broke out in a hotel in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. As soon as the incident was known, the team of the fire brigade reached the spot and the fire has been controlled after a lot of hard work ... Seven people died in the accident. And 30 people have been injured…. Who have been admitted to the hospital…. This hotel was being used as a Covid Center. Corona patients were being treated here ... Relief and rescue work is going on currently ...
