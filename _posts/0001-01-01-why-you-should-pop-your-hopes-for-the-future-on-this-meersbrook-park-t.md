Extinction Rebellion Sheffield has been hosting an art installation at Meersbrook Park, asking members of the public to write their hopes for a greener future on a leaf, and hang it on the tree.  <br>MP Louise Haigh will be visiting the tree, which stands at the top of the hill, just outside Meersbrook Park's Walled Garden, this evening at 7.30pm, to read people’s messages of hope, their dreams, and their creative visions for a greener and fairer future.  <br>Everyone is welcome to attend.
