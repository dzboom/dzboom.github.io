A sand artist from Toronto, Canada captured through timelapse the creative process behind a puppy portrait.  <br>Footage shows the portrait being created with different coloured sand within a jar using a small tool that distributes sand along a tracing of the picture on the glass.  <br>The filmer, James Sun, wrote: &quot;I made this piece of art in a glass jar using coloured sand. It is a rottweiler with cute eyebrows running towards you.&quot;  <br>Sun creates many different portraits online under the moniker @fallinginsand.
