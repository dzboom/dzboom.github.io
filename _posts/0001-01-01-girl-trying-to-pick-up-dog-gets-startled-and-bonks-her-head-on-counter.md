This little girl approached her pet dog cautiously. She stood behind them and stretched her arms to pick them up. However, the dog turned around swiftly, scaring the little girl. She reflexively moved away from them and accidentally bonked her head on the kitchen counter slab. She clutched her head in pain and sat on the floor.
