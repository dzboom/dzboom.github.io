This guy thought of pretending to pass out on the floor to see how his dog, Raggs, reacts. He lay face down on the floor and called his dog. The dog came and stamped his paw on his owner’s head to wake him up. He even barked at him and circled him while trying to figure out what happened. The guy eventually came up and thanked his dog for checking up on him.
