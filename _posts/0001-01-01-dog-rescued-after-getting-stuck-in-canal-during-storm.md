This is the moment a kindhearted father and daughter rescued a dog trapped in a dirty canal during a storm in the Philippines.  <br>Wilmar Binos was on his way home from work when he heard the dog crying for help in Cavite on July 29th.  <br>He rushed home to ask his daughter Marie Cloie Binos to assist him in rescuing the stricken animal.  <br>The pair. used a ladder and food to lure to dog over to them and then hauled it to safety.   <br>The dog was soon after named &quot;Kiko&quot; and was taken to live with the family.
