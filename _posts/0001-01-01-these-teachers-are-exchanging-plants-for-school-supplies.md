A group of teachers from Campaasan Elementary School in Oriental Mindoro organized a project called “Halaman Ko, Barter Mo” to help their students with their needs. The purpose of the project is to fulfill the basic learning needs of the children through a barter system where they will trade the plants in exchange for school supplies like learning modules, activity sheets, and even health kits.
