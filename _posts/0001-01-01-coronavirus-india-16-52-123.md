The condition of coronavirus in India is getting worse from bad to worse. Even months after the epidemic started, the cases are increasing rather than decreasing. In an unfortunate way, on Thursday, India saw 50,000 new cases of Kovid-19 infection in the last 24 hours for the first time. On Thursday, 30 July 2020, 52,123 new COVID-19 patients have been registered in the last 24 hours.
