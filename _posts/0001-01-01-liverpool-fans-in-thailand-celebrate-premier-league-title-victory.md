Dozens of Liverpool fans in Thailand celebrated their team's victory by holding a parade in a traditional ancient Thai style.   <br>The supporters dressed in the old kingdom Ayutthaya period warrior costumes in  Suphan Buri province on July 1. They also had a replica of the Premier League trophy that was paraded on a cow-drawn wooden cart.  <br>Lleader Sujin Wajakij said that Liverpool winning the Premier League is like a dream come true for him and the others fans so he invited them to enjoy the victory together.  <br>He said: &quot;I have been supporting Liverpool since I was young and waited for this moment for a long time, as have all the other fans here.  <br>&quot;I think this is a good event because it has brought us together after sometime apart because of the COVID-19. Now we are here celebrating together.&quot;
