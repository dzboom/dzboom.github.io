On Monday, Papa John's and Shaquille O'Neal announced the launch of the Shaq-a-Roni pizza.  <br>Business Insider reports that the pizza is designed by O'Neal himself.  <br>The Shaq-a-Roni pizza is an extra-large pizza with extra cheese and pepperoni.  <br>The pizza is cut into six slices instead of eight.  <br>The launch comes over a year after O'Neal joined Papa John's board of directors following an $8.5 million endorsement deal with the pizza chain.  <br>A dollar from each Shaq-a-Roni pizza purchase will go to the Papa John's Foundation for Building Community.  <br>Each purchase will also have an augmented-reality experience unlocked by scanning the pizza box QR code in Snapchat.
