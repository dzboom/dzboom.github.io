Love them or hate them, Jeepneys are an integral part of our public transportation system. Given that they're archaic, unsafe, and many would say obsolete, we're far from having a viable substitute that can address the daily mobility needs of millions of our commuters. Here's a perspective from the standpoint of the drivers themselves, on the major difficulties they're experiencing because of the Jeepney ban that's been in place for several months now.
