How to Refresh WhatsApp Contacts on Android?  <br>Step 1: Open 'WhatsApp,' and then login to your account.  <br>Step 2: Tap on 'Chats' tab.  <br>Step 3: Tap on 'New Chat' button at the bottom right.  <br>Step 4: Tap on 'Three Vertical Dots' at the top right.  <br>Step 5: Tap on 'Refresh' link, and then give few seconds. After that, your WhatsApp contacts list will be updated.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
