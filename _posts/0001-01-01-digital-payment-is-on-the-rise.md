In the US, the in-store mobile wallet space is becoming increasingly crowded.  <br>Most customers have an option provided by their smartphone vendor, like Apple, Android, or Samsung Pay.  <br>But those are often supplemented by a myriad of options from other players, says Business Insider.  <br>PayPal, banks, card issuers, major retailers, and restaurants all offer similar cards and deals.  <br>US in-store mobile payment volume will quintuple in the next five years.  <br>Some of the top mobile payment wallets include Microsoft wallet, Samsung pay, and Wisa VDCA.
