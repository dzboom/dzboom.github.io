A huge tornado hit northern China's Inner Mongolia, injuring 33 people and damaging over 100 Mongolian yurts.  <br>The dramatic video, filmed in the city of Baotou on August 9, shows a giant twister swirling near Xilamuren Grassland Swan Lake Resort, damaging the yurts.  <br>Police officers, firemen and paramedics arrived on the site to rescue. All injured people were sent to hospital for treatment.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
