Every season in the Championship feels like a mountain climb of a task. And this season Sheffield Wednesday have to scramble themselves out of a deep ravine before they can even think about scaling any peaks. As the Owls prepare to kick off their league campaign, Alex Miller joins Liam Hoden to reflect as the finishing touches are put in place. They discuss the drab draw at Walsall in the Carabao Cup and how it highlighted just where Wednesday are most short. They talk potential transfer business, what the Owls' aspirations should be and give their views on whether or not they are up to the task. And Alex selects his starting XI for the opening day clash with Cardiff City - and readies himself for more abuse on social media.  <br>
