Texas artist Leon Bridges was inspired to release his song, “Sweeter,” after the killing of George Floyd. He recently performed the soulful track accompanied by Brandon Marcel for Genius' 'Open Mic At Home.' It has already racked up 3 million Spotify plays to date and is produced by Nate Mercereau and Ricky Reed.
