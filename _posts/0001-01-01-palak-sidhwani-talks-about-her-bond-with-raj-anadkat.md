Although Maharashtra government has allowed TV serials to start their shoot amid the pandemic, however, the fans of Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah has to wait a little longer as their shoot is delayed due to some reason. The cast of the show who seems like a perfect family but did you know Palak Sidhwani and Raj Anadkat who plays a major role in Tapu sena aren't close friends in real life. Reportedly, Palak shares a great bond with other Sena members, talking about her friendship with Raj, the actor says, “See there are 80 people on the set, and it’s not necessary to be friends with each one of them. Raj and I, we share a professional relationship and there’s no fight as such, it’s just that I get along with Samay (Gogi), Kush (Goli) and Azhar (Pinku) more, but that doesn’t change the fact that Raj and I share a cordial relationship and he’s my working partner”.
