A female passer-by had a miraculous escape after a heavy generator fell to the ground from a crane.   <br>A crane operator was lowering the generator from the roof of a three-story commercial building in Zikarpur in northern India on July 2 to carry out repair work.   <br>The crane cable snapped, and the generator crashed to the ground destroying two scooters that were parked on the ground below.    <br>A woman, an employee of a salon at a building, had a lucky escape. CCTV footage at a garment shop on the ground floor filmed her walking across the same spot just moments before the incident.
