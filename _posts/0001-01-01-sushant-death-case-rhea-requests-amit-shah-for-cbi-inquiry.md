Sushant Singh Rajput died by suicide on June 14, a little over a month ago. Since then, several people, including certain political personalities have requested for a CBI probe into the death of 34-year-old actor and what led him to this fateful end. Today, girlfriend of Sushant Rhea Chakraborty took to social media to request Home Minister Amit Shah to initiate a CBI probe in Sushant Singh Rajput suicide case. Watch video.
