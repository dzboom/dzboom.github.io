Rural Development Minister Abdul Latiff Ahmad has drawn flak from his former colleagues in Umno for claiming that the Malay-based party was no longer popular in Sabah during a livestream yesterday.  <br>In a statement today, Umno Youth demanded for Abdul Latiff, who left Umno and is now in Bersatu, to resign as a minister over his insulting remark.  <br>Abdul Latiff has since apologised for his remark.  <br>&quot;The statement was my own and does not represent the party (Bersatu)... I humbly apologise if my remarks offended certain quarters.  <br>&quot;The real enemy is the opposition. Let's not fight among ourselves,&quot; he was quoted as saying by the Perikatan Nasional Backbenchers Club (PNBBC) portal.
