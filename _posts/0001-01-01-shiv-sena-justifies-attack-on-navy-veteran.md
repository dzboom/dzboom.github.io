The Shiv Sena on Saturday said the attack on a former Indian Navy officer by some Shivsainiks in Mumbai was a &quot;spontaneous&quot; and &quot;angry&quot; reaction after the veteran shared a &quot;derogatory&quot; cartoon on Chief Minister Uddhav Thackeray.   <br>Issuing a statement, Shiv Sena's Chief Spokesperson Sanjay Raut said, &quot;There is rule of law in Maharashtra. No one who takes law in to their hands will be spared. This is the policy of Uddhav Thackeray.&quot;   <br>Speaking about the attack on the Navy veteran, Raut said, &quot;He had circulated a cartoon of CM which was derogatory in nature. The attack was an angered and spontaneous reaction by Shivsainiks. The attackers were arrested immediately and legal action was taken.&quot;
