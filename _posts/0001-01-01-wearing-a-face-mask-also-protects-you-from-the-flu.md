Now that we are many months into the coronavirus pandemic, many Americans are aware of mask safety.  <br>The majority of the public is infinitely more aware of the precautions necessary to help curb COVID.  <br>Mask-wearing, social distancing, and hand-washing are the main routes of safety, reports Huffpost.  <br>Flu season runs from about October to early May every year, sometimes a bit earlier or later.  <br>Questions around expected flu rates during the pandemic are front of mind for many medical professionals.  <br>It is believed that masks will curb the spread of COVID-19 and influenza co-infection.
