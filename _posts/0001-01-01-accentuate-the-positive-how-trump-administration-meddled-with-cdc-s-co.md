Deaths from the novel coronavirus COVID-19 in America skyrocketed over the course of 2020.  <br>However, the US Department of Health and Human Services routinely tried to muzzle what information health officials released to the public.  <br>Gizmodo reports HHS staffers made repeated attempts to water down the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Reports.  <br>They claimed that the CDC's scientists used the reports to undermine President Donald Trump’s optimistic spin on the pandemic.  <br>Sources say the pressure started upon the appointment of Michael Caputo as HHS spokesperson, a former Trump campaign official with no medical experience.  <br>That's when HHS officials began pressuring the CDC to retroactively make changes and allow them to review the reports in full before publication.
