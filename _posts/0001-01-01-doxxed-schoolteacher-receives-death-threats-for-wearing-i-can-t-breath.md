A Los Angeles high school teacher has had to flee her home after receiving numerous death threats.  <br>According to CNN, the threats came after she wore an &quot;I can't breathe&quot; T-shirt during one of her virtual class sessions.  <br>The teacher initially received backlash last week from a parent upset that race and social justice topics were being discussed in his daughter's English class.  <br>The upset parent allegedly shared the photo of the teacher on one of his social media platforms, resulting in widespread support and hate alike.  <br>Elijah Schaffer, the podcast host of the YouTube show &quot;Slightly Offens*ve,&quot; posted a screenshot of the photo to his Twitter account.  <br>That led to hundreds of his more than 200,000 followers to flood the teacher's inbox with death threats.
