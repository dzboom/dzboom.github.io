Health Ministry Secretary Rajesh Bhushan informed that the case fatality rate in the country is continuously declining. Rajesh Bhushan said, “As recovery is increasing, therefore, the difference between active cases and the recovered cases is also increasing. The case fatality rate is continuously declining. It was at 2.15% in the first week of August, now it stands at 1.70%.”
