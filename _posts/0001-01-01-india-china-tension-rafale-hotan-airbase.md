On the Line of Actual Control (LAC) in Ladakh, the heat of India and China forces continues but these days the tension of China is increasing. Fearing the 5 Rafale of India, China has stopped 36 bomber aircraft at its Hotan airbase. There has been a lot of turmoil at Hotan airbase of China near LAC, it seems that China has deployed all its fighter jets.    <br>फ्रांस से 5 राफेल आने के बाद भारत ने जिस तरह हिमाचल प्रदेश में इस फाइटर जेट से युद्धाभ्यास शुरू किया उससे चीन की चिंता बढ़ गई है.
