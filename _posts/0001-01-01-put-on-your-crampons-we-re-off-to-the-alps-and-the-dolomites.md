The Dolomites, located in the north of Italy, are one of the most beautiful landscapes in the world, classified as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Characterized by steep slopes and vertical walls, sharp peaks surrounded by soft green lawns, the site offers a variety of spectacularly beautiful and grandiose landscapes.
