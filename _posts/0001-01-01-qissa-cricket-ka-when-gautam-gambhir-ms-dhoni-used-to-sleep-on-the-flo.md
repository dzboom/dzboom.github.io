Former India opener Gautam Gambhir has the answer. In the recent episode of Star Sports’ chat show Cricket Connected, Gambhir recalled the time when he had to share a room with Dhoni during a series. “We were roommates for more than a month and all we used to talk about was ‘hair’ because he had those long hair back then,” Gambhir said.
