Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg is resting comfortably in the hospital following a medical procedure.  <br>According to CNN, the procedure was done to replace a bile duct stent that was originally placed last year.  <br>The minimally invasive non-surgical procedure was confirmed by the Supreme Court in a statement released Wednesday.  <br>Bader Ginsburg's hospitalization comes less than two weeks after she announced her cancer had returned.  <br>Chief Justice Ginsburg is 87-years-old.
