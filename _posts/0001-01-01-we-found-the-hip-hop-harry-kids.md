Thanks to a viral TikTok trend, a mid-aughts children’s TV show song has taken over the Internet with a catchy beat by Hip-Hop Harry. The “Go, Go, Go! Who's Next?” on the chorus proved infectious, sparking thousands of videos, tweets, and various memes. Genius News caught up with Hip-Hop Harry, who spoke to us about what makes the song stick in people’s heads. Genius also tracked down every kid featured in the series to hear about how the newfound viral fame has impacted them years later, the process of recording the song, and lessons they learned while shooting the show.
