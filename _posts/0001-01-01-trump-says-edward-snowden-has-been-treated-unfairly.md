Edward Snowden leaked classified intelligence on the National Security Agency's secret surveillance on Americans' private communications.  <br>Snowden eventually settled in Russia and has refused to return to the US because he believes he won't receive a fair trial.  <br>US President Donald Trump once demanded that Snowden be executed.  <br>Now, Gizmodo reports Trump said in an interview with The New York Post that Snowden has 'not been treated fairly.'  <br>However, it's not clear whether Trump is seriously considering a pardon. The DOJ and intelligence agencies remain broadly hostile to Snowden.  <br>However, a huge coalition, including the ACLU, Amnesty International, Human Rights Watch, tech CEOs, and journalists agree he deserves a pardon.
