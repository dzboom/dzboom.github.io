In the wake of the novel coronavirus pandemic, a jewellery store in Surat is selling masks adorned with diamonds worth Rs 4 lakh each. The store in Surat has been manufacturing face masks made of gold, silver, diamonds and even American diamonds, since the past month. The masks are layered with lines of jewels in several patterns.
