Indian actress Sonali Kulkarni at the launch of the Madhubala Postage stamp by Indian Postal Services. Sonali Kulkarni: I have a very old relationship with the Indian postal services. I am from a kind of family who believe in the postal services very much. My mother sends Rakhi to her brothers through post even today. When I was younger there used to be a post card costing 15 rupees and on the top written ' Happy Diwali' or the birthday postcards. I used to send to my cousins but even today I prefer writing letters to people who write to me...
