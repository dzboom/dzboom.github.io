This is the moment a brave snake wrangle caught a ferocious wild python with one hand.  <br>The 13ft long reptile was seen coiled up inside a chicken coop on a farm in Trang, southern Thailand.  <br>Rescue workers were called and one of the team wore a GoPro head camera to record the capture of the snake.  <br>One of the team used a cloth to distract the snake, which was hissing aggressively, while another crept up behind and grabbed the python's throat.  <br>The coop owner Supaporn Chumee, 46, said she has lost more than ten chickens in recent months and she believes the snake is to blame.  <br>She said: &quot;I was mystified by the number of missing chickens in the last couple of months. Now I've caught the culprit red-handed.''  <br>The rescue workers put the chicken into a sack and released it back into the wild far from the coop.
