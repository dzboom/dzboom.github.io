Soha Ali Khan with her mother, Sharmila Tagore at the premiere of 'Khoya Khoya Chand' directed by Sudhir Mishra. Soha Ali Khan is the female lead alongside Shiney Ahuja. Sharmila is in a magenta coloured saree and adorned with lots of jewellery Soha Ali Khan: I am a bit nervous. I am feeling very good that she has come with me and I'm getting her support but I'm nervous of course because I think she is my biggest critic and this film has been based in the 1950s but till the 1960s as well and it was during her time. I am interested to know what she thinks of it. I am playing an actress. When the film opens I am a junior actress, chases the rise of this girl to superstardom and the personal crisis that occur in her life both professionally and personally...
