Typhoon Maysak, locally called Typhoon No. 9, lashed Japan's Okinawa region with ferocious winds and heavy rains on Monday (August 31).  <br>Footage filmed on Tuesday (September 1) shows an uprooted tree in Yaese. Further footage shows uprooted trees lining the streets of Itoman.
