A political storm has arisen in the land of Rajasthan since yesterday. After the rebellious remarks of Rajasthan Pradesh Congress President and Deputy Chief Minister Sachin Pilot, there is a crisis on Ashok Gehlot's government. Sachin Pilot claims that he has around 30 MLAs and all are with him out of Jaipur. On the other hand, Ashok Gehlot is trying to save his government and is gathering his faction.
