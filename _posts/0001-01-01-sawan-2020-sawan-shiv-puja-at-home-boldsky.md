There are only a few days left for the month of Sawan to begin. The month of Sawan has a special significance in Hinduism. Lord Shiva is worshiped throughout this month. You can get the desired fruit by worshiping Lord Shiva in this month. According to the Panchang, the month of Shravan comes in the fifth month of every year starting from the month of Chaitra, while according to the English calendar, the month of Sawan falls in July or August every year. According to religious beliefs, all the wishes are fulfilled by offering prayers to Lord Shiva on the first Monday of the month of Savan. In this month, Shiva devotees also start their Kanvad Yatra.
