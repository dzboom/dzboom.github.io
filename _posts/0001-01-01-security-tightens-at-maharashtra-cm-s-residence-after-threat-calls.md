The security tightened at Maharashtra Chief Minister’s residence, as a precautionary measure after anonymous ‘threat calls’ were received on the landline at Matoshree.  <br>According to Mumbai Police, caller said he was calling on behalf of Dawood Ibrahim and wanted to speak to CM Uddhav Thackeray.  Police is trying to locate the caller.
