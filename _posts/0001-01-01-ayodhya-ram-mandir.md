In the first meeting of the Sri Ramjanmabhoomi Tirtha Kshetra Trust held in Ayodhya on Saturday, it was agreed to do Bhoomipujan on 3 or 5 August for the construction of Ram temple. It was decided to send an invitation to Prime Minister Narendra Modi to perform Bhoomi Pujan on these dates.
