The MY2021 Kawasaki Z1000 has been unveiled globally. The new Kawasaki Z1000 now comes with a new black paint scheme, replacing the old signature-green colour on the previous model. Apart from the new paint scheme, the litre-class motorcycle remains pretty much unchanged. It continues to be powered by the same 1,043cc in-line four-cylinder engine producing 140bhp and 111Nm of peak torque. Watch the video to know all about the new MY2021 Kawasaki Z1000 superbike.
