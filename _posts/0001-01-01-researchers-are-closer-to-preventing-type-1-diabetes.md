Researchers may have found a way to prevent people from developing Type 1 diabetes.  <br>Scientists at the La Jolla Institute for Immunology made the announcement on Wednesday.  <br>According to UPI, the process involved is called neuroimmunology.  <br>Neuroimmunology effectively blocks nerve signals that cause the body's immune system to attack insulin-producing cells.  <br>Researches say that devices can be made that influence nerve function locally by using electrical impulses.  <br>The potential preventative treatments based on the findings are several years away from being used.
