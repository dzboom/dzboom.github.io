A row of 18 shops collapsed due to the heavy rain in northern China.  <br>The video, filmed in the city of Xianyang in Shaanxi Province on August 7, shows a row to shops suddenly falling down.  <br>No casualties were reported as all people had been evacuated before the incident.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
