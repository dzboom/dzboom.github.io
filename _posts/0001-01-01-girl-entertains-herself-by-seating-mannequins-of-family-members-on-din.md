Bored at home during the Coronavirus lockdown, this girl came up with an idea to entertain herself. She created mannequins of her family members using basic stuff she found at home. The girl put photographs of her father, mother, and brother on the balloon-made faces of the dummies and made them sit around the dinner table, having quite a laugh to herself.
