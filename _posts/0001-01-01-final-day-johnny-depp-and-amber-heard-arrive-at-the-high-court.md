The Pirates of the Caribbean star, pictured left, was greeted on arrival this morning by dozens of fans outside the court with flowers, gifts and banners reading: 'Justice for Johnny' and 'Abuse has no gender. Men can be victims too.' Depp was bundled through a boisterous crowd by his bodyguards as he arrived at the main entrance dressed in sharp grey suit and sporting a black bandana. After making his way to the main steps, he stopped and turned, waving and blowing kisses at his fans as he milked the adulation. Depp was at the High Court to hear his barrister David Sherborne deliver his final submission, following a trial which has gripped the showbiz world with sensational details of his marriage to Amber Heard, pictured right and inset with girlfriend Bianca Butt. The actor, 57, is suing News Group Newspapers, publishers of the Sun and executive editor Dan Wootton over a 2018 article which referred to him as a 'wife beater'.
