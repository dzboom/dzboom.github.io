During his acceptance speech for the Republican nomination, Pres. Donald Trump pledged to create 10 million jobs in less than a year.  <br>CNN asked analysts if that was possible.  <br>Covid-19 still not under control.  <br>CNN said the path to economic recovery is unpredictable.  <br>More than 22 million jobs vanished this spring as a result of the pandemic lockdown.  <br>That's the worst jobs loss on record.  <br>The unemployment rate peaked at 14.7% in April.  <br>The good news for Pres. Trump is that the economy has since added back more than 9 million jobs -- also a record.  <br>The consensus forecast for next week's August jobs report is for 1.4 million jobs added, according to Refinitiv.
