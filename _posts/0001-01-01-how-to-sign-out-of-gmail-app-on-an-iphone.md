How to Sign Out of Gmail App on an iPhone?  <br>Step 1: Open 'Gmail' App.  <br>Step 2: Tap on your Profile picture at the top right.  <br>Step 3: Click on link 'Manage Accounts on this device.'  <br>Step 4: Click on 'Remove from this Device' under Gmail account that you want to remove.  <br>Step 5: Read the Message at the Pop-Up, and if you agree, then click on 'remove' link.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
