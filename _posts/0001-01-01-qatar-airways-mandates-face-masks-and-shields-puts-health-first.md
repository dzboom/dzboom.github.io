Face masks on flights have quickly become the norm as travelers tentatively return to the skies.  <br>Qatar Airways has taken this a step further, according to reports at CNN.  <br>Qatar Airways has announced that its passengers will be required to wear a face shield...  <br>...in addition to a face mask or face covering.  <br>The Middle East carrier says it will be issuing fliers with protection kits before boarding.  <br>The kits include face shields, hand sanitizer, a surgical face mask, and disposable globes.  <br>Face shields are obligatory for passengers unless they're eating or drinking.
