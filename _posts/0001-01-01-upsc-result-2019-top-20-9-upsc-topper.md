UPSC, Civil Services Examination-2019 results were released on Tuesday morning. Pradeep Singh has become the All India Topper in the examination. While Pratibha Verma has achieved the highest position among women. Pratibha Verma finished third in the result. Talking about the top 20. 9 girls in the top 20 are named in the list.
