The Indian Council for Medical Research said that wants to launch India’s indigenous COVID-19 vaccine, COVAXIN, for public use by August 15. The 12 shortlisted institutes have been asked by the ICMR to fast track approvals needed to fast track the initiate the clinical trial of the vaccine.
