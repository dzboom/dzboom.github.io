A South Yorkshire charity is providing relief for victims of the 2019 floods who have had to spend months in properties that were damaged by water and not yet repaired due to Covid-19.   <br>Back in November of 2019 floods devastated the Bentley area of Doncaster.   <br>Nine months on and the water is gone but many residents have not yet been able to repair the damage done to their homes.   <br>Alongside fighting insurance companies the flood victims have had to deal with Covid-19.   <br>Many have had to shield or isolate in homes with water damage.   <br>
