Sweating is how your body cools you down, and in most cases, it is normal and healthy.  <br>However, about 3% of US adults have a medical condition called hyperhidrosis, where they sweat too much.   <br>Business Insider spoke to experts about how to stop excessive sweating.  <br>You should try using an antiperspirant.  <br>Stay hydrated.  <br>Avoid certain foods.  <br>Wear more breathable clothing.
