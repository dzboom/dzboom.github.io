An Indian cobra, also known as a spectacled cobra, entered a farmer's house in Cuddalore, Tamil Nadu and swallowed around seven eggs in the early hours of July 12.   <br>The farmer Anbhazhagan, upon hearing noise, checked the chicken cages, and spotted the cobra.  <br>Footage shows snake catcher Cuddalore Chell holding the snake's tail and helping it regurgitate the eggs it swallowed one by one.  <br>The snake was later released in the wild..
