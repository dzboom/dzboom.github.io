What Does Jarrett Stidham's Injury Mean For Cam Newton? | Training Camp Central  <br>Cam Newton is pulling away in the quarterback competition, but it's not completely based on performance. Reports surfaced on Friday that Jarrett Stidham is dealing with a hip injury that has caused him to be limited in the last two Patriots practices. According to Mike Giardi of NFL Network, the injury will likely prevent Stidham from being 100 percent healthy and effective for multiple weeks.  <br>Sierra Goodwill and Evan Lazar discuss what this means for Newton and whether or not he's now penciled in as the Week 1 starter in New England.
