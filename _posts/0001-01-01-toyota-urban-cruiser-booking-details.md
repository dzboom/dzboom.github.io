Toyota Urban Cruiser is one of the much-awaited compact-SUV offerings set to enter the Indian market in the coming weeks. Ahead of its launch, Toyota has announced the start of bookings for the Toyota Urban Cruiser, starting from the 22nd of August 2020. Bookings for the SUV can be made either online or through any of the brand’s dealership across India for an amount of Rs 11,000. Watch the video to find out all the details of the upcoming SUV.
