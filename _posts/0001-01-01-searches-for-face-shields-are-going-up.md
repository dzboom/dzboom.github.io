As the coronavirus pandemic rages on, people are looking for ways they can protect themselves.  <br>While some worry about their own health, others are more focused on preventing others from infection.  <br>Cloth facial coverings are now ubiquitous in many parts of the country, reports Huffpost.  <br>However, clear plastic face shields have been slower to catch on with the public.  <br>Google Trends data shows a large spike in searches for the term “face shields” over the last two months.  <br>The sale of face shields may go up as the pandemic continues, allowing for a new form of protection.
