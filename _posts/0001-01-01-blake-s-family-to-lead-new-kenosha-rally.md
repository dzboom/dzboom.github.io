(Newser) – Family members of Jacob Blake, a Black man who was paralyzed after a Kenosha police officer shot him in the back, are leading a march and rally Saturday to call for an end to police violence, the AP reports. Event organizers said the demonstration would include a march to the Kenosha County Courthouse in Wisconsin and speeches by members of Blake's family, Lt. &quot;We are heartbroken and enraged, but we are steadfast in our demand for justice,&quot; Tanya Mclean, a Blake family friend who helped organize the event, said in a statement. She said Blake's shooting is not an isolated incident, but part of a &quot;brutal, racist system.&quot;
