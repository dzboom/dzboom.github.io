Aja Ekadashi is called Ekadashi of Krishna Paksha of Bhadrapada month. This year the fast of Aja Ekadashi will be kept on Saturday, August 15. Fasting of Ekadashi is considered superior in Sanatan Dharma. On this day, the worshiper of the world, Shri Hari Vishnu is worshiped. Lord Vishnu loves this date. On this day fasting and worshiping Lord Vishnu with true heart fulfills all desires.
