Bille Eilish and her brother Finneas hosted an NPR tiny desk concert from their home.  <br>The award-winning brother and sister team have been performing together since they were children.  <br>According to CNN, the talented duo performed for the series from home because of the coronavirus pandemic.  <br>Eilish sang &quot;Everything I Wanted&quot; and &quot;My Future,&quot; while Finneas alternated between guitar and keyboard as her accompanist.  <br>The two created NPR's intimate &quot;Tiny Desk Concert&quot; setting by using a cardboard cutout.  <br>Eilish told listeners; &quot;Quarantine has been weird,&quot; adding that &quot;the future is something to be super hopeful in.&quot;
