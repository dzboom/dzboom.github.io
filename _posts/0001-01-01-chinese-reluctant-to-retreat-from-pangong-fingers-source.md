After a 14-hour marathon meeting between Indian and Chinese military leaders, the two sides agreed on total disengagement from all flashpoints along the Line of Actual Control (LAC) with the exception of finger area. This comes after more than two months of a bitter standoff between India and China along the LAC in eastern Ladakh.
