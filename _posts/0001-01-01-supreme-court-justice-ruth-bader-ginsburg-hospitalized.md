CNN reports Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg has been admitted to the hospital and treated for a possible infection.  <br>A court spokeswoman says Justice Ginsburg was admitted to The Johns Hopkins Hospital in Baltimore, Maryland, early Tuesday morning.  <br>Justice Ginsberg underwent an endoscopic procedure to clean out a bile duct stent that was placed last August.  <br>The Justice is resting comfortably and will stay in the hospital for a few days to receive intravenous antibiotic treatment. Kathleen Arberg Spokeswoman, U.S. Supreme Court  <br>It's by no means Ginsburg's first medical problem encountered during her time on the bench.  <br>In May, she underwent nonsurgical treatment for a benign gallbladder condition at the same hospital.
