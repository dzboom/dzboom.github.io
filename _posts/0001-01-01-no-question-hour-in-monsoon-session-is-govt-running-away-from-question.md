There will be no Question Hour, a curtailed Zero Hour and no private members' bills during the upcoming monsoon session of Parliament, the Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha secretariats have decided, prompting opposition leaders to accuse the government of trying to &quot;murder the democracy&quot; in the name of the COVID-19 pandemic and reducing &quot;Parliament to a notice board&quot;.
