Zoom, the teleconferencing app used around the world, is down.  <br>Today is the day that many schools are back in session.  <br>It's become the go-to platform for many educators’ remote-teaching.  <br>Now Zoom appears to have crashed for large swaths of the US, according to Gizmodo.  <br>Zoomers across the country have posted on their own accounts that they’ve been unable to log onto the service.  <br>There are an estimated 10,000 reported outages.
