Meet a man from Pune who owns a mask made of pure gold. Shankar Kurade, a resident of Pimpri-Chinchwad of Pune district, has got himself a mask made of gold worth Rs 2.89 lakhs. Kurade said, “It's a thin mask with minute holes so that there's no difficulty in breathing. I'm not sure whether this mask will be effective.”  <br>
