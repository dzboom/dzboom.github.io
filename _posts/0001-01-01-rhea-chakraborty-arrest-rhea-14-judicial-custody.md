Riya Chakraborty has been sent to jail in the drugs case involving Sushant Singh Rajput. The court has sent Riya Chakraborty to judicial custody for 14 days. The NCB sought 14 days judicial custody for Riya Chakraborty from the court. That is, Riya Chakraborty will have to remain in jail till 22 September.
