Una district of Himachal Pradesh, a woman in lieu of superstition looted her millions of rupees on the tantric. Tantrik's greed continued to grow even after cheating the woman with nine lakh rupees and when the woman refused to give him any more money, the tantrik threatened to killher husband and child. The woman complained to the police station after which the police took action and arrested a fellow of the accused Tantrik.
