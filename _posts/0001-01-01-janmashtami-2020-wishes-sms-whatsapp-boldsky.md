The festival of Janmashtami (Happy Janamastami 2020) has special significance in Sanatan Dharma. This year Janmashtami will be celebrated on August 11-12. According to Hinduism beliefs, Lal Gopal of Nanda was born in the midnight of Ashtami on the Krishna Paksha of Bhadrapada. Janmashtami is celebrated as the birth anniversary of Krishna. On Janmashtami, people worship and perform rituals to get blessings and blessings of Shri Krishna, along with fasting.
