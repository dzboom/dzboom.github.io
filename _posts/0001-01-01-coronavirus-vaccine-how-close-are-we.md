Researchers around the world are developing more than 145 vaccines against the coronavirus.  <br>19 vaccines are in human trials but vaccines typically require years of research and testing.  <br>Scientists are racing to produce a safe and effective vaccine by next year, says Business Insider.  <br>The first vaccine safety trials in humans started in March, but the road ahead remains uncertain.  <br>Some trials will fail, and others may end without a clear result.  <br>But a few may succeed in stimulating the immune system to produce effective antibodies.
