Shocking footage shows a container truck toppling over as it takes a tight corner.  <br>The vehicle lost its balance during a tight turn in Nam Dinh, Vietnam on July 18.  <br>Quang Thai Robin was driving behind the vehicle when the accident happened and he recorded the crash on his dashboard camera.  <br>He said that the truck was going slow but it was not able to support its heavy load during the turn so it lost balance.  <br>He said: &quot;The driver seemed to know that he was carrying something heavy so he was driving slowly. I did not drive too near because I am cautious about trucks.&quot;  <br>The truck driver emerged without any serious injuries but the cargo on the truck was damaged.
