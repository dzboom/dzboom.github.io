After seeing all the safety precautions on the sets of his TV show “Aladdin: Naam Toh Suna Hoga”, actor Siddharth Nigam has gained the confidence to resume shooting amid the coronavirus pandemic. In an interview, Siddharth shared, “I was initially a tad bit nervous but after witnessing the measures taken by the team, I am now confident to shoot. The entire set is sanitised at regular intervals and wearing PPE suits, masks and gloves is mandatory. It isn’t easy to work throughout the day in the PPE suits but I really appreciate everyone’s sincerity towards safety. I have immense gratitude towards our production team for such highest quality safety measures that ensures everyone’s well-being.&quot; Aladdin holds a special place in its fans heart as the show is an total family entertainer, as it has flawless mixture of a classic tale mixed with nail-biting adventures and heartwarming romance.
