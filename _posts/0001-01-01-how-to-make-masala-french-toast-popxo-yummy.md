If you haven’t had a savoury masala french toast Indian style, all spiced up, then it’s time to remedy that pronto. Easy to make with simple ingredients easily available at home, this dish is a delicious surprise that you can have for breakfast or as an evening snack with your cuppa tea.   <br>Learn how to make the perfect Masala French Toast in today's episode of POPxo Yummy. Tell us how much in the comments section below.   <br>Step 1) Heat 1 tbsp oil on a pan  <br>Step 2) Add 1 tbsp green chillies and finely chopped garlic  <br>Step 3) Add 1/2 cup onion, tomatoes and capsicum and saute  <br>Step 4) Add 1 tsp red chilly powder, turmeric powder and coriander power.   <br>Step 5) Add 1/4 cup water on the pan and allow the vegetables to cook.  <br>Step 6) Crack open 3 eggs in a separate bowl and add 1/2 cup grated cheese.   <br>Step 7) Add pepper &amp; salt as per taste and beat the eggs.   <br>Step 8) Take a bread slice and evenly spread the vegetables mixture.   <br>Step 9) Cover it with another bread slice and coat well in the egg mixture.   <br>Step 10) Pan fry on medium heat till golden brown and enjoy.
