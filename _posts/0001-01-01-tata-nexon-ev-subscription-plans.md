Tata Motors has introduced monthly subscription plans for their all-electric Nexon EV SUV in the Indian market. Customers can now drive home the Tata Nexon EV through various subscription-based ownership plans.  <br>Monthly prices start at Rs 42,000 and customers can opt from a range of time periods, ranging from 18 to 36 months, depending on the requirement.This plan, however, is currently available only in five cities across India.
