BCCI president Sourav Ganguly, who is known as ‘Dada’ among his fans, Today celebrates his 48th birthday. Dada is regarded as one of the best Indian captain. Under Ganguly, several match winners like Yuvraj Singh, Zaheer Khan, Harbhajan Singh, Ashish Nehra, Virendra Sehwag made their debuts for India. Ganguly, also known as “The Prince of Kolkata”, also led to India to many famous victories. Under him, India won the 2002 NatWest series and gave a tough fight to Steve Waugh’s Australia in Australia in the 2003-04 Border Gavaskar series.
