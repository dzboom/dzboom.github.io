What a momentous week that was for Doncaster Rovers! Digesting everything that has gone on around the Keepmoat, Free Press Rovers writer Liam Hoden is joined by former DFP sports editor Peter Catt, sports writer Paul Goodwin and former Rovers winger John Buckley to discuss big changes and big goodbyes. The panel addresses the end of the Watson family's ownership of the club and how the Covid-19 pandemic has affected financial matters at the club. And of course they discuss the new contract for James Coppinger and the start of his final year at the club. They also talk other contractual issues at Rovers and the future of the U23s group.
