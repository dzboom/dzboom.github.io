Some companies have a hard time measuring ROI (Return on investment).  <br>Wells Fargo's head of social media listening, Brian Wright, spoke with Business Insider about how other companies can measure ROI.  <br>Wright said the key is studying reactions and engaging with costumers on social media.  <br>For many marketers, conversations on social networks have become a key source of information during the coronavirus pandemic.  <br>Wright said analyzing ROI should be a more &quot;custom approach that ties into your business goals and outcomes.&quot;  <br>He said it's a win if a company can take a product to market more effectively because we provided listening insights.  <br>Wright said studying social media can improve ad campaigns and tell companies how their product will be received.
