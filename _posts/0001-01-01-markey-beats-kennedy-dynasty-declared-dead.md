On Tuesday, Sen. Ed Markey crushed Rep. Joe Kennedy.  <br>Markey ended the Democratic Party's family dynasty that had never before lost an election in Massachusetts.  <br>CNN reports that Kennedy was slammed for not articulating a reason for running besides his own ambition.  <br>Kennedy's entire campaign was based on who his family is and that politics was &quot;in his blood.&quot;  <br>His campaign explicitly portrayed him as heir to a political legacy, with ads showing his famous relatives  <br>Markey deflected his opponent's shine by projecting the power of some of the party's brightest stars.  <br>Markey beat Kennedy 55.48% to 44.52%
