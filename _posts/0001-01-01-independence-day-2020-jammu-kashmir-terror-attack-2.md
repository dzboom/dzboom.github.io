A day before Independence Day, terrorists have once again targeted the security forces in Jammu and Kashmir. Militants attacked the police party on the Naugam bypass on the outskirts of Srinagar on Friday morning. Two policemen have been martyred in this attack. At the same time, there is news of a policeman injured. After the attack, security forces have surrounded the entire area.
