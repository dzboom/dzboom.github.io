Kylie Jenner is Vogue Hong Kong's latest cover girl. The 22-year-old makeup mogul dropped the news this weekend via an announcement post on Instagram that features a photo of the stunning cover and Jenner looking as cool as ever in a patent leather ensemble. As per usual, Jenner had her go-to glam squad — Jesus Guerrero and Ariel — get her ready for the shoot. Sadly, she didn't reveal the mastermind who did her nails, but it's safe to assume it may be Los Angeles-based nail artist, Chaun Legend, as he's been responsible for many of Jenner's manicures.
