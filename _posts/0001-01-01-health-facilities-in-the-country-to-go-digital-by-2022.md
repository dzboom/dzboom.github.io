The Ministry Of Ict, Innovations And Youth Affairs Has Kicked Off The Digitization Of Health Facilities Across The Country To Ensure Effective And Efficient Service Delivery. The Ministry Says The Government Plans To Have All The 369 Level Four To Level Six Hospitals Interconnected Through Internet By The End Of The Year 2022.
