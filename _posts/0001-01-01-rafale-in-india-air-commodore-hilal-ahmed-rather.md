The wait for many years is coming to an end. Rafael Fighter Jet is coming to India for the Indian Airforce from France. These aircraft will reach Ambala Air Force Station in Haryana on Wednesday. There were many obstacles in this whole deal with France, because Rafael had to prepare according to the requirements of the Indian Air Force. In this whole process, Air Comrade Hilal Ahmad Rathor of Air Force, who belongs to Jammu and Kashmir, has played an important role.
