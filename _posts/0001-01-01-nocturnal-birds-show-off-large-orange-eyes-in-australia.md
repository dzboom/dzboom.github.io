The Tawny Frogmouth is one of Australia’s most-known nocturnal birds. This family was captured during the day with the parents resting and their two young offspring very wide-eyed and interested in the filmer's presence. The parents were motionless watching through their nearly closed eyes. The young were displaying a threatening pose ruffling their feathers and showing their wide orange eyes. (December 21, 2017)
