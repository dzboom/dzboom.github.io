Photographer Justin Ng explains his obsession with Larry, the beloved Downing Street cat. Justin, an Australian native, is popular on social media for sharing his pictures of the 13-year-old brown and white tabby, that has been a permanent fixture of the British Prime Minister's residence since 2011.
