Joe Biden is against the legalization of marijuana.  <br>However, Se. Ed Markey says Biden may not be able to prevent it.  <br>Markey said if Democrats reclaim the senate and White House they would “move very quickly” to legalize marijuana.  <br>Markey said Democrats are positioned to advance marijuana reform as soon as they have a majority in both chambers of Congress.  <br>Biden remains opposed to adult-use legalization.  <br>“From my perspective, this is another issue that’s just right there on the ballot in November.”
