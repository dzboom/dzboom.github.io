An &quot;SOS&quot; message written in huge letters on the beach saved three missing men.  <br>The emergency communication was spelled out on the shore of a tiny Pacific island.  <br>United States and Australian authorities confirmed three men had set out in a 23-foot boat last Thursday.  <br>The mariners had planned to make a 26-mile journey from Pulawat to Pulap atolls in the Federated States of Micronesia.  <br>According to CNN, the group went off course and ran out of fuel, stranding them on the small island.
