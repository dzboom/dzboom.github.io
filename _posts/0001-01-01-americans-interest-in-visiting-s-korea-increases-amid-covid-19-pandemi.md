&quot;코로나19 이후 미 국민 '한국 여행 선호도' 향상&quot;  <br>More and more Americans are showing an interest in touring South Korea.  <br>The New York office of the Korea Tourism Organization revealed on Sunday... the results of a poll of 250 travel industry workers and 400 consumers.  <br>About 38 percent of those in the industry expressed an increased interest in visiting South Korea,... as did almost a quarter of consumers.  <br>And close to 80 percent of industry insiders said they were willing to recommend the country, while 72 percent said they were inclined to sell packages.  <br>The tourism organization attributes the interest to South Korea's proactive approach to COVID-19.   <br>
