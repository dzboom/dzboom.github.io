Warisan president Shafie Apdal said he informed PKR president Anwar Ibrahim that the main objective is to win the Sabah state election when the pair discussed seat allocations.  <br>&quot;I told Anwar who came to my house... I said I gave many seats last time, but they only won two.  <br>&quot;This is not the season for us to give seats, this is the season for us to win the election. It is not a question of 'Oh our party will be looked down on if we have a smaller number (of seats)'.  <br>&quot;It is not a party's dignity that is our priority. It is the dignity that we want to win the election that is more important,&quot; he said in his speech before announcing the 54 candidates who will be contesting under the Warisan logo in the upcoming state polls at the Sabah International Convention Centre (SICC) today.
