Hot on the heels of his newest project The Kiwi Sound, UK garage producer Conducta is determined to keep the bass boosted and the flag waving. For his Digital FORT performance, he brings things out of the garage and onto his balcony in west London to deliver a mix that splices together material from his label Kiwi Rekords as well as a heavy hand of new material that he's been working on during isolation. It's blue skies and even better vibes. Check it out above, and then head over here to donate to Conducta's chosen charity, the NAACP Defense Fund.
