The case of controversial remarks made by Nepal Prime Minister KP Sharma Oli on Lord Ram and Ayodhya has caught fire. Ayodhya saints are agitated by the Nepali PM's statement. Ram Dalas Maharaj, president of the Ram Dal Trust, has said that his disciples in Nepal will take to the streets to protest against Oli. Referring to the description in the Vedas and Puranas, Ramdas Maharaj said that there is no Saryu in Nepal. He also said that Nepali PM KP Sharma Oli will have to get off the chair within a month.
