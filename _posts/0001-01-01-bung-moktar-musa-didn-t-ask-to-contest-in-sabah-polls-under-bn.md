Sabah BN head Bung Moktar Radin said the reason former chief minister Musa Aman was not given a seat to contest in the upcoming Sabah election was because he didn't ask for one.  <br>&quot;Musa had never requested to contest as a BN candidate,&quot; he told a press conference in Kota Kinabalu today.  <br>Asked if BN took the initiative to offer a seat to the incumbent Sungai Sibuga assemblyperson, Bung said this was not done.  <br>His explanation came after the unveiling of Umno's list of candidates for the Sept 26 state election.  <br>
