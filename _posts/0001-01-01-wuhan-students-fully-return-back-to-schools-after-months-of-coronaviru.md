Students in central China's Wuhan fully return to schools on September 1 after months of coronavirus lockdown.  <br>In the video, pupils were one metre away from each other while walking into the Shen Yang Road Primary School one-by-one. An infrared temperature measuring device was also set up at the school gate to check every students' temperature.  <br>Another clip shows students and teachers wearing face masks in the classrooms.  <br>The school has prepared epidemic prevention supplies which includes face masks, hand wash and thermometers.  <br>2,842 middle schools, primary schools and kindergarten have reopened and 1.4 million students returned back to schools in the city.  <br>It is the first time that primary schools and kindergartens reopened since coronavirus lockdown.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
