Corona virus cases are continuously increasing in the country. Now more than 50 thousand cases are coming every day. Due to more corona cases, corona tests have been increased. The health ministry said in a press conference on Tuesday that more than 6.6 lakh corona tests were conducted in the country in 24 hours.
