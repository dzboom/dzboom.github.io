As soon as the CBSE 10th result is released, the wait of about 18 lakh students eagerly has also ended. The 10th rank has been issued on the official website of the board. The remaining examination was canceled this year due to Corona viral. In such a situation, the result of the alternative evaluation system has been released. Students are given numbers based on internal assessment criteria. This year, the list of toppers of both 12th and 10th classes has not been released.
