Member of the Rajya Sabha and former Samajwadi Party leader Amar Singh passed away at the age of 64 on Saturday. Survived by wife Pankaja and twin daughters, Singh was undergoing treatment at a hospital in Singapore for several months now. The 64-year-old leader had suffered kidney failure in 2013 and had also fainted at Dubai airport while on his way to Singapore for a regular medical check-up in February 2013.
