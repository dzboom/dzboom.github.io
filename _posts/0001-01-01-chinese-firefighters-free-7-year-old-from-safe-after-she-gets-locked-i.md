Firefighters in southern China freed a 7-year-old girl from a safe after she got locked inside while playing hide-and-seek.  <br>The video, filmed in the city of Dongguan in Guangdong Province on August 4, shows firefighters using an angle grinder and a shovel to try to open the safe door but failed.  <br>They then worked with the locksmith to open a hole on the door first before cutting the lock on the safe to free the girl.  <br>According to reports, the girl hid into the safe when playing hide-and-seek with her friend and accidentally got locked inside.  <br>Fortunately, the girl was saved after having been locked inside for half an hour and she was alright.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
