5 facts you probably didn't know about tequila.  <br>Tequila is an alcohol derived from the blue agave plant.  <br>The plant features a core called piña, which closely resembles a pineapple.  <br>Once the piña is harvested, it is brought to a distillery to be roasted to unleash its juices.  <br>Once those juices are released, they are fermented, distilled and aged before being bottled.  <br>In order to be labeled tequila, the production of the liquor has to occur in the state of Jalisco or in some neighboring states.
