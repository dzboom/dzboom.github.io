Political statements have started in Bihar after the meeting of Bharatiya Janata Party MPs in Delhi with party president JP Nadda regarding the upcoming assembly elections in Bihar. In this meeting, BJP MPs have demanded a compromise with the JDU on half of the seats in front of the party leadership, like the last Lok Sabha election in the next assembly election.
