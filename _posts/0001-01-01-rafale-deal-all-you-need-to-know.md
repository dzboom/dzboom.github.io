The Indian Air Force (IAF) will formally induct the Rafale aircraft today (September 10) at the Air Force Station in Ambala. The aircraft will be part of 17 Squadron, the &quot;Golden Arrows&quot;. The first five IAF Rafale aircraft arrived at Air Force Station, Ambala from France on July 27.
