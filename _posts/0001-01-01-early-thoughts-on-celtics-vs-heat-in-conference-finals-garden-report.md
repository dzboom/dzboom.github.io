Early Thoughts on Celtics vs Heat in Conference Finals? | Garden Report  <br>The Boston Celtics have advanced to the Eastern Conference Finals for the third time in the last four years. After outlasting the Toronto Raptors in Game 7, the Miami Heat are the Celtics’ next task. While the Heat are similar defensively to the Raptors and don’t pose a huge size mismatch, the Jimmy Butler powered offense poses more of a threat.  <br>Sierra Goodwill, Jimmy Toscano, Josue Pavon and Bobby Manning discuss the intriguing matchups in this series and share one Boston player who could be the difference-maker.
