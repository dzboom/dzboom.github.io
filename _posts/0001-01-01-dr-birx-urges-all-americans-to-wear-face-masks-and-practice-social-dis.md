Dr. Deborah Birx on Sunday said America is in a new phase in its ongoing fight against the coronavirus.  <br>According to CNN, Dr. Birx said that the deadly virus is more widespread than when it first took hold earlier this year.  <br>&quot;What we are seeing today is different from March and April. It is extraordinarily widespread.&quot;  <br>Her statement comes as the US has reported more coronavirus cases and deaths than any other country in the world.  <br>The United States had reported more than 4.8 million cases of Covid-19 and at least 158,365 Americans have died.  <br>Dr. Birx urged all Americans to follow health recommendations, including wearing a mask and practicing social distancing.
