A mutant frog with four arms amazed superstitious locals - who made offerings to it for good luck.    <br>The amphibian was found in a family’s well in Surin, northeastern Thailand, where it was rescued. It had two extra arms protruding from its right shoulder.  <br>House owner Uan Conaban said that when he noticed the two extra limbs he quickly pulled the frog from the water and took it into his home.  <br>His neighbours are now praying to the frog, hoping it will bring them winning lottery numbers.  <br>Uan said: “There are always many frogs in the well but I noticed one was separated from the others.  <br>&quot;I picked it up to look close and saw that it had six limbs. I have been hand-rearing it in my home ever since.  <br>“When the neighbours found out about my lucky charm they stopped by periodically to worship it and ask for lucky lottery numbers for the upcoming draw.&quot;   <br>Uan said that the frog is strong and healthy since he found it earlier this month on July 16.  <br>''His extra arms are strong and he can move them but not as well as the others,'' he added.
