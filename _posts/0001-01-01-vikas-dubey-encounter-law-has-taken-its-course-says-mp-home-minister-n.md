Home Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Narottam Mishra said law has taken its course on gangster Vikas Dubey’s death in encounter with police on July 10. “Law has taken its course. It could be a matter of regret &amp;disappointment for those who raised questions on Vikas Dubey's arrest yesterday and death today. MP Police did its job, it arrested and handed him over to UP Police,” said BJP leader Narottam Mishra. Minister also hit back at Congress and Samajwadi Party over allegations.
