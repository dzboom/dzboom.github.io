Celebrities Unite for Gov. Cuomo's 'Mask Up America' Campaign  The initiative was launched this  week by New York Governor  Andrew Cuomo. 'Mask Up America' is a series of  public service announcements  with celebrities doing voice-overs. Oscar-winning director  Kathryn Bigelow is  helming the videos. Morgan Freeman and Jeffrey Wright  voiced the first two announcements,  which debuted on July 16. Other stars who will participate include  Jamie Foxx, Robert De Niro and Ellen Pompeo. Andrew Cuomo,  via statement Andrew Cuomo,  via statement According to 'USA Today,' 'Mask Up America'  ads are expected to be shown around the U.S. Celebrities such as Jennifer Aniston  and Reese Witherspoon have also  been advocating for face masks. Tom Hanks and Rita Wilson, who've  recovered since contracting COVID-19  in March, support face coverings as well.
