This person was flying a radio-controlled airplane in a field outside Stockholm in Sweden when a bird swooped in for the kill. The plane turned away, but the bird followed it persistently. As it was about to land, the bird swooped over and attacked the plane, knocking out the head of the toy pilot figure sitting in the plane.
