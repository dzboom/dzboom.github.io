Colleges across the United States are planning to reopen in the fall.  <br>This plan persists even in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, reports UPI.  <br>Many universities plan to test students every two days using a rapid, inexpensive kit.  <br>Unfortunately, these tests are 70% accurate, an analysis published Friday found.  <br>Other strict measures include mask-wearing, social distancing, and isolation of confirmed cases.  <br>Schools hope to lower the potential spread of the coronavirus through these measures.
