Vikas Dubey, accused of killing 8 policemen in Bikaru village of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, is out of police custody even after 5 days. UP Police's action on the encounter of Kanpur, Prashant Kumar held a press conference today. During this, Prashant Kumar said that he will take such action that the criminals will regret their actions.
