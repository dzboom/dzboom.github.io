Ever since Unlock 1.0 which began in June, cases of Covid-19 have been rising rapidly across India.   <br>The ICMR's decision to 'test, track and treat' is laudable, but it hasn't done anything to expand the scope of testing criteria.  <br>While most Tier 1 cities in India are running out of hospital beds, asymptomatic individuals don't meet the criteria for a Covid-19 test, making it harder to track transmission.  <br>On this episode of Backtrack, we look at ICMR's guidelines for Covid-19 testing and attempt to answer why the lack of tests for asymptomatic individuals may be counter-intuitive.
