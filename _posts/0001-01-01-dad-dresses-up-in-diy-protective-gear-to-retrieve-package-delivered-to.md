While quarantining with family during COVID 19 pandemic, this dad created a light-hearted moment for his family by his dramatic approach to receiving a package delivered to his doorstep. With the help of his kids, he dressed up in a DIY protective gear, with masks and gloves and tape and ventured outside to get the goods.
