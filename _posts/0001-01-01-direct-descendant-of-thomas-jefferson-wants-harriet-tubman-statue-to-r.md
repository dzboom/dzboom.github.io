Thomas Jefferson's direct descendant is calling for changing regarding his family's legacy.  <br>He wants the Washington, DC memorial dedicated to the third president to be taken down.  <br>Lucian K. Truscott IV wrote that Jefferson's memorial should be replaced with one to Harriet Tubman.  <br>&quot;To see a 19-foot-tall bronze statue of a Black woman, who was a slave and also a patriot, in place of a white man who enslaved hundreds of men and women is not erasing history.&quot; Lucian K. Truscott IV  <br>Truscott says the nationwide conversation on racism and white supremacy inspired his thoughts.
