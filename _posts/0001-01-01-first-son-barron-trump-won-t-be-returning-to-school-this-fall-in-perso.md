The school attended by President Donald Trump and Melania Trump's youngest son, Barron, has ended on-campus learning.  <br>The news comes as the president demands a return to in-person classes around the country.  <br>On Friday, CNN reports Montgomery County, Maryland, ordered that private schools not conduct in-person learning until October 1st.  <br>Barron Trump is slated to enter 9th grade in the fall. He attends St. Andrew's Episcopal School in Montgomery County, Maryland.  <br>It's not up to the federal government whether schools open or close.  <br>But that didn't stop Education Secretary Betsy DeVos from using her national platform to demand that all schools open.  <br>The novel coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic is still raging in multiple hot zones across the country.
