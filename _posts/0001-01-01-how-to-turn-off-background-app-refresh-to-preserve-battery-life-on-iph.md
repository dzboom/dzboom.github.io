How to Turn-Off Background App Refresh to Preserve Battery Life on iPhone?  <br>Step 1: Open 'Settings' App.  <br>Step 2: Tap on 'General.'  <br>Step 3: Tap on 'Background App Refresh.'  <br>Step 4: Tap on 'Background App Refresh' again at the top.  <br>Step 5: Tap on 'Off.'  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
