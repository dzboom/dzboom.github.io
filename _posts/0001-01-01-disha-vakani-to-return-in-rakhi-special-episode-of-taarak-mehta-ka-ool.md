Ever since Disha Vakani aka Daya Ben left Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah the show seems really dull and incomplete without her presence. Disha has been missing since September 2017. The actress went on to maternity leave but reports said that Disha has quit the show due to some creative difference with the show makers hence they are in lookout for the new actor to portray the character. However she did a special appearance in last year's Navratri episode, but Disha is yet to make her full-fledged entry in the show. Now there are some reports that suggest that Daya will be returning in the Raksha Bandhan special episode as Mayur Vakani who's Disha real and reel life brother has arrived in the city to shoot for the special episode. We all know how amazing their bond is in the show, so there are high chances of Disha returning in the coming episodes. However, makers haven't confirmed the news but we can just hope for the best.
