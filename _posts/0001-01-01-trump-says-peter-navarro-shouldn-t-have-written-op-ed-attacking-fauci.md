President Donald Trump sought to quell reports of a rift between himself and Dr. Anthony Fauci, the nation's top infectious disease specialist, stating again Wednesday that he has &quot;a very good relationship&quot; with Fauci.  <br>Speaking to reporters as he departed for an event on infrastructure in Atlanta, Trump insisted &quot;We're all in the same team.&quot;  <br>The president also looked to distance himself from an op-ed written by his trade adviser, Peter Navarro in USA Today, in which Navarro says Fauci has &quot;been wrong about everything&quot; he has interacted with him on.  <br>Asked if Navarro went rogue in writing the piece, Trump said &quot;he made a statement representing himself. He shouldn't be doing that.&quot;
