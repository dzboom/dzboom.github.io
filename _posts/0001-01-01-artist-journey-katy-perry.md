Katy Perry started performing in 2001, touring acoustically and with musicians like Phil Joel. After signing to Capitol Records, Perry hit her stride as a performer when she joined Warped Tour 2008. Her first official tour was the Hello Katy Tour in 2009. Since then, Perry has performed over 600 shows across 4 concert tours.
