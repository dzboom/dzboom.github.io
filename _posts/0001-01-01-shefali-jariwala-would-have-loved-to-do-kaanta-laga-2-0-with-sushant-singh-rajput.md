Following the shocking and untimely death of Sushant Singh Rajput, the debates related to mental health and nepotism have become the talk of the town. People have been blaming various superstar and star kids for Sushant's death. Former Bigg Boss contestant Shefali Jariwala who knew Sushant says she would have loved to work with the actor in Kanta Laga 2.0 as he's an amazing dancer and good looking guy. In an interview, Shefali said, &quot;If Kaanta Laga 2.0 was created in today's times I would like to see Sushant Singh Rajput doing it with me. May God bless his soul. He was such an amazing dancer and a very good-looking guy. I knew him, I was acquainted with him but I never got a chance to work with. I would have loved to do Kata Laga 2.0 reloaded with Sushant Singh Rajput.” Further sharing her views on nepotism, Shefali shared, she can't say much about it but she believes that there is favoritism in the industry as she's herself an outsider who became prominent right after doing her first song which is still popular.
