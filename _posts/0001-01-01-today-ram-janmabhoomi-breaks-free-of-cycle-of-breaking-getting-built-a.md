Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the gathering on the occasion of foundation stone laying ceremony at Ram Temple in Ayodhya on August 05. While addressing the gathering, PM Modi said, “It is my good fortune that I was invited to witness this historical moment. From Kanyakumari to Kheer Bhawani, from Koteshwar to Kamakhya, from Jagannath to Kedarnath, Somnath to Kashi Vishwanath, today entire country is immersed in Lord Ram.” He further said, “A grand temple will now be built for our Ram Lalla who had been staying in a tent. Today, Ram Janmabhoomi breaks free of the cycle of breaking and getting built again - that had been going on for centuries.”
