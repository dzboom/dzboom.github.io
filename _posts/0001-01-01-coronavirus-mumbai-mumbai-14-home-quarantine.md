In view of the Corona virus infection, the Maharashtra government also ordered people traveling by air in Mumbai to stay home quarantined for 14 days to prevent the number of infected from increasing. It would be necessary to stay in home isolation. This rule will also apply to those traveling by domestic flights. BMC on Friday changed the quarantine rules.
