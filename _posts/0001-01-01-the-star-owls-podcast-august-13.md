The dust has barely settled on one season and the ink still hasn't dried on the points deduction but Sheffield Wednesday are back in training for the start of the 2020/21 campaign - which itself is only two, three or four weeks away, depending on who you ask. Having just about caught their breath from the last lot of matches, The Star's Wednesday writing duo Joe Crann and Alex Miller join Liam Hoden to discuss all the latest from Hillsborough, including the arrival of three new faces to bolster Garry Monk's backroom staff. They ponder what the arrivals will mean for Wednesday going forward as well as the futures of some of the stalwarts of the Owls' coaching staff. They also give an update on transfer activity, the points deduction and look ahead to fixtures, friendlies and everything else that needs to be fitted in before mid-September.
