According to DGCA officials, a digital flight data recorder has been recovered from the plane wreck. The floorboards are being cut for the cockpit voice recorder. Now after getting the black box, the real reasons for the plane crash will be known. After getting the black box, it will be revealed what was the conversation between the pilots at the time of the accident ...
