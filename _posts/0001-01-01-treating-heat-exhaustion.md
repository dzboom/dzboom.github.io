Heat exhaustion is dangerous and can lead to death.  <br>Heat exhaustion symptoms include rapid pulse, nausea, fatigue, and lightheadedness.   <br>Heat exhaustion usually happens in hot, humid environments when the body is dehydrated and can't cool itself properly.  <br>There are simple ways to prevent this.  <br>Stay hydrated.  <br>Try to avoid exercising, or otherwise exerting yourself, during the hottest parts of the day.
