Supporters of the Fatah movement in southern Gaza organised a solidarity rally with candles and waving Lebanese flags at around  6 pm local time on Wednesday (August 5).  <br>Video shows a large crowd of people at Najma Square, holding candles to honor the over 100 casualties of the blast that rocked the Lebanese city on  August 4.
