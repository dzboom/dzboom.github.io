The Sutlej-Yamuna link canal issue seems to be heating up once again. Punjab's current CM Amarinder Singh, who resigned from the Lok Sabha seat of Amritsar at one time in the name of the Sutlej-Yamuna link agreement, has warned the central government in strong words. Capt Amarinder Singh, who attended the meeting on behalf of the Punjab government, said that if the work of the Sutlej Yamuna Link canal is completed, Punjab will rise and there is a big threat to national security.
