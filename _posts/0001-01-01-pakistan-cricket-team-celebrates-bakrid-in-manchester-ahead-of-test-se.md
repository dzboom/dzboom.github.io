Pakistan cricket team is in England for their bilateral series with the home side. The visiting team is in Derby and will move to Manchester before the first Test on August 05. Meanwhile, the Pakistan cricket team players celebrated Eid ul-Adha or Eid al-Adha in UK together and the photos were shared by the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) on its social media channels.
