This timelapse shot by Rob Tilsley of comet Neowise shows how it clearly moves compared to the stars surrounding it.   <br>The extraordinary clip was shot on Saturday (July 11) in Buckfast, Devon, United Kingdom.   <br>Comet Neowise is a retrograde comet with a near-parabolic orbit discovered on March 27, 2020, by astronomers using the NEOWISE space telescope.
