A woman dragged a gas cylinder on a road after she accidentally ignited it when using the fire to clean duck feathers in southern China.  <br>In the video, filmed in the city of Yueyang in Hunan Province on July 28, a woman is seen holding a pipe attached to a gas cylinder to try to use the fire to clean duck feathers.  <br>However, the pipe pointed towards the gas cylinder and ignited it. The woman then attempted to blow the fire out immediately.  <br>Since it did not work, she kicked the cylinder over onto the ground and dragged it along the road.  <br>Fortunately, the fire was finally put out.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
