A chariot at Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple caught fire in Andhra Pradesh’s East Godavari district on September 06.   <br>The incident took place in Antarvedi village of East Godavari district. The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained.   <br>There is no loss of any property and no injuries have been reported yet. While speaking to media, a police official said, “Fire was extinguished by 03:00 am. There is no other property loss or any injury. Cause of fire is yet to be ascertained. Investigation is underway.”  <br>
