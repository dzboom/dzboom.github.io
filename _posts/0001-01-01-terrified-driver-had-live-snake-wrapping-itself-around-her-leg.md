A terrified driver had a live SNAKE wrapping itself around her leg.  <br>Jarunee Wongsawat, 56, was travelling home when she noticed the reptile on her left leg in Chonburi, eastern Thailand.  <br>The driver screamed and shook it off before pulling into a mechanic's garage to plead for help.   <br>Footage shows the 3ft long rat snake peering out from under the steering wheel while Jarunee sits still in the driving seat trying not to provoke it.  <br>Mechanic had to them disassemble the car console so rescue workers could find the serpent.   <br>They later found the 3ft long rat snake hiding among the wires and pulled it out before releasing it back to the wild.  <br>The relieved driver Jarunee said she was shocked as she felt the snake coiling around her leg, but she managed to compose herself.  <br>Jarunee said: &quot;I was genuinely shocked as the snake slithered up my leg but then I tried to talk to and tell it not to harm me. It then slithered back into the console.  <br>&quot;Fortunately, I did not panic otherwise I could have crashed the car and it would have been such a horrible accident.&quot;
