Murphy, the puppy, was adopted during the quarantine and had the undivided attention of their owner as she stayed with them all the time. She decided to take them along to the office as she had to go back to the work post lockdown. Since she was busy with her work and couldn't attend to them all the time, they whined and called for her attention.
