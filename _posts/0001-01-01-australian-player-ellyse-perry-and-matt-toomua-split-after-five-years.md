Australian cricket great Ellyse Perry and Rugby star Matt Toomua have announced an end to their marriage. Perry, who recently won the T20 World Cup, and Toomua went public in 2013 and tied the knot two years later. 'It's with the greatest of respect for one another that we decided to separate earlier this year,' the pair said in a joint statement on Saturday.
