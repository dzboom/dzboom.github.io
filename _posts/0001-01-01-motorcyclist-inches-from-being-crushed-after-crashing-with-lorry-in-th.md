A lucky motorcyclist survived after he lost control and skidded under a truck.  <br>The shirtless man was speeding around a bend trying to overtake the lorry in Bangkok, central Thailand on July 8.  <br>Dashcam footage shows how the truck driver noticed the reckless biker so he quickly stopped the vehicle in time and prevented crushing him.  <br>Luckily, the motorcyclist managed to roll away from the truck wheels so he only suffered minor scratches from the road.  <br>Onlookers rushed to assist the rider and help to wheel his motorcycle onto the grass verge.  <br>One driver, Aek, said: &quot;Why do some people risk their lives by riding vehicles without awareness and concern?&quot;
