Achieve semipermanent freckles through henna. Henna is a plant-derived dye that dates back to ancient Egypt. Traditionally henna is used for holidays, weddings, and birthdays in South Africa, India, and the Middle East. It’s also become a popular method for creating temporary, natural-looking freckles. To create the freckles, a thin-tipped henna cone is used to dot the face. The henna will emerge as a light orange at first, but will appear darker and more intense one day later.
