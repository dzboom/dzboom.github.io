According to CNN Business, FedEx, a major sponsor of the Washington Redskins, has asked the NFL team to change its name.   <br>This comes after mounting pressure from investors who oppose the name's racist connotations.  <br>FedEx said that over 80 groups and shareholders that invest in them demanded that FedEx CEO Frederick Smith &quot;terminate its business and public relationships&quot; with the franchise because of the name.  <br>FedEx sponsors and has naming rights for the stadium the Washington Redskins plays in.   <br>Similar investor letters were also sent to the CEOs of Nike, which produces the Redskins uniforms and equipment, and Pepsi, its snack and beverage partner.   <br>Investors credited the Black Lives Matter movement for shining light on centuries of systemic racism.  <br>Investors added, &quot;It is time for FedEx to meet the magnitude of this moment, to make their opposition to the racist team name clear, and to take tangible and meaningful steps..&quot;
