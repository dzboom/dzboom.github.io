Punjab: 21 people Died due to drinking poisonous liquor, CM Amarinder Singh ordered inquiry. CM Captain Amarinder Singh has ordered an inquiry into the death of 21 people due to drinking poisonous liquor in Amritsar city and Tarn Taran in Punjab.
