Spanish football titans Real Madrid secured the La Liga victory last night (July 16) after a 2-1 win over Villarreal.  <br>Fans were quick to show their support but public celebrations were confined to vehicles in an attempt to curb the spread of COVID-19.  <br>Footage shows many vehicles using their horns on Calle del Almirante to express support for the club's title win.  <br>Spain has had over 28,000 deaths related to COVID-19.
