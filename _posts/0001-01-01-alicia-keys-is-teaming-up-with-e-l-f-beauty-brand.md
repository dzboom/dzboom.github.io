Alicia Keys and cosmetic brand E.L.F. are pairing up to offer a new beauty line.  <br>The award-winning singer-songwriter is teaming up with the cosmetics company for a new skin-care line.  <br>According to Allure, the new venture will be a full-blown &quot;lifestyle beauty brand&quot;.  <br>Kory Marchisotto, E.L.F.'s chief marketing officer says Keys is iconic and inspirational.  <br>The brand will offer dermatologist-developed cruelty-free products.  <br>The new line is expected to launch sometime in 2021.
