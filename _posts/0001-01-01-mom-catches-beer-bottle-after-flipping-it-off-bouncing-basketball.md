This woman decided to give the Baskbetaball Beer Challenge a go. She balanced a beer bottle on a basketball and dropped the ball to the ground. As the ball hit the floor, it launched the beer bottle into the air. The woman caught it and took a swig as she succeeded. She went on to attempt the challenge two more times and nailed it every time.
