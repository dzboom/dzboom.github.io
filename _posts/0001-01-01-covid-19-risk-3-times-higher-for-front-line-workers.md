Front-line healthcare workers in the US and Britain are at a much higher risk of infection.  <br>They are more than three times as likely to contract COVID during this pandemic.  <br>These findings are based on self-reported data from nearly 100,000 American and British clinicians.  <br>They used the COVID Symptom Study smartphone app and recorded between March 24 and April 23.  <br>Preliminary results also suggest that ethnic background and clinical setting influenced diagnosis.
