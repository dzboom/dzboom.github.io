A Slovenian woman's scheme for collecting a $1.2 million insurance payout has backfired horribly.  <br>According to Newser, 22-year-old Julija Adlesic showed up at a local hospital last year, claiming she'd cut off her hand while she was cutting tree branches.  <br>But according to Newser, she didn't bring the hand along to the hospital with her, in an apparent attempt to keep doctors from being able to reattach it.  <br>Eventually, what a Ljubjana court found was that Adlesic cut off her own left hand with a circular saw.  <br>She's been sentenced to two years in jail for insurance fraud.
