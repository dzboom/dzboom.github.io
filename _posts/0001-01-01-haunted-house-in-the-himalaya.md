Our camera team camps next to the Haunted House of the Himalaya, at the wildfilmsindia estate. The building lost half its roof in a summer storm. The walls have crumbled and some of them have collapsed, but it still holds its own and its reputation as the most famous Haunted House in the Himalaya. Fire places still exist, even as 200 year old Cedar and teak beams of wood hold up its semi-complete roof. Meanwhile, our wildlife conservation work continues all around, at the Wilderness Orchard and botanical gardens in the Himalaya.
