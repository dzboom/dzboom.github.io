Firefighters rescued a two-year-old baby boy after his head got stuck inside a container while he was playing in the kitchen in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu.  <br>The incident took place in the village of Rajakkamangalam, Kanyakumari district on July 31.  <br>Dad Kannan alerted the fire department after he and his wife found their two-year-old son Neeraj screaming in the kitchen  <br>The video shows the firefighters trying to cut the container to rescue the toddler's head.  <br>The rescue operation went on for about 20 minutes before Neeraj was rescued without any injuries.
