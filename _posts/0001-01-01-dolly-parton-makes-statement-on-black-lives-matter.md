Country singer Dolly Parton is making it clear that she supports the Black Lives Matter movement.  <br>In 2018, she changed the name of the Dixie Stampede to just Stampede.  <br>This was after she became aware that the term &quot;Dixie&quot; is associated with the Confederacy.  <br>&quot;When they said 'Dixie' was an offensive word, I thought, 'Well, I don't want to offend anybody.&quot;  <br>Other country bands such as Lady Antebellum and The Dixie Chicks also changed their names.
