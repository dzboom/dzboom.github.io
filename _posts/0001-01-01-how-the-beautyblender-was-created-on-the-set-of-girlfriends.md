Rea Ann Silva is the creator and founder of Beautyblender, the famous egg-shaped makeup tool used by celebrities like Kim Kardashian West, Heidi Klum, Meghan Markle, and more. She worked on the sets of music videos, movies, and TV shows throughout her career but came up with the idea of the Beautyblender on the set of the early-2000s show “Girlfriends.” Since then, the egg has gone on to sell millions of units worth millions of dollars, solidifying it as the makeup tool that changed the game.
