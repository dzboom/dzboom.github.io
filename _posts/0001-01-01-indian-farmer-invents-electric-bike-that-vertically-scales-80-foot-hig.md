A farmer in Bantwal, western India has created a machine that can vertically scale trees up to 80 feet high.  <br>Ganapathi Bhat, a 60-year-old farmer, took five years to develop the tree-climbing bike which comes strapped with a back support and safety harness.  <br>The 60-year-old, who has a Bachelor of science, built the bike so farmers could effortlessly reach the coconuts at the tree tops.   <br>Bhat is selling his invention at the price of Rs 75,000 (£764) per unit.  <br>This footage was filmed on August 2.
