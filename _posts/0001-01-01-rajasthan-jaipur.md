Jaipur. On Tuesday, Alsubah Baghera was seen walking in the Palm Court Colony of Jagatpura area. Baghera moved forward after stopping briefly in front of a house in this colony near the shooting range of Track-3 of Jhalana. This picture was captured on the CCTV of the house at that time was 3:38 am.
