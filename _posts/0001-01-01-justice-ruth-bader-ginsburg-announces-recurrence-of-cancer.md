Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg announced Friday that she is undergoing chemotherapy to treat a recurrence of cancer.  <br>According to Justice Ginsburg, the treatment is yielding &quot;positive results.&quot;  <br>The 87-year-old said she remains &quot;fully able&quot; to continue her post on the Supreme Court of the United States.  <br>Friday's announcement indicates that the pancreatic cancer Ginsburg was treated for in 2009 has returned.  <br>Ginsburg had radiation therapy for pancreatic cancer in 2019 and had lung tumors removed in 2018.
