After the elimination of gangster Vikas Dubey, the main accused in the Kanpur shoot out case of Uttar Pradesh, the police have now started clamping down on his close ones. Police arrested Jayakant Vajpayee and his partner Prashant Shukla, close to Vikas Dubey. It is being told that Jayakant Vajpayee aka Jai Vajpayee used to keep accounts of Vikas Dubey's money.
