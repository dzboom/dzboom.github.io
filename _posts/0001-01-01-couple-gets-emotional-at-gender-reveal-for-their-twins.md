This couple decided to do a gender reveal for their twins uniquely during the quarantine. Both the future parents got blindfolded as a woman put fabric paint on their hands. Each parent's hand represented a baby off the twins. The couple printed the impression of their hands on the expecting mother's white dress by placing their hands on her belly. Both the parents seemed ecstatic on taking their blindfolds off and discovering they were going to have two boys. They teared up as they got emotional and couldn't contain their excitement.
