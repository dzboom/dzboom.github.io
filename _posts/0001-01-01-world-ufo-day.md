Every year we celebrate World UFO Day for creating awareness among people about unidentified flying objects. The World UFO Day is celebrated by a few people on June 24 and some other celebrate it on July 2. World UFO Day is celebrated on June 24 as on that date it was reported that aviator Kenneth Arnold had seen a UFO in the United States of America. Whereas July 2 is celebrated as World UFO Day as on July 2, 1947, a UFO had supposedly crashed in Roswell, New Mexico.
