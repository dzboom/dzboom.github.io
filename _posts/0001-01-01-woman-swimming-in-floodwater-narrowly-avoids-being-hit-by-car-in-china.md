A woman swimming in floodwater narrowly avoided being hit by a car in central China.  <br>In the video, shot in the city of Xinxiang in Henan Province on August 4, a woman with equipment swam in the floodwater on a road. However, a car suddenly drove through the area.  <br>Nearby people were heard shouting to notice the car driver: &quot;There is someone in the floodwater!&quot;  <br>Fortunately, the wave pushed the woman away from the front of the car.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
