On this day, Australia captain Aaron Finch slammed 172 off 76 balls in the T20 international match against Zimbabwe at the Harare Sports Club. Finch, whose surpassed his previous best of 156 against England in 2013, smashed 172 off 76 balls before being hit wicket off the bowling of Blessing Muzarabani. His knock included a mind-boggling 16 fours and 10 sixes as the hard-hitting opener scored at a strike rate of 226.32.
