High-profile celebrities such as Kanye West and Britney Spears have been in the news lately, with much speculation about their mental health.  <br>According to HuffPost, while it's natural to be fascinated by unusual or outrageous behavior, it's important to not let mental illness be a subject of ridicule.  <br>Whether you're aware of it or not, mental health issues are invisible illnesses that can affect anyone, including friends, family, and Instagram followers.  <br>So if you're going to comment, make comments like someone you love will read it.  <br>Better yet, positively contribute to the conversation by sharing mental health resources or stories about your own experiences.  <br>Avoid placing blame. Many have blamed Kanye's wife, Kim Kardashian West, for not controlling her husband. Others have blamed Kanye.  <br>But mental illness is nobody’s fault. Likewise, no one can control another human being.  <br>And if these recommendations are all too much? Just consider not posting. Sometimes, silence really is golden.
