Recent polls reveal that Joe Biden maintains his grip on the 2020 race for president.  <br>CNN reports Biden's up 52% to 42% over President Donald Trump among likely voters nationally.  <br>Biden has a 50% to 44% edge over Trump in the key battleground state of Wisconsin as well.  <br>Biden's point advantages are the exact same they were before the party conventions.  <br>The polls are reflective of a race that barely budges even after two conventions, protests and unrest.  <br>With it being such a tight race on both sides, the pressure to vote has never been higher.
