Prime Minister Narendra Modi suddenly reached Leh on Friday morning amidst the ongoing tension at the border with China. His visit comes after a violent clash of Indian soldiers with Chinese soldiers in the Galvan Valley of eastern Ladakh. 20 Indian soldiers lost their lives in this violent clash on the night of 15 June.
