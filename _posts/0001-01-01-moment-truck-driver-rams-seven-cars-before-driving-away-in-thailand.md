This is the shocking moment a truck driver rammed SEVEN cars in a queue of traffic.  <br>The reckless lorry carrying construction debris approached the row of vehicles waiting at a junction in Chonburi, eastern Thailand, this morning (July 8).  <br>However, the driver appeared to be in a hurry and bulldozed his way through the motorists - leaving a trail of destruction.  <br>Seven cars and pickup trucks were damaged in the chaos before the trucker drove away without even briefly stopping to look back.  <br>A shocked driver who recorded the incident said drivers gathered after the crash but nobody knew why the trucker stopped.   <br>She said: ''There was no reason for the crash. The driver accelerated through the traffic. They didn't seem to care. They didn't even stop to look at the damage.   <br>''His brakes might have failed or there could have been a problem with the gears, but if that happened, they would have stopped.  <br>''Everybody who had their car damaged will have to arrange for the costs to be paid. The driver of the truck should take responsibility.''  <br>Police are now investigating the collision and examining CCTV from the area to try and trace the driver and the owner of the truck.
