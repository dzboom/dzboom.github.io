Plenty of people have either dialled down their daily makeup routine or have eliminated it altogether.  <br>After all, who's looking that closely if you don't have to go into work anymore, or have half your face covered with a mask for eight hours a day?  <br>But according to Allure, as communities begin to open up again, re-entry into full-blown makeup mode can throw your skin for a loop.  <br>Dermatologists say breakouts can be due to the skin being unused to foundation after such a long break from it.  <br>Another cause can be from wearing masks for hours at a time--and sweating profusely in it.  <br>Your best bet? Emphasize the eyes. Bright lipstick won't make your skin break out, either, but skip the foundation for now.
