Beijing urged Washington to immediately stop its planned arms sales to Taiwan and cease military contact with Taiwan, foreign ministry spokesman Zhao Lijian said at a press conference on August 17, 2020. Taiwan formally signed an agreement on August 15 to buy 66 F-16V fighter jets from the US. Beijing has firmly opposed the deal since Washington announced it in 2019.   Video source: CCTV
