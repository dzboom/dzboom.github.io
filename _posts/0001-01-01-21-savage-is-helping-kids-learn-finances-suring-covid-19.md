21 Savage is expanding his mission to help underserved children during the pandemic.  <br>As a direct response to the Covid-19 pandemic, the Grammy-award winning rapper made an announcement.  <br>He is launching a multi-tier expansion of his Juma Bank Account Financial Literacy program.  <br>The program offers financial resources nationwide, according to CNN.  <br>He is also making a $25,000 donation to the City of Atlanta's #ATLStrong Covid-19 relief fund.  <br>He is then launching Bank Account At Home, a free online financial literacy resource.  <br>through a partnership with mobile banking services provider Chime and education technology platform, EVERFI.
