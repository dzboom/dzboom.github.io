Union Minister Gajendra Singh Shekhawat is now seen getting caught in another trouble after the allegations of Rajasthan toppling the government. The ADJ Court of Jaipur has ordered an inquiry against Union Minister Gajendra Singh in another case. The case is of the Credit Co-operative Society scam. The SOG will interrogate Gajendra Singh Shekhawat, his wife and his aide.
