A statue commissioned by the Duke of Cambridge and the Duke of Sussex to pay tribute to their mother, Diana, Princess of Wales, will be installed on what would have been her 60th birthday.  <br>The statue was commissioned to mark the 20th anniversary of Diana’s death and to recognise her positive impact in the UK and around the world.
