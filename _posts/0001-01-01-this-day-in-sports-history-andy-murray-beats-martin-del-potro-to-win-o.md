On This Day in Sports History, Britain’s Andy Murray defended his Olympic men’s singles title with a mammoth four-set win over Juan Martin del Potro. Murray needed just over four hours to win 7-5 4-6 6-2 7-5 and secure Team GB's fifth gold medal of the day and 15th of a brilliant Olympic Games in Rio.
