The candidates reached to their respective exam centers with mask on amid COVID-19 pandemic.  <br>Precautionary measures were taken at the examination centers due to coronavirus.  <br>Candidates were allowed in the exam halls with valid admit cards. Engineering entrance exam, JEE-Main, scheduled to be held from Sept 1 to 6.
