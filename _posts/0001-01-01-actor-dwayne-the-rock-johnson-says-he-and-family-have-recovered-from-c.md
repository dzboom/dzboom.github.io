Pro-wrestler turned Hollywood actor Dwayne &quot;The Rock&quot; Johnson said in a video message posted on social media on Wednesday that he, his wife and their two young children tested positive for COVID-19 in recent weeks but that they all have recovered and are healthy.  <br>Johnson, 48, said in the Instagram post that he and his spouse, Lauren, 35, and their daughters, Jasmine, 4, and Tiana, 2, caught the virus about 2-1/2 weeks ago from &quot;very close family friends&quot; whom he said had no idea how they had become infected.
