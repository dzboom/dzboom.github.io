After much hue and cry and attempts to get the exam postponed, the NEET exam was finally held today for over 15 lakh undergraduate medical aspirants. Candidates who qualify NEET 2020 exam, meeting the required cutoff, will be eligible to appear in counselling and admission processes further. NEET exam was held following strict health protocols in the midst of COVID-19 pandemic. Candidates were asked to follow NEET dress code and declare their health status on the NEET self declaration.
