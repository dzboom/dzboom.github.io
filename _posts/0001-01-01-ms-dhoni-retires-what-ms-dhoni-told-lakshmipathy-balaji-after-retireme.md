CSK bowling coach Lakshmipathy Balaji called MS Dhoni the man whose leadership changed the perception of leadership among all captains. Speaking on Star Sports Tamil Cricket Connected, Balaji recalled the immediate moments after Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on August 15.
