Rhea Chakraborty, who has been named in the FIR in the death case of Sushant Singh Rajput, has been issued an official summon by the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) in the drugs conspiracy. A team of NCB officials and Mumbai Police officers reached the actress's home on Sunday morning. On Friday, Rhea's brother Showik Chakraborty was arrested in connection with a case concerning procurement and consumption of illegal drugs.
