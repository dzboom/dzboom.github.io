In this flashback video, watch double celebrations as it is success party of film 'Raja Babu' on completion of 100 days and the launching of 'Joru Ka Ghulam'. Both the films are David Dhawan's directorial and stars Govinda. Raja Babu is an action comedy film staring Govinda, Karisma Kapoor, Shakti Kapoor, Kader Khan, Aruna Irani, Prem Chopra and Gulshan Grover. It was released in 1994 and became popular among the masses. On the other hand, Joru Ka Ghulam is a comedy film starring Govinda, Twinkle Khanna, Kader Khan and Johny Lever. This film was released in the year 2000.
