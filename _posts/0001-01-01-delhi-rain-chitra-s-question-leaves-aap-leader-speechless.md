Within three hours of heavy rainfall in Delhi, a portion of a road in the slum area of Anna Nagar near ITO caved in, washing away a house along with it. Heavy waterlogging was also witnessed in several areas of Delhi after the downpour on Sunday. A person died after getting stuck under a waterlogged bridge at Minto Road. While having a discussion over this issue on Aaj Tak special show Dangal, anchor Chitra Tripathi asked some tough questions from the AAP spokesperson. Watch video for more.
