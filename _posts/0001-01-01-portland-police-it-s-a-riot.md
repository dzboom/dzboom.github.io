In Portland, Oregon, Sunday morning, mass demonstrations were officially classified as riots.  <br>Newser reports the designation came when protesters breached a fence surrounding the city's downtown federal courthouse, where US agents have been stationed.  <br>In a tweet, local police said the violent conduct of demonstrators has created a 'grave risk of public alarm.'  <br>Protesters paused outside a hotel where federal agents are staying, chanting “Feds go home” and yelling the names of African Americans killed by police.  <br>Police demanded people leave the area, and threatened that those who fail to do so may be arrested or subject to teargas and impact weapons.
