Sachin Tendulkar said James Anderson is the only bowler who could bowl a &quot;reverse outswinger&quot; with a wrist position of a conventional inswinger. Tendulkar has termed that 'reverse' reverse swing. Speaking to West Indies legend Brian Lara on his '100MB' app, Tendulkar explained why Anderson could be vital for England in the ongoing Test against West Indies at the Ageas Bowl.
