India has reached another grim milestone as it battles the Coronavirus Pandemic. India has surpassed Brazil to take the second spot in the list of the nations worst-hit by the coronavirus pandemic after a record spike of 90,802 fresh cases in the last 24 hours taking the country's tally past 42 lakh. This is the second consecutive day that the country registered over 90,000 new infections in 24 hours.While the Covid death toll has mounted to 71,642. About 32.5 lakh Covid patients in the country have recovered so far with over 69,000 recoveries recorded in the last 24 hours.
