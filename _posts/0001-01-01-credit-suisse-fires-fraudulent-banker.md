Credit Suisse has fired a fraudulent banker.  <br>The bank found the banker drove a loss of roughly $11 million after forging documentation for an African wealth-management client.  <br>The bank has since dismissed the Zurich-based banker.  <br>Sources tell Bloomberg that Credit Suisse reimbursed affected clients.  <br>The bank told Business Insider they &quot;took appropriate legal measures and informed the affected clients and relevant regulators.&quot;
