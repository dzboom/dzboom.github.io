Actress Naya Rivera is missing.  <br>The 33-year-old &quot;Glee&quot; actress went missing while boating with her son on a lake in Southern California.  <br>Rescuers plan to resume their search Thursday morning at Lake Piru in Ventura County.  <br>The local Sheriff's Office said the search will resume &quot;at first light&quot; and fear it could be a possible drowning.  <br>Officials say a young child was found alone on the boat Rivera had rented.  <br>The child had on a life vest and an adult life vest was found on the boat.
