Delhi Health Minister Satyendra Jain has said that the sero surveillance will be done from the 1st till 5th of every month. Delhi’s sero-surveillance has revealed that 22.86% of the people have been affected by Covid-19 in the city. The survey was conducted from June 27 to July 10 by the National Centre for Disease Control in collaboration with Delhi government.
