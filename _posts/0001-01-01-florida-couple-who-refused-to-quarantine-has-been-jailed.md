Two Florida Keys residents ignored orders to quarantine after testing positive for COVID-19.  <br>Now, they have ended up in jail as of Wednesday night, reports Newser.  <br>Jose Freire Interian, 24, and Yohana Gonzalez, 26, were charged with violating isolation rules.  <br>Quarantine and violating emergency management disaster preparedness rules were in place.  <br>A Monroe County Sheriff’s Office spokesman says Interian and Gonzalez were kept isolated.  <br>They weren't near other inmates in &quot;negative pressure&quot; rooms that did not allow air to escape.
