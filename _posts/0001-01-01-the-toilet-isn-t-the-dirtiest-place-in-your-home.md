Tens of thousands of species of bacteria, bug, and fungus live in your house. Although you might think your toilet would be the dirtiest place of all, it's actually your own body. But being a microbe metropolis is a good thing. Because the more biodiversity in your body, the harder it is for the few dangerous species to get a foothold and make you sick.
