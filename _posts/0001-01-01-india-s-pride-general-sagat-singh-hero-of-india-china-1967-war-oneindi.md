The general of India-China War Hero, Sagat Singh was a very passionate person. When China waged war against India in 1967, at that time India had very limited resources to fight the war, but there was no dearth of enthusiasm of our jawans. General Sagat Singh was one of the heroes of the 1967 victory in the India-China war. In the Indo-China war at Nathu La near Sikkim more than 300 soldiers of China were killed while 65 soldiers of India were martyred. India won the war in the 1967 war. The Chinese army was advancing by firing, in that era, the Prime Minister had the right to order firing .
