Honda Motorcycles &amp; Scooter India has launched the BS6-compliant X-Blade in the Indian market. The Honda X-Blade BS6 motorcycle is offered in two variants: one that features a disc brake at the front, and the other the features disc brakes at the front and at the rear.   <br>The new Honda X-Blade BS6 motorcycle is available at a starting price of Rs 1.05 lakh, ex-showroom (Delhi). Bookings for the new motorcycle are now open across all company dealerships in the country, with deliveries set to begin soon as well.  <br>Watch the video to know more about the Honda X-Blade 160 BS6 model.  <br>
