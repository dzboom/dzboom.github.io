Punjab Health Minister Balbir Singh Sidhu on July 21 flagged off 77 advanced life support ambulances. Ambulances are equipped with state-of-the-art equipments including life support ventilators. Speaking to ANI, he said, &quot;We are going to increase our COVID-19 testing capacity to 20,000 per day.&quot;
