Two rebel Congress MLAs Aditi Singh and Rakesh Singh have got a big relief ... The Congress party had given a petition to dismiss the assembly membership of both the MLAs, which was rejected by the Speaker Hriday Narayan Singh. The Speaker has dismissed this petition for lack of evidence ... This decision is being considered a big setback for the Congress. According to the information received from sources, the Congress can move the High Court against this decision.
