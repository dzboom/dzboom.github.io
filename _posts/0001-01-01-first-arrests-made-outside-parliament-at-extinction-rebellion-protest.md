The first of many anticipated arrests took place as Extinction Rebellion's two-week protest kicked off on Tuesday (September 1).  <br>Protesters were arrested for blocking the roads around Parliament Square, which the Met Police had stated they could not do.  <br>Several activists were seen being carried away by police officers as fellow protesters cheered for the arrestees.  <br>Elsewhere, smoke bombs were released among a dancing crowd of demonstrators, sending colourful clouds into the central London streets.
