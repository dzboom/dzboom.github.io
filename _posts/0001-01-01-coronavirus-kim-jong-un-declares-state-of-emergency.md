North Korea has declared a state of emergency after Kim Jong-un chaired an emergency politburo meeting about a suspected case of Covid-19.   <br>State media said the person defected to South Korea three years ago, before illegally crossing back into the North in the last few days.   <br>After tests, the individual is “suspected to have been infected with the vicious virus.”   <br>The North Korean leader has also imposed a lockdown on Kaesong City, on the border with South Korea.   <br>If confirmed, this would be the first official case of infection acknowledged by North Korean authorities.   <br>So far the country has reported zero cases of infection, claiming it has taken strict measures to prevent the spread of the new coronavirus.
