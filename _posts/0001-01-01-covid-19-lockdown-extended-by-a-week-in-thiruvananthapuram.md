Lockdown in Thiruvananthapuram has been extended for a week to restrict Covid-19 spread. In order to ensure restrictions, police personnel in Thiruvananthapuram are in action. IDs of people are being checked as they move about for their daily chores. Triple-lockdown (more restrictions) has also been imposed in containment zones. The southern state has reported 6,534 confirmed cases so far.   <br>
