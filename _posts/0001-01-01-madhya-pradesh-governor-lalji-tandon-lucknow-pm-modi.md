Lalji Tandon, the governor of Madhya Pradesh and senior BJP leader, has passed away. His son Ashutosh confirmed this on Tuesday morning. Lalji Tandon was ill for several days and he was hospitalized for the last 40 days, that was the reason that the charge of the Governor of Madhya Pradesh was handed over to Anandiben Patel. Now many big leaders are paying tribute after his death, a three-day state mourning has been declared in UP after the demise of Lalji Tandon.
