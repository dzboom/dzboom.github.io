Serena Williams and her daughter Alexis Olympia Ohanian are part of a group that will own the newest soccer team in the U.S.  <br>According to CNN, Natalie Portman is also part of the majority woman founded group.  <br>The National Women's Soccer League announced that Los Angeles will be home to the franchise, The Angel City group.  <br>Portman said in a statement; &quot;I am thrilled by the opportunity to partner with this incredible group of people&quot;.  <br>Other investors include Kara Nortman, Julie Uhrman and Alexis Ohanian, Williams' husband.  <br>The franchise will join the league in the spring of 2022.
