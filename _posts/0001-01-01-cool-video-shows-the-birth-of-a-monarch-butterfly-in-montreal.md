This filmer in Montreal, Canada, shows the cool birth of a monarch butterfly that they hatched over the course of three months and filmed on August 13.  <br>Here's the story in their own words: &quot;I've been raising Monarch eggs into butterflies for the past 3 years. Each year Monarch Butterflies lay eggs in my garden but on the wrong plant called a Himalayan Balsam which is not a food source for Monarch caterpillars. I was forced to intervene or these Monarch Butterfly eggs would have died. Each year the Monarch Butterflies return knowing that I am able to ensure future generations' survival. Today at approximately 7:30 am was lucky enough to witness the birth of my first Monarch Caterpillar which was named after a 2 yr old boy named Harrison. The great thing is that I filmed it so that you too could enjoy this miracle.&quot;
