The country is celebrating the 74th anniversary of its independence. The zeal of Independence Day is high all over the country. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath hoisted the tricolor at the main gate of the UP Legislative Assembly and wished the people of the day of independence.
