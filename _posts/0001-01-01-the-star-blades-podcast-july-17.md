Chris Wilder is fuming, the Blades' hopes for European football hang in the balance and no one knows whether to be disappointed or grateful for the position in which United find themselves. So Blades writer James Shield and The Star's football editor Chris Holt join Liam Hoden to try to determine just that. The panel reflect on the highs of the win over Chelsea and the immediate lows of the defeat to Leicester City that had Wilder fuming. They talk the continued quest for Europe, the potential return of fans to stadiums in the near future and look ahead to the vital clash with Everton.
