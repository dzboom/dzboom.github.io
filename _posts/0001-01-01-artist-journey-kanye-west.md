Kanye West’s 1st tour was The School Spirit Tour. It hit up several college towns across the USA. Later that year he opened for Usher. They both had the biggest selling albums of 2004. Ye launched the Touch the Sky Tour. He was accompanied by a 6-piece string section. Kanye opened for U2 &amp; The Rolling Stones. These tours inspired him to create songs fit for arenas. In 2019, he began his Sunday Service performances. To date, Kanye West has performed over 450 concerts across 6 tours in over 25 countries.
