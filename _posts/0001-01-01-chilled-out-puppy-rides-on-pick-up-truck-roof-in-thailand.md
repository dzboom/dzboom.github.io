This is the bizarre moment a puppy balanced on a pickup truck's roof while it was driving on the road in Chonburi, eastern Thailand.  <br>Passing motorist, Namfon, recorded the two pet pooches riding on the truck appreciating the scenery without being scared of falling off on July 16.  <br>A fluffy Husky was seen in the back looking out of the vehicle and letting one of its paws feel the wind.  <br>Namfon said the two mutts are quite well known among locals. She said: &quot;People in the area usually see them travelling around with the owner. They have learned how to sit well on the truck.&quot;
