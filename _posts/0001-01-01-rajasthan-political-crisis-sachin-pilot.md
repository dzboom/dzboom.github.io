In Rajasthan, Ashok Gehlot's Congress government is facing a cloud of crisis. Rajasthan Deputy Chief Minister Sachin Pilot has taken a rebel stance ... and speculation about his joining the BJP is intensifying. Meanwhile, the Congress has tightened its stance and issued a whip to the legislators for the meeting to be held today. Meanwhile, there are reports that Sachin Pilot has now made up his mind not to convince the Congress. According to the reports, the pilots are not coming to the meeting called by Gehlot today even after the release of the opposition.
