A truck-mounted crane flipped over while it was loading materials from a building site in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.  <br>The vehicle was collecting debris but was overloaded by the weight. Footage shows how the upended vehicle blocked the busy road as locals gathered round to witness the chaos.  <br>The driver emerged unhurt from the wreckage and the crashed vehicle was removed later that evening on July 10.
