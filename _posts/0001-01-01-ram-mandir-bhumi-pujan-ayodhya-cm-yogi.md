Preparations have been made for Bhoomipujan of Ram Janmabhoomi temple in Ayodhya. This time the Bhoomi Poojan ceremony of Ram temple will be grand as well as special attention is also being given to other aspects related to this program. Only two days are left in the Bhoomi Poojan of Ram temple. CM Yogi Adityanath reached Ayodhya on Monday to take stock of the preparations for Bhoomi Pujan.
