Tips for Managing   <br>Your Life Transitions.  <br>These suggestions can help you   <br>through the process of a life transition.  <br>Focus on the aspect   <br>of transition in which   <br>you thrive.  <br>Experts say there are three aspects of transition — &quot;the long goodbye,&quot; &quot;the messy middle&quot; and &quot;the new beginning.&quot; A person is usually better at one transition aspect, so focus on that one.  <br>Be clear about your emotions   <br>and act on them.  <br>Big changes can be scary.   <br>Identifying your fear can help   <br>you process it and other emotions.  <br>Let some things go.  <br>By letting go of aspects of your   <br>old habits or personality, you make space   <br>for new outlooks and ways of doing things.  <br>Get creative.  <br>From cooking to painting,   <br>creativity can help with   <br>feeling grounded when things get chaotic.  <br>Give your   <br>life story a revision.  <br>Transitions are periods in which   <br>old behaviors and fears can be healed.   <br>Remember that you are in control   <br>of the story you tell about your life.
