The Central Crime Branch is conducting raids at Kannada actress Ragini Dwivedi's house in connection to the ongoing Sandalwood drug racket case. The raids began at 6.30 am today with six male cops and a female cop. Ragini was summoned by the CCB on September 3, but the actress had sent her advocates to appear before the officers. In an Instagram post, she also wrote that she received the summon at a short notice, which is why she couldn't appear.
