Anti-government and &quot;anti-maskers&quot; congregate at Marble Arch in London to protest against the government and the new coronavirus rules.  <br>The clip filmed today (July 19), shows the two female speakers talking to the crowd.   <br>One of the speakers, Jacqueline Dunn, claimed the government was a form of religion, and a lot of people will only listen to the government's rules and regulations because 'they like to follow orders'.  <br>The anti-mask movement views masks as 'ineffective, and believe they are 'an unjustified assault on people's personal freedom'.
