The team at Cannon Hall Farm has been praised by Prime Minister Boris Johnson in recent weeks for its part in helping to keep the nation’s spirits up during lockdown - with 132 consecutive daily broadcasts.  <br>And now brothers Robert and David Nicholson have signed off with a special rendition of a piece of music many will recognise from The Last of the Summer Wine, led by one of their farmers, John Hopkinson.
