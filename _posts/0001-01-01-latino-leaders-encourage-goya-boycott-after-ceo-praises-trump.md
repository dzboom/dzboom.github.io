According to CNN Business the hashtag #Goyaway was trending on social media on  Friday.  <br>This comes after the CEO of Goya Foods, Robert Unanue, appeared in the White House Rose Garden on Thursday, and praised President Donald Trump.   <br>Unanue said, &quot;We are all truly blessed ... to have a leader like President Trump who is a builder.&quot;   <br>Trump, who CNN says has called Mexicans &quot;rapists&quot; and accused them of bringing drugs into the U.S., is not popular among most Hispanic Americans, according to   <br>a recent New York Times/Siena College poll.  <br>It suggests that Latino Americans favor VP Joe Biden over Trump for president by  36 percentage-points.   <br>The comments from Goya's CEO angered some prominent Hispanic leaders, including Congresswoman Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez.  <br>She hinted in a tweet that she'll boycott Goya. She tweeted, &quot;Oh look, it’s the sound of me Googling 'how to make your own Adobo'&quot;  <br>Former Housing and Urban Development Secretary Julián Castro acknowledged that Goya is a staple in many Latino homes, but said, &quot;their CEO, Bob Unanue, is praising a president who villainizes and maliciously attacks Latinos for political gain. Americans should think twice before buying their products. #Goyaway.&quot;
