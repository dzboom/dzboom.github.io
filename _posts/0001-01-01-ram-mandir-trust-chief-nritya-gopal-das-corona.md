Prime Minister Narendra Modi performed the Bhoomipudan of Ram temple in Ayodhya on August 5 and laid the foundation stone. During that time, PM Modi, CM Yogi Adityanath, Sangh chief Mohan Bhagwat, Governor Anandiben Patel and Ram Janmabhoomi Tirtha Kshetra Mahant Nritya Gopal Das were seated on the stage. A week after the event, Mahant Nritya Gopal Das has been found corona positive. Surprisingly, when Mahant Nritya Gopal Das shared the stage with PM Modi, he did not put on a mask. Not only this, social distancing was not followed on the stage.
