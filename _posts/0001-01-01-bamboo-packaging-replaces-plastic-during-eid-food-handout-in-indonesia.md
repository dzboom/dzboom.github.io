The Qurban committee at Masjid Al-Munawwarah mosque in Padang, Indonesia has replaced the plastic wrapping for their Eid food handout with a more sustainable bamboo alternative.   <br>Footage from Sunday (August 2) shows meat packaged in bamboo baskets being delivered to the homes of local residents.  <br>Deputy Chairperson of Masjid Al-Munawarah, Amoura Lubis, said: &quot;The replacement of plastic containers is due to the dangers of plastic for human health, the environment and because plastic cannot be destroyed for hundreds of years.&quot;  <br>Lubis added: &quot;Every Eid al-Adha, there is lots of plastic garbage because in Indonesia there is a habit of using plastic.  We are trying to change the habit&quot;.  <br>The Qurban committee prepared 800 bamboo baskets, woven by craftsmen in Padang City, for the meat of 20 cows and three goats.
