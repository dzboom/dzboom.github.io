On Monday, House Speaker Nancy Pelosi got her hair styled inside a San Francisco hair salon.  <br>CNN reports that this visit was a violation of the city's Covid-19 safety regulations that enraged the salon's owner.  <br>The owner of the salon, Erica Kious, was angry that Pelosi had broken the rules requiring such hair treatments to be done outdoors because of the pandemic.  <br>Fox News obtained security footage showing the speaker inside eSalon not wearing a mask.  <br>A Pelosi staffer said the speaker relied on salon employees to inform her of the regulations.  <br>Kious said Pelosi's visit was &quot;a slap in the face that she went in.&quot;  <br>&quot;She feels that she can just go and get her stuff done while no one else can go in, and I can't work.&quot;
