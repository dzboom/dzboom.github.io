HuffPost reports a woman who got a little too comfortable with a herd of bison got viciously attacked by one of them.  <br>A group of motorcyclists was riding through South Dakota’s Custer State Park on Wednesday.  <br>However, the group was interrupted by a large herd of bison crossing the road.  <br>Footage posted to social media shows the woman seeming confident with her close proximity to the wild animals.  <br>She continued to take pictures until a cow bison came from across the road and charged her.  <br>It hooked her belt with its horns and swung her around. Her pants came off and she was knocked unconscious from the fall. She was not seriously injured.
