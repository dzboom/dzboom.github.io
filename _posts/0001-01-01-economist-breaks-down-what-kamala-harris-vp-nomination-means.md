On Tuesday, Kamala Harris was announced as Joe Biden's Vice President for the Democratic Party ticket.  <br>How will that pick effect the economy?  <br>According to Allison Schrager, a senior fellow at the Manhattan Institute, the markets seemed comfortable with Harris on the ballot.  <br>Last summer Harris proposed a tax on stock and bond trades.  <br>However, Schrager does not expect Biden and Harris to make that change if they're elected.  <br>She added that Biden and Harris &quot;aren't particularly risk-takers as candidates.&quot;  <br>Schrager said the nation could expect higher income taxes and corporate taxes under a Biden-Harris administration.
