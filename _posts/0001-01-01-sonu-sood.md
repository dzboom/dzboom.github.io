Actor Sonu Sood provided a tractor to a farmer in Andhra Pradesh within hours on Sunday, doing another heart-winning act. As soon as Sood came to know that the farmer is using his daughters for plowing due to not being able to rent the bull, he immediately helped the farmer family. A video of plowing the field by placing a plow on the shoulders of farmers' daughters became viral on social media.
