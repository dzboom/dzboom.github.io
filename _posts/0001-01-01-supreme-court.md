The constitutional validity of the contempt law is challenged in the Supreme Court. Former Union Minister Arun Shourie, lawyer Prashant Bhushan and senior journalist N. Ram has challenged the validity of the Context of Court Act by filing an application in the Supreme Court. The petitioner has said in the application filed in the Supreme Court that the fundamental right of the Constitution is against the freedom of expression.
