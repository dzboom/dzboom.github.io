When Maharashtra government allowed TV serials to resume shooting under the strict guidelines, actors and crew members were happy to be back on sets. However, from the past few days, we are hearing that despite taking all the precautionary measures some crew members of Ek Mahanayak Dr. BR Ambedkar and Mere Sai have been tested positive for Coronavirus. Now it has been reported that one case has emerged on the sets of Bhabhiji Ghar Par Hain and its Saumya Tandon's personal hairdresser. Following the reports, Saumya has been advised to stay away from the shoot as makers want her to take care of her health first. However other cast and crew members are allowed to shoot in her absence. Producer of the show Benaifer Kohli visited the sets to ensure the proper measures are being taken. Meanwhile, the cases of Coronavirus in Maharashtra are rising rapidly and due to which the states remains the worst affected state in the country.
