Kia Sonet SUV is one of the much-awaited models in the Indian market at the moment. Slated to launch in the coming weeks, Kia Motors India has announced the roll-out of the first SUV from their production-line in their Anantapur facility in Andhra Pradesh.   <br>The Kia Sonet will be positioned as the brand’s newest entry-level offering and is said to be priced in the Rs 8 - 12 lakh range, ex-showroom. Bookings for the SUV are already open.
