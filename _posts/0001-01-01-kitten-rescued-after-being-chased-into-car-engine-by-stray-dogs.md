A kitten was rescued after being chased into a car engine by stray dogs in Chonburi, eastern Thailand.  <br>Local teacher Wallapa Marod, 32, drove her car to school but had to stop after she heard a meow sound.  <br>She returned home and checked in the car engine where she found the kitten stuck inside so she called the rescue service for help.  <br>Footage shows the team opening the hood where they found the kitten hiding inside before they grabbed it and slowly pulled it out of the engine.  <br>The rescuer said the kitten is still small and it helps the catching to be easier.   <br>He said: &quot;The kitten was scared and tried to escape but luckily that it was a small kitten so I could grab it easily.&quot;  <br>The teacher, Wallapa, said the kitten might have been chased by a dog but she will adopt and care for it.   <br>She said: &quot;There are many stray dogs in my house area and they are sometimes fierce.  <br>&quot;The kitten might have been frightened and tries to escape into the car engine but became stuck, so I think I will adopt it to protect it from the dogs.&quot;
