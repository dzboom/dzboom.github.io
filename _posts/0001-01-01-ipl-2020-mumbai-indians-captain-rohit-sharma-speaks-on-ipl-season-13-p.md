MS Dhoni is undeniably the most popular captain in the IPL, Rohit has proved his leadership skills by guiding Mumbai Indians to four titles, the last one coming against Dhoni’s men in IPL 2019. Speaking to PTI, Rohit shared his thoughts on leadership and said as per his own theory of captaincy, he was the least important person in the team while leading the squad. Rohit Says, &quot;It will be a bit challenging. Unless I play, I will not know where I am and how I feel but body is completely fine.
