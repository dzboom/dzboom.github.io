Former White House chief of staff Mick Mulvaney plans to launch a hedge fund focused on financial-sector stocks.  <br>The firm is named Exegis Capital.  <br>Mulvaney talked about the fund on S&amp;P Global's &quot;Street Talk&quot; podcast.  <br>He said the fund will leverage Mulvaney's experience in government to find opportunities linked to legislation and regulation.  <br>He added that investors &quot;like to think the market moves on fundamentals&quot; but policy out of Washington plays an increasingly important role.  <br>Mulvaney is now serving as special envoy to Northern Ireland.  <br>He aims to open the fund on January 1 with investing veteran Andrew Wessel.
