Election Day is just 7 weeks away and the deadline to register to vote is rapidly approaching in most states.  <br>The quickest way to register to vote online is to go to your state’s official elections website.  <br>Gizmodo reports that an even easier way to register is to head over to Vote.gov.  <br>You simply choose your state from a drop-down menu and Vote.gov does the rest.  <br>The website tells you everything you need to perform your civic duty, including how to register and where.  <br>Election day is Tuesday, November 3rd, 2020, and the window to register to vote is closing, so check out Vote.gov.
