A seemingly non-aggressive Black man was arrested and swarmed by five officers at Minneapolis-Saint Paul Airport over a ticket counter dispute.  <br>Footage from July 17 shows the initial encounter and subsequent arrest.  <br>The dispute occurred due to man's ticket not showing up on the airline's system causing him some annoyance.  <br>The situation escalated when the arresting officer - with no explanation - arrested the man, who was then surrounded by four other members of the police and escorted from the terminal.  <br>The filmer explained: &quot;The man was not aggressive, threatening, or harmful in any way, simply frustrated.   <br>&quot;The situation is then escalated by police as the man is aggressively swarmed by five officers and put under arrest for no logical reason.   <br>&quot;During the arrest, the innocent man never resists and remains calm.  <br>&quot;The mood for onlookers was frightening as the police presence grew, and brought with it a reminder of what happened to George Floyd and many other innocent peoples due to systemic racism.&quot;  <br>They later added: &quot;I got the airport and saw there were police at the door, which I thought was odd.   <br>&quot;I got to the desk and the African-American gentleman starts telling me he doesn't understand why the clerk isn't accepting his ticket.   <br>&quot;He seems more confused/mildly annoyed than anything else.    <br>&quot;Then the police walked up and I started filming, I don't know what happened after he was arrested.&quot;
