The lunar eclipse is observed on the day of Guru Purnima on 5 July. The lunar eclipse will start at 8.38 am. It will be in Paramgrass at 09.59 minutes and will end at 11:21. Thus the duration of lunar eclipse will be 2 hours 43 minutes and 24 seconds. This lunar will not be visible in India, but will be seen in Australia, Europe, Africa and parts of Asia. Due to not being visible in India, the Sutak period of eclipse here will also not be valid. The eclipse will not have an inauspicious effect here.
