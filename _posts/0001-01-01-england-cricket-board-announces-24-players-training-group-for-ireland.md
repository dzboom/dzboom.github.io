England have named nine uncapped players in a 24-man training group for their three-match one-day series against Ireland in Southampton. Paul Collingwood will be in charge for the series with head coach Chris Silverwood focusing on Test cricket. England will play two intra-group warm-up matches before announcing their final squad for the series, which begins on 30 July.
