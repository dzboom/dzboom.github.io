Political riots continue in Rajasthan. In the midst of this riot, Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot again held a cabinet meeting on Tuesday, in which the proposal to call the assembly session was passed. This is the third time that the cabinet has passed the proposal for calling the assembly session and has been sent to the governor. It has been said in the meeting that the Governor has to accept the advice of the Cabinet, otherwise there will be a constitutional crisis. The meeting is over at the moment.
