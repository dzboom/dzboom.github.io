A golden retriever stopped a cat after the feline fell from a building and knocked over its owner in northern China.  <br>The video, shot in the city of Harbin in Heilongjiang Province on July 12, shows a cat suddenly falling down from the six-floor of a residential building, strike an elderly man walking his dog under it.  <br>After the golden retriever saw its owner lying on the ground not moving,  it spotted the cat and stopped the feline from running away.  <br>A passerby called the police and the elderly man was sent to hospital for treatment. He had a cervical spinal cord injury.  <br>The old man was discharged from hospital and recovering at home, while he is going to take legal action to get compensation from the cat's owner, reports said.  <br>The video was provided by local media with permission.
