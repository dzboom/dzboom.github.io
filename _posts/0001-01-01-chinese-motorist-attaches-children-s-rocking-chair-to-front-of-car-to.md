A motorist attached a children's rocking chair to the front of their car to block the licence plate as he tried to avoid being caught by the traffic enforcement camera.  <br>The video, filmed in the city of Shanghai on August 1, shows a motorist grabbing a children's rocking chair from the roadside and hanging it to the front of his car before driving away.  <br>In another clip, the children's rocking chair was seen left in the middle of a road and a police officer went to take it away.  <br>Police summoned the motorist named Liu to the station for investigation.  <br>Liu finally admitted that he meant to block his licence plate to avoid being caught by the traffic enforcement camera.  <br>Police fined him and deducted the points from his licence.
