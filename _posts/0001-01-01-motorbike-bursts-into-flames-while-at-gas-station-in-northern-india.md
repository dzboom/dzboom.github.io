CCTV footage captured the dramatic moment a motorcycle burst into flames while filling up with petrol in Chirawa, Rajasthan.  <br>Footage from July 24 shows the incident unfold as the rider and his pillion passenger sit on the bike while one attendant fills up the vehicle with fuel.  <br>Out of nowhere, flames erupt around the fuel tank and quickly engulf the bike and the rider, passenger and attendant run for safety as other onlookers leave the scene.  <br>Subsequently, another attendant arrives with a fire extinguisher and houses the flames in foam.  <br>Local reports suggest nobody was seriously injured.
