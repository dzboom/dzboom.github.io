The controversy that started between Bollywood actress Kangana Ranaut and Sanjay Raut has now reached the breakdown of Kangana's office. On Wednesday, the BMC placed a notice to break the illegal construction at the office of Kangana Ranaut and immediately after that the work of breaking the illegal construction in his office has started.
