Firefighters have rescued a horse left stranded inside a well in Karaikal, Puducherry, India on July 22.  <br>According to reports, the thirsty animal fell into the well while trying to drink water from it.  <br>Residents spotted the animal and alerted the fire department officials who rushed to the spot to rescue the horse.  <br>The video shows a group of firefighters trying to rescue the horse from the well.  <br>The crew first drained the water out of the well to keep the horse afloat. They then used ropes and a fire hose to support the animal from the bottom and hauled it out of the well.  <br>After several attempts, they managed to rescue the animal.
