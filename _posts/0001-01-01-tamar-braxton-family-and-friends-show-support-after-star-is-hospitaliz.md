Family and friends are showing support for Tamar Braxton after she was rushed to the hospital after being found unconscious in a room at the Ritz Carlton Residences in Los Angeles. Los Angeles Police confirmed that they responded to a call regarding a “possible overdose” by a woman on July 16. Braxton was reported to be in stable condition the next day.
