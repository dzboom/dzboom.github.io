The Wuhan city in China, where the deadly coronavirus first originated is now facing massive floods. Till Saturday, eight people have lost their lives to the floods. Rainstorm warnings have been issued for the next 31 days, according to reports. The residents of Wuhan have been asked to stay indoors again. Watch video.
