In the remote village Sirwadi in Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand late on Sunday, cloudburst caused havoc in the village. If debris entered the houses of many people, the field-barn and footpaths were completely destroyed by the debris. After receiving the information of the incident, the administrative officials have left for the spot.
