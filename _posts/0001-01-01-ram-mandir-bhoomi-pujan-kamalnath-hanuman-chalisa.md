Preparations for the Bhoomi Pujan of Ram temple in Ayodhya have almost been completed. The day of August 5 is going to be historic, so special preparations have been made for this. Ayodhya has been decorated as well as a Diwali-like atmosphere has been created. At the same time, before the Bhoomi Poojan, former Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh Kamal Nath has become a saffron. And at home he is reciting Hanuman Chalisa.
