On Tuesday, The Trump Administration said it will not work with an international cooperative effort to develop and distribute a COVID-19 vaccine.  <br>Why?  <br>They do not want to be constrained by multilateral groups like the World Health Organization.  <br>The decision to go it alone was first reported by The Washington Post.  <br>In July the White House pulled the United States out of the WHO.  <br>Trump claims the WHO is in need of reform and is heavily influenced by China.  <br>More than 150 countries are setting up the COVID-19 Vaccines Global Access Facility, or COVAX.
