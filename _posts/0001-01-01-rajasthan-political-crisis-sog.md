Today is the 26th day of political upheaval in Rajasthan. A big news has come to light in the case related to horse trading of MLAs, amid the murmur of the return of the rebel MLAs from their camp, including Sachin Pilot. The Special Operations Group has transferred the case related to horse trading of horsemen to the Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB).
