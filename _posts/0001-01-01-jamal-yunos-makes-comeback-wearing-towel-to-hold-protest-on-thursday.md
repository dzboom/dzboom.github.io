Sungai Besar Umno division chief Jamal Yunos, known for his media stunts, appears to be planning a comeback with his latest protest set to take place on Thursday.  <br>Appearing on a video, wearing a towel, sitting next to a river, the Umno leader called on people to gather outside the  Selangor state secretariat (SUK) building wearing towels to protest against the latest water disruptions that affected close to 1.2 million people in the Klang Valley.
