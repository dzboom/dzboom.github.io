ICC has introduced some new rules as cricket is on the verge of resumption. They have implemented some measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19. West Indies will play their first test starting on 8th July at Hampshire’s Ageas Bowl. Both the second and third matches of the series will be played at Emirates Old Trafford, Manchester starting on 16th July and 24th July. All the matches will be played behind closed doors.
