BJP workers have launched a protest against the death of Bengal BJP MLA Debendra Nath Roy who was found hanging at a shop near his home on Monday. In North Bengal, protesters were seen vandalising buses as police dragged them away. BJP workers have started demonstrations on roads in parts of North Bengal as the party called for a 12-hour bandh in the region over the death of the party MLA. Watch video for more.
