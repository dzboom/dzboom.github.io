A female PCS officer posted in Uttar Pradesh's Ballia committed suicide by hanging herself last night. Police have also recovered a suicide note from the spot in which the woman officer has made serious allegations on the system. It says that he was implicated in politics. The officer has been identified as Mani Manjari Rai, who was posted in the Maniyar Nagar Panchayat as the EO of the Executive Officer, ie, for two years.
