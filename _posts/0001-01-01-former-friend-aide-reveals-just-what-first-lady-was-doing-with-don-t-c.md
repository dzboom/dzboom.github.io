Do u?'  <br>Now, her former friend and aide, Stephanie Winstone Wolkoff, has revealed what that choice was all about.  <br>According to CNN, Wolkoff said Mrs. Trump wore it because she felt it was the only way she could get the attention of the press.  <br>Wolkoff explained Mrs. Trump hadn't realized how much attention the stunt would get, however; and that it would be so fierce.
