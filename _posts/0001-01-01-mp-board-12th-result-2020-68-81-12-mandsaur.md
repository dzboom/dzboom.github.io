The result of the 12th examination of Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education has been released. This year, about 8.5 lakh students have appeared in the examination. This year a total of 68.81% students have passed the Class 12th examination. Compared to last year, this year the pass percentage has decreased. Last year, 72.37% students achieved success.In this year, Mandsaur's Priya has topped the Maths-Science stream in the exam ...
