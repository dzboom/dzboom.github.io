BMW has unveiled a limited-edition version of their flagship X7 SUV, called the Dark Shadow Edition. The new ‘BMW X7 Dark Shadow’ special edition SUV will be manufactured at the brand’s facility in the USA and will be limited to just 500 units worldwide.  <br>The Dark Shadow edition will come with a host of cosmetic updates, added features and also a new paint scheme. The BMW X7 Dark Shadow edition can be had with any of the engine options, available on the SUVs standard model lineup. Watch the video to know all about the new special edition X7 SUV.
