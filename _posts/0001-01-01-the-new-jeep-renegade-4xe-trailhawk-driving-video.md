From July 21, 2020, Jeep dealers in Germany will take orders for Jeep Renegade 4xe and Jeep Compass 4xe, the first plug-in hybrid models from Jeep.  <br>Electrification is a fundamental step in the development of Jeep towards less and less polluting technologies, without restricting the brand values such as freedom, adventure and authenticity. Thanks to the plug-in hybrid technology, the new 4xe models offer the best performance and driving dynamics in the history of the Jeep SUVs and follow the brand motto &quot;Go anywhere - do anything&quot;, which includes adventures off the beaten track as well as everyday trips in urban areas, where customers have a locally emission-free electrical range of up to 50 kilometers (NEDC process). Fun becomes efficient, and Jeep's legendary off-road ability blends even better with respect for the environment.
