How to Open a Recently Closed Tab in Safari Browser on iPhone?  <br>Step 1: Open 'Safari Browser' App.  <br>Step 2: Tap on 'Tabs' button at the bottom right.  <br>Step 3: Tap and hold on the '+' button. This will bring the 'Recently Closed tabs' Screen.  <br>Step 4: Go to the Tab that you want to open, and then tap on it.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
