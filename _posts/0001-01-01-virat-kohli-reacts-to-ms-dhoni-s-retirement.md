MS Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on Sunday. The former India captain brings down the curtain to a 16-year-long international career wherein he won major International Cricket Council (ICC) trophies, including the 2011 World Cup. Team India captain Virat Kohli has reacted to his former captain MS Dhoni retirement announcement and said that he was feeling emotional because of the close bond between the two. Watch video.
