The festival of Hariyali Teej is celebrated on Tritiya Tithi of Shukla Paksha of Shravan month. In some places it is also called Madhushrava Teej. This festival is celebrated to commemorate the union of Maa Parvati and Shiva. This time the festival of Hariyali Teej is on 23 July, Thursday. On this day women worship Maa Parvati. On this day, the young girls swing the swing and sing melodious songs of Saavan. There is also a tradition of performing special dances like ghoomar in Rajasthan. In Jaipur city of Rajasthan, the ride of Teej Mata is taken out on this day and a fair is also organized. Goddess Parvati is pleased by taking some easy remedies on this day.
