Perikatan Nasional (PN), BN and Parti Bersatu Sabah (PBS) in Sabah are now known as Gabungan Rakyat Sabah, said Prime Minister Muhyiddin Yassin.  <br>He said this was decided at a special meeting yesterday with the top leaders of the three parties.
