Federal agents were deployed to Bend, Oregon on Thursday (August 13) after protesters blocked a bus carrying individuals in ICE custody.  <br>Footage filmed in the early hours of Thursday shows federal agents use rough methods to subdue protesters.  <br>The filmer, Kyle Vanden, explained: &quot;ICE agents attempt to retrieve two detained Latinos, hundreds of people show up to stop them.&quot;  <br>Immigration and Customs Enforcement officers deemed the two men in question a threat to the public.
