The number of corona infected patients in the country is increasing rapidly. The extent to which this virus is wreaking havoc can be gauged from the fact that so far 196 doctors of the country have lost their lives fighting this epidemic. According to the figures released by the Indian Medical Association on Saturday, so far 196 doctors have died due to corona virus in the country. The IMA has expressed concern over this.
