The ECM Libra Foundation has set aside RM3mil for its Covid-19 Food Aid Programme for KL’s urban poor. Science, Technology and Innovation Minister Khairy Jamaludin, who helped to distribute the cooked meals during Friday's run, says he will take the matter up to various ministries to ensure that more is done to assist the community.
