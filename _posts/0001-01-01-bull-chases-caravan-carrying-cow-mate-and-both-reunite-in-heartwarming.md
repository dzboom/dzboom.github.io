In a heartwarming moment in India, a bull ran after a caravan that was carrying its friend, which was a freshly sold cow. (July 12)  <br>The scene took place in the Palamedu area of ​​Madurai, as the bull ran after the cow for nearly a kilometer.   <br>After chasing and stopping the lorry, the bull was seen circling the truck and sticking its head in trying to get one last glimpse. Both the bull and cow grew up together.  <br>After seeing the video, state deputy chief minister  O. Panneerselvam's son Jayapradeep took immediate action and ordered for a reversal of the sale so that the two could be reunited back together.
