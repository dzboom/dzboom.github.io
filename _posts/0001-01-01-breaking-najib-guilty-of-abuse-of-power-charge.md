Judge Mohd Nazlan Mohd Ghazali rules that the defence failed to rebut presumptions based on the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission Act 2009 on the balance of probability and failed to raise reasonable doubt on the charge under Section 23(1) of the law.   <br>The judge is now making a ruling on the three criminal breach of trust charges.  <br>Mohd Nazlan rules that &quot;totality of evidence&quot; shows accused knew of transactions into the three AmBank Islamic accounts but &quot;deliberately&quot; kept from public disclosure.  <br>The accused's conduct &quot;at all times&quot; was undertaken to ensure sufficient funds were in the accounts before spending it.  <br>The judge rules that the &quot;fact remains&quot; that Jho Low and the accused's principal private secretary at the time Azlin Alias were in contact with the accused on the bank account.  <br>&quot;The inference is overwhelming,&quot; rules the judge.
