Whitney Reddick teaches special education in Jacksonville, Florida, and has a definite opinion about going back to the classroom.  <br>Duval County public schools are slated to reopen next Thursday with both in-person and remote learning options for students.  <br>According to HuffPost, Reddick responded by writing and posting her own obituary to Facebook.  <br>In it, she proclaims that she died 'while alone in isolation and on a ventilator at a Duval county hospital' last week at age 33.  <br>Even though she shouted from the rooftops, attempted to be unemotional, and educated herself in facts and science, she succumbed to the ignorance of those in power.
