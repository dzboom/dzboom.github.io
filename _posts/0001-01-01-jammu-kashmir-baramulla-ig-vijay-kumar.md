A terrorist has been killed in the terrorist attack on Monday in Baramulla of North Kashmir. The AK-47 has been seized from the terrorist. The operation is currently underway. Kashmir Police IG Vijay Kumar has expressed the possibility of Lashkar-e-Taiba being behind the attack. He said that soon the terrorists will be given a befitting reply.
