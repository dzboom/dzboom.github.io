Due to the heavy rains in Mumbai since last night, water was flooded in the low-lying area, about 2 feet of water has accumulated on the streets of King Circle. Apart from this, there was water logging in Hindmata, Sion, Matunga and Khar subways. According to BMC, more than 230 mm of rainfall was recorded in Mumbai in the last 10 hours.
