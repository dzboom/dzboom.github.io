Assailants shot dead two persons inside a residential society in Uttar Pradesh's Greater Noida. The incident happened in Bisrakh area. DCP of Central Noida, Harish Chandra said, &quot;At around 9 pm, two persons entered the society and shot dead two people who were sitting in the car. Efforts are on to identify the accused.&quot; Further investigation is underway.   <br>
