This is the moment some locals from the Cuddalore district of Tamilnadu state in India freed a stray dog which had been wandering around with a plastic tub stuck on its head.  <br>The animal had reportedly become trapped three days earlier when it attempted to eat something in the container but then couldn't remove its head.   <br>With the dog clearly at risk of starvation, a kindhearted local decided to help.  <br>He and a friend chased the animal for ten minutes before eventually catching it and tearing the plastic tub from its head.  <br>Although their method looks a little rough, the dog was unharmed and ran around wagging its tail immediately afterwards.  <br>The footage was filmed today (August 16th).
