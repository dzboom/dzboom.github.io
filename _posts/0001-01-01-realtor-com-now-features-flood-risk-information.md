Rising sea levels have created a flood risk that could have severe economic impacts.  <br>Realtor.com is taking the flood risk guess work out of home buying.  <br>The real estate website is including flood plain data on both for-sale and off-the-market listings.  <br>According to Gizmodo, homes listed on the site will show a flood risk score between 1 and 10.  <br>The site will prominently display the flood risk data directly below the property’s square footage and home information.  <br>The flood factor score takes into account cumulative flood risk and projected environmental changes.
