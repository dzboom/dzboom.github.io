As Black people nationwide grieve and continue protesting police brutality and racism, the NBA has responded.  <br>3 days after Jacob Blake was shot in Kenosha, Wisconsin, NBA players went on a strike.  <br>Jacob Blake was shot seven times by a police officer named Rusten Sheskey.  <br>Former NBA player Dwyane Wade made his own statement by incorporating the symbolic Black Power fist into his hair.  <br>Wade shared the new hairstyle on Instagram with nine Black fists as the caption.  <br>The former Miami Heat player left his platinum blonde hair bare of color except for the raised Black fist.
