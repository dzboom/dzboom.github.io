#BengaluruMoving  <br>This panel brings together a diverse set of experts to identify key challenges to sustainable urban mobility in Bengaluru. They examine innovative solutions to address these challenges and propose policy interventions required to make sustainable mobility a reality. The panel was organised jointly with the Bangalore International Centre BIC.This is part of the Bengaluru Mobility Summit, a 5-part webinar series organised by the Bengaluru Needs You Initiative of the Resurgent India Trust, launched by Prof. M. V. Rajeev Gowda, former Member of Parliament. The Summit is hosted on Oneindia.com  <br>
