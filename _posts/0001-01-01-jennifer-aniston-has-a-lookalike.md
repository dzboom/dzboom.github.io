It's not every day that you come across someone who looks like Jennifer Aniston — let alone someone who could be her twin. After all, despite having blonde hair and blue eyes, the actress has a very distinct look. So then, you can imagine my surprise when I stumbled upon an Instagram photo of a woman who looks nearly identical to the Friends star (save for her signature beachy blonde hair, that is). After doing a little digging, I learned that Aniston's doppelgänger is a client of California-based hairstylist, Stephanie Carrillo, who had no idea what she was in for when she first posted her customer's headshot.
