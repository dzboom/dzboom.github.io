Former prime minister Dr Mahathir Mohamad has attributed peace between the races in Kuala Lumpur to equitable property ownership between them.  <br>He said racial differences on their own would not lead to violence if there wasn't any wealth inequality.  <br>&quot;In 1969, the rioters believed that everything in the city belonged to the other people, but today many of the owners in the city are of their own race.  <br>&quot;With that, if they riot, their own race will be the victims. That is why, since May 1969, there have been no (race) riots in Kuala Lumpur,&quot; he said in the Dewan Rakyat today while debating the Yang di-Pertuan Agong's address.
