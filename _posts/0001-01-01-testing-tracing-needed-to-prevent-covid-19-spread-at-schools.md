Effective testing and contact tracing are essential for schools and day-care centers to safely reopen.  <br>Of course, it is important at any time but it is currently paramount, during the COVID-19 pandemic.  <br>School testing in the UK could help Britain avoid the second wave of COVID-19 infections.  <br>In one model, if school officials traced 68% of contacts for confirmed COVID-19 cases...  <br>...75% of students with symptoms of the virus would need to be diagnosed and isolated.  <br>This is only ideal for youngsters if they were to return to classes full-time in September.
