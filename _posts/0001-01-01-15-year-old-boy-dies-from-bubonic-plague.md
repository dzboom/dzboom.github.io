A 15-year-old boy in western Mongolia has died from bubonic plague.  <br>According to CNN, the teenager caught the plague after hunting and eating a marmot.  <br>Marmots are large ground squirrels, that are known to be linked to plague outbreaks in the region.  <br>Authorities have imposed quarantine measures in the area where the boy lived and died.  <br>The quarantine began on Sunday and will run until Saturday.  <br>Health officials have isolated 15 people who came into contact with the teenager.
