A doctor in Vietnam extracted a live butterfly from a man's ear in this grisly video.   <br>According to the doctor,  The man went to the hospital in Vinh Phuc on July 22, and said that there was an animal living in his ear canal.   <br>After using endoscopic equipment, the doctor unexpectedly spotted a butterfly in the man's ear canal. The butterfly was still alive and tried to go deeper inside the ear, causing the man pain and irritation.   <br>The doctor quickly clamped the butterfly and pulled it out of the man's ear. The butterfly had caused the interior of the man's ear canal to be slightly scratched.  <br>The doctor advised the man to wear a safety helmet to prevent insects from flying into his ears when travelling at night.
