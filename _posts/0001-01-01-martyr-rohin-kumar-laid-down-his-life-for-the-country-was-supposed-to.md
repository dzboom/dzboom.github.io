In a fresh ceasefire violation by Pakistan Army along the Line of Control on Saturday, an Army soldier lost his life in Balakot sector of Poonch district in Jammu and Kashmir. The martyred has been identified as Himachal Jawan Rohin Kumar of Hamirpur district in Himachal Pradesh. Pakistan Army violated the ceasefire agreement by opening fire along the Line of Control (LoC) in Rajouri sector. In the incident, Sepoy Rohin Kumar was critically injured and he later succumbed to his injuries. Kumar, a resident of Himachal Pradesh, was a brave, highly motivated and a sincere soldier.In 2016, 24-year-old Rohin Kumar was inducted to 14 Punjab regiments.  <br>#RohinKumar #Hamirpur #Ceasefire
