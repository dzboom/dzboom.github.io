Determined 3-year-old Sarah went out to her backyard garden on July 30 and saw a little bird trapped by a protective net put around a sapling.  <br>Feeling bad for the bird, she called her father Manas Ranjan Mohanty, a resident of Jajpur Road in eastern India, to set it free.   <br>Manas told her she could lift the net from below and let the bird out herself.   <br>But Sarah was afraid to get close to the bird thinking it might hurt her.  <br>Manas told her that it was her responsibility to help others when they were in trouble.   <br>“Keep a safe distance and free the bird, it won’t hurt you,” he said.  <br>Sarah went to the net, lifted it tentatively and broke into a sweet smile when the bird, a jungle babbler, flew to its freedom.
