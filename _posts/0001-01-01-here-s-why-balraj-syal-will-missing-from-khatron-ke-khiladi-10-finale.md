Rohit Shetty’s stunt based reality show Khatron Ke Khiladi 10 is all set to bid adieu this weekend and fans are already missing the season as it had so many worth remembering moments and amazing line up of contestants. Recently, the team re united to shoot the season finale, however, Balraj Syal who was evicted but re-entered as wild card won’t be seen in the finale episode. In an interview, Balraj shared the reason behind his absence, he told, &quot;Right now I am in Punjab and I was supposed to come back to Mumbai for the finale shoot of Khatron Ke Khiladi 10. And quarantine myself for 14 days due to government regulations as I was travelling from one city to another. Technically I had to reach Mumbai on 5th-6th so that I could shoot on 20th after completing complete isolation but my aunt passed away due to a sudden heart attack on June 29. I stayed back to participate in the rituals. Also, I wanted to be in the city with my parents in this difficult time. I am glad the makers supported me in this.&quot; Apparently fans are definitely going to miss Balraj’ presence in the finale episode. He used to add comedy to the show along with great action. However, viewers will definitely get to see Balraj's dangerous stunts, as it is already shot in Bulgaria last year.
