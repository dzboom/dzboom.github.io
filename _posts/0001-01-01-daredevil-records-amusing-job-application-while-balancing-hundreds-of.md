A professional slackliner from Germany records an amusing &quot;job application&quot; while balancing hundreds of feet in the air on a slackline.   <br>In the clip, which was filmed on July 1st in  Oberbayern,  Friedi Kühne wobbles on one leg on the high wire in a suit and tie to issue a tongue-in-cheek come-and-get-me to businesses.  <br>Kühne claims he lost his job due to the coronavirus crisis and is genuinely looking for work.   <br>&quot;I'd say I'm quite a balanced person,&quot; Kühne quips.  <br>He signs off by asking potential employers to &quot;take a leap of faith&quot; and hire him.  <br>&quot;I am not afraid of taking a leap of faith,&quot; he says before somersaulting off the slackline.
