Three terrorists were killed after an encounter broke out between militants and a joint team of security forces at Zadoora village of Pulwama in South Kashmir in the wee hours of Saturday. The terrorists have not been identified.   <br>A soldier who was critically injured in the encounter has also succumbed to his injuries, PRO Defence, Srinagar, said.   <br>This is the second encounter between militants and security forces within 24 hours in Jammu and Kashmir.
