Parents in Monterrey can now introduce their newborns safely to others amid the coronavirus pandemic. ‘Baby Cabin Parade’ is a glass box on a truck, that comes with an intercom and air conditioning, so everyone can meet the newest member of the family without an increased risk of infection.
