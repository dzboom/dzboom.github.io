Around 450 Venezuelan migrants camp in makeshift tents beside a Bogota highway as they wait to make their journey back home  amid the coronavirus lockdown in video filmed on Thursday (July 2).  <br>Lockdown measures have restricted the way that migrants can make a living in Colombia's capital.  <br>The camp has little in the way of amenities and some migrants there have been camping there for several weeks.  <br>The video shows several young children among those camping.
