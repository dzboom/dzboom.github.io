After publicly telling a particular lie over 150 times, President Donald Trump was challenged by a reporter.  <br>According to CNN, Trump abruptly ended a Saturday news conference at his Bedminster, New Jersey, golf club, by walking out.  <br>The lie had to do with veterans' health care and the Veterans Choice program. Trump has consistently told audiences he is the one who passed it.  <br>They've been trying to get that passed for decades and decades and decades and no president's ever been able to do it, and we got it done. President Donald Trump  <br>In fact, former President Barack Obama signed the Choice program into law in 2014. In 2018, Trump signed a bill that modified and expanded the eligibility criteria.  <br>CBS News White House correspondent Paula Reid asked Trump why he 'kept saying that' at the Saturday news conference.  <br>Reid continued, 'You said that you passed Veterans Choice. It was passed in 2014...it was a false statement, sir.'  <br>Trump paused, thanked the reporters, then walked away as the song 'YMCA' played.
