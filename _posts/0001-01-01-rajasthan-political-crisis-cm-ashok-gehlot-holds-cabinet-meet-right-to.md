As the Rajasthan Political crisis continues to rock the Congress Government, Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot held a cabinet meeting today in Jaipur, a day after Governor Kalraj Mishra sent back his request, with queries, for an assembly session.  The meeting went on for over two hours. The Congress Legislature Party has sent a memorandum to President Ram Nath Kovind, seeking his intervention to facilitate an assembly session in the state. They claimed in the memorandum to President Kovind that the horse-trading of MLAs was on in Rajasthan to topple an elected government and that Governors of many states are &quot;defying&quot; the Constitution at the behest of the ruling party at the Centre without worrying about the dignity of their posts.
