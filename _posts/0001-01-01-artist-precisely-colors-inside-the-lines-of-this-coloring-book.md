Nicky Kumar is artist living in Vancouver who shares videos of herself precisely coloring inside the lines of the coloring books she illustrates. After spending some time coloring in books she bought, she decided to start drawing ones herself. She has now made a coloring book that is sold on Amazon and teaches drawing classes in Vancouver.
