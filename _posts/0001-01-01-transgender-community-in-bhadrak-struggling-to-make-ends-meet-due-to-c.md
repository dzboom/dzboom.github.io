Members of transgender community from Bhadrak district of Odisha said the toll of COVID-19 have made them vulnerable, and urged the government to extend support for their livelihood. The members of the minority community are &quot;reeling under financial constraints&quot; since the pandemic spread its legs in the country in March. &quot;We have not received any help. We are facing lots of difficulties as we don't have any source of income. Government should help us,&quot; said one of the members.   <br>
