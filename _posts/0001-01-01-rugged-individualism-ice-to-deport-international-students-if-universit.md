The novel coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic has forced many universities to shift to online-only courses.  <br>CNN reports the Department of Immigration and Customs Enforcement has responded swiftly.  <br>ICE announced Monday that international students will have to leave the country or risk deportation if their universities only deliver education online.  <br>Thousands of foreign students come to the US to attend universities and training programs, as well as non-academic or vocational studies.  <br>The agency suggests students currently enrolled in the US consider other measures, such as transferring to schools with in-person instruction.  <br>The bigger issue is some of these countries have travel restrictions on and they can't go home, so what do they do then? It's a conundrum for a lot of students. Theresa Cardinal Brown, director of immigration and cross-border policy at the Bipartisan Policy Center.
