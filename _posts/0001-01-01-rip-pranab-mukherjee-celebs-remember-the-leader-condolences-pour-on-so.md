Pranab Mukherjee passed away at the age of 84 in Delhi. The former Indian President, who served as the 13th President of India, was hospitalized at the Army Hospital on August 10. He underwent surgery the same day for the removal of a clot in the brain. He had tested positive for COVID-19 in the hospital.
