Ever since Ekta Kapoor announced Naagin 5, fans have waiting eagerly waiting for some official cast announcement. However, there were reports about Hina Khan and Deepika Kakkar's involvement in the show but nothing was confirmed. Fans even demanded to bring back old Naagin's but the makers are in the hunt for an actor who has great presence. Although no actor has come out and stated anything related to the show, now Hina Khan denies rumours about her entry in the 5th season, in an interview, Hina said she doesn’t know where this news is coming from, and since it’s a Balaji production, it is best that Ekta announces who will feature in season 5. Fans and the audience should wait until then. Among all the TV stars Hina Khan has been the top contender according to fans. But looks like we have to wait a little longer for the official announcement until then we still have wait and watch how Ekta has planned the grand finale for Naagin 4.
