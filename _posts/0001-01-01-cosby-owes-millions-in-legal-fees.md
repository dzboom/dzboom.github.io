Last year a judge ordered Bill Cosby to pay $2.75 million in legal bills to a California law firm.  <br>The law firm, Quinn Emanuel Urquhart &amp; Sullivan, of Los Angeles, Calif said Cosby hasn't paid the money as yet.  <br>Fox News reports that on Friday, the firm filed papers in Manhattan Supreme Court saying Cosby was delinquent in payment.  <br>The firm says because of 10 percent interest that has accrued, they are now owed $2.98 million.  <br>Cosby is serving a three- to 10-year sentence after a jury found him guilty of drugging and sexually assaulting Andrea Constand in 2004.  <br>Last month, a judge ruled that Cosby can appeal his 2018 sexual assault conviction.
