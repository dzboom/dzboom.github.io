Activists from Beyond Politics roadblocked Trafalgar Square on Saturday (July 25) demanding the UK government steps down and is replaced with citizens' assemblies.  <br>Police attempted to negotiate with protesters who refused to move from the road.  <br>Roger Hallam, a co-founder of Extinction Rebellion, spoke at the event and compared the current situation to the French Revolution. Hallam made headlines last year after he called the Holocaust &quot;just another f***ery in human history&quot;, which lead to German publisher Ullstein pulling his then-upcoming book.  <br>Beyond Politics caused controversy earlier this week after they threw pink paint at NGOs including Greenpeace and Amnesty International, accusing them of not doing enough.
