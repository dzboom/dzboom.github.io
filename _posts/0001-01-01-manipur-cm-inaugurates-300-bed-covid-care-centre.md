Manipur Chief Minister Biren Singh inaugurated a 300-bedded COVID care centre at Manipur Trade and Expo Centre in Lamboikhongnangkhong on August 03. The facility can be further upgraded to a 1000 bedded if need arises. A total of 1,087 active cases have been reported in the state so far.   <br>
