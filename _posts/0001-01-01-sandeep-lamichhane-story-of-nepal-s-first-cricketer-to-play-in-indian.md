Nepal leg-spinner Sandeep Lamichhane, the country’s most recognized cricketer. Lamichhane is the only player from Nepal to have played in the IPL. Sandeep Lamichhane was born on 2 August 2000 in Aruchaur, Syangja, Nepal. During his childhood, he considered Sachin Tendulkar and Shane Warne as his cricketing idols.
