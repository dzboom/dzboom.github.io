Since June 1st America has been consumed with anti-racism protests.  <br>Now, over a month later, the protests show no signs of slowing down.  <br>Yahoo! reports a new Monmouth poll shows conservative Americans are now growing tired of the protests.  <br>Close to 4 in 10 Republicans told Monmouth researchers at the start of last month that protesters’ anger was justified.  <br>That number fell by more than half in the new poll, with just 15% of Republicans saying so.
