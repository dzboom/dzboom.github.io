Racial disparities in the U.S. coronavirus epidemic extend to children, according to HuffPost.  <br>Two sobering government reports released Friday show how race does change how COVID effects us.  <br>One of the CDC reports looked at children with COVID-19 who needed hospitalization.  <br>Hispanic children were hospitalized at a rate eight times higher than white kids.  <br>Black children were hospitalized at a rate five times higher, it found.  <br>The exact cause for this disparity has not been nailed down, as of yet.
