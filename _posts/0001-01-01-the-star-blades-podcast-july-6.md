Another decent result for Sheffield United and another round of talking points for our Blades podcast. United writers James Shield and Danny Hall join Liam Hoden to pick apart the draw at Burnley and the post-lockdown rule changes that have Chris Wilder still smarting. The panel discuss Jack O'Connell's return, the contribution of Jack Robinson and the introduction and future of Jack Rodwell. And they look ahead to the clash with Wolverhampton Wanderers.
