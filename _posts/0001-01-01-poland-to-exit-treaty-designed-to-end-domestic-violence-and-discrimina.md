Poland is to begin the process of withdrawing from a European treaty aimed at combating violence against women.  <br>Justice Minister Zbigniew Ziobro announced Saturday that Poland will exit the Council of Europe's Istanbul Convention on Monday.  <br>According to CNN, the Istanbul Convention aims to protect women against violence and to help bring about the end of discrimination against them.  <br>Poland is facing harsh criticism for the move. Thousands of people gathered in Warsaw and other cities on Friday to protest.  <br>Violence is not a traditional value.
