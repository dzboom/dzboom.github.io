The festival of Kajri Teej is celebrated in Bhadrapada on the third day of Krishna Paksha. Kajri Teej is also known as Kajali Teej. In many places it is also known as Budhi Teej or Saturi Teej. This festival is celebrated with great pomp in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Bihar. This time Kajri Teej will be celebrated on 6 August, Thursday.
