This girl's boyfriend organized a surprise birthday dinner for her in the backyard with some of their closest friends. He then called her up to say that his mom had fainted in the backyard to get the girl to come to the house. However, in a state of panic, she called up the emergency number. After realizing that it was a ruse to surprise her, she and her boyfriend ended up explaining to the 911 call operator that it was a false alarm.
