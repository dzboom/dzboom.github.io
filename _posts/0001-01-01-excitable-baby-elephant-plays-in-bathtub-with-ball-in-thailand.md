An excitable baby elephant plays in bathtub with a ball in Surin, Thailand.  <br>Adopted baby jumbo Phang Saifon was taken in as a newborn by Supattra's family. She said that the baby elephant likes to play with the ball while taking a shower.  <br>Supattra said: &quot;Phang Saifon loves to shower and my children would play with her every day. We have cared for her ever since she was a baby when he was lost from her herd.''  <br>Surin province in the northeast of Thailand is known for its elephants, where the animals would roam freely through the forests.  <br>Thailand has at least 2,000 wild elephants and a similar number in captivity that are held in sanctuaries, zoos or with private owners who sometimes hire them out for religious festivals and weddings.  <br>This footage was filmed on July 25.
