In the fake encounter case of Raja Man Singh of Bharatpur State of Rajasthan, the District and Sessions Court of Mathura on Wednesday announced the punishment. The court sentenced all the 11 convicted policemen to life imprisonment. 3 including Raja Mansingh were killed in a police encounter. According to the sentence pronounced by the court, Section 148 has imposed a fine of 2 years and a fine of Rs 1,000.
