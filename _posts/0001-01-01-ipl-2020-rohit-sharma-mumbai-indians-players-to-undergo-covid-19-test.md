A Mumbai Indian official said in an interview that they have a quarantine and testing procedure in place to tackle coronavirus. The franchise is looking to have a total of five coronavirus tests for each player and support staff member before heading to play the IPL. “The domestic players have started to arrive [in Mumbai] and they all are sent into a 14-day quarantine wherein the only time they are allowed to exit the room is when they need to get their test done.
