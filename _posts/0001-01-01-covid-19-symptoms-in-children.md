Parents often struggle with knowing how sick or hurt their kid really is, says huffpost.  <br>That gets even trickier during a global pandemic, particularly when the virus is a mystery.  <br>In general, the signs of COVID-19 in grownups and children are fairly similar.  <br>They include fever, chills, muscle pain, headache, sore throat, cough, and shortness of breath.  <br>Some people also experience a loss of taste or smell.  <br>Some evidence that kids are less likely to have a fever, cough or shortness of breath.  <br>However, kids might be more likely to experience stomach troubles.
