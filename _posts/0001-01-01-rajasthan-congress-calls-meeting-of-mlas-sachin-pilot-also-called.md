The Rajasthan Congress has called a meeting of the Congress MLAs a little later tonight at 9 pm. Sachin Pilot has also been called in the meeting. Ajay Maken, Randeep Singh Surjewala and state in-charge Avinash Pandey will also be present as representatives of the high command in the meeting. It is being said from the Congress that there is no threat to Ashok Gehlot's government.
