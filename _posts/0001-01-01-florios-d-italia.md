Alessandro Florio, who worked in the old Verrecchia ice cream vans, has bought the old factory in Claybank Road and transformed it into an Italian deli - the first of its kind from Portsmouth. Pasta, veg and more Italian goods can be purchased in-store, with plans to start a delivery service in the near future.  <br>
