How to Enable Dark Mode in Gmail App on an iPhone?  <br>Note: First Update your Gmail App by going to the App Store, if you have not updated it yet. After that, follow the instructions below:-  <br>Step 1: Open 'Gmail' App, and then sign-in to your account, If you have not.  <br>Step 2: Tap on 'Menu (Three Horizontal lines)' icon at the top left.  <br>Step 3: Tap on 'Settings.'  <br>Step 4: Tap on 'Theme.'  <br>Note: If you don't find Theme Section, then you need to sign-out of Gmail App, and then Sign-in again.  <br>How to Sign Out of Gmail App on an iPhone?  <br>Step 5: Tap on 'Dark' in order select it.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
