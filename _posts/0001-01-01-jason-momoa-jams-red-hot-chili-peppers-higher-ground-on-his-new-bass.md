Aquaman star Jason Momoa has posted a video of himself jamming to Red Hot Chili Peppers‘ 1989 cover of Stevie Wonder‘s ‘Higher Ground’.  <br>It came after the star received a new Fender Precision Bass guitar as a 41st birthday gift, courtesy of Fender Custom Shop Master Builder Vincent Van Trigt.  <br>In the clip, which you can watch below, Momoa channels his inner Flea with a brief noodle of the 1989 cover. It featured on the Chilli Peppers’ album ‘Mother’s Milk’.
