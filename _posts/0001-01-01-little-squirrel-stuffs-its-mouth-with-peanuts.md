This little squirrel hopped on to the human friend's lap. It picked up a peanut but instead of eating it right away, the squirrel stuffed a peanut into its mouth packing it into one cheek. It then went on to stuff one more peanut and packed it into the other cheek adjusting them both so that they wouldn't fall out.
