Porsche has introduced the 718 Spyder &amp; Cayman GT4 in the Indian market. The two sports cars from the German manufacturer are priced at Rs 1.59 crore and Rs 1.63 crore, respectively. All prices are ex-showroom (India).  <br>Both models, the 718 Spyder and 718 Cayman GT4 are powered by the same 4.0-litre six-cylinder engine. It produces 415bhp and 420Nm of peak torque and is mated to a standard six-speed manual gearbox. Watch the video to know more about the two entry-level Porsche sports cars in the Indian market.
