7-Eleven's parent company is buying Speedway gas stations in a $21 billion all-cash deal.  <br>After tax, the deal will bring in roughly $16.6 billion for Marathon Gas. Marathon hopes to use the sale to &quot;strengthen [the] balance sheet.&quot;  <br>Business Insider reports the deal will create a 7-Eleven presence in 47 of the top 50 most populated metro areas  <br>With the addition of Speedway locations, Seven &amp; I Holdings will have roughly 14,000 locations in the US and Canada.
