Veterinarians told Business Insider about a few things they think all cat owners should be doing.  <br>Get your kittens and cats vaccinated on time.  <br>Pet cats should be spayed or neutered.  <br>Try to keep your cat indoors whenever possible.  <br>Give your cat plenty of designated scratching areas.  <br>Make sure your cat has access to water at all times.  <br>Owners should always have more litter boxes than cats.  <br>Make sure the litter box is big enough and isolated enough for your cat.  <br>Give your cats stimulating toys to play with.
