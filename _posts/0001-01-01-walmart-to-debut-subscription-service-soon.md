Walmart delayed the July launch of its subscription service.  <br>Business Insider reports that Walmart needs to debut soon to avoid missing out on holiday sales.  <br>The 2020 holiday shopping season is most likely going to move entirely online.  <br>Walmart+ has a clear chance to highlight its value to consumers.  <br>Walmart was originally set to introduce its Walmart+ subscription service this spring.  <br>It pushed the debut back to July due to the coronavirus pandemic, but its launch has now been postponed again.  <br>The service is expected to compete with Amazon's $119-a-year Prime subscription.  <br>The service will offer free same-day delivery, discounts on fuel at Walmart gas stations, and early access to promotions for $98 annually.
