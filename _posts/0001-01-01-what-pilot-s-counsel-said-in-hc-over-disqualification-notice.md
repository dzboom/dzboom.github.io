A feud between the Congress leadership in Rajasthan reached the courts of law on Thursday after Tonk MLA and former Deputy CM Sachin Pilot moved the Rajasthan High Court against disqualification notices issued in the name of 19 MLAs, including himself. Representing Sachin Pilot, senior counsel Harish Salve termed the disqualification notices illegal and demanded that they are withdrawn. He also made amendments to the plea to incorporate challenges against certain provisions of the Representation of Peoples Act. Watch this report.
