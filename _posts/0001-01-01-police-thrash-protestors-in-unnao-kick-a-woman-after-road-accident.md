A protest has been sparked in Uttar Pradesh's Unnao district after one person was killed and two were left seriously injured in a road accident. At a time when nation is debating over the continuos rise in violence against women, the video of a policeman thrashing a woman proves there is certainly no end to it. Shocking pictures of a cop kicking a woman on her back have gone viral on Twitter. In the 1-minute clip, the policeman can be seen running towards a woman and thrashing her mercilessly. She then falls on the ground but he continues to kick her on the back. It is more shameful to see others present in the crowd including two policemen simply watching the incident and not doing anything to rescue her.
