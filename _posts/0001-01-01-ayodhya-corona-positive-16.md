Big news is coming out of Ayodhya in Uttar Pradesh. Corona has knocked here amidst the preparations for the Bhoomi Poojan of Ram temple. Yes, the corona crisis has started looming over the Bhoomipujan program of Ram Janmabhoomi temple. The priest of Ram Janmabhoomi, Pradeep Das has been found to be corona positive ..
