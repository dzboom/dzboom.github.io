This wife called her husband before she was going to reach home. She pranked him by telling him that she was pulled over by cops for overspeeding, and lied by saying she was driving fast because her husband was hurt. She also asked him to pretend like he had slipped as the police were following her home to check the authenticity of her reason. When she reached home, her husband was hilariously lying down on the stairs with groceries scattered around him. She laughed out loud as she enjoyed the success of her prank.
