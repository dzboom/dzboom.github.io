Thousands of protesters have gathered in London today (August 29th) to demonstrate against the government's measures to contain the COVID-19 virus.   <br>Many at the march are against the requirement to wear facemasks and are also worried about the possibility of forced vaccinations.   <br>Under current UK law, no vaccines are compulsory.  <br>In this clip, several demonstrators in Trafalgar Square explain why they feel the measures have gone too far and need to be eased.
