Superstar Salman Khan is all set to come up with the fourteenth season of the controversial reality TV show, Bigg Boss. Makers recently unveiled the show’s first promo, in which we can see host Salman Khan doing farming at his Panvel farmhouse. “Lockdown laya normal life me speed breaker. Isliye uga raha hu chawal aur chala raha hu tractor. Par ab scene paltega,” Salman said in the teaser video. Reportedly, “Bigg Boss 14” will start from September 27 on Colors TV. It has also been reported that theme of the upcoming season is based on the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.
