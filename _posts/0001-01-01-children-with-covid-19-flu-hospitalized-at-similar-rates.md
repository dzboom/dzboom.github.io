Children with COVID-19 are just as likely to suffer from the flu to the same degree.  <br>Hospitalization, admittance to ICU, and mechanical ventilation to breathe are very similar rates.  <br>Kids with COVID: 17% required hospitalization and 6% were admitted to ICU.  <br>Children with the flu: 21% were admitted to the hospital and 7% to an ICU.  <br>In addition, 3% of children with COVID-19 needed mechanical ventilation to maintain breathing.  <br>This is compared to 2% of those with the flu being put on mechanical ventilation.
