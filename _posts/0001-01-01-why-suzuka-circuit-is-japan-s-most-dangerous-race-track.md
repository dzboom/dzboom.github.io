Japan’s legendary Suzuka Circuit easily ranks as the country’s most dangerous race track. The figure eight-shaped course features high-speed corners only meant for the world’s top racers. One of its most thrilling competitions is the 8 Hours of Suzuka, a world famous superbike endurance race filled with high powered motorcycles and crashes galore. We had several of the race’s former winners explain what makes Suzuka such a difficult track to master.
