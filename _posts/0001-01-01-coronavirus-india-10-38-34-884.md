Corona has created a furore in the country .... According to the data released by the Ministry of Health, 34,884 new COVID-19 cases have occurred in India in the last 24 hours. With this, the total number of corona infections in the country has reached 10,38,716. According to the data released by the Health Ministry on Saturday, a total of 10,38,716 positive cases of Corona have been reported so far. At the same time, 34,884 new cases have been registered in the last 24 hours i.e. one day while 671 people have died.
