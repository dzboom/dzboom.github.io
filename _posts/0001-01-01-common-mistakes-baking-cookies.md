Making cookies isn't always easy.  <br>Business Insider spoke to bakers and compiled a list of common mistakes people make when baking cookies.  <br>By not chilling the dough could be messing with the texture of your cookies.  <br>Overcrowding the pan can lead to some wonky cookies.  <br>Avoid overmixing or undermixing the dough.  <br>Using stale baking powder or soda might make cookies fall flat.  <br>Don't forget to adjust baking time if using dark colored baking sheets.  <br>Opening the oven door too many times will make it harder for cookies to bake.
