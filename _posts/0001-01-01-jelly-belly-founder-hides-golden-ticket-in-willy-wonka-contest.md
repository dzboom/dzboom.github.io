The founder of Jelly Belly has launched a Willy Wonka style competition to win a candy factory, complete with hidden golden tickets.  <br>David Klein no longer works for the jelly bean company.  <br>He still works in the candy industry.  <br>He announced the nationwide treasure hunt in a video online.  <br>Cost of entry is $49.99. Then the contest will allow you access to a clue in your state.  <br>According to CNET, different state riddles will be released on different days.   <br>Each winner will receive $5,000.  <br>The overall winner will be given keys to Klein's factory in Florida and a candy-making course at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.
