Journalist Sophia Mitrokostas moved to the UK from the U.S.  <br>She told Business Insider what the differences are between the two countries.  <br>Housing costs are a fraction of what they are in the states.  <br>SUVs and pickup trucks are almost nonexistent where I live now.  <br>People in the UK seem to take more vacations than those in the US do.  <br>Snacks, like cheesy popcorn, are impossible to find in the UK  <br>Going to the doctor typically involves almost no paperwork and zero bills.
