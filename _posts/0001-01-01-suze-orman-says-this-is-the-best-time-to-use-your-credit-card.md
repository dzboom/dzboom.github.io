Personal finance experts typically warn people to use their credit cards sparingly.  <br>Suze Orman was one of those experts.  <br>However, during the coronavirus, she says use your credit card to free up cash, especially if you're out of work or expect to be out of work soon.  <br>Orman told Business Insider people can use cash to pay off any credit card debt they build during the pandemic once they have a steady income again.  <br>Orman suspects credit card companies will lower users' credit limits like they did in the financial crisis of 2008.
