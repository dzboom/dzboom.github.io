Insider asked chefs about their favorite and least favorite dishes to order at barbecue restaurants.   <br>Always try the regional specialty.  <br>Burnt ends are a classic and tasty barbecue dish.  <br>Try the pork butt to gauge the quality of the restaurant.  <br>The queso macaroni and cheese isn't always a worthy side.  <br>Green beans can be a lackluster side.  <br>A hamburger usually isn't the best thing on a barbecue menu.
