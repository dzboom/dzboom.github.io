Police in southern India's Andhra Pradesh arrested two men for smuggling 101 bottles of liquor strapped to their body.  <br>The duo were arrested when police in Polavaram conducted checks as the men's movement and body shape seemed suspicious.   <br>Officers found the 180ml bottles strapped around the bootleggers' chests.  <br>The duo, identified as Devarakonda Srinivas Rao and Devarakonda Rajesh, are from Aswaraopeta in Khammam district of Telangana, a neighbouring state.  <br>Police have seized the bottles and the motorcycle, and a case was filed against them under Section 34A of the Andhra Pradesh Excise Act.   <br>This footage was filmed on September 1.
