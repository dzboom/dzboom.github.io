Antebellum is a new horror movie that stars Janelle Monáe, Marque Richardson II, Eric Lange, Jack Huston, Kiersey Clemons, Tongayi Chirisa, Gabourey Sidibe, Rob Aramayo, Lily Cowles, and Jena Malone.  <br>Successful author Veronica Henley (Janelle Monáe) finds herself trapped in a horrifying reality and must uncover the mind-bending mystery before it’s too late.  <br>The film is directed by directors Gerard Bush and Christoper Renz (Bush+Renz). It is due out on 18th September 2020.
