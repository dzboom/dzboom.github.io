Audi has launched its performance-oriented RS Q8 SUV in the Indian market. The new Audi RS Q8 SUV is available with a price tag of Rs 2.07 crore, ex-showroom (India). The SUV comes powered by a 4.0-litre twin-turbo V8 producing 600bhp and 800Nm of peak torque and is currently the fastest production SUV to go around the Nurburgring Nordschleife, with a time of 7 minutes and 42 seconds.
