Amazing footage shows a family moving house - by driving their home to its new location on tractors.   <br>The traditional wooden Khmer property was driven along a rural road in Kampot, Cambodia on June 17.  <br>There was a tractor driven by farmworkers holding up each corner of the house.  <br>Passing driver Vathana Thun was astonished when he saw the portable house, which was causing traffic to slow.  <br>He said: &quot;As far as I know, the house is old and has been in the family for many years.  So when they moved to a new place, they wanted to keep the same home. It was impressive how easily they were taking it on the road.''
