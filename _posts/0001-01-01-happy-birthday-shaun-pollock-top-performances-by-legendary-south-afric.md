One of the best all-rounders to have played for South Africa, Shaun Pollock celebrates his 47th birthday on Thursday (July 16, 2020). Hailing from Port Elizabeth, Pollock wasn’t the quickest of the Proteas bowlers. However, his ability to swing the ball and accuracy made him a nightmare for the batsmen. Along with his prowess with the ball, Pollock was also a more than handy batsman lower down the order as he has played many match-defining knocks in his illustrious career.
