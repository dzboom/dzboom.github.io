Hospitalized COVID-19 patients with high levels of inflammation in their lungs saw a 75% reduction.  <br>The reduction was in their risk for death after receiving steroid treatment, reports UPI.  <br>These patients also were 75% less likely to require ventilator support to maintain oxygen levels.  <br>Testing patients for levels of inflammation before starting steroid treatment will likely help.  <br>It will identify the best candidates for this approach and make recovery smoother.
