England beat West Indies by 113 runs in Manchester to level the three-Test series 1-1. England began the day by setting West Indies 312 to win as Ben Stokes brought up an enthralling half-century. Shamarh Brooks and Jermaine Blackwood scored half-centuries to lead West Indies’ fightback but once both were dismissed, it was curtains. Dom Bess picked up the final wicket.
