Jay-Z got his start in touring on the No Way Out Tour supporting Puff Daddy in 1997. Two years later he launched his own headlining tour in support of &quot;Hard Knock Life.&quot; Over the next two decades, Jay would set out on a number of more headline tours, co-headline tours, and support tours. To date, Jay-Z has played over 400 concerts across 7 solo tours and 9 co-headlining tours.
