Eight fire engines have been called to a serious fire in Hove today (July 28, 2020).   <br>Emergency services were called to the blaze in the cavity of a five floor building in Brunswick Terrace at about 1.20pm.   <br>By 2.30pm, eight fire engines and an aerial ladder platform were on scene.   <br>Crews are using eight breathing apparatus, three hose reel jets, one main jet and a hydrant to fight the flames.   <br>There are no reports of injuries, according to the fire service.   <br>People are being asked to avoid the area.  <br>
