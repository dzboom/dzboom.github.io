All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen chief Asaduddin Owaisin has targeted the central government over the attacks being carried out by alleged cow guards in the country. Owaisi has said through his tweet that the terror of the guards is forcing Muslims to feel life-threatening.
