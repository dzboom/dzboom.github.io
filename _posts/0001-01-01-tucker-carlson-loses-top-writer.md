The top writer for Fox News host Tucker Carlson has been fired.  <br>For years Blake Neff used a pseudonym to post bigoted remarks on an online forum that is a hotbed for racist, sexist, and other offensive content.  <br>Neff's posts frequently used the n-word and other derogatory slurs for blacks, latinos, Asians, gays, women, Muslims, and liberals.  <br>A Fox News spokesperson said Carlson could not be reached for comment.  <br>Neff did not respond to multiple requests for comment from CNN business.
