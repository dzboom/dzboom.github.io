The National Testing Agency is conducting NEET i.e. National Eligibility Entrance Test for admission to medical courses across the country. In view of the Coronas epidemic, the NTA has taken additional precautionary measures to secure the examination. Examination No.2 It will be from 5 pm to 5 pm. Exams are expected to be attended by more than 15 lakh students.
