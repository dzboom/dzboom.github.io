Residents of Kiamware area of South Mugirango shrugged off guidelines to keep them safe from Covid-19 to protest over the poor state of roads in the constituency. In hundreds they turned up to express their anger on what they described as unending blame games between the area Ward Rep Andrew Kimonge and MP Sylivanus Osoro on who is should carry out repairs on the road.
