Rafale, the Indian Air Force's deadliest fighter jet equipped with state-of-the-art missiles and lethal bombs, will land on the Indian side on 29 July. Five aircraft took off from France on Monday as the first consignment of Rafale fighter jets of India and now in the UAE Al Dafra arrives at airbase. These aircraft will be fueled by French tankers at the UAE airbase.
