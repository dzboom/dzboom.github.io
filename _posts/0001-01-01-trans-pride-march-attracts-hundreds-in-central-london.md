Hundreds marched in central London on Saturday (September 12) as part of the city's second-ever Trans Pride march.  <br>Protesters demanded legal recognition for non-binary people and reforms to the Gender Recognition Act (GRA).  <br>The march went ahead despite outdoor gatherings of more than 30 people being banned due to the coronavirus.
