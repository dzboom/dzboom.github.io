FESTIVE fun lovers dreaming of another bright Christmas at Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh today got the news they were wishing for.  <br>Christmas At The Botanics will dazzle again.  <br>The mile-long outdoor light-trail spectacular is returning for a fourth consecutive year and its longest ever run, with a record 32 selected nights, from November 26 to January 3.  <br>It has been given the go ahead, with pandemic health and measures to protect visitors, and looks set to be one of the few major events to bring cheer to families later this year.  <br>That could lead to bumper bookings and organisers are advising people to book early, to avoid missing out.  <br>Tickets for this year’s show are on sale now. For further information, pricing, timings and bookings, visit www.rbge.org.uk/christmas.
