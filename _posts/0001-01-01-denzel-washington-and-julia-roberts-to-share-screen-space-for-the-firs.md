Hollywood stars Denzel Washington and Julia Roberts are all set to share screen space for the first time in 27 years. Denzel and Julia will be coming together in the film Leave The World Behind, which is based on Rumaan Alam’s upcoming novel which will be out in October later this year. Netflix has acquired the official film rights. Mr. Robot director Sam Esmail is set to direct from his adapted script. Leave The World Behind is about two families, who are forced to stay together on a long weekend that has gone wrong. Roberts will play the mother of the family that rents a house and while Denzel will portray the homeowner.
