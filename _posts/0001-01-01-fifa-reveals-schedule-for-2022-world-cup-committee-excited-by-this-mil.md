The 2022 FIFA World Cup Final is confirmed to take place at 80,000-seat Lusail Stadium on 18 December. Four matches will be played a day throughout the group stage to ensure optimal rest time for the teams. Qatar’s unique footprint allows later assignment of group matches for the benefit and comfort of fans.   <br>
