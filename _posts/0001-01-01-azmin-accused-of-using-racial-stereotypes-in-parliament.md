Two MPs from Pakatan Harapan today called out International Trade and Industry Minister Azmin Ali for using racial stereotypes in Parliament.  <br>This is after Azmin, in talking about incentives by the government for car buyers, used the example of Mak Cik Kiah buying a Myvi and an Encik (Mr) Hong buying a Mercedes Benz S560.  <br>Khalid Samad (Harapan-Shah Alam) then interjected, saying: &quot;This is racial stereotyping.&quot;
