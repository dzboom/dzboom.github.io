With restrictions on international travel in place due to coronavirus, more Brits are opting for a staycation this summer – choosing the great British outdoors for their annual escape. Camping is booming as a result with outdoor shops and campsites seeing a surge in sales. But for those deciding to pitch up for the first time who may be a little unsure about what it entails, we spoke to camping blogger Shell Robshaw-Bryan for some tips.
