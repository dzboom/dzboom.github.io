Asiya and Jawad Javed own a corner shop in Falkirk, Scotland. When an elderly lady showed up sad that she couldn’t find hand sanitiser or a face mask for herself. The Javeds helped her out and have since put together and distributed three thousand coronavirus kits for people who needed them.
