All filming requires a licence from the National Film Development Corporation (Finas), even for personal social media, said Communications and Multimedia Minister Saifuddin Abdullah.  <br>Asked about how this would affect people who use social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok, he said that all content creation is subject to regulations.  <br>&quot;It is mandatory (wajib) for film producers to apply for a film production licence and filming certification letter, regardless of whether they are mainstream media or personal media that publishes their film on social media or traditional channels,&quot; Saifuddin told the Dewan Rakyat today.  <br>
