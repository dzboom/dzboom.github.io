Another exceptional and beautiful offering launched recently is the MV Agusta Brutale 1000rr, the first bike following their limited edition Serie Oro, to be coined ‘Hypernaked’. Offering up 208 bhp, the Brutale promises huge performance. And, of course, as with all MV motorcycles, the Brutale 1000 rr has the looks to match its stonking shove!  <br>This MV Agusta’s design is incredible from every angle, with amazing detail and seriously striking presence. We would argue that the Brutale 1000rr is easily the best looking naked bike currently on sale.
