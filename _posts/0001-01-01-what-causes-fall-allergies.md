Allergies plagues millions of Americans every fall.  <br>What causes allergies?  <br>Business Insider reports that fall allergies are often caused by weeds like ragweed.  <br>Dust also causes allergies.  <br>So does mold.  <br>Fall allergy symptoms can include a runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, or itchy eyes.   <br>Those suffering from allergies should start taking allergy medication at the beginning of September to alleviate symptoms before they start.
