서울시, 코로나19 피해 디자인업계에 20억 지원  <br>The Seoul city government and the Seoul Design Foundation are teaming up to support small designing firms struggling due to the COVID-19 outbreak.  <br>Hosting an open competition, they'll be giving out one-point seven million U.S. dollars in prize money to a thousand winners.  <br>For a chance to win, any individual or company with fewer than 4 employees can submit their design ideas for &quot;COVID-19 and the New Normal&quot;.  <br>Judges will pick the winners based on the creativity of the designs and other factors.  <br>The winners will receive their prize in early September.  <br>The city said the competition is aimed at helping small firms that are struggling to stay afloat due to the COVID-19 pandemic.  <br>
