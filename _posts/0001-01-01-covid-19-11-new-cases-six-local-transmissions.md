Malaysia has recorded five imported and six locally transmitted Covid-19 cases today, bringing the total to 9,094 cases since the beginning of the outbreak.  <br>Health Ministry director-general Dr Noor Hisham Abdullah said that of the locally transmitted cases, three were from the Kurau cluster that was detected in Perak through contact tracing and active case detection.
