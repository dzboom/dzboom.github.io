An Indian army convoy was seen on Tuesday (September 1) moving towards the border region of Ladakh, where a recent clash between Indian and Chinese troops left 20 Indian soldiers dead.    <br>China said on Tuesday that it was seeking a peaceful resolution to its Himalayan border dispute with India following the most violent confrontation between the two nations in decades.  <br>India's Prime Minister Narendra Modi said the country would be proud that the Indian soldiers died while fighting.  <br>The clash in the Ladakh region on Monday was the first deadly confrontation between India and China since 1975.  <br>Experts said it would be difficult for the two nations to ease heightened tensions.
