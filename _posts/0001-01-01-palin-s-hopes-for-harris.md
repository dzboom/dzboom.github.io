On Tuesday, Democratic Presidential nominee Joe Biden named Kamala Harris his running mate.  <br>It's the first time since 2008 that a woman was named the Vice Presidential nominee on a major party ticket.  <br>In 2008, Alaska governor Sarah Palin was named John McCain's VP on the Republican party's ticket.  <br>Republicans quickly slammed the media for what they called &quot;sexist&quot; coverage of Palin.  <br>In an interview with Good Morning America, Palin reflected on how she was treated.  <br>She said that she hopes the media and political analysts treat Harris better than they treated her.  <br>&quot;I hope that the media will treat her candidacy not as personally rough as they treated mine.&quot;
