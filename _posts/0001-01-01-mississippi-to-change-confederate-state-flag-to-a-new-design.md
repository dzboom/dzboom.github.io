Tate Reeves is set to sign a bill Tuesday evening in the southern state of Mississippi.  <br>The bill is to remove the Confederate emblem from the official Mississippi state flag.  <br>Mississippi is the only remaining state flag to feature the Confederate insignia, reports CNN.  <br>The Mississippi state Legislature made history on Sunday by passing a bill to retire the current flag.  <br>They will commission a few new designs a new one to be voted on in the fall.  <br>Confederate symbols have been contentious for decades, if not longer, and change is long wanted for some.
