Shatruhan Lal, a courageous 12-year-old boy, had saved the lives of other children by presenting extraordinary bravery in a frightening railway accident that took place 41 years ago. For this, Prime Minister Indira Gandhi gave the National Bravery Award to Shatruhan on 24 January 1980. The case relates to Shatruhan, who was awarded the National Bravery Award, living in Newai, Durg.
