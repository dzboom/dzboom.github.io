Senior adults with higher levels of a specific protein in their blood have better cognitive function.  <br>According to a new study, those same older adults might also be at lower risk for dementia.  <br>According to UPI, about 6 million Americans are currently living with some form of dementia.  <br>The protein, apolipoprotein E, is found in HDL, which also is known as &quot;good cholesterol&quot;.  <br>Earlier research also shows that apolipoprotein E has a &quot;cardioprotective&quot; effect, meaning it may protect your heart.
