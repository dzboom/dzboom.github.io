Noted Urdu poet Rahat Indori who had tested positive for coronavirus, passed away at an Indore hospital on Tuesday after suffering two heart attacks. Doctor at the  Aurobindo Hospital said that he suffered two heart attacks today and could not be saved. He was admitted to hospital on Sunday, after testing positive for Covid-19. He had 60% pneumonia. Congress leader Rahul Gandhi mourned the passing of Indori Sahab with a couplet. Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan expressed his grief at Indori's demise. Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal expressed grief at the demise of the poet.   <br>#Covid-19
