Former President Bharat Ratna Pranab Mukherjee was cremated on Tuesday. With state honors, Lodhi merged with Pranab da Panchatattva at the crematorium. His son and Congress leader Abhijeet Mukherjee lit him. Pranab Mukherjee's son Abhijeet Mukherjee felt pain on this occasion.
