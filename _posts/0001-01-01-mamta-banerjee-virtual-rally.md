Corona virus is spreading fast .. But preparations are being made in many states. All political parties are engaged in holding virtual rallies. In this episode, West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee addressed a virtual rally in the style of Home Minister Amit Shah on Tuesday. In the rally, he talked about free ration to Corona and from election to CAA-NPR.
