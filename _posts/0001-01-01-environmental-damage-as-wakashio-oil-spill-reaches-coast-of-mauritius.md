MV Wakashio ran aground on July 25 and has since leaked tons of oil into the surrounding waters.  <br>Footage recorded by @gregrouxel and @hugoclementk on Saturday (August 8) shows black oil covering sand and vegetation along the beaches of Grand Port, Mauritius.  <br>Prime Minister Pravind Jugnauth declared a state of environmental emergency on Friday (August 7).
