Actor Rhea Chakraborty is making headlines for all the wrong reasons. She is a prime suspect in Sushant Singh Rajput death case. Rhea and Sushant were dating for more than a year now. Rhea wasn’t much of a popular actor among the masses. She made her Bollywood debut in 2013 and has worked in just 6 Hindi films so far. And all of them bombed at the box office. She made her Bollywood debut with 2013 film ‘Mere Dad Ki Maruti’. The film starred Ram Kapoor and Saqib Saleem in lead roles and the film earned just Rs 12 crores at the box office. Her next film came out in 2014 titled ‘Sonali Cable’. Actor Ali Fazal starred opposite Rhea and the film didn’t turn out well. In the same year, Rhea was seen in the film ‘Dobaara’ also starring Huma Qureshi and Saqib Saleem. This horror film was made with a budget of Rs 10 crore and earned just Rs 1.36 crore at the box office. After 2014, Rhea was seen in 2017 film ‘Half Girlfriend’ in a supporting role. With a budget of Rs 50 crores, the film earned Rs 98 crores. Though the film earned good figures at the box office, but it received mixed reviews from critics and audience. Rhea’s 5th film was ‘Bank Chor’ starring Riteish Deshmukh and Vivek Oberoi in lead roles. The film was super flop. Made with a budget of Rs 15 crore, the film earned just Rs 7.5 crore, thus making losses. Rhea’s last release was film ‘Jalebi’. The film earned just Rs 2.41 crore. That has been the entire filmography of Rhea. She is currently been interrogated in Sushant Singh Rajput case.
