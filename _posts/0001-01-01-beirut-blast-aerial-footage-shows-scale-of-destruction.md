Aerial footage shows the devastating impact of a massive explosion that killed at least 100 people in Lebanon’s capital Beirut.    <br>Officials said the huge blast, caused by 2,750 tonnes of ammonium nitrate, also injured more than 4,000.   <br>It has emerged that the chemical was stored unsafely in a warehouse near the city’s port, where the explosion occurred.   <br>Lebanese Prime Minister Hassan Diab has said that “those responsible will pay the price for this disaster.”   <br>Authorities are still struggling to establish the full scale of the damage, as rescue efforts continue.
