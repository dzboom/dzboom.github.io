9 people have died in a fire in a hotel in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. Information arrived, fire brigade has controlled the fire. This hotel was being used as a covid care. Other people trapped inside the hotel have also been evacuated safely. This incident took place at the Hotel Golden Palace. There were reports of 40 people in the hotel.
