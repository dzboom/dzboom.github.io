Union HRD minister Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank said on Thursday that there has been a lot of uninformed commentary on the exclusion of some topics from the CBSE syllabus for Classes 9 to 12 for the 2020-21 session and requested to &quot;leave politics out of education&quot; .
