Prime Minister Narendra Modi gave his talk on the new national education policy of the country on Friday. During this, he explained why the focus was on the local language. The Prime Minister said that the dialect of the home of the children and the language of learning in the school should be the same, so that the children will be easy to learn. Right now, children will get this facility till the fifth class.
