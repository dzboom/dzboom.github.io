Firefighters rescued a six-foot-long python that was trapped in East Jakarta, Indonesia on Thursday. (August 6, 2020)  <br>According to local residents, the firefighters struggled for nearly two hours to save the snake, and then immediately brought it back to the fire station before releasing it back into the habitat.
