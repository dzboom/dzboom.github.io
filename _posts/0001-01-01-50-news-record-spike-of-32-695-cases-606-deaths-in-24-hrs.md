India has recorded its highest single-day spike of 32,695 coronavirus cases and 606 deaths in the last 24 hours in India. With the new record spike in the coronavirus cases in India, the country's Covid-19 caseload has climbed to over 9.68 lakh. India's coronavirus tally now stands at 968,876 Covid cases. The 9.68 lakh coronavirus cases in India include 331,146 active cases. A total of 612,815 people have cured or discharged. India's death toll is 24,915 deaths, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare said on Thursday.
