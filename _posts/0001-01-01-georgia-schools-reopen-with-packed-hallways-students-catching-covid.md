Reopened schools in Georgia’s Paulding and Cherokee counties drew negative attention this week.  <br>Viral photos showed students packing hallways with little regard for social distancing or mask-wearing.  <br>The most prominent images were taken on Tuesday by a 10th grade student, reports HuffPost.  <br>The student posted them on social media and described the hallway situation as severely jammed.  <br>Other viral images showed students standing in close proximity for senior photos with no masks.
