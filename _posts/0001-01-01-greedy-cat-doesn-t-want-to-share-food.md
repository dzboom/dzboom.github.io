This cat, Krasavchik, loved to eat. When Martin, the other cat in the house was given the same bowl to eat from, Krasavchik could not be patient enough and refused to share his food. Every time his owner pushed the bowl to Martin, Krasavchik kept claiming the bowl back for himself until finally, he could eat alone in peace, as Martin gave up.
