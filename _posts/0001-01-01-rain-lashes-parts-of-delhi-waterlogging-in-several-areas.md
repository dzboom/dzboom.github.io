Heavy rain lashed parts of Delhi and its nearby areas on Sunday morning, bringing a much-needed relief from discomfort of sultry weather. With rain, waterlogging was also seen in some areas of Delhi and Noida. The India Meteorological Department (IMD) said thunderstorm with rain is likely to continue in many places of Delhi and occur in adjoining areas of Adampur, Hissar, Hansi, Jind, Gohana, Gannaur, Barut, Rohtak, Sonipat, Bagpat, Gurugram, Noida, Ghaziabad and Faridabad.
