NFL commissioner Roger Goodell announced on Monday that there will be no preseason NFL games.  <br>The new mandate comes as every aspect of the sporting world has been affected by the coronavirus.  <br>Goodell wrote in his statement; &quot;The NFL in 2020 will not look like other years&quot;.  <br>He went on to say. &quot;Players and coaches will be tested for the virus regularly, including every day for a while&quot;.  <br>According to CNN, sports have resumed in other parts of the world where coronavirus has been more successfully contained.  <br>The U.S. is by far the world leader in coronavirus cases with over 4 million cases reported.
