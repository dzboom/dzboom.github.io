Chennai Super Kings will become the first and the only team to organise a camp in India before IPL 2020. The franchise's officials have confirmed that they have got the state government's permission and that they can organise a seven-day camp in the city. As per reports, players will have to undergo COVID-19 test after landing in Chennai as well. Dhoni and pacer Monu Kumar had submitted their samples in Ranchi on Wednesday. They are cleared to board the flight.
