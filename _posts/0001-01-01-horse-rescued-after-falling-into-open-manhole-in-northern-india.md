A horse that fell into an open manhole at Hapur Adda crossing in Meerut, northern India was rescued by police with the help of locals on September 1.  <br>Footage shows the moment a large crowd pulling the animal out using heavy-duty ropes and cover the manhole with rocks to prevent further incidents.   <br>The rescue operation took one hour, and the horse was bathed before being released, local media reported.
