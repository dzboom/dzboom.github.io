Anant Chaturdashi is also known as Anant Chaudas. In this fast, the eternal form of Lord Vishnu is worshiped. Ganesh Visarjan is also done on this day, so the importance of this festival increases further. This time, the fast of Anant Chaturdashi is falling on Tuesday, September 1.
