Gwinnett County Public Schools is the largest school district in Georgia, reports HuffPost.  <br>Some 260 of their employees have either tested positive or been exposed to COVID-19.  <br>Those people will be quarantined ahead of the start of the fall semester next week.  <br>The district intends to offer all-digital classes with both prerecorded and live components.  <br>However, staff members were still required to come to work and began preplanning activities in-person.  <br>As the reports of exposure and positive results are still coming in, the number of those infected grows.
