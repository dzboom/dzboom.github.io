In a speech given at the foot of Mt. Rushmore on Friday, President Donald J. Trump didn't hold back.  <br>According to CNN, Trump hurled vitriol at protesters demanding an end to systemic racism and police brutality.  <br>Such people, Trump said, were attempting 'to wipe out our history, defame our heroes, erase our values, and indoctrinate our children.'  <br>We will expose this dangerous movement, protect our nation's children, end this radical assault and preserve our beloved American way of life. President Donald J. Trump  <br>The South Dakota event ignored many of the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention guidelines for large gatherings.  <br>Trump spoke before thousands of people, most without masks, who were seated close together in bleacher seats and on black folding chairs.  <br>Because of a local fire code, the seats were zip-tied together, making physical distancing impossible.
