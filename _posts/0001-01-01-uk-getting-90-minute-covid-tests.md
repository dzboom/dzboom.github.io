The British government has procured &quot;millions&quot; of two separate coronavirus tests.  <br>These tests can detect not only coronavirus but other viruses common in autumn, winter, and spring.  <br>Millions of new rapid coronavirus tests will provide on-the-spot results in under 90 minutes.  <br>This will help break chains of transmission quickly, according to reports at CNN.  <br>The department has said that the tests &quot;will hugely increase testing capacity ahead of winter.&quot;  <br>Fast results means people will know they are positive sooner, which will contain the exposure to others.
