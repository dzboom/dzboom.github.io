After Vikas Dubey's encounter, the law has started to be tightened on his properties. The ED of the Enforcement Directorate, ie Vikas Dubey, has sought details from the Kanpur police regarding movable and immovable property.According to police sources, Vikas Dubey traveled to 14 countries in the last three years and during this time he bought properties in UAE and Thailand through his special people.
