Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg said in a statement Friday she is undergoing chemotherapy treatments to treat a &quot;recurrence of cancer.&quot; Ginsburg, 87, said in the statement she began treatment May 19 after a biopsy showed she had lesions on her liver. She said a July 7 scan showed there was a &quot;significant reduction&quot; in her liver lesions &quot;and no new disease.&quot; Ginsburg also indicated that she is &quot;fully able&quot; to continue her capacity as a member of the Supreme Court.
