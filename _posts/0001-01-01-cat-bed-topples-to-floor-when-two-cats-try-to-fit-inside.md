Hennessey, the cat, was chasing his tail while snuggled inside his cat's nest. His brother, Harley, saw him moving inside the nest, which aroused his interest. He hopped towards it, trying to fit inside the small bed. The nest misbalanced when two cats were inside and toppled to the floor. Thankfully, it was a soft fall leaving the cats unhurt.
