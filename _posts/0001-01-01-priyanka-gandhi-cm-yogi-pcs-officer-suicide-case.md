Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi has demanded a probe by the state's Yogi government in the women's PCS officer suicide case in Ballia, Uttar Pradesh. In this regard, he has written a letter to the state government. In her letter, Priyanka Gandhi said that the people of Manimanjari Rai's family are very upset.
