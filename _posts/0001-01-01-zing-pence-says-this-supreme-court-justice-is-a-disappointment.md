Normally rather mild in his public remarks, US Vice President Mike Pence had sharp words Supreme Court Chief Justice John Roberts.  <br>In an interview with the Christian Broadcasting Network this week, Pence called Chief Justice Roberts a 'disappointment' to conservative Americans.  <br>Last month, the Supreme Court rejected a Nevada church’s emergency plea to strike down the state’s temporary 50-person cap on worship services.  <br>Nominated by former President George W. Bush, Roberts has come to be seen as the Supreme Court’s key swing vote.  <br>This year, he sided with the four liberal justices in extending workplace protections to LGBTQ employees.  <br>HuffPost reports Justice Roberts also wrote the 5-4 decision that blocked the Trump administration from rescinding the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program.  <br>President Donald Trump, along with multiple Republican lawmakers, has publicly denounced Roberts for his role in those decisions.
