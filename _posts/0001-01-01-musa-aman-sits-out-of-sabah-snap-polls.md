Musa Aman who triggered the Sabah snap polls after initiating a failed political coup will be sitting out of the electoral contest.  <br>This is after he did not turn up at the Dewan Serbaguna Libaran where the nomination process was held for the state seats of Gum-Gum, Sungai Manila, and Sungai Sibuga.  <br>Musa had previously expressed interest in Sungai Manila, he did not turn up at other nomination centres either.
