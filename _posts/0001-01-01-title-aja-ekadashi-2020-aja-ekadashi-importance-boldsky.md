Aja Ekadashi 2020: Aja Ekadashi is falling on 15 August. The Ekadashi date of Bhadrapada Krishna Paksha is called Aja Ekadashi. On this day, a person who observes Aja Ekadashi fast with true devotion and rules gets the same fruit as Ashwamedha Yagya. According to religious belief, Ekadashi date is very dear to Lord Vishnu. Therefore, they are worshiped on this day.
