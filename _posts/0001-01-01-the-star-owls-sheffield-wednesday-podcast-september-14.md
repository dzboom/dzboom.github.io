Sheffield Wednesday have stormed out of the blocks in the race to zero and if anything is going to keep everyone optimistic, it's a win at Cardiff City. Joining Liam Hoden to reflect on the magnificent 2-0 triumph in the first of a new season of mini-review episodes, The Star's Wednesday reporters Alex Miller and a repatriated Joe Crann discuss where it all went right in the principality. They talk the surprises in the team selection, the performance of the strike pair and THAT huddle at the end. They also discuss the latest transfer news and look ahead to the trip to Rochdale in the Carabao Cup.
