On Tuesday, President Trump gave an interview in which he said he was considering banning TikTok.  <br>Trump echoed earlier comments from Secretary of State Mike Pompeo.  <br>Trump implied a ban on TikTok would be a way of punishing China for the coronavirus.  <br>Business Insider reports that Coronavirus originated in the city of Wuhan.  <br>Trump's rationale was somewhat different from the reason given by Pompeo.  <br>Pompeo said the US was worried TikTok could be a national-security risk.
