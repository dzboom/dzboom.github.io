In a bid to combat COVID-19, quarantine facilities will be set up at Eden Gardens for police personnel. “In a meeting with between Kolkata Police &amp; Cricket Association of Bengal (CAB) officials, it has been decided that quarantine facilities will be set up at Eden Gardens. E, F, G and H blocks would initially be used for setting up of the same,” said Cricket Association of Bengal president Avishek Dalmiya. West Bengal has 8,881 active cases of coronavirus.   <br>
