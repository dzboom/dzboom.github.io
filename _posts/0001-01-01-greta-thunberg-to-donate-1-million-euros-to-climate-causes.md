Climate change activist Greta Thunberg recently won the Gulbenkian Prize for Humanity.  <br>The prestigious prize also included 1 million euros ($1,153,330).  <br>The 17-year-old Swedish activist said she’ll use the money to support environmental causes.  <br>The Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation is dedicated to the promotion of the arts, philanthropy, science, and education.  <br>Thunberg said the sum of money is “more money than I can even begin to imagine” .  <br>She went on to say she would donate it all to charities fighting climate change.
