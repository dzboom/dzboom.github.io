A powerful storm swept into Chicago, Illinois on August 10.  <br>The National Weather Service issued a Tornado Warning for the area, predicted that it could cause tornadoes and widespread significant wind damage. According to the report, a building under construction collapsed due to wind damage, power lines went down and appeared to start an electrical fire.
