President Ramnath Kovind and Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed grief over the plane crash at Kozhikode Airport in Calicut, Kerala. Prime Minister Modi has spoken to Kerala Chief Minister P Vijayan about this accident and got information about the accident. PM Modi assured the Chief Minister of Kerala of all possible help.
