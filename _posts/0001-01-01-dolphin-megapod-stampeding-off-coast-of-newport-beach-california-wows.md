This the remarkable moment a pod of dolphins stampede off the coast of Newport Beach, California.  <br>The clip, filmed on September 7, shows the dolphins stampeding for several minutes while the awestruck crew on a whale watching boat looked on.   <br>&quot;We see this species of dolphin almost every day throughout the year here but typically they're going at a much slower pace or there might just be a couple jumping randomly, so to see them all stampeding is pretty spectacular,&quot; filmer Mark Girardeau, an experienced wildlife photographer based in Orange County, told Newsflare. &quot;We don't know exactly why they do it when they do.&quot;   <br>Girardeau was riding with whale watching tour agency Newport Coastal Adventure.
