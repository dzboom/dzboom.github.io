In her bid for re-election, Sen. Susan Collins (R-Me.) has just lost another major endorsement.  <br>The Human Rights Campaign has decided to back Democrat Sara Gideon in her bid to unseat the Maine Republican.  <br>HRC is one of America's largest LGBTQ advocacy groups and has supported Collins’ reelection campaigns since 2002.  <br>But according to HuffPost, the organization said Wednesday that it could no longer do so.  <br>The HRC explained Sen. Collins' support of Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell and President Donald Trump's agenda was the core cause of the move.  <br>Her...failure to hold Donald Trump accountable is simply untenable. Alphonso David, HRC President
