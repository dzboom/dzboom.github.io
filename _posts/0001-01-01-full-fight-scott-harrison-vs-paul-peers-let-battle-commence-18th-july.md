On the 18th July 2020 longtime World Boxing Organization (WBO) Featherweight King Scott Harrison made his long awaited return to the fray, against Professional Boxing Council (PBC) International Champion Paul Peers in a non-championship 6 round contest on the Lee McAllister promoted 'behind closed doors' LET BATTLE COMMENCE, in association with PAPA JOHN'S PIZZA and NEON ENERGY DRINK sanctioned by the British &amp; Irish Boxing Authority (BIBA) that took place at the Northern Hotel, Aberdeen and originally broadcast live on FITE TV and delayed broadcast on Sports Channel Network in the UK (Freeview Channel 265)
