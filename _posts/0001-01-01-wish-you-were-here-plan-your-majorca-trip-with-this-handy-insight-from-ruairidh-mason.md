A new video shot by an Edinburgh expat shows what Scots can expect if they go on holiday to the Spanish island of Mallorca this summer.  <br>Ruairidh Mason, a travel Youtuber, moved to the island five years ago with his Spanish partner whom he met in the Capital - and they have been hooked on the food and culture ever since.  <br>The 40-year-old has shot a three-minute video which gives you an idea of what it will be like when you get there.
