Amazon is in talks to convert JCPenney and Sears mall stores stores into distribution centers.  <br>The Wall Street Journal reports this move could position Amazon to build out its order capacity amid the pandemic.  <br>Amazon is discussing working with US mall operator Simon Property Group to convert mall stores into hubs to help complete Amazon's online orders.  <br>Business Insider predicts Amazon would have several deals in the works by the end of 2020 to pick up stores from struggling retailers.
