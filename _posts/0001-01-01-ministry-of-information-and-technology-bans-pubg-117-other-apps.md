Ministry of Information &amp; Technology banned popular multiplayer game PLAYERUNKNOWN’S BATTLEGROUNDS (PUBG) in the country. Government has blocked a total of 118 mobile apps which are prejudicial to sovereignty and integrity of India, Defence of India, Security of State and Public Order.  Livik, PUBG MOBILE LITE, WeChat Work &amp; WeChat reading are among the banned mobile apps.
