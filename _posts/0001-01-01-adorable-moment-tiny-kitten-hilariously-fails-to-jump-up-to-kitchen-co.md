This the adorable moment a tiny kitten dreams big - trying to leap up to an impossibly high kitchen counter.   <br>The one-month-old moggy named Star was aiming for the worktop surface at the family home in Yavne, Israel on August 31.  <br>However, she appeared to forget how short her legs were and jumped with all her strength just a few inches off the ground.  <br>Star's owner ,Yafit Weizmann, said: &quot;Star always does silly things and she is brave enough to try and do things beyond her capacity.''  <br>Yafit, a teacher, likened the kitten to a young child trying their hardest to learn at school but needing extra help to reach their goals.  <br>She added: ''Sometimes we all need a lift to get where we want.''
