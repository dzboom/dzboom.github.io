This guy thought of an interesting way to move around instead of just simply walking. He hopped to places on a street trial bike. He came riding his bike and hopped on to a higher platform, balancing the bike on its back tire. He jumped to another platform directly from the first one and spun his bike around. The guy also hopped on huge rocks, park benches, and platforms of different heights on his bike.
