Mahendra Singh Dhoni, former captain of Indian cricket team and two-time World Cup winner, announced his retirement on Saturday (August 15) in his Instagram post. Prior to the announcement of retirement, Mahendra Singh Dhoni wrote a letter to BCCI Chief Saurabh Ganguly. In this letter to the BCCI, Mahendra Singh Dhoni has officially informed about his retirement.
