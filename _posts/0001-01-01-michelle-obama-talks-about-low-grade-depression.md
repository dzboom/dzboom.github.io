Former First Lady Michelle Obama says she's suffering from a &quot;low-grade depression.&quot;   <br>Obama says her depression has been brought on by several factors: the pandemic, race relations in the US and the political strife surrounding it all.  <br>According to CNN Obama talked about the issues on her podcast, &quot;The Michelle Obama Podcast.&quot;  <br>&quot;I'm waking up in the middle of the night because I'm worrying about something or there's a heaviness.&quot;  <br>The former First Lady said exercise usually helps but some days, she says, she feels too low for a work out.
