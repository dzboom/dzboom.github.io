A five-alarm fire burned six commercial buildings in San Francisco on Tuesday morning, the city’s Fire Department said. (July 28)  <br>One firefighter was injured, and dozens of Bay Area denizens were displaced as a result of the flame, which first spread in a commercial area.  <br>The fire was located at 13th Street and South Van Ness Avenue, just off Highway 101.
