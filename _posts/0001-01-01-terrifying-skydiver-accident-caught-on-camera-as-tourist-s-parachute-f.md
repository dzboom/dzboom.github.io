A skydiver fell 20 metres to the ground while attempting a parachute flight from the top of a mountain in Vietnam in this shocking video.   <br>According to eyewitnesses, the man parachuted off the top of a mountain in the northwestern Yen Bai province as part of a paragliding festival on May 31.   <br>He encountered a gust of strong wind blowing in the opposite direction, which caused the man's flight to stall and he plummeted an estimated 20 metres to the ground.   <br>Rescuers quickly approached the place where the man fell and took him to the hospital.   <br>Reportedly he suffered a spinal injury in the fall, but was otherwise not in a life-threatening condition.
