Rain water pours down from a hole at a COVID ward in UP's Bareilly.  <br>coronavirus-designated hospital in Uttar Pradesh's Bareilly was flooded after rainwater gushed through the roof of a ward which was housing Covid positive patients. The cause of the flooding is said to be a pipe leakage, which inundated the hospital amid the rains. The hospital administration took action only when the video of the hospital flooded with rainwater went viral. The video showed water pouring down from a hole in the ceiling as patients look on.
