SpaceX successfully launched an environmental satellite from the Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in the US state of Florida on August 30, 2020. The satellite is designed to help Argentina’s agricultural industry. Two smaller satellites that went up on the same launch were also deployed successfully. The team behind the launch celebrated as the spacecraft’s booster made a perfect touchdown at Cape Canaveral upon returning to Earth.      Video source: AP/SpaceX
