Atumobiles Pvt. Ltd., a Hyderabad-based electric two-wheeler startup has launched their first electric motorcycle, the Atum 1.0 in the market. The Atum 1.0 electric bike is available all across India and is offered with a starting price of Rs 50,000.   <br>The electric powertrain on the motorcycle includes a 48V 250W motor with a portable lithium-ion battery pack. Atumobiles claims the motorcycle to offer a maximum range of 100km on a single charge with a top speed of 25km/h. Charge times for the battery is rated at around 4 hours for a full charge.
