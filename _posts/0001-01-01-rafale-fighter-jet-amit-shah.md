The strength of the Indian Air Force has increased on Wednesday. After a long wait, on Wednesday, five Rafale aircraft from France reached India. Rafale aircraft landed at Ambala airbase in Haryana on Wednesday, where they were welcomed with water salute. Union Home Minister Amit Shah tweeted three quicks when Rafale planes arrived in India.
