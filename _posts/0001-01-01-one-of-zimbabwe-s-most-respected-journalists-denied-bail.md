Prominent journalist Hopewell Chin'ono has been denied bail in Zimbabwe.  <br>CNN reports Chin'ono is facing charges of inciting citizens to 'participate in public violence.'  <br>Arrested alongside opposition leader Jacob Ngarivhume, Chin'ono's been accused of using social media to advocate violent protests.  <br>The award-winning journalist was most recently working on allegations of corruption relating to the health ministry's procurement of COVID-19 supplies.  <br>His representative, Beatrice Mtetwa, said his case would be appealed to Zimbabwe's High Court.
