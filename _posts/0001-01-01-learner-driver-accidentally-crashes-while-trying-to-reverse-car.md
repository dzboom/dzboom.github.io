Shocking footage shows a driver losing control and almost hitting a man while trying to pull out of the garage of a house in Hanoi, Vietnam on June 18.  <br>In the video, a woman is seen entering a car but instead of slowly edging forward she starts to accelerate.  <br>The owner of the CCTV camera, Hong Cuu said his wife insisted to drive the car by herself even though she was just starting to learn.  <br>He said: &quot;I thought she was just joking, but she suddenly jumped in the car and accidentally stepped on the gas pedal so the car went forward.&quot;
