Here's a film clip I put together of The Beatles performing Suzy's Parlour (aka Suzy Parker) on January 9, 1969 during the filming of the Let It Be documentay.  The film only gives us about 0:48 of the full performance, so I inserted clips (and repeated a few) to flesh out the video.  As for the audio, it is sourced from the beyond excellent spectral stereo version found on New Blue Soap.  Special thanks to innerwreckdom for creating this masterpiece.  Of course I hold no rights to the music or images, but hope this video brings some joy to Beatle fans who now have to wait until 2021 for the Peter Jackson documentary.
