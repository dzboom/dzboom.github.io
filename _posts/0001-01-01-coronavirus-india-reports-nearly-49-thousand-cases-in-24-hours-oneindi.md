India remains the third worst-hit country in the world after the United States and Brazil and now has 13,36,861 cases of the coronavirus disease  and 31,358 people have succumbed till date as per the Union health ministry data on Saturday. The country’s tally of the coronavirus disease mounted to more than 1.3 million after 48,916 Covid-19 cases and 757 fatalities due to the viral disease were reported in the last 24 hours. With 32,223 people discharged from hospitals between Friday and Saturday morning, India saw another highest single-day recovery for the fourth consecutive day.
