Lebanon's Prime Minister has told ITV News the people of Beirut have a &quot;right&quot; to be furious after the huge explosion which killed dozens and injured many thousands more. In his first interview since the deadly blast on Tuesday, Hassan Diab said people also had a right to be angry with politicians for what he called three decades of corruption in the country.
