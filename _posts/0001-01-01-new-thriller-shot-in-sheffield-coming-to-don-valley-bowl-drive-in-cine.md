A new British independent thriller, shot on location in Sheffield, is to be screened at a drive-in cinema at Don Valley Bowl.  <br>‘Break’ is a life affirming rags-to-riches story, following the troubled but talented Spencer Pryde (Sam Gittins) - a gifted inner-city kid, wasting his life on petty crime and violence, until a chance encounter with a former ‘eight ball’ pool champion, Vince Qiang (David Yip), and tough guy club owner, Ray (Rutger Hauer), presents him with the opportunity to turn his life around.
