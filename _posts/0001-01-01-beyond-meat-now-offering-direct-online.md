On Thursday, Beyond Meat launched an online portal for buying its products directly.  <br>The company plans to use the platform to test out new items.  <br>The packages of faux meat will cost you between $50 and $72.  <br>Customers will have a variety of options for trying out Beyond’s burger, sausage, and beef.  <br>The company’s chief growth officer, Charles Muth, told Gizmodo they will use the website to introduce new products and gather customer feedback.  <br>In a press release, Beyond Meat said that orders will be shipped in a recyclable box via UPS carbon neutral shipping.
