A group of women was waiting for the heavy rain to stop when they noticed a car stalled in the flooded road. One of them asked the lady who was driving the car if she needed help. She waded through the water along with her family members to bring her out of the car to dry land. She went back again, carried her husband in her arms as he wouldn't have been able to walk through the water. They drove the elderly couple to their home after giving the police their address to return their van stuck on the street.
