As many as 129 animal casualties have been reported in Assam's Kaziranga National Park and Tiger Reserve due to drowning and other reasons, the state government said on Sunday. The casualties include 14 rhinos, five wild buffaloes, eight wild boars, two swamp deer, 95 hog deer, among others.
