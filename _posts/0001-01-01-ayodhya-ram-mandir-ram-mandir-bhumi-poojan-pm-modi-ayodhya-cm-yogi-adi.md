Only a few hours are left in the Bhoomipujan of Ram temple. On Wednesday, during the Bhoomi Pujan program, Prime Minister Modi will lay the foundation of the Ram temple. Meanwhile, SPG has taken over security in Ayodhya. The day of August 5 is going to be historic, so special preparations have been made for this. Ayodhya has been decorated as well as a Diwali-like atmosphere has been created. 5100 urns have been prepared for Bhoomi Poojan and Prime Minister Narendra Modi's reception. Some urns will be used for Bhoomi Poojan and some urns will be placed on both sides of the road from where Prime Minister Narendra Modi's convoy will pass.
