According to Elle magazine the Ellen Degeneres show is under internal investigation after allegation of a hostile and toxic work environment went viral.  <br>Variety reports that WarnerMedia is investigating the show by Telepictures and Warner Bros. Television.  <br>Last week executives sent a memo to staff saying a third party firm would &quot;interview current and former staffers about their experiences on set.&quot;  <br>Staff are happy that the issues on set have become more widely known.  <br>Executive producers Ed Glavin, Mary Connelly and Andy Lassner released a statement to E! News about the claims.  <br>The producers took responsibility for the show and attempted to distance Ellen from the allegations.
