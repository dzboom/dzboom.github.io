Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke actor Sameer Sharma dies by suicide. He was 44. Reportedly, he was at his Mumbai home when he took the big step. Sameer’s body was discovered by a night watchman, who alerted the society residents. Based on the condition of the body, the police suspects Sameer died two days ago. He had rented the Malad flat in February.
