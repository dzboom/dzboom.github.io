(Newser) – Jeffrey Epstein's death is making Ghislaine Maxwell's jail stay all the harder—or so claim her lawyers. In a Monday night court filing, they say Maxwell has been subject to &quot;uniquely onerous conditions&quot; while at the Metropolitan Detention Center in Brooklyn, and that it's clear such treatment &quot;is a reaction to the circumstances surrounding the pretrial detention and death of Mr. Epstein.&quot; The one bit of ease she has experienced is an end to suicide watch, during which she was roused from her sleep every few hours at night, reports NBC News.
