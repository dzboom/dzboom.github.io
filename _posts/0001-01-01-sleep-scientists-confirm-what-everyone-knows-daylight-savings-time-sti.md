The American Academy of Sleep Medicine wants to eradicate daylight saving time for the sake of humanity.  <br>Gizmodo reports the AASM says the twice-a-year shift in clocks not only inconveniences everyone but also increases health problems and car accidents.  <br>They're not alone. Gizmodo reports that in 2020 alone, US lawmakers in at least 32 states introduced legislation to abolish it.  <br>A recent study found that the first week of daylight saving time in the spring was associated with a greater number of fatal car crashes.  <br>The same study also estimated that ending Daylight Savings Time would have prevented more than 600 deadly accidents over a 22-year span.
