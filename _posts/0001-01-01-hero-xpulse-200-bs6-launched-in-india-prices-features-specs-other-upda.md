Hero Xpulse 200 has launched in India at a starting price of Rs 1.12 lakh, ex-showroom (Delhi). Bookings for the motorcycle had begun post the COVID-19 pandemic, with deliveries said to commence soon.   <br>The Hero Xpulse 200 BS6 carries forward an updated version of the same 199cc single-cylinder engine. It produces 17.8bhp and 16.45Nm of peak torque, paired with a standard five-speed gearbox. Watch the video to know more about the new Xpulse 200 BS6 motorcycle in the Indian market.  <br>
