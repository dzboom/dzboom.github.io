In the case of the death of Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput, the difficulties of actress Rhea Chakraborty are increasing. The ED, which has filed a case of money laundering, has sent summons to Sushant's girlfriend Rhea Chakraborty. The ED has called Rhea for questioning on Friday. Not only this, the investigating agency has also questioned Sushant's house manager Samuel Miranda.
