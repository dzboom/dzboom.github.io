Amidst growing cases of Corona virus in the country, now a few days lockdown has started being imposed in some states. In order to prevent the infection of Kovid-19, the Uttar Pradesh government has decided to introduce similar restrictions to the lockdown in the state from Friday to Monday. At the same time, the West Bengal government has also decided something similar. With this, a decision has been taken to put a lockdown in some districts including Patna in Bihar. The entire lockdown has been announced in some areas, including Kerala capital Thiruvananthapuram.
