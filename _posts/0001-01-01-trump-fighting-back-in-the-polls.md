A new Reuters/Ipsos poll puts Joe Biden at 47% to President Donald Trump's 40% among registered voters.  <br>CNN reports the poll was taken partially during the Democratic National Convention and partially during the Republican National Convention.  <br>Biden's advantage in the average of all polls has been consistent, with Biden at 50% or higher.  <br>This new poll is good news because it shows Trump closing the gap.  <br>Axios' Jim Vandehei says the conventional wisdom that Trump can't win is wrong.  <br>CNN pointed out that in 2016, polls showed Hillary Clinton beating Trump.  <br>The general public doesn't buy the polling showing Biden clearly ahead. They think Trump is going to win.
