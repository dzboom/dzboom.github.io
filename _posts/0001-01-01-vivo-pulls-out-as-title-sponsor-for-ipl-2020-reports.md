In a sudden development, Chinese smartphone maker VIVO has pulled out as the title sponsor of the Indian Premier League (IPL) for this year's edition . As per the reports, the Chinese smartphone manufacturing giant pulled out of this season because of the social media backlash in the backdrop of the clashes between Indian and Chinese troops in Ladakh in June.
