Amid the Corona virus crisis, the Indian Railways is considering increasing the number of trains at select routes. Actually, due to Corona virus, a strange trade is being seen of the railway passengers. On all the routes that the railway trains are running, almost two-thirds of them are unable to fill their seats on the routes. However, trains are running full on some routes.
