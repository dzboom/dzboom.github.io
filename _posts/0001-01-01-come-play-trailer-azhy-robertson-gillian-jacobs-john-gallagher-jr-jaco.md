Newcomer Azhy Robertson stars as Oliver, a lonely young boy who feels different from everyone else. Desperate for a friend, he seeks solace and refuge in his ever-present cell phone and tablet. When a mysterious creature uses Oliver’s devices against him to break into our world, Oliver’s parents (Gillian Jacobs and John Gallagher Jr.) must fight to save their son from the monster beyond the screen. The film is produced by The Picture Company for Amblin Partners.  <br>Written and directed by Jacob Chase, the film stars Azhy Robertson, Gillian Jacobs, and John Gallagher Jr.  <br>This is a feature-length film based on Chase's short film, Larry.  <br>Come Play hits US cinemas on 30th October 2020.
