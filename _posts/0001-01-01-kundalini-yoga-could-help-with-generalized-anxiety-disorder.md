A new study shows that practicing yoga is an effective treatment for generalized anxiety disorder.  <br>According to the study, more than half of patients who practiced Kundalini yoga responded well to therapy.  <br>Kunlandi Yoga includes movement, breathing techniques, meditation, and chanting.  <br>Researchers however said that cognitive-behavioral therapy was effective for more patients.  <br>According to UPI, both approaches were more effective than stress education.
