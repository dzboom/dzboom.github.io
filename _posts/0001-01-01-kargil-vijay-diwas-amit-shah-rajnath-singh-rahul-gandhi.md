Today is the 21st anniversary of Kargil Victory Day. It is a symbol of valor and might of the Indian Army. The whole country is paying tribute to the brave soldiers. On this day, brave soldiers of Indian Army forced Pakistani soldiers to flee from the hills of Tiger Hills in Kargil sector. The whole country is paying tribute to the brave soldiers.
