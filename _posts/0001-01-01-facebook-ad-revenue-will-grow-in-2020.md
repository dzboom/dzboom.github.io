Business Insider reports that Facebook's ad revenue will continue to increase this year.  <br>The increase will come despite the growing number of brands pulling ad campaigns amid the coronavirus pandemic.  <br>Net US digital ad revenues at Facebook will increase by 4.9% this year to $31.43 billion.  <br>Facebook came under fire from civil rights groups for failing to remove hate speech and misinformation from its platform.  <br>Some businesses paused advertising on Facebook.  <br>However, it's likely that brands participating won't end up quitting the platform in the long term.
