A college student in Hanoi, Vietnam, has spent up to $23,000 building his own Batmobile.  <br>Nguyen Dac Chung, born in 1998, is a student of architecture and modelled his functional DIY project of the iconic supercar in the Batman film, &quot;The Dark Knight.&quot;  <br>Dac Chung said: “This product is a project that I have been cherishing for three years, and I have been working on it for more than 10 months.&quot;  <br>Cool footage from September 5 shows the intricate design of the Batmobile that measures a nifty 3.6 metres long and 2.3 metres wide.  <br>Dac Chung designed the car in 3D before building it himself. He ordered the tyres and wheels from America and South Korea and installed a full range of vehicle control systems, brakes, and lighting.   <br>Currently, Dac Chung is in the process of finishing the interior.
