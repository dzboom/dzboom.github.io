The mahurat of film Raftaar saw the biggest stars from Bollywood. Steven Segal, Bal Thackeray and veteran actor Dilip Kumar light the lamp. Smita Thackeray accompanies Bala shaeb. Akshay Kumar and Sanjay Dutt can be seen chatting with each other. Later, Manisha Koirala and Juhi Chawla are also called up on stage. Sanjay Dutt is seen with his girlfriend of the time, Rhea Pillai. Originally, Firoz Nadiadwala was about to make an action film “Raftaar”, for which he even signed Akshay Kumar, Sanjay Dutt, Paresh Rawal, and Tabu. However, due to some creative differences with Firoz, Sanjay Dutt walked out from the film. Then Sunil Shetty confirmed his entry in the film.
