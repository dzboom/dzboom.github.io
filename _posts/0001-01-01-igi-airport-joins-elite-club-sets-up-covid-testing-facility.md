IGI Airport has set up a coronavirus testing facility at the multi-level car parking area of its Terminal 3 for arriving international passengers.  The testing facility has been established by the DIAL in collaboration with Genestrings Diagnostic Centre.  The results of samples collected at the facility will be declared within 4-6 hours, and until then, the passengers will be isolated in the waiting lounge or may opt to stay in a hotel.    <br>#IGIAirport #COVIDTestingFacility #InternationalPassenger
