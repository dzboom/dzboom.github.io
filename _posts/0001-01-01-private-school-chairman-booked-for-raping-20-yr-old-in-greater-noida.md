A case has been registered against chairman of a private school for raping a 20-year-old girl in Ecotech-3 area of Greater Noida. DCP (Women Security) of Gautam Buddh Nagar, Vrinda Shukla said, “Chairman of a private school has been booked for raping a 20-year-old girl in Ecotech-3 area of Greater Noida. He raped the girl when she went to take her brother's transfer certificate from the accused. Investigation is underway.”
