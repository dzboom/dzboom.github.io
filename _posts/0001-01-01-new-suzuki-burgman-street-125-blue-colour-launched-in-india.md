Suzuki Motorcycles India has launched a new colour scheme for its premium Burgman Street 125 scooter in the Indian market. The 2020 Burgman Street 125 scooter now features a new 'Pearl Suzuki Medium Blue' paint scheme. The scooter retails at Rs 79,700, ex-showroom (Delhi).  <br>Apart from the new paint scheme, the Suzuki Burgman Street 125 scooter remains unchanged. It is powered by a 124cc air-cooled single-cylinder engine. It produces 8bhp at 6750rpm and 10Nm of peak torque at 5500rpm. Watch the video to know more about the 2020 Burgman Street 125.
