About Face(Quibi) Star: Rosie Huntington  <br>On August 10, model and beauty mogul Rosie Huntington-Whiteley teams up with the titans of the beauty industry for About Face, a six-episode series for Quibi.  <br>In About Face, Huntington-Whiteley explores the entrepreneurship that built the beauty empires of Emily Weiss, Huda Kattan, Jen Atkin, Kylie Jenner, PONY Syndrome and Sir John. From tenacity to triumph, the series tracks the trajectories of the artists who shape tastes and blaze trails. Intimately told, About Face is a celebration of grit, diligence, and ingenuity.   <br>About Face is executive produced by Jane Lipsitz, Dan Cutforth, Dan Volpe, Mark Landsman and Kim Ray for Alfred Street Industries (Project Runway, Top Chef). Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Lily Berg also serve as executive producers.
