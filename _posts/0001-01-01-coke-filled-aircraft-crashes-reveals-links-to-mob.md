CNN reports a light aircraft overloaded with cocaine crashed on take-off on its way to Australia.  <br>Police say the crash exposed a Melbourne-based crime syndicate and led to the arrest of five men with alleged links to the Italian mafia.  <br>The Cesna aircraft was stuffed with more than 1,100 pounds of cocaine. It went down while trying to leave Papua New Guinea on July 26.  <br>Five suspects were arrested in Queensland and Victoria in recent days.  <br>They've all been charged with conspiring to import over 500 kilograms of cocaine and a number of related offenses.
