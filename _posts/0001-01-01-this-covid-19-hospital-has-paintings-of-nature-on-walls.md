A Covid-19 dedicated hospital in Vadodara is undertaking beautification work to paint its interior walls. The paintings here reflect the nature and feel-good factor. In order to promote cleanness and creativity, artists depicted nature through grass. Speaking to ANI, an artist said, &quot;We've tried to depict nature through grass, flowers &amp; pebbles to create positive vibes. We've included elements of spirituality for the feel-good factor.&quot;  <br>
