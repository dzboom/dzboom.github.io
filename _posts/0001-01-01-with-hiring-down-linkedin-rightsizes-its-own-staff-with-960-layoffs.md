Career networking site LinkedIn is full of helpful advice to recruiters and jobseekers alike. In fact, recent figures show a whopping 690 million registered users.  <br>But according to Gizmodo, about 6% of LinkedIn's own staff worldwide is going to be changing their status to 'actively seeking work.'  <br>Writing that LinkedIn hasn't been immune to the global pandemic, CEO Ryan Roslansky announced in a blog post that 960 workers are to be axed.  <br>Most will be cut from the sales and recruitment wings of the company since hiring is down across the board.  <br>When we took a hard look at the business, we decided we needed to make some hard calls. Ryan Roslansky CEO, LinkedIn
