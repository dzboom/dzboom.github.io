In Jabalpur, a Bengal tiger blocked highway traffic for about two hours. This tiger kept sitting on the highway and the vehicles kept waiting. The tiger was relaxing but not ready to move off the highway. People sitting in the vehicles were afraid after they saw a tiger in front of them. Watch video.
