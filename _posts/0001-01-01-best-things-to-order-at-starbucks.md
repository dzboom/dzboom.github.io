A former Starbucks barista spoke to Business Insider and revealed the best things to order from the coffee giant.  <br>Starbucks fanatics love the snickerdoodle hot chocolate (or latte).  <br>The watermelon cold-foam Refresher is a great summer drink.  <br>This mint chocolate chip Frappuccino is refreshing and sweet.  <br>Blonde Americanos are smooth and packed with caffeine.  <br>Most Starbucks baristas should know how to make a London fog, which is perfect for tea drinkers.
