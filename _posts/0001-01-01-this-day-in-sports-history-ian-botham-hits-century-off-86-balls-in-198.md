On August 15, 1981, Ian Botham scripted his third miracle in as many Test matches as he bludgeoned the Australian bowling attack to guide England on their way to a series win. Botham destroyed an attack that included Ashes legends Dennis Lillee and Terry Alderman. Packed full of marvellous strokes, Botham took just 102 balls to score 118. His innings included 13 fours and six sixes including two from Lillee that have to be seen to be believed.
