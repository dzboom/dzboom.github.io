Today is the second death anniversary of former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee. PM Modi reached the Atal Samadhi Sthal to pay homage to him on this occasion. PM Modi and President Ramnath Kovind paid tribute to Atal Bihari Vajpayee at the event organized at Atal Samadhi Sthal 'Always Atal'. Vice President Venkaiah Naidu, Union Defense Minister Rajnath Singh, BJP President JP Nadda, Lok Sabha Speaker Om Birla and many Union Ministers also paid tribute to Vajpayeeji.
