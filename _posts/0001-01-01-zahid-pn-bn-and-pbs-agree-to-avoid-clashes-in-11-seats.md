Perikatan Nasional (PN), BN and Parti Bersatu Sabah (PBS) have agreed not to field their candidates against each other in 11 state seats where they are expected to clash in the Sabah election.  <br>This came after a 90-minute meeting chaired by PN chairperson Muhyiddin Yassin, who is also the prime minister, in what appeared to be the final effort on the eve of nomination day to push for a straight fight among the state opposition parties.
