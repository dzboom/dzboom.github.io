Police personnel have fallen in the capital Delhi .... Delhi Police has taken a big action against the policemen who are not on duty on the occasion of Eid. Delhi Police woman DCP Vijayanta Arya has suspended 36 policemen from North West district who are not on duty on Eid ... This action has been taken when they are not on duty to prepare for the district in Eid.
