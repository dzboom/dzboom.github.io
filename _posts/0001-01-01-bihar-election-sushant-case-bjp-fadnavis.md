After a rather unpleasant experience at a second stint at power in Maharashtra last year, former chief minister Devendra Fadnavis is all set to make a comeback into limelight. The Bharatiya Janata Party is all set to hand over the all-important charge for BJP’s election strategising for Bihar to the 50-year old Fadnavis.
