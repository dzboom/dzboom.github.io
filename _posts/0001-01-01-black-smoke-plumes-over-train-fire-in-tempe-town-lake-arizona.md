A huge fire burst early morning Wednesday (July 29) on a train over a bridge in Tempe Town Lake, Arizona.   <br>The fire was pumping thick black smoke covering the sky with smoke plumes for miles.  <br>Ten train cars derailed at approximately 6:15 a.m. The south side of the bridge collapsed and caught fire.   <br>One person at the park was treated for smoke inhalation.
