The coronavirus pandemic has brought online grocery to the fore says Business Insider.  <br>Consumer interest in avoiding public places and government orders to stay at home helped greatly.  <br>The continued need for groceries has made online grocery delivery services indispensable.  <br>Previously, some consumers resisted the shopping method, to pick their groceries themselves.  <br>But the pandemic has forced many to change their priorities.  <br>The sudden focus on online grocery is set to alter consumer behavior well after the pandemic subsides.
