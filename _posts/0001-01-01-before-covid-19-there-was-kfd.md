In the 1950s, before swine flu, Covid-19 would become household names, Karnataka witnessed the outbreak of one of the modern world's earliest zoonotic diseases in Kysanur, Shimoga : 'Monkey fever' or Kyasanur Forest Disease (KFD). So, what is KFD and does it have any lessons to offer to confront today's zoonotic diseases?
