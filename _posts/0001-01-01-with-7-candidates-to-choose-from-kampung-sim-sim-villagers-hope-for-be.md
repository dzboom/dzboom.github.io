If you have travelled to Sandakan before, you may have also visited Kampung Sim-Sim, a small coastal village famous for its fresh and affordable seafood just 3km away from the Sandakan city centre.  <br>This quiet small village with about two thousand voters recently made national headlines, after the DAP's Sandakan MP Vivian Wong claimed that the Perikatan Nasional government had cancelled a RM4.2 million allocation approved by the former Pakatan Harapan government for repair works of bridges in Kampung Sim-Sim.
