Under the mentorship of actor Akshay Kumar, a Multiplayer Action Game 'FAU-G' is expected to be launched by October end. It would have 20% of its net proceeds donated to ‘Bharat Ke Veer Trust’. Bharat Ke Veer Trust gives homage and support to India’s bravehearts. The development comes just days after the Indian government banned over 100 Chinese apps, including popular multiplayer game PUBG.
