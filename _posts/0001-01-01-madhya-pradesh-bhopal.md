A surprising case has come to light in Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh. Here, a case of leaving the dead body outside the hospital of a person who lost his life from Coronavirus has come to light. On Monday, a patient died outside a hospital. Before death, the patient was being taken to shift to another hospital.
