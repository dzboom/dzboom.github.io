Nepal is planning to send the newly updated map which includes Kalapani, Lipu Lekh and Limpiyadhura as its integral part to the United Nations and Google as well as India. As per the local media reports, the KP Oli government is making the necessary arrangement to publish the map in English and send it to the international community by the middle of August. The Ministry has asked the Department of Measurement to print 4,000 copies of the updated version of Nepal's map in English language and send it to the international community. The Department of Measurement has printed on 25,000 copies of the latest version of the map, which have been distributed around the nation.
