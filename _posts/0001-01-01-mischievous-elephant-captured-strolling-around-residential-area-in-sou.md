This mischievous elephant was caught strolling around the housing quarters of Mettupalayam, a hilly area of Tamil Nadu in southern India.  <br>Footage from August 26 shows the wild elephant strolling past houses, picking up a parked bike, and curiously examining clothes that were drying outside.   <br>Man-animal conflicts are common in the area, local media reported.
