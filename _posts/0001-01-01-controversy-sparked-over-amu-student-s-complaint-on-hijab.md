An engineering student of Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) has got a threat on social media. The Hindu student wrote a post in which she has written about how Hindu girls are being forced to cover themselves in AMU hostels. On this tweet of the student, a fellow student of AMU has sent a threatening reply. The scared student has sent a complaint to AMU administration and police. The AMU administration has set up an investigation in the case, while the police has registered an FIR. Watch video.
