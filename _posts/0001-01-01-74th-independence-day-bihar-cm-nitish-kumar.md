Dis- The country is celebrating the 74th anniversary of independence today. There is an atmosphere of celebration in the entire country regarding Independence Day. Independence celebrations were organized at the historic Gandhi Maidan in Patna in Bihar. Where CM Nitish Kumar hoisted the flag. During this, the Chief Minister inspected the parade.
