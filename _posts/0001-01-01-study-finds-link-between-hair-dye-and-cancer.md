A new study looks to clarify the theorized connection between hair dye and cancer.  <br>The study did not find a link between using hair dye and an increased risk of most types of cancer in women.  <br>It did however find a possible relationship between hair dye and certain forms of breast cancer.  <br>The study also showed possible links between the dyes and ovarian and some types of skin cancer.  <br>That study has kept track of women's health and lifestyle habits since 1976.  <br>More than 120,000 women between the ages of 30 to 55 were initially enrolled.
