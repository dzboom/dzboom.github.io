Have you ever known Kehlani to hold back?  <br>We asked Kehlani to give us her unfiltered opinions on the set of her cover shoot with Bustle. She has a few things to say about what she's fed up with (Trump), who she thinks needs help the most right now (homeless people), and what she loves that other people hate (you'll have to watch to find out). Kehlani is a hard working mother (when her album 'It Was Good Until It Wasn't' came out in quarantine, she shot multiple music videos for it at her home) and sends all her love to other young parents struggling to work, parent, and teach all at the same time in 2020.
