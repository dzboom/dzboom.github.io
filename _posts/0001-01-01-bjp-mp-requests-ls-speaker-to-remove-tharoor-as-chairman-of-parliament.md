Bharatiya Janata Party’s (BJP), Member of Parliament (MP), Nishikant Dubey wrote a letter to Lok Sabha Speaker Om Birla over Facebook row. “I've written another letter to Lok Sabha Speaker today requesting him to persuade Shashi Tharoor to not take meetings of Parliamentary Standing Committee on Information Technology. I've also requested that he be removed from Chairman post,” said Nishikant Dubey to ANI. “We are not favouring any social media. I had said in the Parliament that social media platforms be regulated just like Print and Electronic media, as it propagates a lot of fake and biased news and it infringes on the privacy of people,” BJP MP added.
