Selangor Menteri Besar Amirudin Shari has apologised over a photograph showing a water tanker at his residence, which went viral on social media.  <br>Amirudin said his wife, Masdiana Muhammad, had posted the photo online to show her appreciation for Air Selangor.  <br>“Certain quarters have used this photograph to attack me. However, my wife and I wish to apologise,” he was quoted as saying by Bernama.
