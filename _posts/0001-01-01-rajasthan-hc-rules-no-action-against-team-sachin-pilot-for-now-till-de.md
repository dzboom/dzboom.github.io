Another breather for the Rajasthan Congress rebel Sachin Pilot and another blow for the Gehlot camp as the Rajasthan High Court ruled that No action can be taken against Sachin Pilot and other rebel Congress leaders for now. The court said there will be &quot;status quo&quot; until its decision on the rebels' petition against disqualification notices. The verdict which was expected today has been delayed after the court accepted team Pilot's eleventh-hour request to add the centre to the case so it can weigh in on whether the anti-defection law applies to them.
