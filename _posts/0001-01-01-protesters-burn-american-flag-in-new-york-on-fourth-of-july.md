A group calling themselves the Revolution Club set alight the American flag at Columbus Circle in New York, then marched to Trump Tower and repeated the stunt on Fourth of July.  <br>The small crowd threw the stars and stripes into the center of a circle and ignited the pile in a protest against US President Donald Trump and reject America First policy.   <br>When the group was done with the flag burning in Columbus Circle, an NYPD truck outside Trump Hotel played the national anthem through loud speakers.  <br>On their Twitter profile, NYC Revolution Club describes themselves as, &quot;Fighting the Power &amp; Transforming the People, for Revolution. Based on the New Communism, developed by Bob Avakian.&quot;
