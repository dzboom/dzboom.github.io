Three officers of the Central Bureau Of Investigation (CBI), who are part of the probe into the mysterious death of Sushant Singh Rajput, have independently told the Aaj Tak that the probe team has so far not found any evidence to suggest that Rajput was murdered. The investigation, however, is still open. Sushant Singh Rajput died by alleged suicide on June 14 and was found in his Bandra, Mumbai home. Watch the video.
