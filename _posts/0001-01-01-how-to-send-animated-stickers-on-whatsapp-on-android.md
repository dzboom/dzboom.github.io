How to Send Animated Stickers on WhatsApp on Android?  <br>Note: If you have not updated your WhatsApp, First Update it by going to Play store.  <br>Step 1: Open WhatsApp, and then login to your account.  <br>Step 2: Open any Chat in which you want to send Animated stickers.  <br>Step 3: Tap on 'smiley' icon beside 'Type a message.'  <br>Step 4: Tap on 'Sticker' icon at the bottom, and then tap on '+' button at the top right hand corner.  <br>Step 5: Now, sticker pack having play button in front of it are Animated stickers. To Download, tap on sticker pack. After that, tap on 'Download' button at the bottom. It will be downloaded, and you can find it in 'My Stickers' section. In the same way you can download other Animated stickers if you want.      <br>Step 6: Now to Send Animated stickers, Go back to Chat. Tap on 'smiley' icon beside 'Type a message.' After that, tap on 'sticker' icon. You can find your newly installed Animated sticker packs there. Tap on the pack, and then tap on any 'Animated sticker' that you want to send it.  <br>It's done.  <br>Thanks for watching, please like and subscribe   <br>==============================================  <br>
