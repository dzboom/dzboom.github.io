A Bangalore biker has been caught speeding on his Yamaha R1 superbike in the city amidst a lockdown. The video of him speeding on the Electronic City highway touching speeds close to 300km/h has gone viral. The Bangalore traffic police have since, traced and arrested the rider and seized his motorcycle.
