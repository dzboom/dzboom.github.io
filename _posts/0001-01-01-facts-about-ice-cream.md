Today is National Ice Cream Day.  <br>To celebrate CNN compiled some little known facts about America's favorite frozen treat.  <br>Ice cream sales were boosted by prohibition. New technologies made it easier than ever to manufacture and cool ice cream, sending its popularity soaring.  <br>According to the International Dairy Foods Association. the average American consumes more than 20 pounds of ice cream each year.  <br>What's the most popular flavor in the United States?  <br>According to the International Dairy Foods Association it's vanilla.
