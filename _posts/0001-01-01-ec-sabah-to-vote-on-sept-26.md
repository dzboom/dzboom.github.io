The Election Commission has fixed Sept 26 for the Sabah state election while nominations will be held on Sept 12.  <br>This was announced by the commission after meeting returning officers in Magellan Sutera Resort, Kota Kinabalu, this evening.  <br>The campaigning period will last 14 days. Early voting will be held on Sept 22.  <br>The voter list being used was updated as of July 29.
