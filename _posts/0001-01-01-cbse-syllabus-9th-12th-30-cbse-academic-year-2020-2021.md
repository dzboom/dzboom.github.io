Due to Corona virus epidemic, all schools across the country are closed since the month of March. There has been a lot of loss of students' education due to the long closure of schools. In order to reduce the pressure of students in view of this loss in studies, the syllabus of the school has been reduced by 30 percent for the CBSE Academic Year 2020-2021. Human Resource Development Minister Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank has informed himself about this.
