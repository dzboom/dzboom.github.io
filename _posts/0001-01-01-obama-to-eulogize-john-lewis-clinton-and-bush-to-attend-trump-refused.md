Former President Barack Obama will give the eulogy at US Rep. John Lewis' funeral.  <br>The funeral will take place on Thursday, according to reports at CNN.  <br>Former Presidents Bill Clinton and George W. Bush will attend the service.  <br>Current President Donald Trump said Monday that he wouldn't pay his respects to Lewis.  <br>Trump has refused to honor Lewis while he lay in state at the US Capitol or participate in his service.  <br>Still, the three former commanders in chief each have a role in the service and have paid their respects.
