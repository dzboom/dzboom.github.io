A fire broke out in the middle of the afternoon on Thursday (July 30) in the forest of Chiberta in Anglet near Biarritz, France.  <br>Planes bombers of water work their way to stop the fire. Several houses have been destroyed, and residents are being evacuated.   <br>There are no injuries reported, and the cause of the fire is unknown.
