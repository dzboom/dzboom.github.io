Nag Panchami is considered very important in Hinduism. Which is falling on Saturday, 25th of July this time. On this day, due to Uttara Phalguni and Hasta Nakshatra, Moon's position is Virgo. This day is considered to be the best for worshiping Nag due to the yoga called Parigha and Shiva. On this day, the serpent is worshiped and there is a tradition of feeding snakes.
