Dollhouse, a community of ADMU students, explains that they are not a college organization. Rather, they are a tight-knit family composed of members of the LGBTQ+ community  as well as allies to the cause that hopes to give its members a sense of belongingness and a safe space to freely express who they are and who they want to be. One member shares, “they are the people I can really call ‘my home.’” Learn more about Dollhouse from three of its members.
