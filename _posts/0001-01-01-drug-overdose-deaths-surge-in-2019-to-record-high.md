Drug overdose deaths in America surged in 2019.  <br>The increase in deaths last year by 4.8%, erased the slight decline seen in 2018.  <br>The jump in fatalities has set a new record high for the nation.  <br>Fentanyl and other synthetic opioids are involved in more than half of all overdose deaths.  <br>According to CNN, cocaine and methamphetamine-related deaths also on the rise.  <br>Opioid prescribing however has dropped since the CDC issued opioid prescribing guidelines in 2016.
