A unique rock formation in Montana's Whitehall creates a bizarre ringing noise when struck with a hammer.  <br>Filmer Nickolas Warner talks through his amusement after banging several of the rocks to create a chiming sound.  <br>Warner said: &quot;These ringing rocks in southwest Montana are a truly bizarre, geological oddity.   <br>&quot;Sitting about twenty minutes off highway 90, this massive pile of rusty coloured boulders don’t seem too out of place, that is until you hit them with a hammer and they let out a loud ringing sound.   <br>&quot;Each rock produces a different tone, and one could easily spend hours drumming away one the rocks.   <br>&quot;Mysteriously, when one rock is taken away from the rest, it loses its ringing abilities.&quot;  <br>This footage was filmed on July 22.
