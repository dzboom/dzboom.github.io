Former PKR leaders Azmin Ali and Zuraida Kamaruddin, who recently joined Bersatu after defecting from Pakatan Harapan, have been appointed as the party's supreme council members.  <br>According to a statement by Bersatu secretary-general Hamzah Zainudin, the decision was made by party president Muhyiddin Yassin during the supreme council meeting yesterday.
