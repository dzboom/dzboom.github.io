Olivia is a fictional pig character in a series of children's books written and illustrated by Ian Falconer. The Olivia book series was inspired by Ian Falconer's niece, Olivia. &quot;I was just entranced by her,&quot; he stated. &quot;I wanted to make a little present for her, so I started working on the Olivia book.&quot;   <br>Ian Falconer won a lot of awards for the Olivia series. These include:  <br>Caldecott Honor for Olivia, 2000  <br>Parents' Choice 2000, Gold Award Winner  <br>Nick Jr. Books, Best Book of 2001  <br>American Library Association, Notable Children's Books of 2001, for Olivia.  <br>Child's Best Book of 2001  <br>Los Angeles Times Best Books of 2000 &amp; 2001  <br>Publishers Weekly, Best Books of 2000 &amp; 2001  <br>American Library Association, Notable Children's Books of 2002, for Olivia Saves the Circus.  <br>BookSense Illustrated Children's Book of the Year, 2002, for Olivia Saves the Circus.  <br>Voted &quot;Favorite Illustrator&quot; for Olivia Helps with Christmas by 50,000 children at the Children's Choice Book Awards, 2008
