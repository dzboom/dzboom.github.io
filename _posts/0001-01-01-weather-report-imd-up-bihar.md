As monsoon rains batter southern parts of the country, Kerala will continue to experience heavy showers during the next 24 hours, Indian Meteorological Department said Saturday. Coastal and interior Karnataka and Tamil Nadu will experience similar conditions for the next 3 days. Red and orange alerts have been issued in various districts of monsoon hit Kerala. For today, a red alert has been issued in Idukki, Malappuram, Kozhikode, Wayanad &amp; Kannur districts.
