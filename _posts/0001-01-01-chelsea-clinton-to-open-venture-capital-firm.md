Business Insider reports that Chelsea Clinton is forming her own venture capital firm.  <br>The firm will be called Metrodora Ventures.  <br>It was registered in April and has its own Twitter account.  <br>Clinton hasn't decided whether to commit to the firm full-time, Axios reports.  <br>On Twitter, Metrodora Ventures describes itself as &quot;a values conscious venture capital firm focused on health and learning businesses.&quot;   <br>A spokesperson for Clinton did not immediately respond to Business Insider's request for comment.
