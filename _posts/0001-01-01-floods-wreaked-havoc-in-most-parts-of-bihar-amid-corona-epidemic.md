Most parts of Bihar, people are facing heavy problems due to rain and floods amid Corona epidemic. Rivers are in spate and people have become homeless in low-lying areas in many districts. Mahananda, Koshi, Kamla Balan, Bagmati, Gandak and Budhi Gandak rivers have become vast. Life in the Terai areas of Muzaffarpur, Motihari, Darbhanga and Sitamarhi has become deranged and people are forced to live on the highway. #Flood #Bihar #CoronaEpidemic
