Piaggio has launched an online sales platform for their commercial vehicle range in the Indian market. Customers of Piaggio’s commercial vehicles will be able to view, book and check prices of their interested models, while also being able to apply for loans.  <br>The online sales platform from Piaggio is an industry-first in the commercial vehicle segment in India. It comes a month after a similar digital sales platform was introduced for their Vespa and Aprilia products.
