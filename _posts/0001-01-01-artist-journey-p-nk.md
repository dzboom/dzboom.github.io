P!NK's career lifted off in 2000 after she dropped her debut album and went on a supporting tour with NSYNC. Her 1st official concert tour launched in 2002 - The Party Tour. Since then, P!NK has become famous for acrobatic performances and anthemic pop songs. To date, P!NK has performed over 900 concerts across 7 concert tours.
