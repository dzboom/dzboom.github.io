Hundreds of students demonstrated at Parliament Square in London on Sunday (August 16th) against the markdown of their predicted A-Level grades before marching to the Department For Education.   <br>Many have had their university hopes dashed and fear that even if their results are reviewed they will not be able to attend their chosen universities.
