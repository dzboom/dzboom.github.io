Tourism Ministry to present proposal on reopening travel to green zones

27 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister Datuk Seri Nancy Shukri said the proposal to reopen travel routes with green zone destinations abroad will be presented to the National Security Council next week.
She added that the ministry was in talks with ambassadors of other nations on reopening travel routes, adding that these countries were Japan and Singapore, after launching the Cuti-Cuti Malaysia TV promotional campaign on Wednesday.
