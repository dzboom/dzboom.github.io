Kartik Aaryan REJECTS A Multi-Crore Deal With A Chinese Company?

14 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Bollywood actors don’t just act but influence people as well. And that is why they are often seen promoting one product or the other. We all are aware of the current situation in the Ladakh region as there are rising tensions between India and China. And after the deadly clash between soldiers of both countries in the Galwan valley, people started boycotting Chinese products and even the government announced a ban on 59 Chinese apps. This is when Federation of Western India Cine Employees made a plea to the celebrities to not endorse any Chinese products as Nation comes first. And seems like Kartik Aaryan has quit his association with a Chinese brand. Kartik was the brand ambassador of mobile brand Oppo but after the rising tensions between both the countries, he has called off his multi-crore deal. And this can be evident in a latest picture posted by Kartik in which he can be seen holding the cell phone of a different brand and not of Oppo. Several other actors like Deepika Padukone, Ranbir Kapoor, Katrina Kaif, Ranveer Singh, Aamir Khan, Vicky Kaushal, Sara Ali Khan too promote mobile phones made in China. We now have to see when they will take the much needed step.
