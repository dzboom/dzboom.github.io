The post mortem examination report on Saturday revealed that the eight policemen were shot dead with police weapons which were looted by notorious history-sheeter Vikas Dubey and his henchmen during the failed raid at his residence in Bikru village under Chaubeypur police station area in Kanpur in the early hours of Friday.
