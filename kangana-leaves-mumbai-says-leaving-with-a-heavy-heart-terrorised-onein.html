Kangana leaves Mumbai, says 'leaving with a heavy heart, terrorised' Oneindia News

14 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After locking horns with the Maharashtra Government over demolition of her office in her absence, Actor Kangana Ranaut left Mumbai this morning saying she was doing so with a heavy heart after being terrorised" with attempts to demolish her office. Reiterating her comment she said her analogy about PoK was bang on. After landing in Chandigarh for her return to Himachal Pradesh, she tweeted that her security had reduced significantly and people were greeting her with joy. she took to twitter targeting the Sena as well as Congress president Sonia Gandhi, saying that It seems this time she was saved.
