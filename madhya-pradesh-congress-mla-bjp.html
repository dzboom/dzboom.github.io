Madhya Pradesh : Congress के तीसरे खेमें में सेंधमारी, दो और MLA BJP में शामिल वनइंडिया हिंदी

22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Political turmoil has started once again in Madhya Pradesh. The BJP has also started a breach in the camp of Arun Yadav following the camp of Kamal Nath and Digvijay as a strategy. Within the last 10 days, 2 Congress MLAs have joined BJP. Which Pradyuman Singh Lodhi and Sumitra Devi. At the same time, Minister Govind Singh Rajput claimed that some more MLAs are in the queue.
