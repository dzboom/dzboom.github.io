Rosmah allegedly paid cybertroopers RM100k a month to guard her image online

10 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Rosmah Mansor, the wife of former prime minister Najib Abdul Razak, was allegedly very conscious about her image and purportedly forked out RM100,000 a month to fund cybertroopers who were tasked to counter negative content about her on the internet.
This according to her former aide Rizal Mansor who testified in Rosmah’s graft trial today involving a solar hybrid project which she allegedly helped Jepak Holdings to secure in exchange for kickbacks.
