Workers lay underground cable in Delhi - archaic method for modern tech

05 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Indian workers laying an underground cable in South Delhi, India. Heave ho - and the Volga Boatmen come to mind…? With the introduction of new internet and communications technologies, the need for laying high-end, high-capacity internet and other cables such as fiber / fibre optic has stepped up in India, but it’s still done in a rather archaic and basic fashion!
