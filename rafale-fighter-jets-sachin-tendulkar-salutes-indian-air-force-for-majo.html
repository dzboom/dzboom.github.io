Rafale Fighter Jets: Sachin Tendulkar salutes Indian Air Force for major upgrade वनइंडिया हिंदी

31 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Rafale in India, Rafale, the world’s most dangerous fighter aircraft, has joined the Indian Air Force. Five fighter jets from France have reached India. Indian cricketers have also given their reaction by tweeting Rafael planes in India. Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar has also written in the tweet that with Rafael’s arrival in India, Sachin tweeted- Heartiest congratulations to #IndianAirForce for adding the state-of-the-art fighter jet Rafale, to our fleet.It’s a massive upgrade for our Defence Forces who are tirelessly protecting our nation in the skies.
