Guy Gives Priceless Reaction to his Elder Brother Surprising him With New Laptop During Quarantine

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This guy broke his old laptop beyond repairable during the quarantine. He then used his phone for online classes and watched cartoons on TV or slept during his free time. His elder brother decided to save up for him and surprised him with a new one. When he disclosed his surprise to him, he looked extremely excited.
