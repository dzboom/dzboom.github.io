Andrew Eborn is joined by entertainment titans Ian freeman & Mike Read to celebrate 115 years of The Magic Circle & the work of The British Plaque Trust
On 1st July 1905, at a meeting held in the large hall above the renowned Pinoli Restaurant in Wardour Street, 23 amateur and professional magicians formed a new magic club that was to become The Magic Circle. The meeting was chaired by the Belgian magician Servais Le Roy. Fast forward a hundred years to Wednesday April 13th 2016,and hundreds of magicians, other celebrities and members of the public gathered outside what was Pinoli’s at 17 Wardour Street, to celebrate this momentous occasion with the unveiling of a British Plaque Trust, blue plaque, Dynamo.
