Girl Plays Harp in Forest to Beat Quarantine Blues and Attracts Deer

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This girl dealt with the quarantine blues musically by playing a harp in the forest and attracted a deer who had come looking for food. As she concentrated solely on the instrument, she didn’t seem to realize the animal’s presence while they enjoyed the tune of her harp but got startled by the commotion as they retreated into the woods.
