Villagers puzzled by mysterious noise heard in southern China mountain range

11 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A mysterious noise left locals puzzled after it was heard in southern China’s mountains.
In the video, captured in Weining Yi, Hui, and Miao Autonomous County in Guizhou Province on July 1, the mysterious noise was heard among the mountains which attracted nearby villagers.
The cause of the noise is being investigated. The video was provided by local media with permission.
