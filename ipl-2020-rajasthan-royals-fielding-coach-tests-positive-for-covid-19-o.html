IPL franchise Rajasthan Royals on Wednesday informed that its fielding coach Dishant Yagnik has tested positive for Covid-19.Dishant, who was a wicket-keeper batsman for Rajasthan in domestic cricket and has also represented the franchise in IPL, is currently in his hometown Udaipur and has been advised to get admitted to hospital for his 14 day quarantine.
