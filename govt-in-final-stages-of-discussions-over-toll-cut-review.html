Govt in final stages of discussions over toll cut review

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The government is in the final stages of discussions with various groups on reviewing the toll reduction on PLUS highway, which was implemented by the previous administration, the Dewan Rakyat was told.
Minister in the Prime Minister’s Department (Economy) Datuk Seri Mustapa Mohamed said that in reply to a supplementary question from Tony Pua (PH-Damansara) on the need for a review of the toll rates on Thursday.
