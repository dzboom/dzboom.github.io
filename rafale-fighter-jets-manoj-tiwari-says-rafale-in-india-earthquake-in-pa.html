Report

Rafale, the world’s most dangerous fighter aircraft, has joined the Indian Air Force. Five fighter jets from France have reached India. Indian cricketers have also given their reaction by tweeting Rafael planes in India. Indian cricketer Manoj Tiwari has also written in the tweet that with Rafael’s arrival in India, there will be earthquake in neighboring countries.
