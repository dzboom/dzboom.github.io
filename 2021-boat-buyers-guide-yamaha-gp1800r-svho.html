2021 Boat Buyers Guide: Yamaha GP1800R SVHO

13 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The GP series from Yamaha is the most dominant name in PWC racing history, and this Wave Runner combines that spirit with a ton of high-end amenities. The ergonomics on the GP1800R SVHO are top-notch… Yamaha narrowed up the saddle and widened up the foot wells so you can really dig into turns. It would be easy to pigeonhole this PWC as a racing machine, but it’s so much more than that.
