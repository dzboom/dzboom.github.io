How to wear your mask correctly

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

When you are wearing a mask, make sure that you are putting it on with clean hands. Once it’s on, pinch the wire on the bridge of your nose to make sure it is secure. Your mask should cover both your nose and your mouth, and should fit snugly so that there is the least space between the mask and your face.
