U.S. Women's Amateur: Grace Summerhays Follows in Family Footsteps (Golf)

06 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Grace Summerhays, of Scottsdale, Ariz., the younger sister of 2019 U.S. Junior Amateur champion Preston Summerhays, and daughter of noted swing coach Boyd Summerhays, is relishing her first U.S. Women’s Amateur this week.
