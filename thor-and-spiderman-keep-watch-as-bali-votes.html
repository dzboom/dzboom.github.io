'Thor' and 'Spiderman' keep watch as Bali votes

23 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Indonesians visiting a polling station on the resort island of Bali were treated to an extra level of security in the form of two superheroes on Wednesday as the country underwent presidential and parliamentary elections.
Gusti Ketut Arsa Wijaya who dressed as Thor said he wanted to "motivate and entertain" all voters and make them "feel at ease" while casting their votes.
