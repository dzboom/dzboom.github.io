Vivo X50 Pro: Should You Buy A Premium Vivo 5G Phone Worth Rs. 49,990?

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Vivo’s camera-centric X-series premium smartphones have finally paved their way to the Indian soil. The Vivo X50 has been launched in two variants- 8GB + 128GB at Rs. 34,990 and 8GB + 256GB at Rs. 37,990. While the Vivo X50 Pro has been introduced in just one variant with 8GB RAM + 256GB at Rs. 49,990. Needless to say, the new smartphones mark the brand’s second innings in the Indian premium price-segment.
The Vivo X50-series focuses on delivering a premium design and a pro-grade camera experience. The smartphone comes equipped with a miniaturized gimbal camera system for smooth and steady videos, zooming up to 60x, and to capture bright pictures even in extreme low-light scenarios.
