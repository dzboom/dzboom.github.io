Vladimir Putin साल 2036 तक के लिए बने Russia के President, PM Modi ने दी बधाई वनइंडिया हिंदी

10 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

There was a cellphone dialog between Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Russian President Vladimir Putin. In this, PM Modi congratulated Putin on being elected President for 2036. At the identical time, Putin mentioned that the strategic relationship of the 2 nations can be additional strengthened.Watch video,
