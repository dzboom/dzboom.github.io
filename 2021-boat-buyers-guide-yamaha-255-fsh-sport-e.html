2021 Boat Buyers Guide: Yamaha 255 FSH Sport E

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Yamaha’s new 255 FSH Sport E is a 25-foot version of their 190 and 210 FSH boats, which were so successful inshore they decided to expand to a center-console with offshore capabilities. The new hull gives great lift, fast acceleration, and––because of the articulated keel––excellent tracking, which is going to help you manage following in head seas when you’re working through rough weather. And like any good offshore boat, the Yamaha 255 FSH Sport E has excellent electronics and more.
