How To Make Paneer Papad Delight - POPxo Yummy

08 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Paneer Papad Delight are are basically paneer fritters made with bread crumbs and papad. The snack is very easy to make and is a delicious crispy treat that every member of your family can enjoy.
Watch today’s episode of POPxo Yummy to make paneer papad delight from the comfort of your home.
Step 1) Take 4 tbsp tomato sauce in a bowl
Step 2) Add 1 tbsp mustard sauce and 1 tbsp chilli sauce
Step 3) Add 1 tbsp red chilli powder and salt to taste and mix well
Step 4) Add 2 tbsp corn flour and mix again.
Step 5) Add 250 grams of paneer cubes and marinate the paneer in the mixture
Step 6) Crush 10-12 papads and roll the paneer cubes in them
Step 7) Fry the paneer cubes and serve with sauce of your choice.
