CanBeat 3:60 Challenge - Episode 1 - Feat. Candy Rookies Allie, Raffy, and Sofia

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

It’s the first episode of the CanBeat 3/60 Challenge! This week, we’re having Candy Rookies Allie, Raffy, and Sofia in a round of song challenge! Who’s going to take home this week’s crown?
CanBeat 3/60 Challenge is a new series where three players battle in different challenges under 60 seconds to become the episode’s CanBeat Contender. The previous episode’s winner will get to join a new set of contenders in the next episode to defend their crown. Wanna become a contender? Stay tuned for a chance to join!
