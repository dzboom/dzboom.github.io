UK eight-year-old shows off his drumming skills

04 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This eight-year-old boy from Lincolnshire, England has incredible drumming skills.
The clip, filmed on August 1 at Millers Sports and Music Bar, in Chapel St Leonards, shows the eight-year-old Jack drumming along to the performer on a bench.
"My wife, Sara Larder, known on stage as Suki Soul, was performing at a gig when she noticed Jack dancing, and air drumming along with me. I realised he was catching the attention of the audience, my wife clearly thought he had something special about him, seeing that he could really feel the music.
"Next thing we knew his sister had brought his drumsticks to him and he started drumming on the table, we could tell he was an amazing drummer and my wife wanted to highlight to the audience his ability. He stayed and played with her for well over an hour non-stop, to different songs and genres!" the filmer Ian told Newsflare.
