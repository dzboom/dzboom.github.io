We Compare Body Transformations: Plastic V Muscle MIAMI MUSCLE

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

IN THE latest episode of Miami Muscle, two women who have both undergone big transformations meet for the very first time. It took Rahki Giovanni years to transform herself from someone with poor mental health, low confidence and unhealthy habits, into someone who trains hard and loves their body. Although most of this transformation took place in the gym, Rahki does not make a secret of the breast augmentation she has undergone. And the fitness influencer also confirms that plastic surgery in a place like my Miami can be very appealing; “Miami is almost the holy grail of plastic surgery, it’s very popular, it’s the norm. I trained for years for my body, but I still felt like I wanted a boob job, to feel better.” Klara Lima, 29, otherwise known as ‘Kitana Savage’ understands this dilemma all too well. The Miami based rapper and model takes her appearance very seriously and has had four Brazilian butt-lifts and four breast enhancements, spending over $1Million on her transformation. Having overcome eating disorders and a drug addiction, as well as the death of her parents at a young age, Kitana says she is now the woman she always wanted to be. Rahki goes to meet Kitana at her home, to find out more about her story, and although the two women dealt with their transformations in different ways, she is surprised at just how similar they are.
