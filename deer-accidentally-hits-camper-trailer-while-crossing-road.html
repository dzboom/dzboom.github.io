Deer Accidentally Hits Camper Trailer While Crossing Road

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This deer was trying to cross a road when a camper trailer drove by. However, he miscalculated the distance and hopped to reach the other side of the road. The deer landed on the gap between the trailer and the car pulling it, crashing the camper. Fortunately, he wasn’t hurt and went on his way after cracking the trailer open.
