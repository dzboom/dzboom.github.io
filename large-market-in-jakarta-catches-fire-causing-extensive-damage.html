Large market in Jakarta catches fire causing extensive damage

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Timbul Tomang market in western Jakarta caught fire in the early hours of August 7 causing extensive damage.
Footage shows residents in panic as the sky glows orange through a small thoroughfare.
The blaze prompted 25 units of the local fire brigade to tackle the flames.
