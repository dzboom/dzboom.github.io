A socialist tax plan in Congress was written by Trump in 1999 [Mic Archives]

02 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

There’s a radical socialist tax plan in Congress right now — and it was written by Donald Trump in 1999. He outlined the bill 18 years ago when he was flirting with the notion of running for president. Now, Rep. Juan Vargas, a Democrat, has introduced Trump’s exact 1999 plan in Congress. In a statement, he said, “In the spirit of bipartisanship, I am introducing the Donald J. Trump Wealth Tax Act of 2017 to allow the president to follow through on his original idea.”
