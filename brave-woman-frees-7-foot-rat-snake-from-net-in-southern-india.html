Brave woman frees 7-foot rat snake from net in southern India

25 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A brave woman painstakingly cut free a 7-foot-long rat snake from a net in southern India on Saturday (August 22nd).
Footage filmed in the Mangalore area of Karnataka state shows Rajani using scissors to cut the net around the reptile.
It took around 90 minutes to free the non-venomous snake which was later released in a nearby forest.
