India's GDP Shrinks -23.9%, 1996 నుండి ఎప్పుడూ లేనంతగా V Shaped Recovery Oneindia Telugu

06 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

On Monday, data released by the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation showed that India’s Gross Domestic Product growth rate had contracted by 23.9% for the April to June quarter. But From July India is witnessing a V shaped recovery, finance ministry Said. India is witnessing a ‘V’ shaped recovery, the finance ministry said on Friday, pointing to a clutch of indicators such as manufacturing purchasing managers’ index (PMI), auto sales, railway freight, steel and power consumption, e-way bills, highways toll and retail financial transactions.
