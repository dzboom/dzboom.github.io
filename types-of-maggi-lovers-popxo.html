Types Of Maggi Lovers - POPxo

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Maggi isn’t just an ordinary, easy to make food item in India. It is in fact, an emotion that Indians strongly resonate with. We’ve all grown up eating & loving Maggi and irrespective of the plethora of other noodle brands available in the market, it is Maggi that will always be our comfort food.
Interestingly, while our love for Maggi maybe the same but we’re all different types of Maggi lovers. From the ‘Gourmet Chefs’ to the ‘Experimenter’ kinds, we all have different ways of enjoying our Maggi and in today’s episode of POPxo Types of Maggi Lovers, we showcase some of the most common kinds.
Watch the video and share with us in the comments section below what kind of Maggi Lover are you?
