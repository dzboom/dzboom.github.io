26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Earlier this year, the legendary Tarrus Riley returned with a new single, "Healing." It’s at the top of his queue for his Digital FORT set, in which the reggae artist draws from selections across his catalog, including "Superman" and "Gimme Likkle One Drop." Backed by the power of the Blak Soil Band, it’s not to be missed.
