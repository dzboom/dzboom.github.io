CBSE 10th-12th Result : 15 जुलाई 2020 को आएगा CBSE Board का रिजल्ट वनइंडिया हिंदी

11 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Central Board of Secondary Education i.e. CBSE has recently announced the result date. Please tell that CBSE Board Class 10th, 12th exam results will be declared on July 15, 2020. All the candidates eagerly waiting for the CBSE result 2020 should keep a close watch on the official website of the board
