Trail camera in US records adorable moment deer family drinks at pond

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A trail camera in rural Idaho recorded the adorable moment a family of wild mule deer came to drink at a pond.
"When mother shows up, one fawn attempts to snag a quick snack from her udder, but she isn’t interested in that and walks off," said the filmer Rick Tolman.
The cute scene was captured in Adams County on July 1st.
