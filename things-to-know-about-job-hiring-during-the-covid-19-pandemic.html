Things to Know About Job Hiring
During the COVID-19 Pandemic Job interviews can be nerve-wracking
even under the best of circumstances.
These tips can help you to make your
best impression during this pandemic. The hiring process will most
likely be 100 percent virtual. Make sure to check your tech,
set a professional scene and plan ahead so
that roommates or pets don’t interrupt. It’s possible there
will be more than
one round of interviews. Because the process is now online,
it may be necessary to do several calls
with different people. It’s good to ask
about next steps and what to expect. Collaboration and
communication skills are
super important. Liz Keyes, SHRM-CP,
via The Muse Interviewers will take stock
of how you face challenges. It’s a good idea to prepare stories
from your background about how
you’ve been able to meet unexpected
challenges you’ve had to face. The process could be bumpy. It’s important to be open, patient and honest
about your needs in this time of
remote-hiring and remote-work expansion.
