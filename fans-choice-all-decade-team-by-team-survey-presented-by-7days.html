Fans Choice All-Decade Team by Team survey, presented by 7DAYS

17 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The decade between 2010 and 2020 was one of historic growth in the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague, providing fans of every club plenty to celebrate. Over 10 years and nearly 2,500 games, a non-stop parade of top players gave their supporters plenty to cheer about, night after night. Now, with the Fans Choice All-Decade Team by Team survey, presented by 7DAYS, fans can show their appreciation to all their favorite clubs!
