Rajasthan Board 10th class exam results have been declared…. Rajasthan Education Minister Govind Singh Dotasara announced on Monday that the results will be released on Tuesday. Earlier Rajasthan Board has declared the results of 12th. The remaining examinations of Rajasthan Board 10th were held at the end of June
