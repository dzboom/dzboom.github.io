Mahindra Mojo BS6 Bookings Open Expected Launch Date, Prices, Features & Other Details

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Mahindra Mojo BS6 bike bookings have begun across the country. Interested customers can now book the upcoming BS6-compliant Mahindra Mojo for a token amount of Rs 5000, ahead of its launch next month.
The new Mahindra Mojo BS6 motorcycle will receive a host of new features, along with an updated 295cc single-cylinder engine. Watch the video to know more about the upcoming Mahindra Mojo BS6 bike.
