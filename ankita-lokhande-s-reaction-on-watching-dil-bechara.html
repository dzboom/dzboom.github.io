Ankita Lokhande's Reaction On Watching Dil Bechara

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Late actor Sushant Singh Rajput’s last film Dil Bechara released yesterday. And social media was filled with posts in the actor’s memory and his heart winning performance. Several people from within the television and film industry also shared posts on social media. One such post came from Sushant’s ex-girlfriend Ankita Lokhande. Ankita shared a motion poster of the film and wrote a very emotional caption. She wrote, “From Pavitra Rishta To Dil Bechara, one last time.” A few days ago, she had shared a picture from her temple where she lit a candle in Sushant’s memory and wrote, “Hope, Prayers and Strength. Keep smiling wherever you are!” Ankita and Sushant met each other on the sets of their daily soap ‘Pavitra Rishta’. The much loved on-screen couple soon started dating in real life as well. The duo dated for six long years but parted ways in 2016.
