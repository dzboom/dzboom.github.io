PM CARES Fund को NDRF में Transfer करने की मांग खारिज, Supreme Court ने क्या कहा? वनइंडिया हिंदी

26 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Supreme Court on Tuesday dismissed a petition seeking the transfer of money in PM CARES Funds to National Disaster Relief Fund. The SC said that fresh national disaster plan for coronavirus COVID-19 is not needed and observed that funds collected in PM CARES fund are meant for charitable trust.Watch video,
