Man Makes Music Video Depicting Weekly Quarantine Routine With Family

22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

To depict the challenges of quarantine life of being stuck indoors, he made a hilarious song covering his weekly routine. He is seen binge eating, playing video games, tracking online orders, doing various activities with his kids, and drinking to deal with the stress.
