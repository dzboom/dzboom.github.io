Woman drags gas cylinder on road after accidentally igniting it in China

21 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A woman dragged a gas cylinder on a road after she accidentally ignited it when using the fire to clean duck feathers in southern China.
In the video, filmed in the city of Yueyang in Hunan Province on July 28, a woman is seen holding a pipe attached to a gas cylinder to try to use the fire to clean duck feathers.
However, the pipe pointed towards the gas cylinder and ignited it. The woman then attempted to blow the fire out immediately.
Since it did not work, she kicked the cylinder over onto the ground and dragged it along the road.
Fortunately, the fire was finally put out.
The video was provided by local media with permission.
