Sand artist creates cute portrait of puppy in cool timelapse

07 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A sand artist from Toronto, Canada captured through timelapse the creative process behind a puppy portrait.
Footage shows the portrait being created with different coloured sand within a jar using a small tool that distributes sand along a tracing of the picture on the glass.
The filmer, James Sun, wrote: "I made this piece of art in a glass jar using coloured sand. It is a rottweiler with cute eyebrows running towards you."
Sun creates many different portraits online under the moniker @fallinginsand.
