18 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Sanju, Bollywood’s Bad Boy is mobbed by the media in front of a sound recording studio. On being pressed about his new venture in singing, as opposed to acting, he shares more. The Indian composer Anand is heard joking with Sanjay Dutt. When asked about his interest in singing , he admits he enjoys singing.
