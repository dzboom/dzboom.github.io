Muhyiddin: Cops will bring Jho Low back even if he looks like a 'bear'

22 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The police will get fugitive financier Low Taek Jho back to Malaysia even if he changed his appearance to look like a "bear" to evade arrest, says Home Minister Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin.
Muhyiddin told reporters at Parliament lobby on Monday (Nov 4), stressing that the government would not give up effort to bring the fugitive back to the country to face charges.
Asked if Jho Low would be brought back to Malaysia by the year&apos;s end or within a specific time frame, Muhyiddin said he could not give a definite answer on this.
