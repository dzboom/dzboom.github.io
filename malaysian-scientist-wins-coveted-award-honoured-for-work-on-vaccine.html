Malaysian scientist wins coveted award, honoured for work on vaccine

07 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Universiti Malaya’s Dr Chan Yoke Fun was recently awarded the coveted Asean-US Science Prize for Women 2020 for her research which proved that a “multivalent vaccine” (a vaccine which can combat more than one specific virus) could be made to fight HFMD and brain diseases among children.
