Masked and Answered, Episode 33: Nathalie Kelley Shares Her Routine for Ethical Skincare

15 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Actress Nathalie Kelley knows a thing or two about sustainable beauty, and she’s sharing all her secrets for maintaining gorgeous skin without harming the environment. From a patio with a gorgeous view, Nathalie reviews a honey mud face mask from May Lindstrom Skin and opens up about her beauty routine. And it’s not just skincare products — from a sound bath to facial acupuncture to red light therapy, she’s got a ton of amazing tips that we’ll definitely be trying ASAP.
