Love O2O (Chinese: 微微一笑很倾城; pinyin: Wēi Wēi Yī Xiào Hěn Qīng Chéng) is a 2016 Chinese television series based on the novel of the same name written by Gu Man, starring Zheng Shuang and Yang Yang. It aired on Jiangsu TV and Dragon TV from 22 August to 6 September 2016. The drama is a commercial success in China.
Love O2O revolves around the love story of a first-year and a final year student who fell in love with each other while playing an online video game. It revolves around the couple as they overcome different challenges and numerous obstacles.
