Independence Day 2020 from LoC, Kashmir A day with the BSF Oneindia News

17 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

India is celebrating its 74th Independence Day amid an unprecedented pandemic. We take you to a BSF camp located 26 kilometres away from the Line of Control in Kashmir’s Gulmarg. Covid or no Covid, these men who patrol the LoC here are our essential workers all year round. How do these men keep their ‘josh’ up at a tense frontier, how much do they miss their own homes and what is their message for us on Independence Day?
