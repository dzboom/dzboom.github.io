In Punjab, the figures of death by drinking spurious liquor are constantly increasing. Not only this, action has also been taken on many senior officials of the state, after which the case has caught fire. Since Wednesday, a large number of death cases have been reported from Tarn Taran, Batala and Amritsar districts of the state
