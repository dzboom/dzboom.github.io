Report

Former Union Minister Manoj Sinha has been sworn in as the Lieutenant Governor of Jammu and Kashmir. Manoj Sinha is the first leader who has taken charge of the Union Territory. Chief Justice of Jammu and Kashmir Geeta Mittal administered him the oath of office at Raj Bhavan.
