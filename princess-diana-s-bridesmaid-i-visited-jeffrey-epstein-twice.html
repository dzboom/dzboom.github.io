Princess Diana's Bridesmaid: I Visited Jeffrey Epstein Twice

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Jeffrey Epstein scandal has reached its tentacles all the way back to the marriage of Britain’s Prince Charles and Lady Diana Spencer.
According to Elle Magazine, British writer Clementine ‘Clemmie’ Hambro was a young bridesmaid at the royal couple’s wedding.
Hambro has issued a statement clarifying her connection to Epstein, and the purpose of two trips she made to visit him. One of those trips was to his private island.
She says one trip was with female colleagues, to look at Epstein’s new Santa Fe home to discuss what art he was going to buy.
Hambro said the visit to Epstein’s island was by personal invitation. She says was not abused, and saw no one else being abused on either trip.
My heart breaks for all the survivors, now I know what happened on that island. Clementine ‘Clemmie’ Hambro Statement
