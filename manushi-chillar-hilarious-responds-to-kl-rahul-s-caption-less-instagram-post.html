Manushi Chillar hilarious responds to KL Rahul’s ‘caption-less’ Instagram post. India batsman KL Rahul on Monday took to Instagram to share a new picture where he could be seen covering his face with his hands. Thinking of a caption Rahul wrote as he shared the picture.
