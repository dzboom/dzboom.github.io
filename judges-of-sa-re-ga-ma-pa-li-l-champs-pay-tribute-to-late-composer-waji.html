Judges Of Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li’l Champs Pay Tribute To Late Composer Wajid Khan

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Singers Himesh Reshammiya, Javed Ali and Alka Yagnik, took a trip down memory lane to share anecdotes while remembering late composer Wajid Khan. In the upcoming episodes of Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li’l Champs, the judges will pay tribute to Wajid through his songs. Talking about the late comser, Himesh said, “Wajid and I were planning to watch a movie together for quite some time now but never got around to watching it, despite the number of times we met. I think this is the only thing that is left pending. I really miss Wajid”. Javed considers it sheer luck that he got the chance to sing Naina lade in the movie Dabangg 3 for Sajid-Wajid, he shares, “That was the last time I met him, and I clearly remember he kept telling us to just enjoy the song while singing.”
