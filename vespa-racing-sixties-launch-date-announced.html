Vespa Racing Sixties Launch Date Announced

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Piaggio has announced that they will be launching the Vespa Racing Sixties scooter in the Indian market on the 1st of September 2020. The new Vespa Racing Sixties will be a limited edition model based on the brand’s SXL150 scooter offering, featuring a special livery/paint scheme.
The Vespa Racing Sixties although remains mechanically identical to the standard model. The Vespa Racing Sixties is powered by a 150cc single-cylinder engine producing 10.3bhp and 10.6Nm of peak torque. Watch the video to know more.
