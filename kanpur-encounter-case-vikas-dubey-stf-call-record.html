Report

UP STF has made a big disclosure in the case of 8 policemen during the Dabish on July 3 in Vikru village of Chaubepur police station area of ​​Kanpur. In the audio captured by the STF, two policemen have been found who gave information about Dabish to Vikas Dubey. Not only this, Vikas is heard saying in this audio that today we will deal with the police
