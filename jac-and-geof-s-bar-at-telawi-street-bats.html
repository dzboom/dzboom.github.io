Jac and Geof’s Bar At Telawi Street BATS

06 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Best known for her dreamy voice, Jaclyn Victor has partnered with friend Geoffrey Leembruggen to open the swanky restaurant BATS, which simply means Bar At Telawi Street.
Tastefully designed with elegant lighting and breath-taking artwork, BATS’ tantalising menu of western and local eats is to-die-for. And there’s something extra too – live music.
It’s just the place to sit back, enjoy good food and listen to easy-listening tunes – and a song or two by the lovely Jaclyn too.
