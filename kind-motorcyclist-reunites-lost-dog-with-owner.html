Kind Motorcyclist Reunites Lost Dog With Owner

02 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

While [Kota]( was out riding his motorcycle, he came across an adorable dog that seemed to be lost. He pulled over to check the pooch’s tag, and found it’s name was Cheetah. Kota called Cheetah’s owner — and even waited with the pup to make sure it stayed safe until its human arrived. What a sweet rescue!
