Vikas Dubey Arrest : Kanpur से Ujjain कैसे आया विकास दुबे, जानिए पूरी डिटेल वनइंडिया हिंदी

14 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The most wanted gangster of Uttar Pradesh and Vikas Dubey, accused of killing eight police personnel in Kanpur, are finally in police custody. The police of Ujjain in Madhya Pradesh arrested on Thursday morning. Now there are many disclosures in this matter. The first disclosure about Vikas Dubey has been that he was tricking the police of four states with fake IDs. Vikas Dubey used the name to go to Ujjain. That was Paul.
