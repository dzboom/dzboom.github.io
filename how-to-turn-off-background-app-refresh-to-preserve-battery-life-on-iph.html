How to Turn-Off Background App Refresh to Preserve Battery Life on iPhone?
Step 1: Open ‘Settings’ App.
Step 2: Tap on ‘General.’
Step 3: Tap on ‘Background App Refresh.’
Step 4: Tap on ‘Background App Refresh’ again at the top.
Step 5: Tap on ‘Off.’
It’s done.
