Can You Make Maggi In 2 Mins_ Ft. POPxo Team - POPxo Game On!

10 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Who doesn’t love Maggi noodles? We’re sure that everyone does. It is in fact, the most-loved meal across all ages. And, everyone has their own style of cooking it. Some like it soupy, some love it dry and there are at least over a hundred more recipes of this product involving eggs, cheese, vegetables, mushrooms and so much more. Maggi is one of those rare products in the country that has remained the ‘favorite thing to eat’ for generations with it’s simple marketing premise - ‘Made in only 2 minutes’. But anyone who’s ever tried to cook maggi would know that actually cooking maggi takes way more than just 2 minutes in most cases. Can Maggi be cooked in 2-minutes? Well, team POPxo decided to check it out.
Watch as in today’s episode of POPxo Game On- Shantanu, Tijo, Himani and Anukriti battle it out to see who can cook Maggi in 2 minutes.
