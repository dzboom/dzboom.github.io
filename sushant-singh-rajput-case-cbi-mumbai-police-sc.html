Sushant Singh Rajput Case की जांच CBI करेगी या Mumbai Police, आज आएगा SC का फैसला वनइंडिया हिंदी

27 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Supreme Court will pronounce its verdict today whether CBI should investigate the mysterious death by suicide of actor Sushant Singh Rajput in Mumbai two months ago.Justice Hrishikesh Roy will give the verdict on a petition filed by Rajput’s friend and actor Rhea Chakraborty, who had sought transfer of the FIR lodged by Rajput’s father in Patna to Mumbai where the actor committed suicide.
