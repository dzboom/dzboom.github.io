Report

ED is investigating the case along with CBI in Sushant Singh Rajput suicide case … On Friday, ED questioned Sushant’s girlfriend or Chakraborty for about 8 hours. The agency also questioned Riya’s father Indrajit Chakraborty and brother Shouvik Chakraborty. According to ED sources, in the investigation till date in Sushant case, ED has not received Rs 15 crore in Riya’s accounts.
