Report

ED is investigating the case along with CBI in Sushant Singh Rajput suicide case … On Friday, ED questioned Sushant’s girlfriend or Chakraborty for about 8 hours. The agency also questioned Riya’s father Indrajit Chakraborty and brother Shouvik Chakraborty.
