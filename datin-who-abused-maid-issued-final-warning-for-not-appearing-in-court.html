‘Datin’ who abused maid issued final warning for not appearing in court four times in a row

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Rozita Mohamad Ali, the Datin housewife who made headlines for grievously hurting her Indonesian maid in 2016 and sentenced to eight years jail, was slapped with a final warning by the Court of Appeal on Monday (July 20) for failing to appear in court for the fourth consecutive time.
Judge Datuk Seri Kamaludin Md Said, who led a panel of three judges, said the court would not compromise with Rozita who always gave asthma and knee pain as an excuse for her absence.
