Mohabbat Tujhe Alvida, Episode 12 Promo, Official HD Video - 26 August 2020
The plot of "Mohabbat Tujhe Alvida" revolves around a charming couple played by Zahid Ahmed and Sonya Hussyn from a middle-class background.
