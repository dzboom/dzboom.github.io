Brian Austin Green Talks About Possible Reconciliation With Megan Fox

04 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Green and Fox married in 2010 after several years together and are the parents of three children.
Over the weekend Green did his first Instagram Live.

He responded "I never say never."
