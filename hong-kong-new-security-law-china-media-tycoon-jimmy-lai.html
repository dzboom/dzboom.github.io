Hong Kong New Security Law: China media tycoon Jimmy Lai को किया गिरफ्तार वनइंडिया हिंदी

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Hong Kong media tycoon Jimmy Lai has been arrested for allegedly breaching the city’s new national security law in the most high-profile detention since the legislation was imposed by Beijing in June. The move is likely to add to growing tensions between China and the US, which last week imposed sanctions on Hong Kong and Chinese officials responsible for the growing crackdown on the territory’s pro-democracy movement.
