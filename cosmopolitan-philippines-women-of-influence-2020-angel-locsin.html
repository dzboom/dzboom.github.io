Cosmopolitan Philippines Women Of Influence 2020: Angel Locsin

12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Now on its second year, Cosmopolitan Philippines’ Women Of Influence Awards shines the spotlight on inspiring celebrities who used their influence for humanitarian aid in response to the COVID-19 pandemic and to inspire a new generation of Filipinos to stand up and use their voice, especially when it is needed the most.
In this video, Angel Locsin shares details about her #UniTENTweStandPH and #ShopAndShare2020 initiatives amid the COVID-19 pandemic.
