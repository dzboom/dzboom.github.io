Assam Flood: बाढ़ और भूस्खलन से 107 लोगों की मौत, PM Modi ने CM Sonowal से की बात वनइंडिया हिंदी

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday assured all support to Assam in dealing with the flood havoc, which has claimed 81 lives and affected over 54 lakh in 30 districts. PM Narendra Modi spoke with Assam Chief Minister Sarbananda Sonowal about the flood situation in the state and expressed solidarity with the people.Watch video,
