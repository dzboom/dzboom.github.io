Coronavirus In India: Daily Growth Rate में गिरावट, Recovery Rate 66.30% Covid-19 वनइंडिया हिंदी

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Due to the continuous increase in cases of corona virus in India, many countries are leaving behind. In the country for two days more new cases of corona are coming out of America. But the Ministry of Health says that the country is seeing a decline once again in the case of corona infection. The daily growth rate of cases has declined in the last one week. In the last 10 days, the daily growth rate of the country has come down from 3.67 percent to 3.25 percent.
