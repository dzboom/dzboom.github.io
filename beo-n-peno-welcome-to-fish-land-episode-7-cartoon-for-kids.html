A red fox lures both Beo and Peno into taking a tourism package to fish land. However, later they realize that they have been cheated when they are dropped off at a desert. The entire tour idea of the red fox is to trick Beo and the Peno to feed the pack of red foxes in the desert. Landed in the desert and chased by hungry foxes Beo and the Peno team up, fight the red foxes and finally find their way back home.
