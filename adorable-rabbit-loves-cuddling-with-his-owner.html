Adorable rabbit loves cuddling with his owner

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This is the cute moment a bunny enjoyed lying on his owner’s pregnant belly and staring like an innocent baby.
Li Zhumei owns the dodge dwarf rabbit named Li Tubao whom she’s been taking care of for almost six years now.
The pet owner said that the rabbit was handed to her by previous owners who are negligent and did not want to raise him anymore.
She said: "Tubao is always like that maybe because he wants to treasure all our time together while my baby is still in my tummy.
"Lying and staring is a clear sign of rabbit behaviour telling the owner that they are happy."
The video was recorded inside their home in Taipei, Taiwan on June 5.
