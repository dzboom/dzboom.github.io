Woman find creepy insect with glowing eyes

24 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A mysterious insect with creepy glowing eyes entered a woman’s bedroom in French Guiana recently.
Insect enthusiast Lo Bov said the strange beetle flew through the open window of her house in Amazonia on August 4th.
She said: "We live close to the forest in an open house. We thought it was just because of the lamp light, but it still glows even with lights off."
Lo added that the insect was immediately released back into their garden.
