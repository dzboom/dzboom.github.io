Christen Dominique's Nighttime Skincare Routine Go To Bed With Me

22 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Makeup artist turned beauty influencer, Christen Dominique, is one of the OG beauty YouTubers. She’s been active on the platform since 2009 and has amassed a following of over 4 million subscribers. In addition to makeup tutorials, she’s known for her viral "two face" tutorials that compare American makeup trends to those in other countries. In this episode of Go To Bed With Me, Christen walks us through her 7-step skincare routine to combat dry skin.
