“Beach Ball Bouncing” is an original children’s song, written, arranged and produced by Jeff Hammer, and performed by Kidzone. It is published by CYP Music.
This video is produced to encourage young children to join in with singing the song alone or with their friends.
“Beach Ball Bouncing” is based on the track on the album “Summer Holiday”. Originally published by CYP Limited in 2008, this album is available on:
