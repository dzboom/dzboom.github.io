MS Dhoni has been enjoying his time away from the game at his farmhouse in Ranchi. Despite him being away from the limelight, the fans have managed to get glimpses of the star player. A few months back, the fans were shocked to sees him in the white beard as Dhoni looked unrecognisable. A week back, another photo of Dhoni went viral where he was seen with short hair and a black beard.
