Karnataka Tunga River में तेज बहाव की वजह से पेड़ों पर शरण लेने को मजबूर सांप वनइंडिया हिंदी

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Several parts of Karnataka have received heavy rainfall in the past. It is still raining in some areas. In such a situation, the flow of Tunga river flowing through Shivamogga of the state has intensified. The rapid flow of the river has forced the snakes to take shelter on the trees. Let us know that many parts of Karnataka are facing flood situation for the third consecutive year after torrential rains.
