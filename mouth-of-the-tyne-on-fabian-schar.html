Mouth of the Tyne on Fabian Schar

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Liam Kennedy is joined by fellow Newcastle United writer Miles Starforth and North East Non-League expert Mark Carruthers to talk all-things black and white. In this preview from the July 16 edition, they discuss Fabian Schar’s decline. In the episode they also discuss Saudi Arabia’s decision to ban BeIN Sport from streaming in the country - and what it could mean for the proposed £300m takeover. They also chat about Matty Longstaff and the loan players’ futures.
