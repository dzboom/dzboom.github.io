Boy drives tractor to help family collect ocean trash in Cambodia

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A young lad was seen skillfully driving a field tractor to help his family collect trash on the beach near a small village in Preah Sihanouk, southern Cambodia.
The youngster was with his mother, Neak, and his father and other relatives when they went to collect the rubbish.
Neak said that they collect ocean pollution to help keep the area clean and then they recycle it for money.
Cambodia’s poverty rate in the past six years has been rising, which has affected more than five million native people who live in the countryside remain near-poor.
The clip was filmed on July 20.
