Tijuana Jackson Purpose Over Prison Movie Clip - Plot synopsis: The movie, Tijuana Jackson: Purpose Over Prison is about an ex convict named Tijuana Jackson (TJ) who is about to get out of jail with hopes of becoming a world renowned motivational speaker. Think of him as a life coach to the streets. TJ is hilarious and deep. He gives you realistic solutions to breaking emotional and psychological cycles that hold us back, but can’t seem to get out of his way when it comes to his own life. His probation officer is at her limit, his family has heard his promises a million times before and even his nephew Lil’ Eric finally confronts him with the reality of his situation, but in the end, can TJ really change his ways?
