Guy Falls off Boat Into Water While Trying to Catch Drone

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This guy was standing on a boat operating a drone. He was trying to get the drone down and when it did, he moved near to the boat’s edge to catch it. However, he lost his balance and leaned too forward that he fell directly into the water.
