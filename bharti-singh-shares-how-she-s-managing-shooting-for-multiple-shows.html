Bharti Singh Shares How She’s Managing Shooting For Multiple Shows

22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Ever since nationwide lockdown was announced everyone was missing Kapil Sharma Show and Archana Puran Singh’s amazing behind the scenes videos from the sets. While the actor was home, she was constantly entertaining her followers with hilarious videos with her family, especially with her lovely maid Bhagyashri. Now in the latest video, Archana is finally back on the sets of The Kapil Sharma Show, the actor takes us behind and ask her team members how they feel to be back on the show, sharing the video, Archana wrote, "Behind the scenes #TKSS, we all met each other excitedly but couldn’t hug. So made do with excited hellos. There’s always so much FUN happening on the sets of The Kapil Sharma Show (even with all the stringent protocols.) And God. How I’ve missed it. So good to be back!"
