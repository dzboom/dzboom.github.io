PM’s aide implicated in Isa Samad’s CBT and bribery case

19 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Former Felda chairman Tan Sri Mohd Isa Abdul Samad has been granted bail for his 10 charges of criminal breach of trust and corruption in relation to the purchase of an overpriced hotel in Kuching by Felda in 2014.
The prime minister’s political secretary Muhammad Zahid Md Arip has been implicated as the middleman between Mohd Isa and a Gagasan Abadi Properties Sdn Bhd director.
