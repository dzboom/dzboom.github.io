2021 Rolls Royce Ghost Unveiled

06 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The British luxury marque, Rolls Royce, has unveiled its new Ghost for the world. 2021 Rolls Royce Ghost has not changed much in terms of design. However, there are several changes addressing the core values of the Rolls Royce Customers.
The 2021 Ghost features an all-new aluminium spaceframe architecture, a new planar suspension, new enhanced interiors with starlight illumination, new headlamps and an all-new 6.75-litre twin-turbo V12 engine. The new Ghost also features four-wheel drive and four-wheel steer. Watch the video to know more about the 2021 Rolls Royce Ghost.
