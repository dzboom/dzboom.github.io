Don’t Be Surprised Pusha-T Is Still Dissing Drake For The Record

15 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Last week Pusha-T stunned the internet with yet another presumable Drake diss on Pop Smoke’s leaked song “Paranoia,” which was left off the Brooklyn rapper’s posthumous album, ‘Shoot for the Stars Aim for the Moon.’ Although the battle between Drake and Push hit its peak back in 2018 when the rappers dropped “Duppy Freestyle" and “Story of Adidon,” it’s not shocking that shots are still flying years later. As Genius’ VP of Content Strategy Rob Markman points out in the latest episode of For The Record, hip-hop beefs rarely come to an abrupt end even after both parties release their marquee diss tracks.
