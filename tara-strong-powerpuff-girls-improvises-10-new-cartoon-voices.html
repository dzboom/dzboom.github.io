Tara Strong (Powerpuff Girls) Improvises 10 New Cartoon Voices

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Voice actor Tara Strong tries her hand at improvising the voices of 10 random cartoon characters that she has never seen before. Using a similar technique she used for Bubbles (The Powerpuff Girls), Raven (Teen Titans), Timmy Turner (The Fairly Oddparents), Dill Pickles (Rugrats), and more, Tara brings these characters to life.
