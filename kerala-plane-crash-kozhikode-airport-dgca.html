An Air India Express plane at Kozhikode Airport in Kerala fell victim to the accident on Friday evening, in which 18 people, including two pilots, were killed. It was raining heavily on Friday in Kerala. The aircraft coming from Kozhikode from Dubai under the Vande India Mission slipped into the ditch at the time of landing due to water in the runway and fell into the ditch 35 feet down … in fact it is a ‘tabletop’ runway.
