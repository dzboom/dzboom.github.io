03 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Raksha Bandhan, a symbol of brotherly love, was celebrated on Monday with great enthusiasm, there was a lot of enthusiasm among the siblings about Raksha Bandhan from morning, the sisters dressed up and tied a rakhi to the brother with arti and sandalwood, sweet and other ingredients in the plate. . Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Defense Minister Rajnath Singh and many Union ministers and BJP leaders on Monday greeted the countrymen on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan. And big news of the day.
