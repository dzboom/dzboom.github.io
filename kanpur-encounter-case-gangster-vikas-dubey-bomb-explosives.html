Report

Historyheater Vikas Dubey is absconding since the ordeal took place in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. More than 4 days have passed but nothing has been known about Vikas Dubey. More than 20 teams of UP Police are busy in finding Vikas Dubey. But till now Vikas has not lost his heart. ADG Law and Order also reached Prashant Kumar Vikas Dubey’s house. The UP police got several indigenous bombs from home. The ADG Law and Order said that 2 kg of explosives, firearms and bombs have been found after the search was done after getting information about the development house’s weapons.
