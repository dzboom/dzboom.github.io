ENG vs WI, 1st Test 2020, Stat Highlights: West Indies Beat England By Four Wickets

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The day five of the Test match between England vs West Indies at Ageas bowl was quite an interesting one as the results swung like a pendulum. However, it was the Caribbean side who had the last laugh. West Indies beat England in the first match of the three-game Test Series as they beat England by four wickets. Shannon Gabriel picked crucial five-wicket haul to push England on their back foot. Jermaine Blackwood also displayed nerves of steel and scored a half-century in the game. Blackwood played a vital role in West Indies’ win over England by scoring 95 runs. Now, let’s have a look at the stat highlights of the match.
