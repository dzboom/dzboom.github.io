Kerala Gold Smuggling Case: How did Swapna Suresh become a gold smuggling queen from a clerk ? Layers of the gold smuggling racket have started to open up after the 30 kg gold seized at Thiruvananthapuram Airport in Kerala. The gold came in the name of the UAE embassy. Investigations have revealed that former embassy employees were involved in the smuggling.
