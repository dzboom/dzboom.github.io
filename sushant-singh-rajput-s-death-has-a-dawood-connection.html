Sushant Singh Rajput's Death Has A Dawood Connection?

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sushant Singh Rajput’s death has followed several conspiracy theories. Now, the latest one is from the ex-RAW officer NK Sood and if there’s any truth to it, it will shock you to the core. NK Sood has posted a video on YouTube, in which he claims that Sushant Singh Rajput has been murdered by Dawood Ibrahim and his gang. He also said that some close people of Sushant were also involved. He supported his statement with several claims. He said that Dawood Ibrahim’s gang threatened Sushant Singh Rajput over calls and to avoid threats from Dawood’s gang, the actor changed as many as 50 sim cards and used to sleep in his car. He said that these threats became a reason for his depression as well. He said CCTV cameras going off, a day before Sushant’s suicide, points towards a pre-planned murder. He pointed finger towards Sushant’s servant, his friend Sandip Singh and also other Bollywood big guns, for the execution. What is the truth is still a mystery!
