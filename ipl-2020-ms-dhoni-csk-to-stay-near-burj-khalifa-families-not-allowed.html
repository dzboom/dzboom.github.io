IPL 2020: MS Dhoni & CSK to stay near Burj Khalifa, families not allowed

08 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

CSK is also likely to make ICC Academy in Dubai as its practice base. That is the reason team management has finalized a five star property for the team near Burj Khalifa which is not very far from the ICC Academy in Dubai. families not to travel with the team.
