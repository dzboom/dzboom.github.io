Report

New pictures of superstar and politician Rajinikanth are becoming quite viral on social media. Rajinikanth was seen driving a luxury car with a mask on his face. 69-year-old Rajinikanth drove a Lamborghini. His picture has rocked the social media. #LionInLamborghini is trending top on Twitter. Rajinikanth, who looks stylish on the big screen, lives with a lot of simplicity in real life. They have been seen outside along with sahed kurta and lungi. This time too, he was seen in this style.
