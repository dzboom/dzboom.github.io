Regis Philbin Dead At 88

04 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

TV personality Regis Philbin has died.
He was 88 years old.
Philbin was best known for hosting “Live! With Regis and Kathie Le."
Then, he hosted “Live! With Regis and Kelly.”
Later he was the host of “Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?.”
Philbin holds the record for the most hours on US television with 16,746.50.
