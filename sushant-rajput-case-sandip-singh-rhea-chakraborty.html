CBI investigation in Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput case is going on. At the same time, many types of questions are being raised about the late actor on filmmaker Sandeep Singh. Sushant’s family also said that he does not know Sandeep Singh. Now the filmmaker has broken his silence on this entire episode. Sandeep Singh said, ‘People who are making allegations against me, they should answer why when they got news of Sushant Singh Rajput’s death, why should they go to their home or hospital Why did not go, or did not attend his funeral. Why didn’t he stand with the family? ‘
