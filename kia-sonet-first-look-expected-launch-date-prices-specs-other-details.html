Kia Sonet First Look Expected Launch Date, Prices, Specs & Other Details

10 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

#KiaSonet #KiaSonetFirstLook #KiaSonetWorldPremiere
Kia Motors India has globally premiered the much-awaited Sonet compact-SUV in India. The all-new Kia Sonet is the brand’s first sub-4-metre compact-SUV offering and the second ‘Made-In-India’ model, following the Seltos.
The Kia Sonet will go on sale in the Indian market very soon and is expected to be priced competitively as well. It will rival the likes of the Hyundai Venue and the Maruti Vitara Brezza; among others. Ahead of its arrival in the Indian market, here is a first-look & walkaround video of the all-new Kia Sonet.
