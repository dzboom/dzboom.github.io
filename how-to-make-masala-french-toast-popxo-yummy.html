How To Make Masala French Toast - POPxo Yummy

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

If you haven’t had a savoury masala french toast Indian style, all spiced up, then it’s time to remedy that pronto. Easy to make with simple ingredients easily available at home, this dish is a delicious surprise that you can have for breakfast or as an evening snack with your cuppa tea.
Learn how to make the perfect Masala French Toast in today’s episode of POPxo Yummy. Liked the recipe? Tell us how much in the comments section below.
Step 1) Heat 1 tbsp oil on a pan
Step 2) Add 1 tbsp green chillies and finely chopped garlic
Step 3) Add 1/2 cup onion, tomatoes and capsicum and saute
Step 4) Add 1 tsp red chilly powder, turmeric powder and coriander power.
Step 5) Add 1/4 cup water on the pan and allow the vegetables to cook.
Step 6) Crack open 3 eggs in a separate bowl and add 1/2 cup grated cheese.
Step 7) Add pepper & salt as per taste and beat the eggs.
Step 8) Take a bread slice and evenly spread the vegetables mixture.
Step 9) Cover it with another bread slice and coat well in the egg mixture.
Step 10) Pan fry on medium heat till golden brown and enjoy.
