Justin Wong, DAP's candidate for Sri Tanjung arrives at nomination centre

12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

DAP candidate for the Sri Tanjung seat, Justin Wong arrived at the nomination center in Tawau this morning.
His supporters who had followed him waved the Warisan flag and also chanted ‘Hidup Warisan’ outside the nomination center.
His father Jimmy Wong Sze Phin had wong the seat in GE14.
