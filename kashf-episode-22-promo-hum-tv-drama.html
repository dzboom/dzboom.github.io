Kashf, Episode 22 Promo - Official HD Video - 1 September 2020
The plot of Kashf revolves around Kashf, the eldest daughter of Imtiaz, an arrogant and indolent person who dreams of becoming rich without making any efforts. Kashf, unlike her sisters and father is a realist but she is haunted by lucid visions and nightmares until she realizes that they are not just dreams but the future of people around her. Word gets out when she saves her friend from marrying a person who has sinister design, and from there the story unfolds into unpredictable turns and twists. To some she is a curse and to others a miracle!
Starring:
Hira Mani, Junaid Khan, Waseem Abbas, Munza Arif, Hajra Khan, Samina Ahmed, Saleem Mairaj, Saji Uddin, Lubna Aslam, Amir Qureshi, Abeena
Directed By: Danish Nawaz
Written By: Imran Nazeer
Produced By: MD Productions
