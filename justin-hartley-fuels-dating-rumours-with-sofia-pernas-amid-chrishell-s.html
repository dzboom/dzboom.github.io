Justin Hartley Fuels Dating Rumours With Sofia Pernas Amid Chrishell Stause Divorce

16 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Meanwhile, Justin Hartley and Sofia Pernas are adding more fuel to the blazing rumours of them dating each other. The alleged couple took to their Instagram stories to share photos from their refreshing day at the pool. In one of Sofia’s pictures, a man’s arm can be seen holding a glass of Guinness. Even though the man’s head is out of frame in the IG story, the watch on his wrist appears to be similar to the one Justin wore during his appearance on the Ellen DeGeneres Show. The 31-year old actress captioned the story quoting Lionel Richie’s song lyrics, “Easy like Sunday mornin.” On the other hand, Hartley also posted a picture of himself by the pool with his dog Paisley, and captioned it, “Paisley like Sunday morning.” The similar references in their captions made the netizens speculate that Justin and Sofia spent their Sunday by the pool together. However, while the rumours of Justin and Sofia’s outing made rounds on the internet, Netflix viewers were busy watching Selling Sunset, which features the former’s ex-wife Chrishell Stause. The couple’s divorce was one of the highlights of the episode that aired on the streaming service on August 7. The hearsay suggests that the estranged couple split back in November 2019.
