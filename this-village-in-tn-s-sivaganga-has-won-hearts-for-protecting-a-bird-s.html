This village in TN's Sivaganga has won hearts for protecting a bird's nest

30 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Potthakudi village in Tamil Nadu decided to plunge itself into darkness for over 40 days just in order to protect a mother bird that that had built its nest inside the main electric pole that supplied electricity to the street lights lighting the pathway to about 100 houses.
