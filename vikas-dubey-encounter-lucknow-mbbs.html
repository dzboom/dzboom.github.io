Vikas Dubey के Encounter के बाद बड़ा बेटा लौटा Lucknow, विदेश से कर रहा है MBBS वनइंडिया हिंदी

17 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Gangster Vikas Dubey was killed on Friday morning at the hands of Uttar Pradesh’s STF. After this, the ED has sought information about his property from the police. Vikas Dubey’s elder son is studying MBBS abroad. Now he has returned to Lucknow. After returning to Lucknow, he has come to visit his grandmother.
