Vending machine installed at Dadar Railway Station provides masks, sanitisers to commuters

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

In a bid to contain the spread of Covid-19, a vending machine installed at Mumbai’s Dadar Railway Station provides masks, sanitisers, and gloves to commuters. The machine will provide two types of masks one is N-95 and other is a disposable mask. The hand sanitisers ranging from Rs 50-100 are available and free size gloves are also available.
