Slim by-election: Voters did not forget to buy famous buns

29 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Many voters in the Slim by-election dropped by the famous Kilang Roti Mokhtar in Slim River town to buy some freshly-made buns.
The bakery is said to be one of the oldest traditional bakeries in the area. It was started by the current proprietor’s uncle in 1949.
