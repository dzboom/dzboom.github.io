Rare collection of posters of Hindi films from 1940 - 1970

17 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Rare and unseen collection of posters of Bollywood films from 1940 to 1970’s . Posters of old films like 1857 (1946), Baharen Phir Bhi Aayengi (1966), Sholay (1975), Bharosa (1963), Man-Mauji (1962), Kaagaz Ke Phool (1956), Aar-Paar (1954), Babul (1950) and many more can be seen here. This footage is part of the broadcast stock footage archive of Wilderness Films India Ltd., the largest collection of HD imagery from South Asia. The collection comprises of 150, 000+ hours of high quality broadcast imagery, mostly shot on 4K, 200 fps slow motion, Full HD, HDCAM 1080i High Definition, Alexa and XDCAM.
