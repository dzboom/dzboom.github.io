22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After winning the toss, West Indies elected to bowl first at the Old Trafford Cricket Ground in Manchester. The team is already leading three-match Test series 1-0. West Indies won the toss and elected to bowl first. The visitors started the game on a high note as they snapped early wickets in form of Rory Burns, Zak Crawley and Joe Root. Domini Sibley who was dismissed on a duck in the first innings of the last game put up a better show this time around. He smashed a well-made half-century. Currently, the scoreboard reads 207/3.
