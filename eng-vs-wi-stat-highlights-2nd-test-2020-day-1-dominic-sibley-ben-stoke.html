ENG vs WI Stat Highlights, 2nd Test 2020, Day 1: Dominic Sibley & Ben Stokes’ 50 Gives Edge To ENG

22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After winning the toss, West Indies elected to bowl first at the Old Trafford Cricket Ground in Manchester. The team is already leading three-match Test series 1-0. West Indies won the toss and elected to bowl first. The visitors started the game on a high note as they snapped early wickets in form of Rory Burns, Zak Crawley and Joe Root. Domini Sibley who was dismissed on a duck in the first innings of the last game put up a better show this time around. He smashed a well-made half-century. Currently, the scoreboard reads 207/3. Now, let’s have a look at the stat highlights of 2nd Test, Day 1.
