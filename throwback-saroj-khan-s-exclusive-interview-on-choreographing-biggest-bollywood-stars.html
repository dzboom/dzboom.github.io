In this throwback video, watch the Exclusive Interview of late choreographer Saroj Khan as she talked about her experience working with the biggest stars of Bollywood. Saroj Khan passed away on 3 July 2020 due to cardiac arrest. She was not keeping well for some time. Saroj had a remarkable career of four decades. She choreographed around 2,000 songs and was honoured with National Award three times.
