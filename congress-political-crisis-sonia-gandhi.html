The party has started taking action against leaders who had ‘letter bomb’ against Sonia Gandhi in Congress. The 23 disgruntled leaders who wrote a letter to the interim president Sonia Gandhi, demanding a major change in the party, have now come under target. In fact, on Thursday, the Congress has formed a 10-member committee to decide the party’s strategy on subjects related to Parliament. Five members of both houses have been included in this.
