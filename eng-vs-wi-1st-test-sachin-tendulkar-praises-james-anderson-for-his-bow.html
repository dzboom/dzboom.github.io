ENG vs WI, 1st Test: Sachin Tendulkar praises James Anderson for his bowling skills वनइंडिया हिंदी

13 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sachin Tendulkar said James Anderson is the only bowler who could bowl a "reverse outswinger" with a wrist position of a conventional inswinger. Tendulkar has termed that ‘reverse’ reverse swing. Speaking to West Indies legend Brian Lara on his ‘100MB’ app, Tendulkar explained why Anderson could be vital for England in the ongoing Test against West Indies at the Ageas Bowl. According to Tendulkar, when he checked Anderson’s wrist position, he saw him bowling the reverse outswinger but with wrist position of conventional inswinger, which is unheard of.
