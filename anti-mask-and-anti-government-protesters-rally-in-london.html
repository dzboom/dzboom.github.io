Anti-mask and anti-government protesters rally in London

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Anti-government and "anti-maskers" congregate at Marble Arch in London to protest against the government and the new coronavirus rules.
The clip filmed today (July 19), shows the two female speakers talking to the crowd.
One of the speakers, Jacqueline Dunn, claimed the government was a form of religion, and a lot of people will only listen to the government’s rules and regulations because ‘they like to follow orders’.
The anti-mask movement views masks as ‘ineffective, and believe they are ‘an unjustified assault on people’s personal freedom’.
