03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

They are with him when he is happy, they are with him when he is sad - meet the lovely Jazz players that accompany Paddington on this, his journey of self-discovery. See if you can spot a quick Cameo by the creator of Paddington - Michael Bond!
