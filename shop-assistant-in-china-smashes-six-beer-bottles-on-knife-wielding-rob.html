Shop assistant in China smashes six beer bottles on knife-wielding robber's head to subdue him

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A shop assistant smashed six beer bottles on a knife-wielding robber’s head to subdue him in northern China.
The CCTV video, shot in the city of Dalian in Liaoning Province on June 17, shows a man attempting to rob a shop by using a knife to threaten a shop assistant.
When the robber tried to put the knife onto the assistant’s neck, he grabbed the nearby beer bottles and smashed them on the robber’s head.
After the robber fell onto the floor, the assistant ran out of the shop to call the police.
Police has arrested the robber named Liu and he confessed the crime, while the shop assistant‘s action was justified as self-defence. The case is in further investigation.
The video was provided by local media with permission.
