Faculty and Staff Raise the Alarm Over Reopening College Campuses

07 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

With college students poised to return to campuses this month, faculty and staff have concerns.
Many across the U.S. are pleading with campus administrators and state lawmakers.
The only COVID appropriate return would be online learning until it’s safe to reopen physically.
As cases rise across the US, many colleges are reversing previous decisions to reopen.
In other places, faculty, staff and students continue to raise the alarm says NEAToday.
Union leaders say reopening plans must ensure the safety of all community members.
