Miners risk their lives in the search for gold in Sudan

15 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Miners in Sudan are risking their lives searching for gold. There are estimated to be 40,000 gold mining sites in Sudan, but often workers find themselves working with little safety precautions. While the gold industry is booming, the miners say they are struggling to make a living.
