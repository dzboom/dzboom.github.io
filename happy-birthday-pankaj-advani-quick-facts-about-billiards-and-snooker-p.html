Happy Birthday Pankaj Advani: Quick Facts About Billiards And Snooker Player

28 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Pankaj Arjan Advani was born on July 24, 1985 in Pune, Maharashtra. Pankaj was just 12 when he won his first-ever title. He then went on to set many records at state and national level. Regarded as one of the finest sportspersons in India, Pankaj has won gold medals at the Asian Games. As Pankaj turns 35, we take a look at some facts associated with the billiards and snooker champion of India.
