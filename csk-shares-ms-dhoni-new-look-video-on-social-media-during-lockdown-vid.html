CSK shares MS Dhoni New Look Video on Social Media during Lockdown, Video goes Viral. MS Dhoni made a rare appearance in a video that has surfaced online. The former India captain still has the black beard but has got his hair trimmed. Dhoni looks much younger in his new look if compared to past photos that had gone viral on social media.
