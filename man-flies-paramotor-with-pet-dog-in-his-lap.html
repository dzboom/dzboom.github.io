Man Flies Paramotor With Pet Dog in his Lap

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This man was a certified pilot who enjoyed flying his paramotor. For this flight, he took his pet dog, Jackson, along with him. He strapped Jackson to himself and covered his ears with earmuffs for his protection. They became airborne and Jackson stood comfortably on his lap, barking at the cows below.
