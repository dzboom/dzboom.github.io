After A Year Without Pay, 351 Workers of UP's 181 Women's Helpline Sacked

27 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

At least 351 women employees of the ‘181’ helpline, run by the Uttar Pradesh government, have launched an indefinite protest in Lucknow against the non-payment of their salaries since July 2019. Adding insult to their injury, the state Cabinet announced the decision to integrate the ‘181’ women helpline with the ‘112’ police helpline – essentially rendering all 351 women jobless.
