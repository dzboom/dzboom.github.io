Pubgoers in Greenwich enjoy a beer garden on Super Saturday

07 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Pubgoers on Super Saturday evening (July 4) in Greenwich, London, enjoyed a beer garden at The Gipsy Moth pub next to Cutty Sark.
Hand sanitiser is seen at the entrance to the pub where staff were taking customer contact information to help NHS Test and Trace.
A street performer played the guitar nearby as customers waited.
