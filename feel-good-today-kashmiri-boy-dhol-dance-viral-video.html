Feel Good Today: Kashmiri Boy ने Dhol पर किया जबरदस्त Dance Viral Video वनइंडिया हिंदी

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

In a video being widely circulated online, a boy is seen dancing without a care in the world and praise has been pouring in for him on social media.The undated video was reportedly recorded in Dachchan which is in the Kishtwar district of Jammu and Kashmir. In the video, the boy is seen dancing vigorously to the beats of a dhol, while a shehnai is also heard.Watch video,
