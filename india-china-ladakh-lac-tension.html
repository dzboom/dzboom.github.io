Report

For one thing, you must have heard the proverbial stigma from the top .. but it is also being seen on the Indo-China border ie Ladakh border. After East Ladakh in the month of June, once again on 29-30 August night, Chinese soldiers tried to enter the Indian border. This time, Indian troops were killed by Chinese troops trying to infiltrate the southern shore of Pangong Lake. China has become stung by this thing. And is giving a shout.
