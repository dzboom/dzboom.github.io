Hukou Waterfall - The Yellow River's Largest Waterfall

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Hukou (Mouth of Teapot) Waterfall is the only yellow waterfall in the world and the second largest waterfall in China after Huangguoshu Waterfall in Anshun City, Guizhou Province.
It is a naturally formed waterfall when the mighty Yellow River, the mother river of China, flows through Jinxia Grand Canyon on its middle reaches. The width of the waterfall changes with the season, usually 30 meters (98 feet) wide but increasing to 50 meters (164 feet) during flood season.
