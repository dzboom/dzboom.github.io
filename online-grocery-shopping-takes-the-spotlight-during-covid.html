Online Grocery Shopping Takes The Spotlight During COVID

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The coronavirus pandemic has brought online grocery to the fore says Business Insider.
Consumer interest in avoiding public places and government orders to stay at home helped greatly.
The continued need for groceries has made online grocery delivery services indispensable.
Previously, some consumers resisted the shopping method, to pick their groceries themselves.
But the pandemic has forced many to change their priorities.
The sudden focus on online grocery is set to alter consumer behavior well after the pandemic subsides.
