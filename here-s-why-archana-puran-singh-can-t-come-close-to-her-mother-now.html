Here's Why Archana Puran Singh Can't Come Close To Her Mother Now

17 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Archana Puran Singh who recently resumed shooting for The Kapil Sharma Show, says she can’t come close to her mother due to fear of COVID -19. The actor share a glimpse of a conversation with her mother where they can be seen talking about how coronavirus has changed the world, sharing the video, Archana wrote, "Agar distance aapke bhale ke liye hai, toh distance acchhaa hai. Hai na. So keeping elderly family members safe… by keeping them at a distance."
