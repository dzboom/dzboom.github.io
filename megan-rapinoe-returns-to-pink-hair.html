Megan Rapinoe Returns To Pink Hair

18 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Frustrating as it is, we’ve all had to go without salons and hairstylists while isolating for the past few months. That’s meant no drastic haircuts and no professional hair coloring — even for world-famous athletes like soccer player Megan Rapinoe, who’s known to play around with vivid and pastel hues. The athlete revealed her new and improved pink hair to Instagram on July 10, writing, "It’s been so long I just HAD to grid this pic ‍ACTUALLY, it has been nearly a year to the day since my last full bleach and tone + PINK. Hairstylist Riawna Capri is entirely to thank for the look, which she says took a whopping six hours to complete from start to finish.
