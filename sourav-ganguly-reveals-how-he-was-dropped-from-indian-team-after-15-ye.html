Sourav Ganguly reveals how he was dropped from Indian team after 15 Years वनइंडिया हिंदी

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sourav Ganguly has spoken about the most difficult chapter of his glorious cricketing career. Ganguly said the ‘biggest setback’ of his career was when he was sacked as India captain and subsequently dropped from the Indian side in 2005.
