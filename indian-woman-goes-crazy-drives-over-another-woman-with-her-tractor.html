Indian Woman goes crazy, drives over another woman with her tractor

31 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This woman seems to have gone crazy and drives around private property in her tractor, even running over a woman who comes in her way. she seems to have a revolver tucked into her sari dress. This every-day human drama takes place in Bijnour, Uttar Pradesh, India. Other women get pissed off with her, start screaming and hold her down by tightening down her dress, while a man comes and shuts down the tractor and takes away the keys. A policeman lands up and books her for lawlessness and dangerous driving. He takes pictures and video of the complainants, as evidence. A whole posse of policemen land up as the matter seems to be getting more and more serious. Since policemen cannot arrest and take away a woman in India, policewomen next come into the picture and escort her to the police station, for filing a case against her. The machinery of law does work in India!
