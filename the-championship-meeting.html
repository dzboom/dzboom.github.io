The Championship Meeting

28 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Social distancing can’t keep the head coaches of the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague from debating who should be crowned as the 2020 Turkish Airlines EuroLeague champion. Ettore Messina of AX Armani Exchange Milan, Pablo Laso of Real Madrid, Dimitris Itoudis of CSKA Moscow, Ergin Ataman of Anadolu Efes Istanbul, Ioannis Sfairopoulos of Maccabi FOX Tel Aviv and Sarunas Jasikevicius of FC Barcelona got together for a video conference, which doubled as the annual EuroLeague Head Coaches video to promote the 2020-21 season. Do you think they were able to agree on a deserving champion for 2020? Make sure you watch to the very end for an appearance by a surprise guest!
