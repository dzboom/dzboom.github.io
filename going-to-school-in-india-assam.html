Going to School in India - Assam

02 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A boy awakes and brushes his teeth. Mother bathes her son in the early morning and gets him ready for school, after dressing him up. After getting ready, he watches his favorite cartoon show on television while his mom is seen busy feeding him breakfast. Mother packs the lunch box of her young kid in the kitchen as the kid gets ready for school. A daily routine in India with sacrificing mothers and indulged children. But whereever you go, the focus on and importance of education remains! Children enjoy and share their tiffin with their friends during lunch hour in the school. This Assamese boy goes to school after that and attends to his lessons. After finishing school, mother comes to pick him up. And he says goodbye to his friends and goes back home.
