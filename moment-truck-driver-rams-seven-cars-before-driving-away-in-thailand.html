Moment truck driver rams SEVEN cars before driving away in Thailand

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This is the shocking moment a truck driver rammed SEVEN cars in a queue of traffic.
The reckless lorry carrying construction debris approached the row of vehicles waiting at a junction in Chonburi, eastern Thailand, this morning (July 8).
However, the driver appeared to be in a hurry and bulldozed his way through the motorists - leaving a trail of destruction.
Seven cars and pickup trucks were damaged in the chaos before the trucker drove away without even briefly stopping to look back.
A shocked driver who recorded the incident said drivers gathered after the crash but nobody knew why the trucker stopped.
She said: ‘‘There was no reason for the crash. The driver accelerated through the traffic. They didn’t seem to care. They didn’t even stop to look at the damage.
'’His brakes might have failed or there could have been a problem with the gears, but if that happened, they would have stopped.
'’Everybody who had their car damaged will have to arrange for the costs to be paid. The driver of the truck should take responsibility.’’
Police are now investigating the collision and examining CCTV from the area to try and trace the driver and the owner of the truck.
