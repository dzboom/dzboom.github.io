World of Warcraft: Shadowlands Afterlives Maldraxxus

11 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Afterlives: Maldraxxus, the second installment in Blizzard’s new Afterlives series of animated shorts created for Shadowlands, is now available! Part two offers a glimpse at the turmoil brewing between the five great houses of Maldraxxus as they seek to control the armies of the Shadowlands.
Afterlives is a four-part series of animated shorts that provides a look into the covenants that hold dominion over the expansion’s new zones.
