Doston, hamaare solar system ka star yaani ki hamaara sooraj.. ye hamaare solar system ke center me hai. Iske gravitational force se hi solar system ki har body judi hui hai aur iske influence me iske chaaro or orbit kar rahi hain. Matlab aap, main aur ye poori prithvi.. lagataar sun ko orbit kar rahe hain.. now just imagine.. aap subah-subah uthkar apni balcony me baithe ek haath me chaay ka cup liye dhoop senk rahe hain aur bahaar ke shaandaar nazaare ka maza le rahe hain. Tabhi peeche se TV par breaking news aati hai ki ek bada sa tukda hamaare sun se toot gaya hai aur prithvi ki or aa raha hai. What?? Aap jaldi se aasmaan ki or dekhte hain aur paate hain ki yaar yaha to sab theek hai lekin kab tak?? Ab apni imagination se baahar nikaliye aur sochiye ki agar aisa sach me ho gaya to? Matlab agar hamaare sun ka koi tukda usse alag ho jaae to? Sunne me thoda ajeeb lag raha hai na? To chaliye aaj hum milkar isi sawaal ka jawaab dhoondhte hain.
