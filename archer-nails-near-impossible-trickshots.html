Archer Nails Near-Impossible Trickshots

08 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Archer and YouTuber [James Jean]( is putting a ring on it! He nails so many trickshots with his bow and arrow it’s almost hard to keep track. But in this video, he does the near impossible. James throws a two-inch ring in the air and shoots right through it, nailing it on the target. James even said himself that it was the most difficult target he’s hit yet!
