Nainital: Leopard enters the house and takes the puppy away Oneindia News

Report

A leopard entered a house in Uttarakhand’s Nainital and took away a puppy. There have been incidents of wild cat entering residential areas in hill town. Earlier a leopard broke into the hotel room of a young couple in Nainital. But the big cat didn’t attack the couple and took refuge in the bathroom.
