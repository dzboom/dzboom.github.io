As COVID Grows, California Shuts Down Again

15 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

California’s governor on Monday clamped new restrictions on businesses, reports HuffPost.
The rules also include the state’s two largest school districts, Los Angeles and San Diego.

Governor Gavin Newsom, a Democrat, ordered another round of shutdowns.
