India beats China, enters FIDE online chess olympiad quarterfinals Oneindia News

26 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Indian team has entered the quarterfinals of the FIDE online chess olympiad after defeating China 4-2 in the final round of the top division. 15-year old Praggnanandhaa R shined for India. Although he was on the ropes, he managed to turn the tables on Liu Yan & finish with a perfect 6/6 score. The young boy was congratulated by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman.
