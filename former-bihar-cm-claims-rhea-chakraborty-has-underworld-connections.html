Former Bihar CM Claims Rhea Chakraborty Has Underworld Connections!

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Former Bihar CM Jitan Ram Manjhi has made explosive comments about Rhea Chakraborty. He says that the actress has underworld connection as he too believes that Sushant was murdered. Jitan Ram Manjhi also applauded Bihar’s current CM Nitish Kumar for his recommendation of a CBI enquiry in the case. He also said that Maharashtra Govt is guilty, and hence the matter isn’t been investigated properly by Mumbai Police. Rhea Chakraborty had demanded a CBI enquiry in the case after pressure from fans. But when Sushant’s family filed an FIR, she took a U-Turn and filed a petition in Supreme Court stating that the case should remain with Mumbai Police. Sushant’s family has accused her of cheating, blackmailing and forcing Sushant to suicide. After the hearing in Supreme Court, the case has been transferred to CBI and the investigation agency has summoned Rhea for interrogation on Aug 7.
