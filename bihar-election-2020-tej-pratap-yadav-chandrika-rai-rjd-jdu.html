Bihar Election 2020 : Tej Pratap Yadav अपने ससुर Chandrika Rai पर भड़के RJD JDU वनइंडिया हिंदी

22 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The date of Bihar assembly election is yet to be announced. But political mercury has started climbing. Chandrika Rai joined Lalu Prasad Yadav’s Janata Dal United. On Friday, erupted when former Bihar Health Minister Tej Pratap Yadav was asked questions regarding father-in-law Chandrika Rai’s joining the JDU.
