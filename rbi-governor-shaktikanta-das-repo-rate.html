08 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Reserve Bank of India’s monetary policy review meeting is over. This meeting lasted for three days. But people expected it. Water fell on him again. No decision has been taken in this meeting regarding the repo rate. The change in repo rate means that you will not get new relief on EMI or loan interest rates. Reserve Bank Governor Shaktikanta Das informed about the results of the meeting. He said that it has been decided to keep the repo rate at 4 percent.
