सचिन तेंडुलकरने अन्न Waste ना करण्याचा दिला संदेश

25 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Team India former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar has been quite active on social media during the lockdown. Now he has posted a video urging his fans and followers to not waste food. Sachin asks his fans to live a sustainable life and protect the nature. Previously he had shared a video of himself playing in the rains and asked his fans to never let their inner child get lost. Before that he had posted a video of himself making mango kulfi with his daughter Sara Tendulkar. It can be said that Sachin is having a gala time at home during the lockdown.
