Bollywood Hookstep Challenge ft Kashika & Krithika - POPxo Game On

16 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Bollywood dance numbers have all the masala or you can call it ‘the inseparable ingredients’ of pumping beats, groovy music and some signature moves. No wonder some steps are so infectious that they become a rage amongst the millennials.
After all, as they say - anybody can dance, right?
So, we at #POPxo decided to have an in-house #BollywoodHookStepChallenge. Two teams face off for the ultimate Bollywood hook step challenge where they are given with a popular dance number from Bollywood and they have to catch and perform the famous hook step for that song.
The one who does the hook step right gets a point. And, if they are able to guess the hook step of Bollywood song along with the movie, they get an additional point for that too! No points given to the one who guesses the movie but fails to do the hook step.
You might find this funny Bollywood dance quiz quite entertaining at the outset, and we bet you won’t be able to resist trying this at home with your buds!
