Mint Tins Spelling Out COVID 19 Fall With Domino Effect

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

These guys decided to get creative during the lockdown and use old mint boxes to play out a trick. They placed them strategically next to each other while spelling out ‘COVID 19’. As one of the guys pushed the first mint box, most of them fell to the floor one by one, with the domino effect. The chain ended to a note saying "Stay Safe".
