Virat Kohli shares 'Fly Pushup' Video on Instagram after Seeing Hardik's Workout वनइंडिया हिंदी

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Virat Kohli on Thursday made an attempt to perfecting all-rounder Hardik Pandya’s jumping push-ups challenge and ended-up acing it as he added a clap twist to it. Virat Kohli, who is known for his unbelievable fitness standards was certainly impressed after seeing all-rounder’s unique work-out video and decided to attempt the challenge.
