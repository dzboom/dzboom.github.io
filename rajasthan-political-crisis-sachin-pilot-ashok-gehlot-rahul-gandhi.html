Rajasthan Political Crisis: Sachin Pilot ने Ashok Gehlot की खोली पोल Rahul Gandhi वनइंडिया हिंदी

18 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Political mercury still continues in Rajasthan. After several days of dragging, Congress has shown Sachin Pilot a way out of the party. Sachin Pilot refused to join the BJP and is making further strategies. Meanwhile, a big news has come out. And this is disclosed by Sachin Pital. Sachin Pilot, in a special conversation with India Today magazine, said that Ashok Gehlot has dominated the gang party since Rahul Gandhi left the post.
