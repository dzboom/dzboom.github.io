Negotiations between the Indian and Chinese military continue .. But the tension on the Ladakh border has not subsided yet. China is not deterring its habits in the Galvan valley of Ladakh. In the area of ​​Pangong Lake, China has made a significant number of its bases. Not only this, in the Mandarin language, the dragon has written China on the plains surface, while he has also made a map of his country. These things are revealed through the latest satellite photos of Galvan
