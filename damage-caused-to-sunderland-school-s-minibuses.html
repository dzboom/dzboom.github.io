Damage caused to Sunderland school's minibuses

14 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

St Aidan’s Catholic Academy mini buses used to deliver daily food parcels to families each day since school closure have been damaged in an attempted theft.
It was reported that between 10pm on Saturday and 3pm on Sunday offenders had cut the lock to the school’s gates and caused damage to two vehicles that were parked inside.
