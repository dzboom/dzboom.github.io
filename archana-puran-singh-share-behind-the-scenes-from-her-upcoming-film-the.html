Archana Puran Singh Share Behind The Scenes From Her Upcoming Film The Walk

27 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Alongside The Kapil Sharma Show, actor Archana Puran Singh has kickstarted her Bollywood film shoot, the actor recently shot the behind the scenes from the set, sharing the video, Archana wrote, "Behind the Scenes of a film shoot. Each time I go on a film set I feel both nervous and excited. But more nervous. I feel I won’t be able to act. I wonder "how did I act earlier? Ab toh nahin hoga". And then the first shot is canned. The director yells ok. And the exhilaration is euphoric. I tell myself : "You can act. Its ok!" I guess it’s like cycling. You can get rusty. But you never forget how. And you definitely don’t forget how it makes you feel. Film called ‘The Walk’ produced by @officialrahulroy."
