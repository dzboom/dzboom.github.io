Myanmar landslide में 100 से ज्यादा लोगों की गई जान Jade mine landslide वनइंडिया हिंदी

10 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

A painful accident has emerged in Myanmar. Here more than 100 people have died due to landslides in the Jade Mine. After the landslide in northern Myanmar, the bodies of at least 100 jade miners were evacuated after the landslide on Thursday. The Myanmar Fire Services Department said in a Facebook post that workers were depositing stones in the Jade-rich Hapkant region of Kachin state after the heavy rains near the Chinese border in Kachin state, according to news agency Reuters. Where the miners died due to landslides caused by the mining. And big news of the day.
