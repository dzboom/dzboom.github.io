Karan Johar and Rakesh Roshan at New Year Party- Hrithik Roshan a fine actor

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Bollywood filmmaker and director Rakesh Roshan with Karan Johar at a New Year party. While speaking to press at the event this is what Mr Roshan has to say about his son and actor Hrithik Roshan. Karan Johar and Rakesh Roshan are seen embracing each other at the event. Rakesh Roshan: Hrithik as a son has made me very proud. Hrithik as a star he is shining. His goodness shows on his face. He is a fine actor. In his 5 years career, different kinds off role. He has proved himself.
