02 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

More dangerous disease has come in the country than the corona virus. The first case of this disease has been seen in Surat, Gujarat. Symptoms of this disease have been seen in a child in Surat. This disease is called Multi System Inflammatory Syndrome. It is also called MIS-C. After the first case surfaced, the concern of people in Surat and Gujarat has increased.Watch video,
