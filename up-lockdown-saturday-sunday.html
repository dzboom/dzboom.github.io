12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Yogi government of UP is working on a new plan to deal with the corona virus. And now the Yogi government is going to implement the weekend lockdown formula to deal with Corona. In order to prevent corona infection, UP will now have a weekend lockdown every week. There will be a lockdown on Saturday and Sunday in the state every week.
