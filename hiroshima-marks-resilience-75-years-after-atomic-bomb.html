Hiroshima marks resilience 75 years after atomic bomb

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Hiroshima, Aug 6 (EFE/EPA).- The worst predictions after the atomic bomb was dropped on Hiroshima indicated that nothing would grow in the area for 75 years. But on Thursday, exactly three-quarters of a century later, commemorations attested to the resilience of this Japanese city. (Camera: DAI KUROKAWA. Editor: HATAIPHAN TUNGKANANUKULCHAI).
