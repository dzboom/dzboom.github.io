Sushant Singh Case: Rhea Chakraborty बोलीं- अब मैं भी चाहती हूं आत्महत्या कर लूं वनइंडिया हिंदी

31 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Around two months after the death of actor Sushant Singh Rajput, girlfriend Riya Chakraborty has broken her silence. In the interview given to the news channel till date, Riya has clarified for the first time the allegations leveled against her. Riya has called all the allegations against him baseless. During this, Riya said that if I commit suicide, who will be responsible for this.
