Shoaib Akhtar : Story of Rawalpindi Express Shoaib Akhtar who terrorised batsman वनइंडिया हिंदी

16 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Shoaib Akhtar is one of the fastest bowlers to have ever played the game. A colourful character, both on and off the field, Shoaib polarised teammates and fans. Shoaib was born in Rawalpindi, Punjab, which is why he got his famous Rawalpindi Express nickname. Shoaib revealed in an interview with The Cricket Monthly that his friends used to think he was mad for doing so, but the entire exercise gave him the necessary muscles to bowl fast. In years to come, he emerged as one of the fastest bowlers the game had ever seen.
