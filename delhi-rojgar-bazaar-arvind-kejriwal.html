Delhi Rojgar Bazaar: Arvind Kejriwal ने लॉन्‍च किया रोजगार बाजार, मुफ्त में नौकरी वनइंडिया हिंदी

31 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The spread of Corona virus infection in the country’s capital has decreased in the last few weeks. Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal associates this success with the ‘Delhi Model’. Along with this, CM Kejriwal also announced the launch of the portal named He said that both the job seekers and job seekers can benefit from this portal. CM Kejriwal has named it Rozgaar Bazaar.
