The Star Owls podcast July 20

22 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

An incredibly frustrating afternoon for Sheffield Wednesday has boss Garry Monk and supporters alike raging. Attempting to keep a level head, The Star’s Owls writing duo Alex Miller and Joe Crann join Liam Hoden for our latest podcast episode to reflect on the defeat at Fulham that raised yet more questions about Wednesday going forward. Monk thinks he has the answers, but where might he be looking for that? Our panel discuss defensive dismay, the future of Marmite man Atdhe Nuhiu and the EFL charge, which continues to hang over the Owls with two days to go before the final game of the season. And they discuss that final game too, and Neil Warnock’s latest and possibly last visit to Hillsborough with Middlesbrough.
