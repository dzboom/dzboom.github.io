Georgian artist makes silver pipe from scratch in this ASMR step-by-step

07 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Georgian jewellery artist, Andy Frol creates a unique sterling silver pipe from scratch in this cool ASMR step-by-step video from February 20.
Using classic metalwork techniques, combined with modern technologies such as a blow torch, Frol reveals the secrets of an ancient process and makes this one of a kind object, that he says is inspired by the work of modern Ukrainian poet Serhiy Zhadan.
