How To Make McDonald's Style Spicy Paneer Wrap At Home - POPxo Yummy

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

On days we are craving to eat something spicy and delicious, a wrap with some delicious fillings of our choice sounds like the best food option. McDonald’s India’s Spicy Paneer Wrap is one such wrap that is loved by kids and adults alike. Filled with veggies and the goodness of paneer, this wrap is the perfect thing you need to treat your taste buds.
In today’s episode of POPxo Yummy, watch as we share with you the simple recipe of how to make McDonald’s Style Spicy Paneer Wrap at home. If you liked the recipe, try and let us know of your experience in the comments section below.
Step 1) Take 2 tbsp corn flour in a bowl.
Step 2) Add 1/4 tbsp salt & pepper, a few drops of water and mix.
Step 3) Take 1 cup of finely blended nacho separately and add 2 tbsp bread crumbs.
Step 4) Add 1/4 tbsp chilli powder & salt to taste and mix.
Step 5) Take thin and long slices of paneer and evenly coat in both mixtures.
Step 6) Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and stir fry the paneer cutlets.
Step 7) Place some lettuce on a tortilla wrap
Step 8) Add some onion, sauces of your choice and place the paneer cutlets.
Step 9) Add some cheese slices on top and wrap.
Step 10) Drizzle some oil on a grill and lightly toast the wrap for a minute.
