Football team hiring DJ to simulate crowd chants with no fans

14 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

From the top of the stands of the Maracanã Stadium in Rio de Janeiro DJ Frank turns a dial on his mixer and the voices of thousands of fans roar across the stadium. As the pandemic left sporting events without fans, Brazilians teams have decided to hire DJs to personify their supporters.
