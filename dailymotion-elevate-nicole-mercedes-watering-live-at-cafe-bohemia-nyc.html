Dailymotion Elevate: Nicole Mercedes - 'Watering' live at Cafe Bohemia, NYC

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Nicole Mercedes sits down for a One On One Session at Cafe Bohemia New York.
For more info visit: Audio & Video by: Ehud Lazin
Setlist:
Hyperballad (Bjork)
Mediterranean
Watering
At Ease
Based out of Brooklyn, Nicole Mercedes is a dream pop multi-instrumentalist and singer/songwriter who creates a sound that is perfectly pop and haunting all at once.
Collaborating with producer Joe Rogers (Laura Stevenson, Rubblebucket, etc.), and mostly recording in her bedroom, Mercedes has achieved that elusive coexistence of brain-staining tunefulness and hauntingly evocative aura – the stuff of long afternoons gazing through rainy panes, or the throbbing craving for even temporary escape.
