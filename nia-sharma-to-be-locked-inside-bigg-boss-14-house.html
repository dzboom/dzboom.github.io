Nia Sharma To Be Locked Inside Bigg Boss 14 House?

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

India’s most controversial yet entertaining show Bigg Boss is all set to return with its 14th season. The makers on Sunday shared the first teaser of the show, which was shot at Salman Khan’s Panvel bungalow. Fans have been eagerly waiting for the contestant’s list but makers haven’t hinted anything about it. However, the production hasn’t confirmed any names yet but a source close to the show has revealed that Nia Sharma is finally on board the 14th season. Initially, the Naagin star rejected the offer but the makers tried their level best to convince her to the show. Reportedly, "Nia was having a lot of apprehensions. In fact, she refused it initially but finally, she is convinced and will get locked inside the BB house this year." Nia is quite a popular TV personality and she’s currently seen in Khatron Ke Khiladi Made In India. As per reports, celebs such as Amir Siddiqui, Shubhangi Atre, Surbhi Jyoti, Jasmin Bhasin and Chhahat Khanna have been approached by the makers but nothing has been confirmed yet.
