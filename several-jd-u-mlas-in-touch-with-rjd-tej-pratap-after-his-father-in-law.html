Several JD(U) MLAs in touch with RJD: Tej Pratap after his father-in-law switched sides

30 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Chandrika Rai, father-in-law of RJD leader Tej Pratap Yadav joined JD(U) on August 20. Speaking on this, Tej Pratap Yadav said many JD(U) MLAs are in touch with RJD and will give news within 4 to 5 days. “Janta Dal (United) won’t gain anything from Chandrika Rai joining their party. In fact, many JD(U) MLAs are in touch with us and we will give you news of them joining RJD within 4-5 days,” said Yadav.
