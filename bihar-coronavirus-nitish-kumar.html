Report

There is no doubt that Nitish Kumar is responsible for what is happening in Bihar. The responsibility is also made on Nitish Kumar because he kept the command in his hand from the beginning of the Corona crisis. Their biggest mistake came from the initiative to stop the movement of special trains in the month of March, because the infection did not spread that much in March and if they allowed the workers to come at this time, then they would not have to suffer in the outside states.Watch video,
