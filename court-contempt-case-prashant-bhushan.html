Senior advocate Prashant Bhushan has been found guilty in the contempt of court. The Supreme Court on Friday ruled in a contempt proceeding against Advocate Prashant Bhushan for allegedly making two derogatory tweets to the judiciary. Now the sentence will be heard on 20 August. It is now being discussed that Prashant Bhushan will be punished. So let us tell you that the law under which Prashant Bhushan has been found guilty. How much punishment can be found in it?
