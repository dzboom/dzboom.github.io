Smoke Billows Out of Mask When Woman Tries to Vape With Mask On

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This woman wanted to see the outcome of vaping while wearing her mask. While seated in her car, she drew in the vapor in her mouth and covered her face with the mask again. When she released the breath, smoke billowed out of the mask dramatically, making her cough.
