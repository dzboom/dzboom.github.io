Viral images: Satellite से ली गई Day and Night के मिलन की खूबसूरत तस्वीरें वनइंडिया हिंदी

07 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Astronaut Robert L Behnken shared stunning images showing the boundary between day and night on Earth. Two incredible pictures of the boundary between day and night shared by an astronaut have created a buzz on social media. The photos were shared by astronaut Robert L Behnken, nicknamed Bob, on Twitter. He is currently aboard the International Space Station.
