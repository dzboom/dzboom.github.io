Swara Bhaskar Comes In Support Of Rhea Chakraborty

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Actor Swara Bhaskar has come out in support of Rhea Chakraborty. Her support comes after Rhea approached the Supreme Court against the media trial that she has been facing in Sushant’s death case. Supporting Rhea, Swara wrote on Twitter, “Rhea is being subjected to a bizarre & dangerous media trial fuel led by hugely problematic impulses of mob justice. I hope the Hon’ble #SupremeCourt will pay heed & take fake news spinners & conspiracy theorists like Republic, Poopindia & others to task. Let the law decide.” Swara’s tweet has angered people who are now trolling the actress. A user slammed Swara and wrote, “This woman is talking about law, while a few months back she was provoking masses not to have any faith in the Supreme Court and supported mob justice.” Meanwhile Rhea, her brother and father have been questioned twice at the ED office.
