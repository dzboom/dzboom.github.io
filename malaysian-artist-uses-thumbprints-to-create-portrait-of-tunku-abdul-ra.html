Malaysian artist uses thumbprints to create portrait of Tunku Abdul Rahman

29 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

In conjunction with National Day, speed painter Vivian Ng decided to create a portrait of Malaysia’s first prime minister, Tunku Abdul Rahman, using only thumbprints.
The 28-year-old lass hopes her work, titled ‘Hargai Keharmonian’, will remind Malaysians to appreciate the nation’s freedom enjoyed for the past 63 years.
