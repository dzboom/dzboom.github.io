Report

Sushant Singh Rajput case: Bihar Police DGP urges Actress Rhea Chakraborty to join investigation. Amid a call from several NDA leaders for a CBI probe into the death of actor Sushant Singh Rajput, Bihar DGP Gupteshwar Pandey on Saturday said the state police are competent enough to deal with it.
