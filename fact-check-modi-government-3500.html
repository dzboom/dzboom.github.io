Report

There is no scheme by the Centre under the name of ‘Pradhan Mantri Berojgaar Bhatta Yojana’ in which it is providing an unemployment allowance of Rs 3,500 per month, the Fact Check Unit of the Press Information Bureau (PIB) clarified today.Watch video,
