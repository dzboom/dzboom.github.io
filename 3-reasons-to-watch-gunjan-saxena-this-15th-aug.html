3 Reasons To Watch Gunjan Saxena This 15th Aug

16 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl is finally released on an OTT platform and it has received good reviews from critics. For the last many years, we have seen a patriotic film releasing on 15th August. But this year is different with no cinema halls open and no patriotic movie to give us goosebumps. But you can still watch Janhvi Kapoor’s Gunjan Saxena at the comfort of your home. And here are 3 reasons to watch it. Firstly, because it is a real life story of a real hero. It is based on former IAF officer Gunjan Saxena who played an important role during the Kargil War. If for no reason, watch it to know the struggles and achievements of the Kargil Hero. The film has power-packed performance by every actor. Janhvi is both innocent and fierce. Angad Bedi too does justice to the role. But the winner here is Pankaj Tripathi who will win your heart. Even though India got independence on 15th August 1947, but it has taken decades for the women of this country to be independent. The film showcases the liberation of a girl who not only chose to dream in a patriarchal society but also achieved it fighting all the odds. For an inspiring and emotional story, this film deserves a watch.
