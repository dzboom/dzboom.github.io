Kerala Plane Crash: Big accident postponed at Ranchi Airport, bird crashed into aircraft engine. After the painful aircraft accident in Kerala, another aircraft accident in the country was averted. The bird collided during take-off from the Air Asia aircraft going from Ranchi to Mumbai. All passengers are said to be safe.
