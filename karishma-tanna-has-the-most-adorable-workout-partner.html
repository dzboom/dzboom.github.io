Karishma Tanna Has The Most Adorable Workout Partner

04 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Actress Karishma Tanna’s new workout partner is her four legged friend Koko. Karishma took to her Instagram, where she posted a motley picture of herself doing yoga. Her pet dog Koko can be seen besides her. Sharing the image, Karishma wrote, "Sunday workout wit my fur ball. My munchkin @koko_tanna #mood #love #koko." Karishma was recently announced the winner of season 10 of the adventure reality show, "Khatron Ke Khiladi". She took to Instagram and penned a lengthy note, sharing how she broke several stereotypes to achieve her dreams in life.
