Ku Li- Bersatu shouldn't come into the picture

07 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Umno advisor Tengku Razaleigh Hamzah said, problems would arise if Umno continue their cooperation with Parti Peribumi Bersatu Malaysia in the next general election.
In an interview with Channel News Asia, he said Bersatu should not come into the picture as Umno members feel it should be just Muafakat Nasional along with traditional partners of Barisan Nasiona, which are MCA and MIC.
