Clueless kid Insists on Going for Potty Break Amidst her Soldier dad's Homecoming Surprise

07 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This kid was clueless about her soldier dad’s homecoming. Her mom made her wait outside the house for her surprise. While waiting, she insisted on going to the washroom for a potty break and innocently almost ruined the surprise. Thankfully, her dad showed up at the right time, and she forgot about her potty urges. They hugged each other and got emotional on reuniting.
