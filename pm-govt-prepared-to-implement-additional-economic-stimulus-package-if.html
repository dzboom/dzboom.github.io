PM: Govt prepared to implement additional economic stimulus package if needed

02 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The government is prepared to implement an additional economic stimulus package or other initiatives to help the people in facing economic constraints if necessary and if the government’s financial situation allows it, says Prime Minister Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin.
He said this when officiating the closing of the Perikatan Nasional Administration’s Half-Year Convention on Tuesday.
