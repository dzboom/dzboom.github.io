Barnsley's Big Weekend: 50th Virtual Mayor's Parade

14 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

BARNSLEY’S Big Weekend of online fun will celebrate the 50th Mayor’s Parade virtually - due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
Community organisations and businesses have this year entered video clips of what would have been their floats and entertainment, which will be shared as a Barnsley Council Facebook Page watch party on Saturday, July 11, at 1pm - visit
There will also be a special showing of the 1985 May’s Parade.
Comedy, dance, live music and more will also feature in two days of family fun.
Follow #BarnsleysBigWeekendOnline for more info and watch on Barnsley Council’s Facebook channels on Saturday and Sunday. July 10 and 11.
