Bollywood and Underworld - Bharat Shah investigates, Salman Khan called for clarification

05 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The connection of Bollywood and the Underworld has been a subject of thrilling and also the root of many cases in the film industry. In the case of alleged nexus by Nazim Rizvi and Bharat Shah and few others with the underworld. The search at the offices and property by the Mumbai Police. Reporters pressing the police officers even after their request to talk to the superiors. Reporters and cameraman swarm one of the buildings belonging to Bharat Shah and his VIP enterprises, a business and film production house. During a press conference Police clarifies the actions taken towards Bharat Shah following the allegations. Police also slams the media for creating panic in the film industry by writing stories of arresting Bollywood actors and personalities. The police officer also recounts incidents involving film personalities and the Underworld and stressed on the service of Mumbai Police in trying to curb the Underworld. In the making of the movie ‘Chori Chori Chupke’ starring Salman Khan, Preity Zinta and Rani Mukerji, the producer Nazim Rizvi and his associate businessman Bharat Shah were accused of their links with the underworld.
