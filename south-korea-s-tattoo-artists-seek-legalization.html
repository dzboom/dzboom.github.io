South Korea's tattoo artists seek legalization

12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

South Korean tattooist Doy counts Hollywood superstar Brad Pitt and members of K-Pop band EXO among his celebrity clients, but his delicate, detailed designs could land him in prison. Now he is leading a campaign for the abolition of a law that reflects tattoos’ long-marginalized status in South Korea, where they were once associated almost exclusively with organized crime.
