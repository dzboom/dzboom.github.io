Trailer for The Devil All the Time, starring Robert Pattinson, Harry Melling, Tom Holland, Riley Keough and Haley Bennett.
In Knockemstiff, Ohio and its neighboring backwoods, sinister characters – an unholy preacher (Robert Pattinson), twisted couple (Jason Clarke and Riley Keough), and crooked sheriff (Sebastian Stan) – converge around young Arvin Russell (Tom Holland) as he fights the evil forces that threaten him and his family.
Spanning the time between World War II and the Vietnam war, director Antonio Campos’ THE DEVIL ALL THE TIME renders a seductive and horrific landscape that pits the just against the corrupted. This suspenseful, finely-woven tale is adapted from Donald Ray Pollock’s award-winning novel.
