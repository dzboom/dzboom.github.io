Ankita Lokhande Speaks Up In Sushant Singh Rajput Case

03 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After keeping silent for a long time, actress Ankita Lokhande has finally come out to raise her voice in Sushant Singh Rajput case. It’s been over 40 days since Sushant’s mysterious death, but Ankita kept herself away from media. Even her Instagram posts were all about praying for Sushant’s soul resting in peace. And today, for the first time she has shared her views. She wrote on her Instagram ‘Truth Wins’. Her post comes after Sushant Singh Rajput’s father filed an FIR against Rhea Chakraborty accusing her of breaching Sushant’s trust, cheating him and forcing him to take such a drastic step. Without writing much, Ankita has tried to say a lot through her post. A few days ago, Kangana Ranaut had told a news portal that she talked to Ankita about Sushant. Kangana said, “When I spoke to Ankita, she said right from the beginning, there was so much humiliation that he could not take it. This is how she summed it up. She said, over a period, the bad PR, the ganging up, the public humiliation, he just could not take it. He has had enough – that’s what she said.”
