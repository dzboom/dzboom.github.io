Sushant Rajput Case: Aditya Chopra और Sanjay Leela Bansali के बयानों में बड़ा अंतर वनइंडिया हिंदी

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The statement of Yash Raj Films’ Aditya Chopra in the Sushant Singh suicide case was recorded at Versova police station from around 8:30 am to 12:30 pm. He was called to the Versova police station to discuss the Sushant Singh case being investigated by the Bandra police. According to police sources, a considerable difference has been found in the statements of Aditya Chopra and Sanjay Leela Bhansali.
