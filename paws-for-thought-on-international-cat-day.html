Paws for thought on International Cat Day

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Videos of frolicking felines and gifs of purring kitties will be how most animal enthusiasts mark International Cat Day on Saturday, August 8.
But for owners who believe their pets are scratchy, aggressive and mean, the problem is probably you, according to a Hong Kong-based cat consultant who has owned 50 in her lifetime, "a lot of the time it has more to do with the pet owners than the cats."
