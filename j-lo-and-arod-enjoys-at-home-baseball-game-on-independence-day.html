J’Lo And Arod Enjoys At-Home Baseball Game On Independence Day

14 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

"Jennifer Lopez celebrated July Fourth with a backyard baseball game that included all members of her family. The 50-year-old ""Let’s Get Loud"" singer and her 44-year-old fiance Alex Rodriguez gathered their loved ones and squared off against each other in their vast backyard at Long Island , New York, for a friendly game. The former baseball player took the day to share a clip on Instagram, which showed J-Lo giving her her best shot.
Meanwhile like so many others who were planning a wedding even before to the pandemic, the star is dealing with the same disappointment of not knowing when she will say ""I do.""
Well,via video chat at the "" Today Show"" she said “I’m a little heartbroken because we did have some great plans but I’m also like, you know what? God has a bigger plan so we just have to wait and see,” Jlo added, “Maybe it’s going to be better. I have to believe that it will be.”For now, J.Lo, claims she and A-Rod have postponed their plans. “There’s no planning right now. You just have to kind of wait and see how this all plays out.”
