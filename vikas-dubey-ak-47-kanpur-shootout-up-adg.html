Vikas Dubey के घर से मिली AK-47, Kanpur Shootout में लूटे गए हथियार बरामद UP ADG वनइंडिया हिंदी

15 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After the encounter of gangster Vikas Dubey, another accused of Kanpur shootout Shashikant has been arrested by the police. With this, the weapons looted from the policemen have been recovered. ADG Law and Order Prashant Kumar held a press conference today. In which he said that many weapons looted from the police have been recovered from Bikaru village.
