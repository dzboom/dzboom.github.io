Beauty vlogger Anna Cay isn’t only a household name in the beauty community for her sought-after honest reviews and makeup expertise (she recently did a makeup look you can totally wear with a mask on!), but also her luxury haul videos whenever she travels. So it was only natural that we asked her to share with us her top five designer items for this episode of Designer Favorites.
