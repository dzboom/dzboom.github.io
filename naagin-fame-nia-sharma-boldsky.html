Naagin Fame Nia Sharma बनी दुल्हन । शादी को लेकर किया बड़ा खुलासा । Boldsky

19 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Nia Sharma is currently working in Naagin 4 Serial. In a picture shared on Instagram, Nia is dressed in a bridal look in white and red lehenga. The blouse of Gota Patti Work and the white net dupatta have also been painted with red color. At the same time, Nia is looking gorgeous with red bangles and heavy golden neckpieces in her hands.
