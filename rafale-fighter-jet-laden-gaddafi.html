Rafale Fighter Jet: Laden की फौज से लेकर Gaddafi की फौज पर कहर बरपा चुका है राफेल वनइंडिया हिंदी

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The moment that the country was waiting for has now arrived. The landing of Rafale fighter jets took place at Ambala airbase in Haryana on Wednesday afternoon. This is the first batch of aircraft coming from France. When the five fighter planes reached Ambala, they were welcomed here through the water salute. The plane left France on Monday. So let us tell you the history of Rafael. This Rafale carried out dangerous military operations in Afghanistan, Libya, Mali, Iraq and Syria
