30 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Known as the "King of Pop," Michael Jackson, who was born on this day in 1958, was a multi-talented musical entertainer who enjoyed a chart-topping career both with the Jackson 5 and as a solo artist. He released one of the best-selling albums in history, ‘Thriller,’ in 1982, and had other number-one hits on ‘Bad’ and ‘Off the Wall.’.
