JEE-Main Exam 2020 Guidelines: Candidates Must Follow Rules JEE-Main Exam 2020 Guidelines

05 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Engineering entrance exam, JEE-Main exams began in the country on Sept 01. The candidates reached to their respective exam centers with mask on amid COVID-19 pandemic. They were seen queuing outside the exam centers at various places. They were provided hand sanitizers at the examination centers. Candidates were allowed in the exam halls with valid admit cards. Body temperature was also being checked of the candidates. Engineering entrance exam, JEE-Main, scheduled to be held from September 1 to 6.
