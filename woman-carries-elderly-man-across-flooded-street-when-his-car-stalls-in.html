Woman Carries Elderly Man Across Flooded Street When His Car Stalls in Middle of Road

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This woman came to the aid of an aged man whose car was stalled in the middle of a flooded street after heavy rainfall. She waded through the waterlogged road and carried him in her arms across the road. She and her sister later drove him and his wife to their home.
