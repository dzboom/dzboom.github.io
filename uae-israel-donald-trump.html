16 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Israel and the UAE have signed the historic peace deal, forgetting the years of enmity. The special thing is that US President Donald Trump has played an important role in reaching this deal. The deal will also mark the beginning of a new diplomatic relationship between the UAE and Israel. The White House has reported that the agreement has postponed Israel’s plans to occupy the West Bank area.
