China confirms WHO experts have arrived in Beijing for coronavirus origin search

21 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

China has confirmed that two World Health Organisation experts are in Beijing and working with Chinese counterparts to identify the source of the Covid-19 pandemic. Hua Chunying, a Chinese foreign ministry spokeswoman, said on July 13, 2020, that work to trace the origin of the coronavirus will also take place in other countries.  Video source: CCTV
