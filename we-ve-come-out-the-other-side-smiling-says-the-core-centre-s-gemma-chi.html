We’ve come out the other side smiling!
That’s the positive message from The Core Centre manager Gemma Chidgey who says retailers and shoppers are happy to be back after an ease of lockdown.She was speaking  in the latest #BacktoLeeds interview series from LeedsBID as part of a Welcome Back To Leeds campaign to help restart the city centre’s economy.Gemma told how they are opening back up slowly but surely and what a great vibe Leeds has right now.
