Sheffield illustrator launches duo of mindful books for kids aged 5-8

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The books, ‘Chill Out’ and ‘It’ll Be Okay,’ are illustrated by Josephine Dellow and published by Upside Down Books, and the proceeds from the release will go to The Shaw Mind Foundation - a global charity that focuses entirely on mental health.
The books, aimed at children aged 5-8, encourage children and young people to embrace their feelings.
