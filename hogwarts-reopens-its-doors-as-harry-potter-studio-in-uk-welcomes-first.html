‘Hogwarts’ reopens its doors as Harry Potter studio in UK welcomes first visitors since pandemic

25 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Class is back in session at “Hogwarts”, at least for visitors who are again allowed through the magical doors of the studios where much of the Harry Potter film series was filmed in Leavesden, Hertfordshire, UK. Closed since March 2020 because of the coronavirus pandemic, the Warner Bros Studio Tour London – The Making of Harry Potter reopened on August 20.
