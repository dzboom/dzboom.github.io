We tried four popular lip glosses from $5 to $85. Here's the best one.

03 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Lip gloss has had a major comeback in recent years. However, most lip glosses don’t have great staying power and need to be reapplied a bunch of times, which can be annoying and also waste product quicker. A perfect lip gloss should be super shiny, not sticky, and last more than a couple of hours. Producer Nico Reyes went on the hunt for the perfect lip gloss. She tried out glosses from NYX, Fenty Beauty, Pat McGrath Labs, and Christian Louboutin to see if she could find one all-around great gloss that lasts through anything.
Lip glosses used in this video:
NYX Butter Gloss, $5
Fenty Beauty Gloss Bomb Lip Luminizer, $19
Pat McGrath Labs LUST: Lip Gloss, $28
Christian Louboutin Loubilaque Lip Gloss, $85
This video is not sponsored. Insider purchased all products for the purpose of reviewing for this video.
