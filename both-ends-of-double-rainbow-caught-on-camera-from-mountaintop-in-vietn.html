Both ends of double rainbow caught on camera from mountaintop in Vietnam

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Two beautiful parallel rainbows were filmed from a mountaintop in Yen Bai, Vietnam.
According to the filmer, he accidentally discovered the two rainbows while watching the scenery from the top of the mountain on June 30.
The rainbows appeared after a rain shower and crossed two valleys at the foot of the mountain.
This was the first time the man had seen two rainbows parallel to each other.
