Report

After Maharashtra, heavy rains have caused havoc in other states too .. The department has issued warnings of heavy rains in many states of the country from August 7 to August 9. Heavy rains may occur over parts of Kerala, Odisha, Karnataka and West Bengal … The Meteorological Department has forecast rain over the next three days over a large area of ​​Gujarat and has said that it is expected to continue throughout the week in central and northern Kerala.
