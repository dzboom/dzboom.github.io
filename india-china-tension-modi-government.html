Report

There has been a lot of bitterness in relations between India and China these days. Recently, there was anger among the people due to the manner in which there was a clash between the soldiers of the two countries and many soldiers of India were martyred on the border. Chinese goods are being boycotted somewhere. Meanwhile, another news is coming that China may get another blow this week. Now the government is going to implement such a rule, which can affect China badly. So let’s tell you about the new rule.
