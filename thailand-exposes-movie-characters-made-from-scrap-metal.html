Thailand exposes movie characters made from scrap metal

06 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

An hour from Bangkok, in Ang Thong, there is a warehouse full of figures from the world of cinema built over a decade of scrap metal. In this particular "museum" we can find replicas of Spiderman, the Transformers, Predator, Hulk or various Tony Stark armor.
