Acrobatic Duo Fails as They Crash Into Each Other While Making Formation

10 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This acrobatic duo was practicing for their performance. One guy was on his back on a chair with his feet in the air, while the other guy tried to perform a backflip over his feet. Unfortunately, the guy on the top couldn’t land properly and crashed into the one on the chair. Thankfully, they didn’t seem hurt and perfected their trick post a couple of more attempts with hard work and dedication.
