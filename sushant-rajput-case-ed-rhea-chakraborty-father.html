Sushant Rajput Case: ED ने Rhea Chakraborty के Father को भेजा समन वनइंडिया हिंदी

29 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Enforcement Directorate has again summoned Indrajeet Chakraborty, father of actor Rhea Chakraborty, in connection with Sushant Singh Rajput death case, an official said on Thursday. Earlier, Rhea and her brother Showik Chakraborty were also questioned by the ED regarding the case.Watch video,
