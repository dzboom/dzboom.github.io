How To Talk To Your Boss About Stress

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Millions of people lost their jobs when the pandemic hit.
So, having a steady job amid the pandemic can be considered a privilege.
Even people who still have their jobs during the coronavirus can experience stress and anxiety.
Business Insider suggested talking to your boss or company if you’re experiencing issues during the pandemic.
You can write down specifically what you need, like a few days off or a lightened workload.
You should also ask for it sooner rather than later.
Business Insider reminds you not to wait until you’ve reached your breaking point to seek help.
