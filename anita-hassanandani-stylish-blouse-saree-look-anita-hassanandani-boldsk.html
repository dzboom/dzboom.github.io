Anita Hassanandani के Stylish Blouse और Saree Look में ढ़ाया कहर Anita Hassanandani Boldsky

12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Anita, the actress of the small screen, is in the headlines for her acting as well as the glamorous look on TV. Apart from appearing in Naagin’s negative role in Naagin 4, she has also appeared in Star Plus show Yeh Hai Mohabbatein. Many of the girls in her stylish blouses and sarees worn in this serial were crazy. Even still their looks remain in discussion and can be copied easily. So if you want fashion inspiration for a stylish blouse, then Anita’s look is very special.
