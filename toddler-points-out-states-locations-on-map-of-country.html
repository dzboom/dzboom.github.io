Toddler Points Out States Locations on Map Of Country

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This brainy toddler showed his grandma his knowledge of the states’ locations on a map. He pointed out whatever state his mom and grandma asked him to locate and corrected himself if he didn’t get it right. His grandma looked very impressed and appreciated the kid with celebratory claps as soon as he answered their questions correctly.
