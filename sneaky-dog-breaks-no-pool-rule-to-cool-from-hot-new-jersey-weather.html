Sneaky dog breaks 'no pool' rule to cool from hot New Jersey weather

19 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Charlie, a sneaky dog in Ocean County, New Jersey, decides to cool off from the hot summer heat on Thursday (July 16) in a pool that his grandparents have clearly declared to be for no dogs.
"My parents have a pool with a strict no dogs allowed policy. No dog including their own has been allowed in the pool, until now. Charlie thinks that rule doesn’t apply to him, so he went for a swim and cooled off," said Aaron Talasnik, who can be found @ATalasnik on Twitter.
