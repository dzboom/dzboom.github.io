Mitchell Starc’s Wife Alyssa Healy slams a Twitter user for making a meme on hubby. Australian women’s cricketer Alyssa Healy recently took a stand for her husband and Australian pacer Mitchell Starc against a meme. A Twitter user made a joke on Starc for choosing T20 World Cup 2020 over IPL 2020 as now the World Cup stands cancelled.
