Family of black man killed by LA police demands justice

05 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Around 100 protesters march and demand answers in a south Los Angeles neighbourhood where sheriff’s deputies shot and killed 29-year-old Dijon Kizzee, a black man initially stopped for a cycling violation which led to a violent confrontation the previous day.
"Two officers emptied their whole complete clip into my nephew’s back. And he had no weapons. It has to stop." says Sequarier McCoy, the aunt of Dijon Kizzee.
