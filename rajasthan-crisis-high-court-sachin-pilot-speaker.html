Rajasthan Crisis पर High Court में Sachin Pilot और Speaker के वकीलों ने दी ये दलील वनइंडिया हिंदी

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The political arrogance that has been going on in Rajasthan for the past several days is not taking the name of the end. On one side there is a verbal war on the road. At the same time, the battle is also being fought in the High Court of Rajasthan. What plea was laid in the Rajasthan High Court on Tuesday. Let’s tell you Let us first say that a petition was filed on behalf of the Sachin Pilot faction against the notice of the Speaker. Mukul Rohatgi presented arguments on behalf of Sachin Pilot faction.
