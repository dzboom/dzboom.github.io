Indian Railway: Railway Board के पहले CEO बने Vinod Kumar Yadav वनइंडिया हिंदी

04 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The Indian Railway Board has appointed its first CEO. Railway Board chairman VK Yadav has been appointed as the CEO of the board. Let us tell you that this is the first time in the history of Railways when the Railway Board has appointed the CEO and VK Yadav has been appointed the first CEO of the Railway Board. The Railway Board has appointed the Chairman of the Board VK Yadav as the CEO of the Board. The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved his appointment. Earlier, the Cabinet approved the reorganization of the Railway Board.
