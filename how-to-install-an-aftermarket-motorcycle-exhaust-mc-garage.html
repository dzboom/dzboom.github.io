How To Install An Aftermarket Motorcycle Exhaust MC Garage

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Modern production motorcycle exhaust systems are highly engineered to find the best balance between power, sound decibels, and emissions requirements to keep our air clean. However, when headed to the track, to participate in a close-course event, all you are concerned about is power. Today on MC Garage Red Bull Street Freestyle athlete Aaron Colton shows you the ins and outs of installing a racing exhaust. In this episode he installs the exhaust on an Indian FTR 1200 S, however the installation process is similar for most new and used motorcycles.
