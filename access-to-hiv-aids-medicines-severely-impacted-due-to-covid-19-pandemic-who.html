Access to HIV AIDS medicines severely impacted due to COVID-19 pandemic: WHO

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

유엔 "코로나19가 에이즈 대응 위협…치료제 보급 차질"
The World Health Organization has warned that the world could run out of HIV/AIDS treatment as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
During a virtual international AIDS conference organized by the UN,… the WHO said 24 countries were reporting either a critically low stock or disruptions in the supply of such life-saving drugs.
The head of the WHO stressed that countries need to do all they can to ensure access to the treatment,… adding the world cannot let COVID-19 undo the hard-won gains in the global response to HIV/AIDS.
