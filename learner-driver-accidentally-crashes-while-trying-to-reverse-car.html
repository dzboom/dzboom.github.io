Learner driver accidentally crashes while trying to reverse car

19 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Shocking footage shows a driver losing control and almost hitting a man while trying to pull out of the garage of a house in Hanoi, Vietnam on June 18.
In the video, a woman is seen entering a car but instead of slowly edging forward she starts to accelerate.
The owner of the CCTV camera, Hong Cuu said his wife insisted to drive the car by herself even though she was just starting to learn.
He said: "I thought she was just joking, but she suddenly jumped in the car and accidentally stepped on the gas pedal so the car went forward."
