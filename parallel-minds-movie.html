Parallel Minds Movie Trailer HD - Plot synopsis: Alberta shot sci-fi thriller, Parallel Minds, is about love, greed and ego. Canadian film director and writer, Benjamin Ross Hayden, came to Jump Studios with a distinct vision which was brought to life through the color grade. Jump played off the fluorescent green lights in the lab scenes to enhance the sci-fi feel and applied a sepia tone for the flashback scenes. Check out this vignette to see the end result.
Director: Benjamin Ross Hayden
Writer: Benjamin Ross Hayden
Stars: Greg Bryk, Tommie-Amber Pirie, Madison Walsh
