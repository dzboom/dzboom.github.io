Zoom Is Down

28 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Zoom, the teleconferencing app used around the world, is down.
Today is the day that many schools are back in session.
It’s become the go-to platform for many educators’ remote-teaching.
Now Zoom appears to have crashed for large swaths of the US, according to Gizmodo.
Zoomers across the country have posted on their own accounts that they’ve been unable to log onto the service.
There are an estimated 10,000 reported outages.
