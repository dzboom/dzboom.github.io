Triumph Street Triple R Launched In India

15 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Triumph Motorcycles India launched the Street Triple R in the Indian market. The new performance motorcycle is priced at Rs 8.84 lakh, ex-showroom (India).
The Street Triple R is powered by the same Moto 2 derived 765cc, in-line three-cylinder engine as the RS variant. It features new body graphics, restyled rear-view mirrors, redesigned exhaust and two new colour options: Sapphire Black and Matt Silver Ice.
