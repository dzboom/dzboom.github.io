Rajasthan Political Crisis: Ashok Gehlot Government in Danger. The Congress government of Rajasthan is under threat. State Deputy Chief Minister Sachin Pilot has arrived in Delhi with supporters and has stayed at a resort in Gurugram. Sources say that he is in touch with senior BJP leaders with the support of 19 MLAs.
