Tired Of Watching Screens And Baking Bread? Try Doing This Instead

03 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

When writer David Sedaris moved to England, he started an unusual pair of hobbies: hours-long walks and picking up roadside litter.
According to CNN, Sedaris had walked right into a potent combination of physical, psychological, and environmental benefits.
Not only that, but it’s also a pandemic-friendly activity.
Walking is an excellent, low-impact exercise, and it’s easy to scale the distance and pace to suit any fitness level.
Meanwhile, doing helpful things — such as leaving your local area a little bit cleaner — has been shown to boost happiness.
It’s a great time to do it, too. The pandemic has created a surge of plastic waste, including disposable surgical masks and gloves.
However, if you’re going to help pick up pandemic trash, stay safe: Wear a mask and gloves, and always put litter directly into a sturdy garbage bag.
