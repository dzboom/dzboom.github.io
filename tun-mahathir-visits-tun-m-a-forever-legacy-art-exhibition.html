Tun Mahathir visits Tun M: A Forever Legacy art exhibition

18 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad visited an art exhibition Tun M: A Forever Legacy hosted by the Perdana University which features more than 100 artwork inspired by the Prime Minister by artists from 40 countries.
Dr Mahathir who is also the founding chancellor of the university,was accompanied by his wife Tun Dr Siti Hasmah Mohd Ali.
