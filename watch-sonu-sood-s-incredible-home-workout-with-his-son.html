Watch Sonu Sood's Incredible Home Workout With His Son

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After winning our hearts by helping thousands of migrant workers reach their home amid coronavirus pandemic, Sonu Sood is now winning us by his latest workout video with his son. Sonu has finally got some time for his family and he is spending it in the most productive ways. In the video, he was seen doing some pushups with his son Eshaan. Sonu helped over 20,000 migrant workers by providing them food, essential items and arranging buses, trains and even flights in order to send them home. The reel life villain came out as a real-life hero in times of crisis.
