Three men walk free after High Court acquits them of murder

07 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Three unemployed men escaped the gallows after the Johor Baru High Court found them not guilty over the murder of a businessman at a petrol station some three years ago.
Gan Jia Ji, 22, Yip Kah Hou, 26 and Woon Kian Hui, 25, walked away from the courtroom as free men after the court ruled that the prosecution failed to prove a prima facie case against them.
