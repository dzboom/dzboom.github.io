Tata Motors Health & Hygiene Accessories

13 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Tata Motors has introduced a host of genuine health and hygiene accessories for its models in the Indian market. Tata Motors customers can now purchase a host of health and hygiene accessories for their cars such as air purifiers, health-pro sanitization kits and a few others as well.
The introduction of the health & hygiene accessories is part of the brand’s steps to ensure the safety of their customers, while travelling. The accessories are available on the company’s website and all dealerships across India as well.
