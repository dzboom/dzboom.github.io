The Star Owls podcast July 9

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

The positive start to the resumption of football seems a long time ago for Sheffield Wednesday. The defeat to Preston North End looks like being the end of optimism and the time to start worrying for Garry Monk and co. And so it is a downbeat panel that convene to go over the defeat to the Lilywhites and discuss the major points of concern. The Star’s Owls writers Joe Crann and Alex Miller join Liam Hoden to discuss the declining condition of the squad, just how Monk is going to get his players through the final four matches in just an 11 day period and what shape they will be in for Saturday’s trip to Queens Park Rangers. They do manage to find at least one positive - honest.
