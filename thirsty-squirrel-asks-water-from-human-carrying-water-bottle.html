Thirsty Squirrel Asks Water From Human Carrying Water Bottle

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This squirrel approached a tourist visiting the Grand Canyon and gestured towards their water bottle. The person immediately understood that the squirrel was thirsty and opened the bottle, pouring out the water for them. The animal quenched its thirst with the refreshing drink of water, finishing its entire contents.
