Piyush Goyal का Rahul Gandhi पर तंज,देश को लूटने वाले सब्सिडी को बता सकते हैं मुनाफा वनइंडिया हिंदी

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Former Congress president Rahul Gandhi is an attacker on the Modi government at the Center on various issues these days. Meanwhile, Rahul said on Saturday that the central government is engaged in recovering profits from the poor even in times of disaster. Railway Minister Piyush Goyal has hit back at Rahul. Piyush Goyal has written in a tweet, that only those who loot the country can tell the subsidy is a profit. Railways invested more money in running labor trains than the amount taken from state governments. Now people are asking what happened to Soniaji’s promise to give ticket money?
