Rajasthan: How suspension of two MLAs will impact Pilot camp

23 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

The Congress has suspended two rebel Rajasthan MLAs, Bhanwar Lal and Vishvendra Pratap Singh on Friday. Rajasthan Congress also demanded an FIR against the two MLAs, citing audio recordings in which the MLAs are purportedly heard discussing horse trading and toppling the Ashok Gehlot government.
