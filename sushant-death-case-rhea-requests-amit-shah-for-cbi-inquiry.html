Sushant Death Case: Rhea requests Amit Shah for CBI inquiry

19 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sushant Singh Rajput died by suicide on June 14, a little over a month ago. Today, girlfriend of Sushant Rhea Chakraborty took to social media to request Home Minister Amit Shah to initiate a CBI probe in Sushant Singh Rajput suicide case. Watch video.
