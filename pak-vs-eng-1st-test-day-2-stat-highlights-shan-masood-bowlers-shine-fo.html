PAK vs ENG 1st Test Day 2 Stat Highlights: Shan Masood, Bowlers Shine For Visitors

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

At the close of play on day two of the first Test between England and Pakistan at the Old Trafford Cricket Ground in Manchester, the hosts were 92 for four with Ollie Pope and Jos Buttler batting on 46 and 15 respectively. After Shan Masood’s impressive 156, Pakistan posted 326 in their first innings. In response, England lost four quick wickets as they trail by 234 runs.
