Hardik Pandya reveals name of his Baby Boy through Instagram Story वनइंडिया हिंदी

Hardik Pandya reveals the name of his Newborn Baby Boy in latest Instagram Story. India all-rounder Hardik Pandya and his wife Natasa Stankovic had taken the social media by storm after announcing that they were blessed with a baby boy on July 31. Hardik had shared a picture with Natasa thanking her for giving him the best gift ever.
