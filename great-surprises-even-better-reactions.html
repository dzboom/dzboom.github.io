Great Surprises, Even Better Reactions

09 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Airman first class Scotty hasn’t seen her family in eight long months, so she thought she’d make a surprise visit to the family cookout. The only thing better than her secret surprise is the reactions from her loved ones (especially Mom!) when they see her. Time to celebrate!
And the sweet surprises don’t stop there! JaDacia hasn’t seen her mom, Sherika, since she and her man welcomed their baby girl and moved across the country a year ago. Now, they’re surprising Sherika by flying home — and not only does she get to see them, she also gets to meet her granddaughter for the very first time! This one’s warming our hearts.
