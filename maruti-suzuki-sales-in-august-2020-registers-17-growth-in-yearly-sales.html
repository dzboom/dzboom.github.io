Maruti Suzuki Sales In August 2020 Registers 17% Growth In Yearly-Sales

04 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Maruti Suzuki has announced a growth of 17.1% in yearly-sales for the month of August 2020. The company registered 124,624 units of sales in the previous month, compared to the 106,413 units sold during the same month in 2019. Maruti Suzuki also announced a 20.2% growth in the domestic market as well, although exports sales are said to have dropped during the previous month by 15.3% compared to August 2019.
