Harrdy Sandhu - Dance Like Lauren Gottlieb Jaani B Praak Latest Hit Song

11 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

After the massive success of "Kya Baat Aye", “Naah” & "Backbone", the dream team is back with the most sensational pop single of 2019 - “Dance Like”.
The song has been Written & Composed by Jaani & Arranged by B Praak. Yet again Harrdy Sandhu with his mesmerising vocals creating the magic and has got the gorgeous and super talented Lauren Gottlieb grooving to it.
Song : Dance Like
Artist : Harrdy Sandhu
Starring : Harrdy Sandhu, Lauren Gottlieb
Lyrics & Composition : Jaani
Music : B Praak
Mixing & Mastering : Eric Pillai (Future Sound Of Bombay)
Song Recording : Saffron Studio (Gurjinder Singh & Akash Bambar)
Guitarist : Shomu Seal
Video Director : Keoni Marcelo
Editor : Adele Pereira

Choreographer : Sahaj Singh , Shreoshi Kumar
