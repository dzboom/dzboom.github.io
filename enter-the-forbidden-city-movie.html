Enter the Forbidden City Movie Trailer HD - In Imperial China, two opera singers risk their lives to perform for the Emperor in Beijing and redeem the reputation of their troupe. - Plot synopsis: Yue Jiu, the most famous opera singer in all of Imperial China, is expelled from the capital city for vulgarity. He heads south, where he encounters Wang Runsheng, a promising young opera singer for the Chuntai Troupe. When the Emperor invites all of China’s opera troupes back to the capital city for a celebration, Yue Jiu and Runsheng are forced to choose between their reputations, their safety, and their love of opera.
