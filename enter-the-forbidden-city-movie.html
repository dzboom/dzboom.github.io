Enter the Forbidden City Movie Trailer HD - In Imperial China, two opera singers risk their lives to perform for the Emperor in Beijing and redeem the reputation of their troupe. - Plot synopsis: Yue Jiu, the most famous opera singer in all of Imperial China, is expelled from the capital city for vulgarity.
