Grandma Flinches at Seeing 'Ugly' Baby Photo Before Realizing She Was on Video Call With Parents

13 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

This guy decided to pull the "ugly baby prank" on his grandma. He came up to her to show a friend’s newborn’s photo on his phone. She peers at the phone and grimaces at the photo, finding it displeasing. Her grandson immediately told her that he was on a video call with the baby’s parents, and they saw her reaction. She looks horrified and embarrassed, trying to cover up the awkward moment by looking elsewhere.
