Rajasthan Political Crises : सचिन पायलट ने की घर वापसी,गहलोत रहेंगे CM वनइंडिया हिंदी

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Sachin Pilot, who has taken a rebellious stand against the Ashok Gehlot government in Rajasthan, has now agreed. With this, the political crisis which has been continuing for the last several days is now averted. However, the youth brigade of Congress i.e. Rahul Gandhi and Priyanka Gandhi played the most role behind persuading Sachin Pilot
