Erica Fernandes Finally Opens Up About Her Relationship Status

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona
Report

Post Erica Fernandez’ alleged break up with her Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 co-star Parth Samthaan, the actor now finally share details about her rumoured boyfriend and no, the pretty actor is not dating someone from industry. In an interview, Erica shared her relationship status and reveals how her boyfriend reacts when she’s seen romancing on television.When asked if she’s dating someone from the industry, to which she replied, “I don’t think so, because he is not from the industry.” Talking about her equation with her boyfriend, she mentioned, “Aisa nahi hai ki relationships mein ups and downs nahi hote. Hote zaroor hai. Woh understanding hona bohot zaroori hai ki jab koi gussa hai toh doosra chup rahe aur samjhe. Baad mein, jab sab kuch cool down ho, tab hum discuss karein” She further revealed she’s been dating from last 3 years, although he watches her work up close on television but he doesn’t like watching Erica romance any other guy. Well now we’re eagerly waiting to see the lucky guy in Erica’s life.
